# Wine Not eXp — Monthly Presentation (June 2026)

> [[Gogo Bethke]]'s monthly last-Wednesday-of-the-month recruiting/opportunity walkthrough of the full [[entities/eXp Realty|eXp Realty]] business model — open to the public, hosted with [[entities/Kristy Waker|Kristy Waker]], featuring icon agents [[Stefanie Porter]] and [[Kat West]] — pitched to prospective and existing agents to bring them onto the [[entities/#teamgogo|#teamgogo]] organization.

## Key Takeaways
- **Core eXp economics:** 80/20 split, $16,000 cap nationwide (identical in every US state), $149 at application (includes first month), then $85/month. Per-transaction: $25 broker/transaction fee + $60 E&O (risk-management) fee; E&O caps at $750/year (~13 transactions).
- **After capping:** the 20% split stops and a $250 transaction fee kicks in for the next 20 transactions, then drops to $75/transaction. Pre-cap, an optional 5% stock option also comes out of each commission.
- **ICON program:** cap (~$2.6–2.7M production) PLUS 20 transactions in your anniversary year earns the full $16,000 cap back in company stock; icons sit in the top 6% of the company. Gogo has never iconed but says the program was her #1 reason to join.
- **Mentor program:** brand-new agents (<3 prior transactions) run a 60/40 split for their first 3 deals. Of the 40%: mentor 10%, eXp University 10%, brokerage 20%. A mentor earns ~$1,000/deal × 3 ≈ $3,000 per mentee. Mentor pay does NOT count toward production/units; referrals over $1,000 generally count as commission but do NOT count toward Top 250 (Stefanie missed Top 250 at ~49–52 closings because two were referrals).
- **Five ways to earn stock:** 1 paid — the optional 5% stock-option program (bought at a 5% discount, vested immediately); 4 gifted/awarded (vest after 3 years) — $200 for your first closing each year, $400 for each recruited agent's first closing, $400 at cap, and the full $16,000 at icon.
- **Ticker change:** eXp is NASDAQ-listed (since 2018); the stock ticker changed from **EXPI to AGNT**. Founded 2009; operates in 27 countries.
- **Revenue share (7 generations):** eXp works off 50% of revenue, keeps 10%, and puts 10% into the rev-share bucket paid across 7 down-line generations. Per-generation maximums: L1 $1,400 · L2 $1,600 · L3 $1,000 · L4 $600 · L5 $400 · L6 $1,000 · L7 $2,000. Pays out ~the 20th of each month in real US dollars.
- **NEW first-year-cap bonus:** $4,000 when a recruited frontline agent caps in their first year; $1,400/frontline agent/year thereafter. Level access: L1–3 always open; 5 levels open when capping; all 7 open when icon. **FLQA** (frontline qualifying agent) = has collected ≥$5,000 commission, qualifies for 6 months; need 30 FLQAs to open L7.
- **Co-sponsorship 5-day rule:** existing eXp agents (not yet under Gogo) can name a co-sponsor to unlock all #teamgogo benefits — but only within **5 calendar days (not business days)** of eXp *email* activation, not license activation.
- **Four team types:** husband/wife (domestic partnership), self-organized, standard, and Mega Icon. Gogo's advice: 99% of couples should **stay separate** to each build a downline and "double-dip" rev share — plus a shared bank account gains an extra level (Dwayne's L7 = her L8). Only blend if a non-producing spouse can't realistically build 30 FLQAs.
- **Programs named:** FastCap & Fast Attract (6-week; only 2,000 of ~89,000 agents at a time; FastCap grads get free Realty.com leads for a year); Revenos referral program (paid out $970K in 2024, >$1B commissions in 2023); **[[concepts/REMLO|REMLO]]** (Real Estate Mortgage Loan Originator) — agents earn 0.5% per mortgage generated (Melissa Albert earned +$5,800 in a month). Also eXp Luxury, eXp Commercial (higher cap, CoStar/LoopNet access), International.
- **Tools/platforms:** free website (firstname.lastname@exprealty.com); CRM choice of Lofty / KVCore (now "Bold Trail") / Cloze / discounted HubSpot; SkySlope (file processing); free Canva Pro; eXp World (3D avatar world); the **Hub** (replacing Slack, which retires end of month); Build-A-Sign; free Regus co-working. 24/7 support line 833-303-0610.
- **Referral-agent license:** $50/month — can't transact, but can refer, build a downline; no stock, website, MLS, or mentor access. Up to 3 personal/family deals per year where collecting commission is optional.
- **#teamgogo June 2026 scale:** $3.5B closed in 2025; ~$250M closed in May 2026; **$15B cumulative** since Gogo joined (~7.5–8 years); 7,451 transactions in the trailing 12 months. As of this call the dated dashboard showed Gogo at 99 FLAs / 47 FLQAs. Per the brain's canonical figure, #teamgogo is **1,660 agents**. Year-by-year production: 2019 $70M · 2020 $200M · 2021 $1B · 2022 $1.8B · 2023 $2.1B · 2024 $3B · 2025 $3.5B. Gogo + husband Dwayne are ~$5M cumulative rev share across two lines.
- **#teamgogo extras:** Wednesday 1pm ET training (recorded, private YouTube), own onboarding department, Facebook group/chat, Google Drive of unbranded docs, the Team Gogo referral map, free Agent Attraction bootcamp (a Two Comma Club product), NAEA certification (free via the Honey Badgers upline), and gifts/birthday calls for milestones.
