# Master Your Production in 2023 — Gogo Bethke GoGetEm Webinar (Part 1)

> 3-hour GoGet'Em webinar (February 4, 2023) — Gogo's comprehensive production system for the year: lead generation, time blocking, database management, and accountability.

## Key Takeaways

- A "production mastery" webinar — the annual kickoff session focused on systems and results, not just goals.
- "You're going to be safely the best by the time you're done" — Gogo's framing: consistent improvement over perfection.
- The session had a high-energy musical opening (Regina Turner concert atmosphere) — Gogo's signature style of making training feel like a celebration.
- Core theme: **production is a system**, not a talent. Agents who out-produce aren't more gifted — they have better systems.
- Focus areas: CRM use, database size and health, lead sources, daily non-negotiables, accountability metrics.
- This is the February 2023 session — Part 1 of a two-part production series that year.
