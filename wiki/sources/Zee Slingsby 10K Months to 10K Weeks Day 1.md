# Zee Slingsby — $10K Months to $10K Weeks (Day 1)

> Day 1 of Zee Slingsby's GoGet'Em training on scaling from $10K months to $10K weeks — mindset, messaging, and the foundations of building a digital income stream alongside real estate.

## Key Takeaways

- Zee's core teaching: the path from $10K/month to $10K/week requires a **mindset shift** first, then a **messaging shift**, then an **offer suite**.
- Day 1 focuses on: who you are (identity), what transformation you offer (messaging), and why people should buy from you specifically.
- **Positioning**: you don't need to be the most expert person in the world — you need to be 10 steps ahead of your ideal client.
- **The income ceiling**: $10K/month is achievable trading time for money; $10K/week requires leverage — digital products, group programs, courses.
- Real estate agents have a built-in audience of clients and prospects — they can teach what they know (first-time buyer education, local market knowledge) as a digital product.
- Zee trains agents alongside real estate — the same mindset and marketing skills that attract buyers/sellers attract coaching clients.
