# Pick Your Lane: Buyers, Sellers, Property Type Mastery

> Content = clients. You attract exactly what you post about. Want sellers? Stop posting buyer content. Choose your lane, then create content exclusively for that lane.

## Key Takeaways

- **Content = client type:** An agent kept getting buyers when she wanted sellers. Reason: all her content was first-time buyers, rates, starter homes. "You're never going to get a seller. You create content for buyers."
- **The fix is simple:** Pick your lane. Decide whether you want to work with buyers or sellers. Then create content exclusively for that lane.
- **Then specialize within the lane:** What type of sellers? Ranch and land, luxury, lakefront, cookie cutter neighborhoods, condos, townhomes, high-rises? Pick one and go deep.
- **Volume argument:** "There are 2,500 homes between 300 and 350,000 in our town. I'd rather have massive amounts of potential customers than specialize in luxury that is less than 6% of the market."
- **ChatGPT to find seller content:** Explain yourself — who you are, transactions done, area, what you want to specialize in. GPT gives you content ideas for that niche.
- **Ask your audience:** Post on Facebook: "I specialize in sellers in 2026 — what questions and information do you need when thinking about listing?" They'll tell you → create content around the answers.
- **Don't overthink it:** "It is truly that simple."

## Quotes

> "If you want to break into working with sellers, you create seller content."
