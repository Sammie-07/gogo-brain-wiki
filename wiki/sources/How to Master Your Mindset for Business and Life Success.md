# How to Master Your Mindset for Business and Life Success

> GoGet'Em Community session on cultivating a 7-digit success mindset and the business structure to support it — from hourly rate to trackers to VA hiring.

## Key Takeaways

- **Mindset is #1.** Without belief you're deserving and capable, you'll never take the actions needed to use any tracker or build any team.
- **Know your hourly rate.** Take everything you made last year, divide by 2080 (40 hrs × 52 weeks) = your hourly rate. Never do anything below that rate.
- **Green light / red light exercise** to sort what stays on your plate vs. gets delegated — covered in depth as the entry point for hiring.
- **Find, hire, train, and pay your team step-by-step** — the session walks through the full VA hiring process.
- **Trackers are essential.** You track leads, money, time, social media — all discussed as tools for the business structure.
- Full session is gated (GoGet'Em Community membership); clip shows introduction and framework only.

## Quotes

> "If you don't believe that you're deserving, if you don't believe that you have what it takes, if you don't believe that you're an amazing leader, you're never going to take action."
