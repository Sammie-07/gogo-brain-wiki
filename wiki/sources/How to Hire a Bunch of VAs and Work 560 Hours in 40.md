# How to Hire a Bunch of VAs and Work 560 Hours in 40

> Monster Nation speaking engagement. Gogo's core talk on leveraging virtual assistants to multiply productive output — working the equivalent of 560 hours while only personally working 40. Combines hourly rate formula with VA hiring strategy.

## Key Takeaways

- **Core concept:** If your hourly rate is $X, any task that can be delegated to a VA at lower cost should be. Every hour you spend on low-value tasks is money lost.
- **560 hours in 40:** By managing a team of VAs handling concurrent tasks (social media, admin, follow-up, content, lead tracking), one person can produce what would take 14× the hours alone.
- **Hourly rate formula:** Annual income ÷ 2,080 = your hourly rate. Know this number — it changes how you make every delegation decision.
- **VA categories:** Social media VA, admin VA, ISA (Inside Sales Agent), TC (Transaction Coordinator) — each frees a different category of your time.
- **We Clone You** referenced as a VA placement service Gogo uses and recommends.
- **Monster Nation** — entrepreneurial speaker event (not real estate specific); Gogo crossover audience.
