# Time Management and Multiply Yourself — Gogo Bethke (GoGetEm)

> 2.5-hour GoGet'Em webinar — Gogo on calculating your true hourly rate, which tasks to delegate, how to hire and manage VAs, building SOPs, and Zapier automations. Guest: Sammy (VA agency).

## Key Takeaways

### Calculating Your Hourly Rate
- Formula: Take last year's gross income ÷ 2,080 (40 hrs/week × 52 weeks) = blended hourly rate.
- **Listings vs. buyers** (NAR data): seller = ~10 hours of work; buyer = ~17 hours.
  - Listing commission ÷ 10 = effective hourly on listings side
  - Buyer commission ÷ 17 = effective hourly on buyers side
- Real example: $6,000 listing = $600/hour; $6,000 buyer transaction = $353/hour.
- **Blended rate drops to $47/hour** because email, social media, and admin drag the average down.
- Core insight: "Everything that dilutes you from your $600/hour activity brings your effective hourly down to $47."
- Prescription: stay in your lane (listings = highest $/hour), hire a buyer's agent for buyer-side work.

### The 7 VA Roles Gogo Identifies
1. Social media VA
2. Admin/transaction VA
3. CRM/database VA
4. Content creation / design VA
5. Video editing VA
6. Tech/automation VA
7. Prospecting/outreach VA

### Multiply Yourself
- VAs are the mechanism for "multiplying yourself" — the same work gets done while you focus on high-value activities.
- Gogo uses Sammy's VA agency as a recommended source for hiring.
- SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) are essential: VAs can only execute what's documented.
- Zapier automations: connect tools without coding — automate lead follow-up, tagging, notifications.
