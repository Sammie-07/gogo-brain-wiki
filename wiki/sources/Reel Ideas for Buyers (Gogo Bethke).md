# Reel Ideas for Buyers (Gogo Bethke)

> The Wednesday buyer content system: posting cadence, 10 specific buyer reel ideas, ChatGPT idea generation workflow, "buyer" ManyChat keyword CTA, batch filming, and the no-pre-scheduling rule. Part of GoGo Social Media Challenge 2026.

## Key Takeaways

- **Weekly content calendar:** Monday = sellers, Wednesday = buyers, Friday = agent attraction, Sunday = emotional
- **Wednesday rule:** Post one buyer-focused reel every Wednesday without exception
- **Why buyer content works:** Speaking expertise on camera automatically positions you as the expert in the viewer's mind — they call you when they're ready to buy
- **10 Buyer Reel Ideas:**
  1. Waiting to buy vs. buying now
  2. Why most buyers lose their first three offers
  3. Renting feels safer — here's why it's not
  4. What your monthly budget actually buys you (rent vs. own payment comparison)
  5. The myth of the perfect first home
  6. Why online home estimates mislead buyers
  7. First-time buyers overcomplicating the pre-approval step
  8. What buyers don't notice during showings
  9. Why more listings doesn't always help buyers
  10. The real reason buying feels so overwhelming
- **ChatGPT idea generation prompt:** "Take me as the best realtor in [County]. I want to create a video for buyers about [topic]. Give me 5 good reasons [point]." Print bullet points; glance while filming. Don't read — talk through them in your own words.
- **Batch approach:** Ask ChatGPT for 12 ideas → 3 months of Wednesday content done in one session. Batch-film 4 on a Sunday → done for the month.
- **Niche-specific prompt:** "Give me 10 buyer video ideas catered around [lakefront properties] in [Livingston County]."
- **Gogo's teleprompter rule:** Never uses one. "I could never use other people's words." Uses sticky notes or notebook with 5 bullet points max to glance at.
- **Call to action structure:** Every buyer video ends with: "If you're interested in [topic], comment 'buyer' and my system will automatically [action]." The "buyer" keyword in ManyChat routes them to the buyer website (calendar + home search).
- **No pre-scheduling:** Never use Meta's scheduling tool or any third-party scheduler for feed posts — "the views are just not there." Post live through the app on posting day. Stories can be scheduled occasionally; feed posts never.
- **Storage workflow:** Save batch-filmed videos in a phone folder → post day-of directly from the app
- **Done-With-You program:** Team available twice a week for members who need help setting up systems
