# How to Eat an Elephant — Your 2025 Goals Reverse Engineered (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo's November 2024 GoGet'Em webinar on goal setting for 2025 — the Internal GPS Framework, reverse engineering from outcome to daily action, and using ChatGPT to build your goal plan.

## Key Takeaways

### The Internal GPS Framework
- "Get real about where you are. What happens when you get into your GPS? It tells you your exact location. Then you say, but this is where I'm going."
- You must own your current location — even if you don't like it. That discomfort is the fuel for change.
- In entrepreneurship: accountability to where you are is the first step to changing it. "As soon as you learn to take 100% accountability to everything that's going on in your life, the faster you can fix it."

### The Goal Must Scare You
- "If your goal doesn't give you the chills and make you question your own sanity, you're settling."
- "If your dreams don't scare you, they are not big enough."
- Visualization: picture the result in vivid sensory detail — what are you wearing? What perfume? Where are you standing?

### Reverse Engineering
- Start with the 12-month goal. Work backward: What needs to happen in Q3 for Q4 to work? Q2 for Q3? This month for Q2?
- Break annual goal → quarterly milestones → monthly targets → weekly activities → daily non-negotiables.
- ChatGPT prompt for goal planning: describe your goal and ask ChatGPT to reverse engineer the daily activities needed to hit it.
