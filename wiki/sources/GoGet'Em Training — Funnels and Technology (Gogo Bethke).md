# GoGet'Em Training — Funnels and Technology (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo teaches GoGet'Em members the why behind funnels — framing technology adoption as professional survival, not optional trend-chasing.

## Key Takeaways

- **Technology evolution argument:** Agents who refused to adapt to digital (fax → carbon copy EMD → digital contracts) are no longer in business. The same principle applies to funnels and social media.
- **The rule:** You don't have to have a funnel. But you need to *understand* what a funnel is and make an educated decision about whether to use one.
- **What a funnel does:** Takes people OFF of social media (Instagram, Facebook) and routes them through YOUR system — your website, your capture page — instead of staying in the platform's ecosystem. You own the lead; the platform doesn't.
- **When you need a funnel:** If you use social media for business. If you share links, drive people to content, or post calls-to-action — a funnel captures those visitors.
- **When you don't:** If you do zero social media and your entire business is referral-based or in-person prospecting, you may not need one.
- **The point:** "I don't want to be like, 'I'm not doing that, it's stupid.' Okay, WHY is it stupid? If you can answer me why it's stupid, I'll take that as an educated decision."
- **Team teaching model:** Gogo teaches the strategic why; Melissa handles technical implementation on her team. Members can have their funnel built by Gogo's team.
