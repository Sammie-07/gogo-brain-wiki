# Getting Video Testimonials from Clients

> Gogo's full playbook for capturing client testimonials on video — 6 formats, how to incentivize clients, and a marketing release form strategy that eliminates permission friction forever.

## Key Takeaways

- Video testimonials are social proof that you're not just doing transactions — you're a good human who cares for clients. "Monkey does, monkey do."
- **Incentivize when needed:** offer appraisal waivers (coordinate with lender), dinner gift cards, or happy hour in exchange for testimonials.
- **Sign a marketing release form** (face, image, voice, and likeness) at the same time as the buyer's agency or listing agreement — then you never have to ask permission again.

### 6 Testimonial Video Formats

1. **Closing Table Moment** — Go to every closing. Shoot B-roll of signing + selfie-style talking head. Ask: "How excited are you 1–10? Tell me about the process." Catch them at their happiest.
2. **Client Couch Talk** — Send 3 questions via text, have them film vertical video at home, send back. Edit into a reel under 1.5 minutes. Incentivize with a dinner gift card.
3. **"Why Did You Choose Me?"** — Separate video. Let clients name the reason: social media visibility, reviews, referral. Powerful for agent attraction.
4. **Before & After Testimonial** — Horror stories from previous realtors + what changed. Highlights your communication, punctuality, negotiation skills.
5. **Home Tour Celebration** — Return 2 weeks after move-in. Shoot before (listing photos) vs. after (settled in). Ask: "What's your favorite part? What school district are you in now?"
6. **Office Invite** — Bring them back for a podcast-style interview. Give dinner/happy hour after.

### Marketing Release Form
- Add a face/image/voice/likeness release to every buyer's agency and listing agreement from day one.
- Covers: closing table videos, property walkthroughs with clients in background, ads, stories, podcasts.
- Script: "This is how you found me in the first place. I need to do the same thing with you."

## Quotes

> "Monkey does, monkey do. When people start seeing other people working with you and being super happy, they also want to work with you."

> "I got you when you were happy. That's what you need."

> "You're not just talking about yourself — it's coming from other people, reassuring that you're not only doing a lot of transactions, but you're a good human."
