# The Art of the Gift — Gifting Strategy for Relationship Building (Katie Lawrence)

> GoGet'Em Live Training (also streamed to Facebook). Katie Lawrence — mortgage loan officer at Union Home Mortgage, ~30 years in mortgage. Specialty: construction loans (1 in 500 LOs who truly understands the product). Topic: systematic gifting as a relationship-building and prospecting strategy.

## Key Takeaways

- **Katie's background.** Title insurance → construction loan draws at a bank → originating (1994) → managed 26 direct LOs + processing staff → back to solo origination. Depth of product knowledge (construction loans) creates market differentiation.
- **Brand identity first: flamingos and pink.** "Since my childhood I have loved pink flamingos." Has 30+ flamingo gifts in her office — realtors and clients bring them as gifts now. Principle: choose something authentic, consistent, memorable. When agents go on vacation and see a flamingo, they think of Katie. This is the goal.
- **5-step gifting framework:**
  1. **Have a brand** — pick something authentic and own it completely
  2. **Have a plan** — gifting is systematic, not random. Know who, what, and when.
  3. **Have a list** — know exactly who you're reaching out to and maintain it
  4. **Gifting plan** — small token gifts as door openers; consistency beats cost
  5. **Birthday club** — track birthdays, send something every year; almost no one else does this → top-of-mind advantage
  6. **Drip campaign for new friendships** — ongoing contact rhythm for new relationships
- **Cost ≠ quality.** Gifts don't need to be expensive. Thoughtfulness and consistency create the emotional connection.
- **From coaching to doing.** Katie coached her LOs to "go make friends, go out and get new realtors, go make new relationships." When she went back to solo production, she put her own advice into practice.
- **For lenders:** target = realtors. For agents: target = past clients + prospective clients.
