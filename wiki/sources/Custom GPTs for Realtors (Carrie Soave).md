# Custom GPTs for Realtors (Carrie Soave)

> GoGet'Em Community training. Carrie Soave: international coach, consultant, and speaker on AI; 15-year realtor (commercial + residential); official AI adviser to eXp Realty; Team Gogo agent partner. Built a $1M business in 5 months with 1 employee using AI.

## Key Takeaways

- **AI is not tech — it's the art of communication.** Agents are already communication experts. Zero tech background required.
- **ChatGPT vs. Custom GPT:**
  - ChatGPT: Wide, vast knowledge on everything
  - Custom GPT: Narrow and deep — does ONE job/workflow/task/system extremely well
- You can build your own Custom GPTs and set exactly how outputs should look. Tight or loose parameters.
- **Real estate-specific GPTs** trained on your industry produce far better output than generic ChatGPT.
- **Prompt Engineering** = the art/science of asking the right questions to get optimized results. Custom GPTs have these prompts built in — so you don't have to re-engineer every time.
- **GPT Store:** Thousands of free Custom GPTs. Quality varies wildly — only as good as the prompt engineer who built them. "Some are useless garbage."
- **Three privacy settings:** Private (only you), Shareable by link, Public (free in GPT store).
- **ChatGPT Plus required:** $20/month. Unlocks: custom GPTs, image/PDF/spreadsheet upload, web access, Dall-E, GPT-4.
- **Sora** (OpenAI video generator from text): rolling out; will be a game changer for marketing video B-roll.
- Carrie's GPTs went viral; companies asked her to put paywalls on them — she declined. GPT store will eventually evolve like the Apple App Store.
- **$1M in 5 months proof:** With 1 employee and AI leverage. "That's the power of leverage you can get from AI."
