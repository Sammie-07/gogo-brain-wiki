# How to Leverage, Build a Team and Systems

> Rise Retreat Day 2 session featuring Amy Gregory. Focused on hiring, growth management, and building systems that allow a real estate business to scale without the owner becoming the bottleneck.

## Key Takeaways

- **Rise Retreat** is a Gogo-led event (Day 2 content) — multi-day immersive training for agents.
- **Amy Gregory** — featured guest/presenter on team building and leverage.
- **Core message:** Systems and team structures must be built *before* you feel ready. Waiting until you're overwhelmed means you've waited too long.
- **Growth management:** Scaling a team requires processes for onboarding, communication, accountability, and role clarity — not just hiring bodies.
- **Leverage sequence:** Automate first, then delegate to VAs, then hire in-person staff. Don't hire human help for tasks that can be systemized.
- **Key hires discussed:** TC (Transaction Coordinator) and VA as first leverage points; in-person team for showing, lead follow-up, listing management.
