# Get Instant MLS Alerts for New Listings

> Step-by-step walkthrough for setting up a saved MLS search with ASAP notification — so you're the first agent to know when a new listing hits your niche area.

## Key Takeaways

- **Set up a saved search in your MLS** exactly as you would for a buyer — using your niche area criteria (school district, location, price range, bedrooms).
- **Status filter:** Set to "Coming Soon" + "Active" only. You want to know before it's fully on market.
- **Keep criteria wide or narrow:** Can filter by price, bedrooms, etc. — or set it broad (any home in Howell School District). Your choice based on your niche.
- **ASAP notification — non-negotiable:** When saving the subscription, change the alert frequency from "weekly" to "ASAP." The whole point is knowing the moment it hits — not days later.
- **Platform shown:** Flex MLS (same logic applies in any MLS system).
- **Result:** You receive an immediate email the moment a new listing matching your criteria goes live — first mover advantage in your niche.

## Why This Matters

Knowing first = contacting sellers and buyers first. The 78% first-agent rule applies here: 78% of buyers and sellers go with the first agent who reaches them. ASAP alerts eliminate lag time and let automations and speed of response do the rest.

## Quotes

> "I don't want it on weekly. I want ASAP. So I know as soon as that listing hits the market, it's going to notify me immediately. That's the most important thing."
