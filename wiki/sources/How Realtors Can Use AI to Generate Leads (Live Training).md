# How Realtors Can Use AI to Generate Leads (Live Training)

> Live training from Gogo Bethke on using AI and social media systems to generate real estate leads. Covers niche strategy, hashtag formula, geo-tagging, funnel design, KV Core IDX, ManyChat, and follow-up speed.

## Key Takeaways

- **Ideal client niche strategy:** Define your exact ideal client before building content or funnels. Everything downstream depends on specificity.
- **Hashtag formula:** 20 tags per post:
  - 10 lifestyle/interest hashtags (matching your ideal client's world)
  - 10 post-topic hashtags (matching the specific content)
- **Always geo-tag:** Every post, every reel. Location signals Instagram's algorithm to distribute to local audience.
- **Funnel = collect info before giving anything:** Never give a direct link to content. Gate everything behind a name + email capture.
- **KV Core IDX link → lead capture:** Visitors get 3 property photos for free, then must create an account (login = lead capture). Automated lead generation from content-driven traffic.
- **ManyChat keywords:** Buyer / Seller — DM automation triggers when someone comments or DMs those words, sends them into a sequence.
- **78% go with first agent:** Statistic reinforcing the importance of speed. The agent who responds first captures the client in 78% of cases.
- **5-minute follow-up rule:** Respond to new leads within 5 minutes. Speed is the competitive advantage.
- **GoGet'Em Community tech support:** Tuesdays and Thursdays at 3pm ET (note: another source says Wed/Thurs — verify).
- **14-person team:** Gogo's team size at time of recording.
- **$60/month:** GoGet'Em Community price point at time of recording.
