# The Season of Disappointment — Mindset in a Real Estate Recession (Aaron Novello)

> GoGet'Em Live Training. Aaron Novello — licensed since 2006, 2000+ personal transactions, coaches $1M+ GCI clients, built a large eXp downline. Topic: navigating the "season of disappointment" — 2024 was the toughest year to sell real estate in 15 years, with fewer transactions than 2008.

## Key Takeaways

- **2024: toughest real estate year in 15 years.** Statistically on track to do fewer transactions in 2024 than in 2008 (the Great Financial Crisis). "If you made it through this year, give yourself some claps because you made it through the toughest time to sell real estate in 15 years."
- **Two camps.** A very small camp is having a strong year. The vast majority are in a season of disappointment. Both are real.
- **Seasons are cyclical, not permanent.** Most agents treat temporary seasons as permanent conditions. The problem: they make changes (quit, pivot, stop prospecting) precisely when consistency matters most.
- **"Season of disappointment" is instructive.** The hardest seasons contain lessons that cannot be learned in winning seasons. Disappointment is data, not a verdict.
- **Actions during invisible results determine future results.** What you do when results aren't showing up is what determines where you'll be when conditions improve.
- **Mindset under pressure.** Separate identity from results. Be coachable even when frustrated. Maintain activity levels when motivation is low.
- **Resilience framework.** Don't quit. Don't pivot without cause. Stay visible. Keep prospecting. Serve the community.
- **Coaching profile.** One-on-one coaching clients doing over $1M GCI. Coaches top producers in US and Canada. Combines spiritual perspective with tactical business advice — direct, no-excuses style.
