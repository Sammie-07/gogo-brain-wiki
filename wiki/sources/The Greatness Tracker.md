# The Greatness Tracker

> Gogo introduces the Greatness Tracker — a physical clipboard-based daily/weekly activity tracker that keeps agents accountable to the high-value behaviors that drive production.

## Key Takeaways

- **Physical clipboard** — intentionally analog. Having it visible on your desk makes it impossible to ignore. Gogo carries it daily.
- **Weekly targets:**
  - **15 face-to-face meetings per week** — any in-person interaction with a person (client, sphere, open house visitor, coffee meeting)
  - **5 breaking bread per week** — sit-down meals or coffee with someone; deeper relationship building
  - **60 great phone calls per week** — substantive calls (not just logistical), averaged ~12/day across a 5-day week
- **Also tracked:** Lead calls, referral partner meetings, open houses attended/hosted, social media posts published.
- **Why it works:** Most agents track transactions but not *activities that lead to transactions*. The Greatness Tracker closes that gap — you manage what you measure.
- **Accountability mechanism:** Review weekly. If your numbers are low, you know exactly why your pipeline will be light in 30–90 days.
