# How to Build a Real Estate Team — Key Contract Components (Gogo Bethke)

> GoGet'Em training on the foundational components of building a real estate team — focusing on the contracts, expectations, and structures that protect the team leader and retain top agents.

## Key Takeaways

- **Time is Life** — the framing behind building a team: you build a team so your time buys more life, not just more income.
- **"Multiply yourself"** — the team exists so the leader isn't the ceiling. Every agent properly set up multiplies the leader's capacity.
- Key contract components for team agreements:
  - Split structure clearly defined (what the agent earns, what the team takes)
  - Exclusivity clauses — does the agent work only for this team or can they take outside deals?
  - Lead handling expectations — response time, how leads are assigned, what happens with unconverted leads
  - Marketing and brand standards — can agents post their own content? Use team branding?
  - Termination and exit clauses — how does someone leave without taking the team's database?
- **Hire for attitude, train for skill** — the character of who you bring on determines team culture.
- "You don't need perfect. You need coachable."
- Common mistake: hiring without a proper agreement in place, then discovering misaligned expectations post-onboarding.
