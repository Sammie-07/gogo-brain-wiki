# Where Are You At — Brand Tracker

> Part of [[Gogo Bethke]]'s *Gogo's Social Media Challenge 2026* series. Introduces the Brand Tracker document — a master sheet centralizing all brand assets — and the monthly Growth Tracker habit.

## Key Takeaways

- **Brand Tracker** is a single Google doc/sheet containing every brand asset: name, username, all social profile links, website(s), Google Business Profile, branded email, headshot links, bio link, kvCORE smart phone number, brand hashtags.
- It is the go-to "brand kit" — everything needed to represent your brand in one place, shareable and auditable at any time.
- Required document for the **Audit Club** — Gogo personally audits the top 100 members' full brand presence; members bring this doc to the call.
- **Growth Tracker**: set a phone reminder for the 2nd of every month; log follower counts per platform and average monthly views. Purpose: accumulate proof of compounding growth over the year.
- > "The only reason you should ever look back is to see how far you've come."
- **Gogo's brand evolution**: *Gogo's Real Estate* → *Gogo Bethke* → *Gogopreneur* → *[[GoGet'Em Community]]* brand
- **Gogo's active platforms**: Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn (no TikTok at time of recording)
- **Gogo's websites**: gogobethke.com (personal domain), gogorealestateteam.com (kvCORE real estate site)
- **Gogo's brand hashtags**: #gogobethke, #gogorealestate, #gogetemcommunity
- Brand username used across platforms: *Gogo's Real Estate* (original; later expanded into personal brand)
