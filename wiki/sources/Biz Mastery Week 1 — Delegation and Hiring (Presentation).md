**"If you can't automate it, delegate it. If you can't delegate it, eliminate it."**

**Full VA Hiring Process:**
1. Do the Red Light / Green Light exercise to identify what to delegate
2. Create a Google Form to collect applicant info; attach to a spreadsheet
3. Post on VA/freelancer Facebook groups (join as many as possible)
4. Create email template for applicants; ask for: two DISC assessment results, previous work samples (video, design, etc.), a short intro video
5. Color-code applicant spreadsheet: green = strong, red = no, yellow = unsure; add Notes column
6. Interview promising candidates; propose date/time, send calendar invite
7. New hire: send contract to e-sign → have them attend all meetings for 2-4 weeks → give tasks list / SOPs → watch them do tasks in week 2 → week 3: do alone
8. Three-month evaluation period → decide to keep or remove
9. Remove all access immediately if not keeping

**Two DISC Assessments Required:** Tony Robbins DISC assessment in addition to standard DISC

**Cross-Training Rule:** Ask current VAs for referrals — they know the work and culture

**"Sometimes your current VAs have great references — start there."**

**Delegation Option:** A capable VA can run the entire hiring process for you — you just provide the ideal profile

**VA Payment Systems:**
- TopTracker: web-based timeclock VAs use to clock in/out (mandatory)
- Alternative: manual digital timesheet template
- Master tracker: Kristy compiles all individual timesheets into one master view showing each payment + total
- International payments: Veem — "absolute best payment platform" for paying VAs worldwide

**Teaching Method:**
- "I do it → you watch me → you do it and I'll watch you → you do it on your own"
- "Did you Google it? Did you ask a coworker before you called me?"
