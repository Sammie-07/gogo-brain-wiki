# Propy — Blockchain and Crypto Real Estate Transactions (Eric Cruz)

> Eric Cruz, VP of Sales at Propy, presents to GoGet'Em members on the future of real estate transactions — blockchain-based settlements, crypto purchases, and how Propy eliminates wire fraud and title fraud through on-chain recording.

## Key Takeaways

- **Propy's firsts:** First company to NFT a real property; first to transact real estate with cryptocurrency; first to record a deed on a blockchain.
- **Settlement speed:** Traditional closing = 30–45 days. Propy's blockchain settlement = under 24 hours. "Click, click, you're done."
- **Wire fraud epidemic:** $10 billion lost to wire fraud since 2022. In just one month in Colorado, $32 million lost to wire fraud. The expensive title insurance policy buyers and sellers pay for covers less than 4% of claims — meaning most wire fraud losses are uncovered.
- **Zero fraud record:** Propy has processed nearly $5 billion in transactions with zero wire fraud and zero title fraud — because every transaction is permanently recorded on-chain.
- **Blockchain transparency:** Contrary to the perception that crypto is anonymous, blockchain is MORE transparent than traditional systems. Every transaction is permanently recorded in an immutable ledger that the FBI and law enforcement can trace.
- **KYC/AML:** Know Your Customer + Anti-Money Laundering verification built into every Propy purchase. Every crypto buyer is verified before any transaction proceeds.
- **Coinbase collaboration:** The only real estate company with a formal collaboration with Coinbase, one of the largest US crypto exchanges.
- **The problem being solved:** Real estate title and escrow are 100% manual data entry — susceptible to human error, wire fraud, and predatory lending. Blockchain removes the human error vector.
- **Political tailwind:** The administration at time of recording was crypto-friendly and blockchain-enthusiastic — regulatory environment moving in Propy's favor.
- **For real estate agents:** Understanding crypto buyers as a segment; facilitating faster closings; marketing access to blockchain-verified transactions as a competitive differentiator.
