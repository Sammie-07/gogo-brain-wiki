# ManyChat Automation Workshop — Money Chat (GoGetEm)

> GoGet'Em Community training. Called "Money Chat" because ManyChat = money automation. 2-hour hands-on workshop. Gogo presents the concept and slides; Jonathan (tech person, shared EXPcon stage with Gogo) demonstrates live step-by-step setup. Attendees work on their own accounts simultaneously.

## Key Takeaways

- **ManyChat = automations that turn social media followers into leads and conversations.** Set it up once; it runs 24/7.
- "Cheapest form of leverage" — no hiring required, no ongoing time investment after setup.
- **Workshop format:** Gogo teaches conceptually → Jonathan demos technically → attendees work live (split-screen encouraged).
- **Setup process covered:**
  - Download ManyChat and create account
  - Connect to Instagram or Facebook Business Page
  - Split-screen Mac shortcut: green button → hover → "tile window to left/right" OR press F3
  - Set up keyword triggers (someone DMs or comments "FREE" → automated sequence fires)
  - Personalize messages so they sound human, not robotic
- **Gogo's ManyChat history:** $65K earned from automations. Multiple keyword sequences. Strong CTAs. (See also [[sources/How to Use ManyChat]] for full detail.)
- **Delegation model:** "I come up with the idea and the concept and they make it happen." Jonathan handles the technical execution.
- This workshop = setup from scratch for those who haven't started yet.
