# Time Blocks, Scheduling & Productivity (Kurt Shewell)

> Kurt Shewell's system for structuring a real estate agent's week using time blocks, color-coded calendar zones, and the 4-day work week model. Includes income math showing the path from $100K to $400K through disciplined scheduling.

## Key Takeaways

- **Red/Yellow/Green calendar zones:**
  - Red = income-producing activities (appointments, calls, prospecting) — protect these blocks at all costs
  - Yellow = support activities (follow-up, admin, email) — batch into designated windows
  - Green = personal/family time — blocked in advance, non-negotiable
- **4-day work week structure:**
  - 8:30–11:00 AM: New business only — prospecting, new lead outreach, listing presentations
  - 11:00 AM–12:00 PM: Follow-up on existing pipeline
  - Afternoons: Flexible/appointments only
  - One full day off per week minimum; two days reserved for family
- **Open house strategy:** Open houses are not just transaction tools — they are lead generation engines. Shewell structures open houses on Sundays and blocks the following Monday morning for same-day follow-up while the conversation is fresh.
- **$100K → $400K math:** The income ceiling for an unstructured agent is roughly $100K–$150K because all hours are interchangeable. Time-blocking creates the capacity to take on 2–3× more clients without working more hours, enabling the jump to $300K–$400K+ without adding staff.
- **Follow-up system:** Every new lead gets a same-day touchpoint within 1 hour. Every active pipeline contact gets a weekly check-in. Scheduled in the yellow zone, not improvised.

## Quotes

> "Your calendar is your business plan. If it's not on the calendar, it doesn't exist."

> "The agents making $400,000 aren't smarter than the agents making $100,000. They just protect their red time."
