# Content Pillars for Agent Attraction — Friday Strategy (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo's nine Friday content ideas for agent attraction — behind the scenes, local events, income proof, team culture, testimonials, "why I joined" story, model breakdown, number breakdown, and team-specific benefits. Establishes the three-pillar content calendar: Mon sellers, Wed buyers, Fri agent attraction.

## Key Takeaways

- **Three income pillars = three content days:**
  - Monday → sellers
  - Wednesday → buyers
  - Friday → agent attraction (revenue share, stocks, eXp model)
- **9 Friday content types:**
  1. **Behind the scenes + lifestyle** — show what eXp changed in your life (home, office, vacations, team). People want to want your life.
  2. **Local events** — realtor association, board meetings; attend, create content, form relationships, attract agents.
  3. **Income proof** — with income disclaimer. Success stories from your downline (first time capping, ICON status, first team).
  4. **Team culture** — eXp Con, masterminds, Friendsgiving, team shirts, agent interviews. "An underlying invitation into your organization without having to spell it out."
  5. **Agent testimonials** — ask existing partners to share why they joined; share those videos.
  6. **"Why I joined eXp" story + 3 pillars** — pick the 3 things that mattered most to you (Gogo's: revenue share, stocks, ICON program). Not everything — just 3. CTA: "Comment PARTNER."
  7. **Break down the model** — one benefit per Friday. Health insurance → ICON cap-back → 27 countries → etc. Never run out.
  8. **Break down the numbers** — whiteboard or iPad + Zoom screen share. Side-by-side math: 10 transactions at eXp vs. another brokerage. Visual learners make the switch when they see numbers.
  9. **Team-specific benefits** — what YOUR organization offers beyond eXp. Examples from Team GoGo: weekly Wednesday training, interactive referral map (auto-adds every new agent) → $4B in referral sales in 2025 within the organization.
- **The 5-year watcher:** Gogo was on a call with an agent who had watched her for 5 years before joining. "You never know who's watching." Consistency over time compounds into trust.
- **If agent attraction isn't your focus:** Replace Fridays with a fourth income-producing content pillar (whatever makes you money).
- **GoGet'Em Community calendar** has all three pillar days pre-loaded.

## Quotes

> "I was on a call today with an agent who said, 'I talked to you five years ago and for five years I did not make the choice to come over.' You just never know when they are watching you."

> "Showing team culture is like an underlying invitation into your organization without having to spell it out: 'Please raise your hand and tell me that you want to be a part of it.'"

> "Most people are visual. You can explain the model until you go blue in the face — not until they see the actual numbers will they make the switch."
