# Google Ads Strategy for Realtors

> Training by [[Ashley Brock]] (CEO, Paid Ads Academy), presented inside [[Gogo Bethke]]'s community. Covers the full Google Ads strategy for realtors: intent-based advertising, LSA vs. search ads, campaign structure, landing pages, quality score, and the find + remind retargeting play.

## Key Takeaways

- **Intent is Google's edge.** Social media users are passive; Google searchers know exactly what they want. They're actively raising their hand for what you offer.
- **Two types of Google inventory:**
  - **LSA (Local Service Ads):** Pay per lead. Requires license, insurance, and sometimes a background check. Ranked by review volume and star rating. Slower to build momentum but high-quality leads. Disputed bad leads can be refunded.
  - **Google Search Ads:** Pay per click ($3–$30/click for realtors). Nearly immediate. Text ad triggered by keyword searches.
- **Budget:** Can start at $5–$15/day. Algorithm needs data to optimize — more spend accelerates learning. Ashley recommends spending at your "discomfort level."
- **Google Business Profile (GBP):** Essential. Reviews pull directly into ads. Automate review collection — agents ranked higher have often systematized this.
- **Three keyword groups:** (1) Brand — your name, (2) Category — "realtor near me," (3) Competitor — "best Keller Williams agent [city]"
- **Campaign structure:** Campaign (budget + goal + location) → Ad Group (keyword theme) → Ad (text copy)
- **Landing page:** Simple 3-page site (home, about, contact). Not a property search tool — a *you* page. Goal = form submit → unique thank-you page URL → Google reads this as a conversion signal.
- **Quality Score (1–10):** Determines your cost-per-click. Three inputs: landing page relevance + ad relevance + expected CTR. High score = lower CPC. Example: $50/day at $10 CPC = 5 clicks; at $5 CPC = 10 clicks — double the traffic for the same spend.
- **Find + Remind:** Google gets people to your site (find). Facebook/Instagram retargeting brings them back (remind). 80% of buyers need 5+ touchpoints before converting.
- **Pixel tracking:** Install Facebook pixel + Google pixel on your site to capture all visitor data for retargeting, even if they don't submit the form.
