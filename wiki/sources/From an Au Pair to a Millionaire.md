# From an Au Pair to a Millionaire

> Day 5 of "Master Your Mind" — a GoGet'Em Community series. Gogo traces her journey from growing up under communism in Romania to becoming a self-made millionaire, weaving in core mindset principles.

## Key Takeaways

- **"The ship sinks when the water gets inside."** You can't pick your family, but you can choose who you let into your life and your energy. Some people bring you up, others bring you down — only if you let them.
- **"You are the average of the five people you surround yourself with."** Do the math literally: write down your 5 closest people's incomes, divide by 5, see where you land. To change your income, change your circle.
- **Broke friends are still friends — but not business advisors.** Don't cut them out; just don't discuss business ideas with them or ask their opinion. "Never ask a broke friend how to make a million dollars — they'll tell you it's impossible because they don't know how."
- **"We all live in cages with the doors wide open."** — George Lucas quote. Something in your childhood or life made you accept a limitation. The door is wide open; you can walk out at any time.
- **Break the rules, not the law.** "It's better to ask for forgiveness than for permission." Rules were created by someone who could only see one possibility — push them.
- **"Whether you think you can or you think you can't, you're right."** — Henry Ford. Change your mind to change your result.
- **Faith over fear.** Fear only creeps in when faith is missing. Listen to your gut.
- **Honor your time.** Calculate your hourly rate. Stop trading time for money; build income streams that work for you.
