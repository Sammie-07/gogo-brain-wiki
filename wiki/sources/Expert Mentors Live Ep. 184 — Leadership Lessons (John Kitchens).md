# Expert Mentors Live Ep. 184 — Leadership Lessons (John Kitchens)

> Solo episode. John Kitchens on leadership: the size of your business is elastic to the size of your leadership capabilities. Background story, self-leadership principles, blind spots, and the transformation path.

## Key Takeaways

- **Business size = leadership size.** "The size of your business is elastic to the size of your leadership capabilities." If your business isn't where you want it, your leadership capabilities aren't where they need to be.
- **Self-leadership first.** "We cannot lead and coach another human unless we can lead and coach ourselves first." The goal: transformation — first for yourself, then those around you.
- **Awareness is the foundation.** "Without awareness, we don't have a choice to make change." Most people are not problem-aware. "What we reveal we can heal."
- **Transformation path.** New level of awareness → choice → change → transformation. Sequential — you can't skip steps.
- **Blind spots.** Everyone has them. Run too fast and you can't see them. Need people around you with tight feedback loops. A Circle (accountability group) shines light on what you can't see.
- **Zoom in and zoom out.** Great leaders see the tree AND the forest. "See the tree but also see the forest."
- **Kitchens' background.** College dropout. Worked at Ridge Mar Golf and Country Club — his "MBA." 4 businesses in 4 years with friends, then sold them. Bar business taught him what he didn't want. Met Kinder in 2004; 17 years of partnership. Coaching agents 11–12 years.
- **Honey Badger Nation.** Started with <1,000 people; now 7,000+ and growing. Expert Mentors Live started with Kyle Whissel, Daniel Beer, Kinder, Stasic, and Kitchens.
- **Expert Mentors origin.** "The brainchild of Kyle and Daniel Beer and Kinder and Stasic and myself around being able to tap into the horsepower of our Honey Badger Nation community."
