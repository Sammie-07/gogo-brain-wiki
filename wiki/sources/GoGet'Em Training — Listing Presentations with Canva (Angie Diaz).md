# GoGet'Em Training — Listing Presentations with Canva (Angie Diaz)

> Angie Diaz — on-track for ICON at eXp and active in the REMLO program — trains GoGet'Em Thursday members on listing presentations using Canva templates.

## Key Takeaways

- **Angie Diaz:** REMLO program participant (earning on both real estate and mortgage sides). On path to ICON at eXp. Gogo describes her as "a rock star making very good money."
- **Topic:** How to win listings — what to prepare for listing appointments, how to get invited to compete for listings in the first place.
- **Canva for presentations:** Angie uses Canva to create professional, customizable listing presentation templates. Agents who purchase her templates need Canva to personalize them per property/client.
- **Gogo's doctor story (context):** Gogo opened the session with her operating-room closing anecdote — a surgeon so busy that Gogo had to go into his OR in scrubs to get signatures between procedures, and signed without reviewing. Point: the highest-value clients trust their agent completely; your job is to BE worth that trust through professionalism and preparation.
- **Thursday training model:** GoGet'Em Thursday sessions are member-led — if you're really good at something, you come teach it. This is peer-to-peer knowledge transfer within the community.
