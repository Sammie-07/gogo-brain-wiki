# Agent to CEO — Full Presentation

> Comprehensive flagship presentation covering the full journey from agent to CEO mindset, systems, team building, and brand. One of Gogo's primary speaking decks.

## Key Takeaways

**Bio / Introduction:**
- Came to this country with $200, knew no one, didn't speak English well; had work ethic and belief
- Career milestones: Au Pair → Real Estate → Top Producing Agent → #teamgogo → Bootcamp → Podcast → AirBnB → Syndications → NASDAQ → 3x 2 Comma Club → TedTalk → SUCCESS Magazine
- 9 companies total
- 1st woman at eXp to personally sponsor 100 agents
- Promise to audience: by the end, they'll have a licensed assistant, TC, 7 VAs, s-corp, long-term rentals, full-service agent status, Coinbase, fractionalized RE

**Agent vs. CEO Mindset:**
| Agent | CEO |
|---|---|
| "I can do everything" | "Learn to leverage: it needs to get done just not by me" |
| "If I work harder I'll grow faster" | "Automate it, delegate it, or eliminate it" |

**CEO Questions:** "What can I delegate?" / "How can I make this repeatable?" / "Who can do this better than me?" / "What's the vision and how do we scale to it?"

**Vision and WHY:**
- Know your WHY: financial freedom, impact, generational wealth, lifestyle
- Reverse engineer big vision into daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly goals

**Hourly Rate:** GCI ÷ (hrs/week × 52); listing = $1,000/hr (10 hrs); buyers = $400/hr (30 hrs); Gogo's current rate = $3,086/hr

**Red Light / Green Light:** Red = emails, social posting, appointments, graphics; Green = coaching calls, deals, money-making activities

**Dream Team:**
- DISC Assessment (Tony Robbins) used for hiring
- VA roster: Executive Assistant, Social Media, Lead Designer, Tech/Numbers, Onboarding, FB Groups Manager, Podcast, Website Creator
- Cross-trained: all VAs have backup; all have SOPs
- Training method: I do → you watch → you do, I watch → you do alone

**SOPs:**
- An SOP is a detailed written document describing how to perform a specific task consistently and correctly
- Easiest way to create: record yourself while teaching — never have to teach it again
- Creates a knowledge base accessible to the whole team

**Systems Stack:**
- Google Calendar (color-coded: red = work, green = trips, blue = family, purple = team bdays)
- Trello board
- WhatsApp group chats
- Veem for international payments
- GoHighLevel (CRM, funnels, email marketing, lead management)

**Meeting Structure:**
- Monday: back-to-back meetings for all companies
- Tuesday: coaching calls, GoGetEm webinar (2nd Tue each month)
- Wednesday: #teamgogo day, Wine Not eXp, weekly catch-up
- Thursday-Sunday: OFF (no exceptions, unless working ON the business not IN it)

**Remove Buy-Side:**
- Build team of buyer's agents; you keep listings
- Eventually remove from listing side too when hourly rate makes it unjustifiable ($3,086/hr = $30,860 for 10-hr deal)

**Personal Brand / Social Media:**
- "Why people call me the Social Media Queen of Real Estate"
- Must be on every platform: LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, etc.
- Social media pillars: picture, name, bio, links, posts/reels, hashtags, location, keywords, tagging, consistency, ManyChat, Facebook-Instagram link, VA distribution, ChatGPT, Remini (photo editing), CapCut (video editing)
- ManyChat example: 9,000-person speaking event → keyword → 6,000 messages in 20 seconds
- Automation + Meta Business Suite + GoHighLevel + Trello keep brand active when Gogo is not

## Quotes

> "Your current mindset got you to where you're at. You need a new one to get somewhere else."

> "If it doesn't move the needle, it doesn't belong on your calendar."

> "The easiest way to create SOPs is when you're teaching someone — record yourself so you don't have to ever teach it again."
