# How to Add or Update Your Bio on GoHighLevel Funnels

> Short walkthrough by Rafael Mino (Team Gogo support) on placing your bio inside a GoHighLevel funnel or website. Part of *Gogo's Social Media Challenge 2026*.

## Key Takeaways

- In GoHighLevel: **Sites → Funnels** (or Websites tab if you built a website) → click **Edit** on the homepage.
- Place a **short bio / name** near the top of the page — the first thing visitors see when they land.
- Place your **full bio** lower on the page — full background, achievements, real estate experience.
- It's plug-and-play: remove placeholder text, paste in the bio you've already created.
- GoGet'Em Community members can add a pre-built template to their GoHighLevel account via the link in the lesson description.
