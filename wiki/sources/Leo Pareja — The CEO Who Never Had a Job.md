# Leo Pareja — The CEO Who Never Had a Job

> GoGet'Em Webinars session with Leo Pareja, CEO of eXp Realty. Leo shares his background and vision for eXp's future. Notable for his unconventional path: first sold a home at 19, built a tech company (Remine), and eventually became CEO of the world's largest cloud brokerage — without ever having a traditional job.

## Key Takeaways

- **Leo Pareja** — CEO of eXp Realty (as of this recording).
- **Never had a traditional job:** Went directly from selling real estate at 19 to building companies. No W-2 employment history — always owner/entrepreneur.
- **First home sold at 19** — started in real estate young, built volume rapidly.
- **Built Remine** — a real estate data and predictive analytics tech company. Established his credibility in both real estate and technology before taking the eXp CEO role.
- **Why Leo at eXp:** The agent-owned, cloud-based model aligned with his tech/entrepreneur background. eXp's structure is closer to a tech company than a traditional brokerage.
- **Vision for eXp:** Continued expansion of agent tools, technology investment, and the revenue-share/stock model as differentiators in a consolidating industry.
- **Gogo's framing:** Having direct access to the CEO in a webinar is itself a proof point of eXp's culture — agents and leadership are connected, not siloed.
