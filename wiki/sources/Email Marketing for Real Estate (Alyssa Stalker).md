## Key Takeaways

- **Email marketing persists when social media changes algorithms.** You own your email list. Social accounts can be shut down.
- Building a database from zero: anyone can start. You don't need a list to begin today.
- Email is the **third organic reach lever** alongside social media and SEO/website.
- Beth Manning proof point: Plugged in to every Wednesday training for one year, didn't miss one session → $2.7M → $10M production in year 1. Now lives part-year in Italy with husband.
- **Team Gogo Wednesday trainings:** Every Wednesday 1PM ET for org members. Open to public last Wednesday of every month at gogosevens.com.
- **Gogo's referral rule:** "I would never pitch a service or product I don't personally use." Randy has been her lender since day one.
- Randy Gammo mortgage update: 1% buydown (National Mortgage pays it for Team Gogo clients); affiliate loan-status dashboard releasing for agent visibility.
- Gogo's email practice: "I send one email a day." (See existing [[sources/I Send One Email a Day]] for full details.)
