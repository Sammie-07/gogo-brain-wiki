# Up to Date Bio

> Part of [[Gogo Bethke]]'s *Gogo's Social Media Challenge 2026* series. Why your bio must stay current, how to maintain a master bio, and the ChatGPT workflow for adapting it to each platform.

## Key Takeaways

- **Keep a running master bio** — as you grow (speaking gigs, magazine features, new products, new achievements), you'll forget things if they're not documented. A bio that lives in the background and stays current.
- **6-month review reminder** — set a recurring calendar reminder. Remove irrelevant old items, add new achievements. Bio should always reflect where you are *now*.
- **ChatGPT bio creation workflow:**
  1. Tell ChatGPT: "Here's who I am and my history — create the most amazing bio"
  2. Then: "Take this lengthy bio and give me 3 different Instagram bio versions"
  3. Pick the best version, paste into Instagram
  4. **Remove the AI signature** (the long em-dash or line ChatGPT sometimes appends) — looks unprofessional if left in
- **Adapt to each platform:** LinkedIn allows long-form bios; Instagram requires short. Use ChatGPT to create platform-specific versions from the same master bio.
- **Stale bios actively hurt you** — leaving old coaching links, expired offers, or outdated titles in your profile signals that you're not on top of your brand.
- **Brand Tracker tie-in:** Add your bio link to the Brand Tracker doc — everything in one place.

## Quotes
