# GoGet'Em Brand Audit Call #3 — Stacey and Gina Brand Reviews

> Third of 10 planned live brand audit calls for the year. Gogo reviewed Stacey's and Gina's full brand presence in real time — covering LinkedIn strategy, YouTube content, bio critique, and the brand consistency lesson anchored in the Target/Starbucks/Home Depot analogy.

## Key Takeaways

- **10 brand audit calls per year:** Members submit their brand tracker doc and Gogo reviews every platform live — LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, website, bio.
- **LinkedIn strategy:** ManyChat automation is not available on LinkedIn — DM response must be manual or VA-managed. Have a VA check LinkedIn messages daily. Volume is low (a few per day); manageable with a dedicated block. Don't post CTAs on LinkedIn unless someone is ready to respond.
- **YouTube:** Long-form, long shelf life. Best content: market updates, neighborhood tours, "moving to [city]" guides. Most agents don't post consistently — starting is a differentiator.
- **Bio feedback (Stacey):** Start bio with a capital letter. Remove jargon audiences won't know (e.g., "HAR"). Turn long paragraphs into bullets. Make it audience-focused, not credential-focused. Good example: "I help you scale your business with system simplicity" — clear transformation language.
- **Brand consistency — the most important lesson (Target/Starbucks/Home Depot analogy):**
  - Close your eyes and imagine Home Depot, Starbucks, Best Buy, Walmart. You can see them because that's a brand — same everywhere.
  - "It's the same in freaking Kentucky and it's the same in Virginia. That's a brand."
  - For a real estate agent: same photo, same colors, same name, everywhere. When people see you in Target locally, they know: "That's the real estate person."
- **When you have a brand:** When people know your name, what you do, where you do it, and how to reach you — that's a brand.
- **Same name + same photo = everywhere.** Every platform, every marketing piece, every sign, every card.
- **Gina TR audit:** Personal "foodie" Instagram generates more business than her business account — but mixed-use accounts limit ad capability. Brand needed restructuring: no lowercase starts, clearer niche, bullets. All brand tracker links must be clickable (not just text). VA (Ayana) was given direct assignments live on the call.
- **Consistency document:** Shared with members to maintain weekly habits.
