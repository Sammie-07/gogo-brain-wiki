# My Money Lessons

> Day 3 of Gogo's "Master Your Money" series inside the GoGet'Em Community. She was broke just 6 years prior to recording; this session distills everything she learned to reverse that.

## Key Takeaways

- **"Six years ago I was broke."** The turning point: made a vow to learn everything about money.
- **Money mindset:** Money is not shameful — learn it, manage it, invest it.
- **Three types of income:**
  - *Recurring* — comes in every month from a built system (e.g., revenue share)
  - *Passive* — earns without active labor (e.g., investments)
  - *Active* — trades time for money (commissions, coaching calls)
  - Goal: shift income mix from active → recurring/passive over time
- **Investment apps** she uses — covered in full session.
- **Cost segregation** — advanced tax savings on real estate investment properties; reduces taxable income significantly.
- **RealBricks** — investment platform she uses/references.
- **Credit repair** — repaired her own credit to above 800. Credit health = ability to leverage and qualify for investment properties.
- **Board of directors** — CPA, tax attorney, business advisor, financial advisor all in the same room. She tells them where she is and where she's going; they advise strategy.
- **Monthly numbers meeting** — Kristy manages all income/expense spreadsheets; Gogo reviews each line, each company, each tab monthly.
- Full session is gated (GoGet'Em Community membership required).
