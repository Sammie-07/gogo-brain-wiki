# Gogopreneur — Your Network Is Your Net Worth (Episode 5)

> Gogopreneur Episode 5. Gogo's team gathers in person at a co-working space (Hyatt House, Tampa) for a face-to-face meeting — used to illustrate why in-person connection matters even in a remote/cloud brokerage.

## Key Takeaways

- **"It doesn't matter what kind of business you are in — you are in the people business."** Core principle reinforced through this episode.
- **eXp is a cyberspace company** — no physical offices. Agents work entirely from phones/laptops. *Because* of this, Gogo makes a point to create in-person moments with her team.
- **Face-to-face meetings are irreplaceable.** Most people have defaulted to remote-only post-COVID. Showing your face to clients and coworkers builds relationships that screens can't.
- At this point Team Go had brought over 200 agents to the organization (early 2023).
- Max Powers mentioned — a Tampa-based agent Gogo sponsored, who then built his own organization within eXp.
