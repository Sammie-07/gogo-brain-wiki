# Tax-Free Family Business Strategies — Part 2 (Barbara Schreihans)

> Barbara Schreihans returns to the GoGet'Em community for a second deep-dive session on tax strategy. She opens with "8 Steps to Get the First $400K Tax-Free" using family business structures. Inc. 5000 two years in a row. Session also features a 5-minute opener by Carrie on ChatGPT 5 (connectors, automations, data privacy).

## Key Takeaways

- At $400K revenue: typical agent keeps only $125K after expenses ($200K) + taxes ($75K+)
- Self-employment tax = 15.3% on top of all other taxes — the "penalty for being a business owner"
- **Step 1 — S-Corp:** eliminates self-employment tax; 35x less likely to be audited vs Schedule C
- **Step 2 — Spouse as Employee** (not owner): unlocks MERP — Medical Expense Reimbursement Plan
  - MERP has NO LIMIT; covers any out-of-pocket medical: prescriptions, chiropractor, massages, peptides, red light, cold plunge, sauna, hormones, IVF, adoption
  - Barbara herself deducted $200K in cancer treatment costs through MERP
  - Double retirement contributions (both spouses get 401K → $70K × 2 = $140K deduction)
  - Double home office deductions
  - Business vehicle for spouse
  - Travel with spouse = 100% business deduction
  - Dependent care FSA: $5,000 for daycare/summer camp/school care
  - Aging parents as employees: MERP covers their medical costs, supplement Medicare
- **Step 3 — Board of Directors/Advisers:**
  - Every entity needs one; odd number of members
  - Travel with board members = business deduction
  - Under 18 = Board of Advisers (unpaid); Over 18 = Board of Directors (paid optional)
  - Barbara has a "board bundle" template on her website
- Steps 4-8 cover additional family and investment strategies
- Free travel guide (QR code) explains domestic + international travel deductions
