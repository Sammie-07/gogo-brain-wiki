# How Being a Slave to Your Feelings is Sabotaging Your Success (Gary Coxe)

> Gary Coxe on the Justin Colby podcast — how emotional control separates those who achieve from those who stall.

## Key Takeaways

- Gary Coxe grew up in a cult, survived extreme personal losses, and rebuilt from $350K in debt — his authority on resilience is experiential.
- Core thesis: most people are **led by their feelings** rather than leading their feelings. This is the #1 saboteur of success.
- Success requires **reprogramming the mind** — not just positive thinking, but identifying the specific mental patterns that produce bad results.
- **Persistence + emotional control** = the combination most high achievers share. Those who quit do so because discomfort wins.
- Self-awareness is the entry point: you cannot change what you cannot see. Most people are running on autopilot patterns installed in childhood.
- Trauma can be transformed into an asset — the same emotional intensity that caused pain can fuel achievement when redirected.
- Massive action is the cure for most mindset problems — motion creates clarity.
- Gary teaches a method for identifying the **belief and value formula** driving behavior: your values determine what you pursue; your beliefs determine whether you think you can get it.

## Quotes

> "True success comes from leading your feelings rather than being led by them."

> "Identify and break the mental patterns holding you back, transform trauma into triumph."
