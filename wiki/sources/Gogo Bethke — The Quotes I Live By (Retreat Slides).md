# Gogo Bethke — The Quotes I Live By (Retreat Slides)

> 41-slide branded quote deck (@gogosrealestate) used at retreats. Organizes Gogo's core philosophy into eight thematic clusters — a master reference for her mindset curriculum.

## Key Takeaways

- Gogo attributes quotes precisely: her originals are marked "@gogosrealestate"; borrowed quotes carry the correct author (Henry Ford, George Lucas, Grant Cardone, Rockefeller, Tony Gaskins, Zig Ziglar, Stephen Covey)
- "Limits" cluster opens the deck — childhood conditioning, the elephant story, the airplane story, the George Lucas cage quote — establishing that most limitations are self-imposed
- Heavy emphasis on business-as-math reframe: billionaires aren't hard workers, they're smart leveragers; "An hour of my time is an hour of my life"
- Visibility and reputation section (who knows you > who you know) ties directly to her content and social media philosophy
- Leadership/trust arc: let them know you → love you → trust you → buy anything you sell — the entire case for personal branding in one sentence
- Spirituality thread runs throughout: God as active force, inner voice as navigation signal, energies as real filters for who gets close access
- Closer slide: Zig Ziglar's "You can have everything in life you want — if you will just help other people get what they want."
