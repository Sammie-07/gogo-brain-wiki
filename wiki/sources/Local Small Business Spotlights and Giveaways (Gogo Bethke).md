# Local Small Business Spotlights & Giveaways (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo's system for batch-shooting monthly local business spotlight videos + weekly Instagram/Facebook giveaways — zero cost, viral follower growth via tag mechanics, secondhand trust from business owners' audiences, and personal prize delivery for additional content.

## Key Takeaways

- **The insight:** Local businesses have already earned the trust of their community. By partnering with them, you inherit that trust secondhand. Their followers become your followers.
- **Batch shoot one Saturday a month, 4 businesses.** Pre-schedule with each owner/manager. Allow ~1 hour per business. Come with interview questions. All 4 = one month of weekly giveaway content, shot in a single day.
- **Five-week months:** Do 5 businesses on that Saturday.
- **Ask for a free item from each business** — no cost to you. Example: bouquet from the floral shop, free appraisal from the lender, free orchid with any purchase that mentions your name. They give the prize; you give them exposure.
- **Post on Tuesday; run until Saturday; pick winner Sunday.** Stick to this schedule — it's already in the GoGet'Em Community calendar.
- **Giveaway rules (the viral formula):**
  1. Follow your account
  2. Follow the business's account
  3. Tag 3 friends (who must also follow both accounts)
  4. Multiple entries = tag more people
- **Make the business a collaborator** on the post → appears on both accounts automatically → doubles reach.
- **Deliver prize in person.** Show up, hand over the gift, record it. Tag the winner, tag the business, make winner a collaborator → their followers now see your account.
- **The compounding chain:** Local business followers → you. Winner's followers → you. All at zero cost.
- **Equipment:** Collapsible phone stand (~$30, purse-sized, extends to 7ft). Optional clip-on lavalier mic for noisy locations. No videographer needed.
- **Origin:** Gogo used this in Pinckney, MI — population 9,000, one blinking yellow light, no restaurants. If this works there, it works anywhere.
