# Highlights and Pinned Posts (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo's strategic overview of Instagram Highlights and Pinned Posts — what each is, why both are essential, and what should occupy those spots on your profile.

## Key Takeaways

### Highlights — Strategic Context
- Highlights = collections of Stories that would otherwise expire in 24 hours
- They live as circular bubbles below your bio, organized by category
- Can pull from your story archive — expired stories are not gone, just archived
- Categories should match your business: Home Tours, Testimonials, Speaking Engagements, etc.
- Complements [[Instagram Highlight Bubbles — Creating and Organizing Story Collections (Laura, GSMC)]] which covers the technical how-to

### Pinned Posts — Strategic Context
- Profile shows 3 posts at a time on mobile; **only 3 posts can be pinned** to the top permanently
- Pinned posts are the first 3 things any new visitor sees — they are your most prime real estate on the platform
- Choose carefully: they must represent your most important, evergreen content
- Can be changed at any time — swap when something is outdated or a higher-priority post exists

### What to Pin (Gogo's Framework)
Two posts are non-negotiable:
1. **Root Story** — who you are (10 bullet points, one sentence each)
2. **How We Can Work Together** — your 3 money makers with keyword CTAs

Third pin: situational — a high-performing post, a current promotion, a community event, etc.

### Gogo's Example Pinned Post (HWCWT)
Posted: April 10, 2025. Still pinned: February 24, 2026. Nearly a year old — still working because it's evergreen.

### How to Pin
1. Go to the post
2. Tap the three dots (top right corner)
3. Tap "Pin to your profile"

### The Combined Strategy
| Tool | Purpose | Duration |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Highlights** | Organize expired Stories into permanent themed collections | Lives until you delete the highlight |
| **Pinned Posts** | Lock your 3 most important posts at the top of your feed | Evergreen; can swap anytime |
| **Stories** | Daily behind-the-scenes; relationship maintenance | 24 hours, then archived |

## Quotes

> "These are the highlights and these are the pinned posts. And we need to have both of them. Highlights for the stories that would have expired otherwise and you kind of want them to live on forever in specific categories. And then the pinned posts are the posts that you want to be on top of your profile."

> "If I visit your profile today and I am brand new to your profile, these are the three things that are going to capture my attention and you want me to know about them."
