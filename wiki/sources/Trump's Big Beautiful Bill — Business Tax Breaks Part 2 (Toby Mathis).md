# Trump's Big Beautiful Bill — Business Tax Breaks Part 2 (Toby Mathis)

> Toby Mathis, Esq. and Scott Estill, Esq. on the business tax provisions of the One Big Beautiful Bill Act — specifically what it means for S Corps, LLCs, partnerships, and real estate investors.

## Key Takeaways

- **Corporate tax rate for C Corps**: remains unchanged at flat 21% — preserves the 2017 Tax Cuts and Jobs Act benefit.
- **Qualified Business Income (QBI) deduction**: originally set to expire, now made **permanent** — 20% deduction for pass-through entities (S Corps, partnerships, sole proprietorships).
- QBI permanence is a massive win for small business owners and real estate investors using pass-through structures.
- **Income phaseouts apply** for specified service industries (law, accounting, consulting) — real estate is NOT a specified service industry, so realtors get the full deduction.
- Bonus depreciation provisions and other business-friendly measures extended or made permanent.
- Strategy implication: S Corp and LLC structures are now even more advantageous given QBI permanence.

## Quotes

> "The QBI deduction made permanent is a huge win for small businesses looking to lower their taxable income."
