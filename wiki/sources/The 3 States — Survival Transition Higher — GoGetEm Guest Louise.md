# The 3 States — Survival, Transition, Higher — GoGet'Em Guest (Louise)

> GoGet'Em guest training by Louise, a faith-based business coach, on the three states entrepreneurs move through: survival, transition, and higher/flow state. Focuses on recognizing which state you're in and learning to access the higher state intentionally.

## Key Takeaways

- **Three states**: Survival (fear, anxiety), Transition (uncertainty, stress of new endeavors), Higher State (flow, wisdom, creativity).
- The incubation/cocoon state is often mislabeled as failure — it's actually deep preparation for the next season.
- Transition state is where most entrepreneurs are when building something new — uncertainty is inherent. "You can be certain of uncertainty."
- Higher state = wisdom, creativity, flow — accessed through intentional practice (worship, nature, whatever takes you there).
- This is a **choice** and a **pattern of thinking** — you can train yourself to access the higher state on purpose.
- For Louise, higher state = connected to God, found in worship, in helping others, in doing work she loves.
- Key insight: entrepreneurs often hit higher state in passion areas but drop to survival state around money and selling. Reframing selling as "an exchange of gold for gold" reconnects worthiness and identity.

## Quotes

> "You can be certain of uncertainty."

> "What you have is gold. It literally is an exchange of gold for gold — and that ties into worthiness and identity."
