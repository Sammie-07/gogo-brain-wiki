# How to Build a Real Estate Team: Key Contract Components

> GoGet'Em Community webinar. Gogo walks through the essential components of a real estate team contract and the split structure she uses at eXp Realty. Full training gated at gogetemwebinars.com.

## Key Takeaways

- **Contract is non-negotiable.** Without a contract, agents get comfortable and slack off with nothing to hold them to. "To be number one you have to have a contract and hold your agents to it."
- **How Gogo built her contract:** Consulted Tina Call and Mark Z (both mega-team leaders) for starting templates. Took pieces from each, then added her own social media marketing requirements (which neither mega-team template included). Customized for her model.
- **eXp team splits:** Standard minimum is **25% of every transaction**. Cannot legally go below this for agents on a standard team. Cannot offer agents reduced caps without charging at least 25%.
- **Reduced cap structure on her team:** Gogo qualifies (based on production) to offer agents a reduced cap — **$8,000** vs. standard **$16,000**. But the 25% minimum applies to every deal.
- **Practical example:** Even a transaction involving a team member's grandmother — Gogo must legally take 25% minimum.
- **Commission structures vary** — determine what your brokerage requires before designing splits. Run the math to ensure profitability.
- **Benefits cost analysis:** If offering benefits (e.g., reduced cap, tools, leads), know exactly what each costs you and build it into the deal. You're not running a nonprofit.
