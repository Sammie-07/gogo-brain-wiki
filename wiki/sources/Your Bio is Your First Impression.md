# Your Bio is Your First Impression

> Gogo on why your bio must always be current, how to use ChatGPT to generate platform versions, and how to tie it to your Brand Tracker.

## Key Takeaways

- Keep a master bio **running in the background at all times** — update it as you earn new achievements, features, speaking gigs, and products.
- Set a **6-month calendar reminder** to review: remove what's no longer relevant, add what's new.
- Use cases for a polished bio: public speaking invitations, newspaper/magazine features, platform bios (LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube description).
- **ChatGPT workflow:** Paste your full bio → prompt: *"Turn this into 3 different Instagram bio versions"* → pick your favorite → update your profile.
- Always **remove the AI signature** (the long em-dash line ChatGPT appends) before publishing — leaving it looks unprofessional.
- Add the direct link to your bio into your **Brand Tracker** — one document for everything.

## Quotes

> "As you are growing and achieving new things, you're going to forget things."

> "Make sure when you get that back from ChatGPT, you remove that AI signature — so you look professional when you send it to people."

> "I want everything to be in that one document for you."
