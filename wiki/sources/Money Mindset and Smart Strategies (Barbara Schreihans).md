# Money Mindset and Smart Strategies (Barbara Schreihans)

> GoGet'Em Community session with Barbara Schreihans — tax coach and CPA. Practical wealth-building strategies including Roth IRA math, backdoor Roth, high-yield savings, automated investing, and mortgage optimization.

## Key Takeaways

- **Roth IRA compounding math:** $134.61/week → $1M tax-free by retirement (age 35→65). Visual proof that consistent small contributions compound dramatically over time.
- **Backdoor Roth:** Strategy for high earners who exceed Roth IRA income limits. Convert a non-deductible traditional IRA contribution to Roth. If your financial advisor hasn't mentioned this, fire them.
- **High-yield savings:** Move cash from standard savings (0.005% interest) to high-yield accounts (4–5%). Immediate, risk-free yield improvement on existing cash.
- **Acorns app:** Automated micro-investing. Started at $5/week → grew to $100K. Proof that automation beats intention.
- **Auto-transfers:** Set up automatic transfers to savings/investment accounts on payday. Remove friction and the decision point.
- **Bi-weekly mortgage payments:** Paying half the monthly mortgage every two weeks results in one extra full payment per year.
  - On a $1M loan: saves $170K+ in interest, pays off in 25 years instead of 30.
- **Fire your advisor test:** If they haven't told you about backdoor Roth, you need a better advisor.

## Quotes
