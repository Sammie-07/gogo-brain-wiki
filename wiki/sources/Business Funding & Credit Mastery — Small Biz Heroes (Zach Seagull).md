# Business Funding & Credit Mastery — Small Biz Heroes (Zach Seagull)

> GoGet'Em community training on business funding hosted by Natalia with Zach Seagull (Small Biz Heroes CEO) and Ben (Army veteran + GoGetter member). Covers the hero's journey to capital, building business credit from scratch, and the full funding ecosystem.

## Key Takeaways

- 82% of small businesses fail due to cash flow; 8 in 10 struggle with it
- Banks require all 3 C's: Credit, Collateral, Cash Flow — fintech lenders only need 1
- Business credit foundation requires consistent address across: IRS, Secretary of State, Dun & Bradstreet, Equifax Business, Experian Business, 411 directory, website, business checking, merchant processing
- Do not use @gmail or @yahoo for a business email — use branded domain
- Four business credit scores: Paydex, Equifax Business, Experian Business, SBSS score
- Build trade lines with net 30/net 15 vendors (Grainger, Uline, Quill, Maverick, Restaurant Depot) — confirm they report to bureaus
- Start with 3 trade lines → let them report → add 3 more → repeat
- Once foundation solid, can get cars/vans/equipment on EIN-only (no SSN, no personal guarantee)
- Funding options by stage: startup = 0% credit card stacking; 1-3yr = revenue-based; 5yr+ = SBA loans
- Small Biz Heroes products: SBA loans, lines of credit, working capital, merchant cash advances, equipment financing, invoice factoring
- "Just because one lender says no doesn't mean it's the end of the road" — Disney borrowed on his life insurance to fund Disneyland
- Training was Ben's idea — he connected Gogo to Zach as a GoGetter member
