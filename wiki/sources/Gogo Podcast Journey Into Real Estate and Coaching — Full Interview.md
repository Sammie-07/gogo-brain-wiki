# Gogo Podcast — Journey Into Real Estate and Coaching (Full Interview)

> Full ~33-minute podcast interview with Gogo covering her complete origin story from broke agent to multi-millionaire, her coaching evolution, social media philosophy, AI perspective, and how The Circle works.

## Key Takeaways

### Origin
- Licensed 2011. Year 1: $16K. Doubled every year: $36K, $74K, $156K. Year 4 broke six digits.
- No sphere, no English, no college, immigrant — found clients on Facebook. Created "Gogo's Real Estate" on Facebook, later moved agent conversations to Instagram.
- Discovered strangers were on Facebook and built her entire business on organic social media with no paid ads.

### Coaching Evolution
- Gogo's Bootcamp Social Media: started ~$400 → grew to $7K "Done With You" — **three 2 Comma Club awards**.
- Switched to eXp in year 8 → Team Gogo: **3,471+ agents, $2.7B in sales YTD**.
- Gogo's Bootcamp Agent Attraction → another 2 Comma Club award.
- GoGet'Em Community: one place for all learning — brand, lead gen, team, money, investing.
- The Circle: max 20, six-month commitment, minimum six figures (average $600K/year in the program).

### Coaching Philosophy
- Start with one-on-one, 3-month commitment — but move to group ASAP. One-on-one can't scale.
- Group efficiency example: 5 clients × 1 hr/week = 5 hrs. Put them in a group = 1 hr, same money.
- **Organic social closes 9.5/10 clarity calls.** Paid leads: might need 40 calls to close one.
- Results-driven: "If I don't see results in their life, I feel like I suck at my coaching."

### Social Media + Email
- Don't know how any coach makes millions without social media.
- Email list: 25K+ database × 1 email/day × 365 = 9M+ emails/year. Built list with free downloadables.
- 90-day trust-building framework: identify pebble in shoe → 90 days of free daily content → then sell.
- ChatGPT workflow: define coaching niche + target audience + content goals → 90-day content plan → ManyChat keyword for lead capture.

### AI Perspective
- Excited about AI — helps clients build full plans from bullet points.
- Belief: AI will INCREASE demand for human coaching because people will crave human connection more.
- AI handles admin/boring work; coaches focus on human relationship.

### Key Quotes
> "Don't ever ask a broke friend how to make a million dollars."

> "Time is money and time is life."

> "The bigger the problem, the more people are willing to pay for it."

> "I make more money now than I ever have when I was a top producing agent, but I haven't sold a single house. So maybe you should listen."

> "Out of 10 clarity calls, I close like 9 and a half because they've followed me for years and already trust me."
