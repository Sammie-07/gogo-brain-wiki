# GoGet'Em Training — Kat and Jeremy — Buyer Consultations and Buyer Agreements

> Kat (multiple-ICON eXp agent) and Jeremy (700+ new construction transactions) train GoGet'Em members on buyer consultations, buyer agreements, and working the new construction niche.

## Key Takeaways

- **Kat:** Multiple-ICON agent at eXp. ICON = capped at $2.7M in production AND completed 20+ additional transactions in anniversary year.
- **Jeremy:** Kat's husband. 700+ real estate transactions on the new construction side. Kat retired him from corporate America approximately 2 years prior.
- **Both now ICON agents** working together, running a local team and partnering with a local brokerage.
- **Future:** Adding mortgage origination (REMLO-style) to their service offering.
- **Core topic — buyer consultations:** Training GoGet'Em members to hold real buyer consultations and get buyer agreements signed before showing homes. Many attendees were not yet doing this consistently.
- **Post-NAR settlement context:** Buyer representation agreements became mandatory in many markets after the 2024 NAR settlement. Kat and Jeremy framed this as an opportunity, not a burden.
- **New construction niche:** Jeremy's 700+ transactions demonstrate the depth of specialization possible when you go all-in on a single product type.
- **Team model:** Husband-wife team; Kat handles the buyer consultation/listing side; Jeremy handles the new construction expertise.
