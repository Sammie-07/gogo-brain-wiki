# The Circle Coaching Contract

> The official legal agreement between Gogo Bethke (Coach) and each Circle Member. Defines program structure, payment terms, participation standards, termination rights, NDA obligations, non-solicitation, and IP ownership.

## Key Takeaways

- **Program definition:** The Circle Mastermind is a group of up to 20 handpicked, high-caliber entrepreneurs. Minimum one (1) year commitment. Focused on reaching a seven-digit income goal.
- **Format:** Weekly Office Hours every **Tuesday, 12:00–1:00 PM EST** via video conference. Group coaching / Q&A style — not 1:1 calls.
- **Pricing:** $3,000/month (total $36,000) or $30,000 flat (10% discount = 2 months free). Monthly plan is auto-billed on the same date each month.
- **Payment failure:** System retries up to 4 times within a 7-day grace period. After that, access is suspended. Suspension does NOT pause the one-year term. Late fee: 3% ($90). Interest: 1%/month from day 8.
- **Chargeback = material breach:** Initiating a chargeback/dispute triggers immediate suspension and the member remains responsible for full outstanding balance.
- **Tech support:** One 60-minute private 1:1 tech support session per month with Gogo's Support Team. Additional support: GoGet'Em Community Tuesday/Wednesday calls at 3 PM EST.
- **Recordings:** Posted in private WhatsApp community weekly. Can be paused, edited, or withheld to protect confidentiality.
- **GoGet'Em Community:** Required for all Circle Members — must maintain an active membership.
- **Support channel:** Private WhatsApp community and group chat.
- **Non-refundable:** All payments are non-refundable, including early termination. Monthly plan members remain obligated for all remaining months.
- **Not a subscription:** A binding one-year agreement. Monthly payment option does not reduce the financial commitment.
- **Renewal:** In month 11, member is given the option to continue for another year.
- **Termination by Coach:** Gogo can terminate any member who fails to meet participation standards. Termination is final. Fees non-refundable.
- **NDA:** All content, strategies, systems, and member information shared inside The Circle is confidential. Obligation survives termination.
- **Non-solicitation:** Members cannot solicit, recruit, or poach other Circle or GoGet'Em members — during the program AND for 12 months after.
- **No competing programs:** Members cannot create, market, or promote similar coaching programs during + 12 months post-program.
- **No circumvention:** Members cannot bypass Gogo by directly engaging her support staff, business partners, or vendors for competing purposes.
- **IP ownership:** All systems, frameworks, strategies, and materials shared by Coach remain exclusively Gogo's property. Members cannot reproduce, distribute, or sell them.
- **Media release:** Members grant Gogo a perpetual, royalty-free license to use their name, image, likeness, voice, and testimonials for marketing — no compensation, no approval rights.
- **Notice procedure:** Written notice via email to assistant@gogosrealestate.com. Two business day response window. "2nd Attempt" subject line for follow-ups.
- **Governing law:** State of Florida.
- **Coach acceptance:** No countersignature required — acceptance is by performance (processing payment / granting access).

## Participation Standards (Section 3.6)

1. **Engagement** — Attend Office Hours when possible. If you miss, watch the recording to stay current.
2. **Confidentiality** — Strategies, member information, and shared content stay inside the group.
3. **Professional Conduct** — Respectful, non-discriminatory communication. No harassment or intimidation.
4. **Contribution over solicitation** — No spam, mass recruiting, or hard pitches inside community channels unless expressly permitted by Coach.
5. **Tech readiness** — Functional audio/video. Do not record or redistribute sessions without written consent.
6. **IP compliance** — Respect intellectual property of Coach, Support Team, and Members.
