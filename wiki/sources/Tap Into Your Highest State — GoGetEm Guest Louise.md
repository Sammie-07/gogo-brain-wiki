# Tap Into Your Highest State — GoGet'Em Guest (Louise)

> GoGet'Em guest training by Louise on accessing the higher/flow state through stillness, neuroscience of prayer, and the principle of "everything is permissible but not everything is profitable."

## Key Takeaways

- **"Everything is permissible, but not everything is profitable."** — God-given phrase to Louise when she burned out from doing more and more. Creative people must learn to filter ideas by profit, not just possibility.
- The brain generates ~1,400 words per minute — creative women may experience triple that. Learning to slow the brain is a prerequisite to accessing higher state.
- **"Be still and know"** — the ability to be still is a learnable skill set. You have a knowing inside you; accessing it requires stillness.
- **Neuroscience evidence**: Andrew Newberg studied nuns and monks in prayer — they experienced loss of time and space, mapped on SPECT brain scan. This aligns with spiritual intelligence and the intersection of God and brain design.
- Higher state characteristics: loss of time and space, quiet intensity, working without frenzy. Louise's metaphor: "like a whale under the water birthing — very, very quiet."
- The goal: access prophetic strategy, maps, and blueprints that answer your questions faster than tactical thinking.

## Quotes

> "Everything is permissible, but not everything is profitable."

> "Be still and know. You have a knowing inside of you."

> "I was just strongly, elegantly going through the water — and I got to take people in my slipstream and then teach other people how to do the same."
