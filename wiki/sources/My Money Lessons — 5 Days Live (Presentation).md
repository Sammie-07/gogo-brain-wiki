# My Money Lessons — 5 Days Live

> Five-day live training on Gogo's personal money philosophy. Disclaimer upfront: not a financial advisor; street smart, no college education; verify everything with a professional.

## Key Takeaways

**Three types of income:**
- Active: trading time for money (hourly rate)
- Passive: using money to make money
- Recurring: worked for it once, keeps paying (revenue share, memberships)

**Money Relationship:**
- Examine your first instinct about money ("money doesn't grow on trees," "hard work = money," "root of all evil")
- Money is neutral — it just makes you more of who you already are

**Saving Money:**
- No one ever got wealthy on saving money
- 2023: savings accounts paid 0.49% while inflation ran 3.4% — you lost 2.9% by "saving"

**Investing:**
- Stocks: invest in companies you already use (VISA, Delta, Ford, Verizon) + S&P 500 (SPY — 10.47% compound annual return over 30 years)
- Bitcoin: 24/7, fractionalized, accessible to poor and rich alike
- Apps: RobinHood, eTrade, Charles Schwab, Coinbase

**Real Estate:**
- Home equity = your own bank; use HELOC to access it
- HELOC example: AirBnB property $670k purchase → $3k/year net cost; cost segregation saved $58,973 in taxes (37% bracket on $159k AirBnB income); Zillow equity grew $22k in one year
- Types: LTR, STR, MTR, Syndications, Fractionalized, Flipping (Gogo doesn't like flipping)
- Syndications require accredited investor status ($200k+ income or $1M net worth excluding primary residence); LP = passive, GP = active
- Fractionalized real estate via RealBricks: as little as $10, as young as 18

**Credit Cards:**
- Never use cash — use credit cards for points, travel, hotels
- Favorites: Chase Ink, AMEX
- Pay on the 1st AND the 15th (statement date vs. due date)
- No credit is worse than bad credit

**Trackers and Meetings:**
- Affiliate income tracker, revenue share tracker, monthly income/expenses tracker (personal and business)
- Monthly numbers meeting with team; quarterly meeting with board of directors

**Board of Directors:**
- Build a personal board of directors across all key domains: investing, making money, credit cards, saving, real estate, equity, money relationship, stocks
