# What Copywriting Actually Is (Gogo Bethke)

> Opening video of a new copywriting series in the GSMC 2026 curriculum. Gogo defines copywriting in plain English, establishes her five core principles, and reveals her actual email workflow (Carol the copywriter → Monday review → schedule and send).

## Key Takeaways

- **Definition:** Copywriting = your voice online. "A written version of your thoughts." Not a craft for experts — it's just you, typed out.
- **Five principles:**
  1. **Sound like you** — most crucial. The accent, the misspellings, the "monkey does monkey do" — don't fix it. That's your power. "If I change that, I lose my authenticity."
  2. **Be yourself, always** — don't mimic others. Your unique voice, stories, and experience are the differentiator. "Your only job on this earth is to be you."
  3. **Copy should sell but not feel like sales** — Gogo's model: present a problem → present a solution → give a button. No hard pitch. "I talk from the heart."
  4. **Know your audience deeply** — find the pebble in their shoe. Money exchanges hands when problems get solved. Speak to their pain points, dreams, and what keeps them up at night.
  5. **Consistency is key** — same voice across email, social, stage, website. One email per day (skip national holidays, sometimes Sundays). Repurpose: social post → email version. Don't create from scratch twice.
- **Only sell what you've personally done.** No ISA department digital product because she's never run one. "15 years of experience" = credibility = confident copywriting.
- **Email workflow (scaled version):** Carol (copywriter) writes all emails → brings drafts to Monday meeting → Gogo reads, adjusts to sound like her → scheduled and sent one per day
- **Email list:** 25,000–26,000 active emails → 6.5 million emails sent in one year
- **Cross-platform consistency:** Email, social media, stage, website — every word must reinforce the same value and tone. If it feels off, it's not aligned.
- **Repurposing shortcut:** What did you post today? Write an email version. Done. No separate content creation needed.
- **ChatGPT integration:** Train ChatGPT to sound like you → it can draft posts, emails, websites in your voice → easier scaling
