# Reel Ideas for Sellers (Gogo Bethke)

> Monday is seller day in the GSMC 2026 weekly content calendar. This video gives 10 reel ideas targeted at potential sellers, explains why math-heavy content builds authority, and walks through the ManyChat "seller" keyword CTA system.

## Key Takeaways

- **Monday = seller content** in the weekly posting schedule
- All 10 ideas are math/numbers-oriented — Gogo's deliberate strategy for authority building
- **10 Seller Reel Ideas:**
  1. How to calculate the price buyers will actually pay for your home
  2. Simple math tells you whether waiting to sell costs you money
  3. How buyers decide what your home is worth in under 60 seconds
  4. Before you accept an offer, run it through this test
  5. How to know if a price reduction will help or hurt you
  6. How buyers use days on market against you in negotiations
  7. Three numbers that matter more than your list price
  8. How to tell if your home is actually priced right in the first 10 days
  9. How much renovations actually add to your sales price
  10. How to estimate what you will actually walk away with after selling
- **Delivery tip**: Number-heavy content works best on a whiteboard, or screen-sharing an iPad while crunching numbers live
- **ChatGPT prompt**: "Every Monday I am creating a reel for my sellers. I'm a realtor in [County]. Consider me a top-producing badass. Give me 12 ideas." → 12 ideas = one full quarter
- **Batch or shoot day-of**: Gogo batched all of March in one session on January 19 — same outfit throughout
- **CTA system**: Say "DM me or comment the word seller" → ManyChat "seller" keyword triggers automated message → link to seller page on website → home evaluation form → collects name, email, address, phone → marketing report sent automatically
- Math videos make agents look like experts; most people can't do real estate math without help
