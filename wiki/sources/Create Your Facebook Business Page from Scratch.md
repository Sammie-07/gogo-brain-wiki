# Create Your Facebook Business Page from Scratch

> GSMC 2026 module by Kristy Waker. Step-by-step walkthrough for agents who don't yet have a Facebook Business Page. Companion to the "Update Your Facebook Business Page" module.

## Key Takeaways

**Step-by-step to create:**
1. Log into Facebook with your **personal account**
2. Click the **9-dot menu** (top right) → **Create** → **Page**
3. Enter **page name** (e.g., "Kristy's Real Estate Team")
4. Set **category** to Real Estate
5. **Skip the bio** for now — come back once you've completed the bio module
6. Skip optional fields (website, location, hours, WhatsApp) — click through
7. Page is created

**Immediately after creation — set your username:**
8. Go to **Settings → Page Setup → Edit username**
9. Type your brand handle → confirm it's available → Save
10. Result: your page URL becomes `facebook.com/yourusername` instead of an ugly auto-generated string
11. Copy the clean URL → paste into your **Brand Tracker** (Facebook link field)

- The page name and username are set separately — the name gets entered during creation; the username must be set as a follow-up step.
- Profile photo, cover photo, and bio are added later (covered in subsequent GSMC modules).
- The finished link is what goes in the Brand Tracker document.
