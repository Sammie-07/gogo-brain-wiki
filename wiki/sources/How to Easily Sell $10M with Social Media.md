# How to Easily Sell $10M with Social Media (Tamra Minch)

> GoGet'Em Webinars session featuring Tamra Minch, a #teamgogo eXp agent. Tamra shares how she built her entire business through social media and referrals — zero cold calling, zero paid ads.

## Key Takeaways

- **Tamra Minch** — eXp Realty agent, member of #teamgogo (Gogo's organization).
- **ICON status achieved in first year at eXp** — one of eXp's highest production awards; earned on her first anniversary with the brokerage.
- **100% social/referral business** — Tamra gets all leads from social media presence and referral relationships. No cold calling, no paid leads, no door knocking.
- **The model:** Consistent social media content builds trust → warm inbound leads → high conversion rate because they already know, like, and trust you before reaching out.
- **Key message:** $10M in annual production is achievable purely through organic social media if you show up consistently and authentically.
