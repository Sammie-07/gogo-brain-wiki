# How-To Buyer/Seller Content (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo's framework for creating recurring educational how-to videos — 10 buyer-side and 10 seller-side ideas, a ChatGPT workflow for generating bullet points, and a weekly calendar structure (Mon sellers, Wed buyers, Fri agent attraction).

## Key Takeaways

- **Purpose:** Saying real estate knowledge out loud on camera, consistently, makes you the expert — even if you feel like a beginner. "Most people buy 3 homes in a lifetime. You have more experience than they do."
- **Start simple:** Don't explain complex concepts you don't fully understand. Talk about things you already know — "any small step in the real estate transaction."
- **10 buyer-side ideas:** How to buy with minimal stress / How much money you really need / How to get pre-approved the right way / How to win in a competitive market without overpaying / How to read a listing like a pro / How to decide new construction vs. resale / (+ 4 more)
- **10 listing-side ideas:** How to prepare without over-renovating / How pricing too high costs you money / How to get multiple offers / What to fix vs. leave alone / (+ 6 more)
- **ChatGPT as research tool:** For any topic on the list, ask ChatGPT: "I'm creating a video on [topic]. Give me 5 bullet points I should cover." Use those as your speaking notes. Same way ChatGPT generates market report data.
- **How-to works for agent attraction too:** "How to join eXp," "How to build an organization of 1,500 agents," "How to have a niche in your area," "How to break into a neighborhood."
- **Recommended weekly calendar:**
  - Monday → seller how-to videos
  - Wednesday → buyer how-to videos
  - Friday → agent attraction how-to videos
- **GoGet'Em Community calendar** already has these pre-loaded — log in and pick the topic for the day.
- **Never run out of topics:** Industry changes constantly. New rules, new market conditions, new tools = new how-to topics indefinitely.
- **"How do you eat an elephant? One bite at a time."** — Don't overthink the topic. One video, one idea, done. Keep adding to the list weekly.

## Quotes

> "Most people maybe buy three homes in a lifetime. They don't have as much experience as you do. So here you are — the expert."

> "How do you eat an elephant? One bite at a time. Don't overthink it."

> "As time passes and the industry is changing, you can just change these out at all times. You're never going to run out of how-tos."
