# How to Market Your Digital Product on Social Media (Zee Slingsby)

> GoGet'Em Community session with Zee Slingsby on converting social media content into digital product sales. Covers two content types, language shifts, email pre-launch sequences, and CTA placement.

## Key Takeaways

- **Two content types:**
  - **Growth content** — generates saves, shares, follows. Specific, educational. Attracts new audience. Does NOT directly sell.
  - **Conversion content** — sells. Pain point → solution → DM/keyword CTA. Speaks to existing warm audience.
- **"YOU" language shift:** Change "I help people" to "you can have X by doing Y." Speak in the client's outcome, not the coach's offer.
- **5-email pre-launch sequence:** Build anticipation before product launch. Sequence primes list before cart opens so sales hit immediately on launch day.
- **CTA placement:** High-volume posters should put CTA at top of caption (audience sees enough posts to tolerate it). Lower-volume posters should earn the CTA with value first, then ask.
- **Growth content = specific education:** The more specific the lesson, the more saves it earns. Saves signal algorithm reach.
- **Conversion content structure:** Identify the pain point → show the solution exists → direct to DM or keyword (ManyChat trigger).

## Quotes

> "Growth content saves you followers. Conversion content saves you sales. You need both."

> "Stop saying 'I help people.' Say 'You can have X by doing Y.' Put them in the outcome."
