# How to Generate Buyer and Seller Leads (Amy Gregory)

> GoGet'Em Community session with Amy Gregory — Gogo's eXp business partner for 5 years, co-speaker at Rise Retreat, and leader of PowerHouse Group. Full funnel system for generating real estate leads from social media.

## Key Takeaways

- **Amy Gregory:** Gogo's eXp business partner for 5+ years. Leader of PowerHouse Group. Co-presents with Gogo at Rise Retreat.
- **Stan.store:** Her primary funnel tool.
  - **30% higher conversion rate** than other funnel platforms tested.
  - Set up in **5 clicks** from a template.
  - **$99/month** subscription.
  - Email sequences built in; automated follow-up.
- **Lead magnets that work:**
  - Home value report (highest intent — seller leads)
  - City guide (broad awareness — buyer leads)
  - "How to hire a realtor" (trust-building; positions agent as expert)
- **ManyChat results:** **71.5% click-through rate** on DM sequences. One post generated **226 potential clients** added to her pipeline.
- **Reels shown to non-followers:** Instagram's algorithm distributes reels beyond current followers — one reel can reach thousands of non-connections. Lead magnets capture them.
- **"Lead not a lead until off the app":** A social media follower or commenter is NOT a lead until you have their name + contact info off-platform. Goal of every interaction: move them from Instagram/Facebook into your CRM or email list.
- **Rise Retreat:** Annual event where Gogo and Amy co-present. Invitation-only / high-level agent community.

## Quotes
