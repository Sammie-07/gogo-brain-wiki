# Realtor to CEO — Agent-to-CEO Transition (Beth Manning)

> GoGet'Em Live Training. Beth Manning: former KW agent who grew from $2.7M to $10M production in one year after joining Team GoGo and plugging into training. 29 agents in her eXp organization. Topic: the agent-to-CEO transition — the fundamental shift from doing the work to building the business.

## Key Takeaways

- **From $2.7M to $10M in one year.** "One of the reasons how I've been able to grow my business in one year from 2.7 to 10 million is because of the people that I've associated with and it's because I plugged into every single training."
- **Plugged into everything: Wednesdays (production), Thursdays (tech), Fridays (agent attraction).** Also attends all events and randomly reaches out to people doing better than her.
- **CEO works ON the business, not IN it.** The agent-to-CEO transition requires letting go of doing everything yourself. Build infrastructure, not just more transactions.
- **Build systems while still producing.** Don't jump out of production too early — build systems and processes first, then step back. Cash flow bridge required.
- **Delegate lowest-value activities first.** Hire for what's consuming your time at the lowest dollar value per hour.
- **Gogo connection.** Gogo sponsored Beth at eXp. Beth was on a buy-side deal (son and mom in Michigan), took it all the way through.
- **Nervous energy → results.** "I'm actually nervous as I'm speaking right now but I can tell you that the more nervous you are and the more you're putting yourself out there you too can more than double your business."
- **Goal: retire husband (Italian).** Has 29 agents in her organization.
