# My AI Brand Voice — Custom GPT Setup (GSMC)

> A GoGet'Em Community team member walks through the "My AI Brand Voice" custom GPT — a free tool built specifically for the community to help members train ChatGPT to write in their personal voice. Covers FastTrack and Guided Interview paths, how to set ChatGPT custom instructions, and the voice starter prompt.

## Key Takeaways

### What It Does
"My AI Brand Voice" is a custom GPT available in the ChatGPT GPT store. Its purpose: analyze your existing writing samples → generate a detailed brand voice profile → give you ready-to-paste ChatGPT custom instructions so every new chat sounds like you.

**Find it:** ChatGPT → Explore GPTs → search "My AI Brand Voice" → Start Chat.

### Two Setup Paths

| Path | When to use | How |
|------|------------|-----|
| **FastTrack** | You have existing written content | Paste 3–5 writing samples + note your profession and ideal audience. GPT generates your voice profile instantly. |
| **Guided Interview** | Starting from scratch / no prior content | GPT asks a series of questions and builds your voice from your answers. |

**What counts as valid writing samples:**
- Instagram captions (written by you, not AI)
- Emails
- Blog posts
- Text messages
- Transcripts of Zoom calls or live videos where you were just talking

**Critical:** Do NOT paste AI-generated content. The point is to capture how *you* actually write and speak.

### What the Voice Profile Contains
After FastTrack, the GPT outputs a detailed profile covering:
- **Brand Voice** (overall positioning statement)
- **Tone** (confidence level, emotional register)
- **Personality in Writing** (archetypal descriptors)
- **Writing Style** (sentence structure, whether it "feels like talking," repetition patterns)
- **Confidence level** (how assertive vs. tentative)
- **Words/phrases you use often**
- **Words to avoid** (things that sound off-brand)
- **Content style**
- **Ideal client description**

*Example output for Gogo (from 3 Instagram posts):* "Brand Voice: Disruptive leader meets unapologetic feminine power. Tone: Confident, borderline defiant (in a good way). Personality: Rulebreaker. Self-made. Slightly rebellious. Writing style: Feels like talking, not writing. Slightly messy in a human way. Don't over-polish."

### Setting Up ChatGPT Custom Instructions
1. Copy the entire AI Voice Guide output from the GPT
2. ChatGPT → Settings → **Personalization** → **Custom Instructions**
3. Paste → Save
4. Every new ChatGPT chat will now follow those instructions automatically

### Voice Starter Prompt (Save This)
The GPT also generates a short "voice starter prompt" — a single paragraph you can paste at the start of any new chat (including Gemini or other AI tools) to re-inject your brand voice even without custom instructions set.

*Example for Gogo:* *"Write everything in my voice. Confident, slightly rebellious female entrepreneur in real estate who built success through social media instead of traditional methods. Keep it direct, human, and a little unfiltered. No corporate language. Make it feel like I'm talking, not writing."*

Save this in Notes/Google Docs. When ChatGPT starts sounding generic, paste it and reset.

### After Setup
Once voice is in custom instructions, use ChatGPT for:
- Instagram captions
- Email drafts
- Website copy
- Hooks and CTAs in your voice

The GPT can also rewrite existing content to "hit harder" or build hooks and CTAs tuned to your exact audience.

## Quotes

> "Do not use content that was created by AI because you want it to sound like you."

> "If your AI is not sounding like you, you can just plug that in and it's a prompt in any chat no matter where you are."
