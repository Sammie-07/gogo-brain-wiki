# Agent Attraction Hashtags (Gogo Bethke)

> Instagram reduced the maximum hashtag count from 30 to 5. Gogo's five specific hashtags for agent attraction posts: organization name, brokerage full name, #Realtor, #Agent, and one more.

## Key Takeaways

- **Instagram changed from 30 hashtags max → 5 hashtags max.** This is a recent platform rule change. Requires much more intentional selection.
- **Hashtags = keywords.** Someone searching #TeamGoGo or #eXpRealty will find every post tagged with that hashtag — you want agent attraction posts to surface in those searches.
- **Hashtags must match the audience.** Agent attraction posts → agent hashtags. Buyer/seller posts → buyer/seller hashtags. Never mix.
- **Gogo's 5 agent attraction hashtags:**
  1. #TeamGoGo — your organization/downline name (builds searchable archive of all your agent content)
  2. #eXpRealty — brokerage full name (not just #eXp)
  3. #Realtor
  4. #Agent
  5. (Implied: one more specific to your market, niche, or organization)
- **Adapt for your situation:** Replace #TeamGoGo with your own downline name. Replace #eXpRealty with your brokerage.
