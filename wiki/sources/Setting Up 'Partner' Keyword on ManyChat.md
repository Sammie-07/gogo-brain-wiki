# Setting Up 'Partner' Keyword on ManyChat

> GSMC/GoGet'Em training walkthrough of Gogo's live "partner" keyword automation in ManyChat — the exact sequence used to capture and convert agents interested in joining eXp under her sponsorship.

## Key Takeaways

- **Trigger:** Anyone who comments or DMs with the word "partner" on any of Gogo's posts enters the flow automatically.
- **Full sequence:**
  1. **Immediate message:** "I am so excited you are interested in changing brokerages and maybe joining me at eXp." → Button: "I am in"
  2. **After button click:** "Perfect. Jump into my calendar and let's talk." → Button: "Partner with me" (links to Gogo's partner page — full sign-up and next steps)
  3. **23-hour follow-up (if button not clicked):** "Hey, did you get a chance to jump into my calendar yet? Let me know below."
     - "Yes, I did" → congratulatory message
     - "Not yet" → prompt to schedule a call with Gogo
- **Tone rule:** Write every ManyChat message the way you naturally speak. Mannerism and tone must sound like you — not generic.
- **Template available:** A cloneable ManyChat template exists in the lesson description. Clone it → swap out your own messages → done.
- **Prerequisite:** Must have a ManyChat account (link in lesson description).
- This flow is specifically for **agent attraction** (eXp sponsorship), not buyer/seller lead gen — different keyword, different audience.
