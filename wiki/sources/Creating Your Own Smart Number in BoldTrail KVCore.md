# Creating Your Own Smart Number in BoldTrail/KVCore

> GSMC 2026 training by Kristy Waker. Explains why every agent needs their own personal smart number in BoldTrail/KVCore — and the danger of using the default brokerage-shared number.

## Key Takeaways

- **KVCore is now called BoldTrail** — same platform, rebranded.
- **The default eXp number is a shared round-robin line.** Any call to it from someone not already in your database routes to the next available agent in the office — not necessarily you. Your leads go to other agents.
- **Buy your own smart number: ~$27/month** via BoldTrail. Non-negotiable if you use this platform.
- **What your personal smart number does:**
  - Rings directly to you and only you
  - Automatically adds every caller to your database as a new lead
  - Texts route directly into your lead inbox
  - Supports sign riders ("Dial 123 for more info on this property") tied to specific listings
- **Setup:** BoldTrail → Profile → Phone → Buy Now → follow prompts. Set "Forward Agent Replies" to your personal cell number.
- **Local area code matters** — use an area code local to your market. Leads are more likely to answer and trust a local number.
- **Where to use it (plaster everywhere):**
  - Yard signs and sign riders
  - Website and KVCore/BoldTrail IDX site
  - Email signatures
  - Facebook, Instagram
  - Business cards
  - Brand Tracker (smartphone number field)
- **Every CRM has this feature** — if not using BoldTrail, get your own smart number through whatever CRM you use.
