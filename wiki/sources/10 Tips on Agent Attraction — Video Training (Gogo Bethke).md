# 10 Tips on Agent Attraction — Video Training (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo presents 10 tips for attracting agents to eXp (or any brokerage/team) with heavy focus on social media tactics. A different presentation from the South Florida Rally PDF — this version goes deep on platform-level execution.

## Key Takeaways

- **Tip 1 — Don't be a secret agent:** Agents who only post just-listed/just-sold with no personal presence are invisible. Must be on camera and in front of people. "If you don't feel comfortable doing that, you should probably stop this presentation right now."
- **Tip 2 — Social media presence:** Set up Instagram (business page), Facebook (business page), LinkedIn (professional profile). All three linked and consistent: same name, same headshot, everywhere.
- **Profile essentials:** Who are you, where do you work, how to contact you. Contact button must be visible — "millennials have the attention span of a goldfish" and won't dig for it.
- **Link in bio:** One all-in-one link (first/last name .com). Gogo's link routes to buyer agent, listing agent, speaking, YouTube, podcast, affiliate linktree, and Partner With Me tab.
- **Tip 3 — Tag location:** Tag cities and neighborhoods you want agents from. Agents in Chicago follow Chicago hashtags — your content finds them without you living there.
- **Tag people:** When you tag someone, your post reaches their audience too.
- **Hashtags:** Think of them as search words. `#healthinsurancerealtors` = people searching that topic find your post.
- **Tip 4 — Content formats:** Reels (Instagram) = TikTok = YouTube Shorts. Same vertical video, different platform names. Adapt per platform, don't recreate from scratch.
- **Consistency is non-negotiable:** Gogo posted 70,000+ times across platforms in 11 years. "I didn't attract 230 agents by posting one time four years ago."
- **Done-for-you option:** Gogo's bootcamp agent attraction course includes a "done for you" option where her team builds your all-in-one website.
