# A Conversation Between Two Immigrant Women (Vilma Núñez)

> GoGet'Em Webinars guest session with Vilma Núñez — PhD, marketing expert, and immigrant CEO. Candid conversation between two women who built businesses in a country not their own.

## Key Takeaways

- **Vilma Núñez** — PhD, CEO of Convierte Más (Spanish-language digital marketing agency), immigrant entrepreneur.
- **Immigrant resilience as business advantage:** Both Gogo and Vilma discuss how immigrating forces resourcefulness, risk tolerance, and work ethic that native-born entrepreneurs often lack. Failure is not an option when you have no safety net to return to.
- **"I can have it all" mantra** — Vilma's philosophy: you don't have to choose between career, family, and personal fulfillment. This resonates directly with Gogo's own "you can have it all" belief.
- **Marketing expert angle:** Vilma brings a PhD-level marketing lens to the conversation — data-driven, systematic approach to growing an audience and converting leads.
- **Shared values:** Both women built from zero in America; both lead large teams; both believe immigrant grit compounds over time.
