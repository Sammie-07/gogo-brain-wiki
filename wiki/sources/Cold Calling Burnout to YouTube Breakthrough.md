# Cold Calling Burnout to YouTube Breakthrough (Kaila Lindsey)

> GoGet'Em Webinars guest session with Kaila Lindsey. Kaila shares her journey from cold-calling burnout to generating leads exclusively through YouTube content.

## Key Takeaways

- **Kaila Lindsey** — real estate agent; hit $24M in annual production while pregnant, then experienced burnout from cold-calling model.
- **The breaking point:** $24M is impressive — but if it requires 6+ hours/day of cold calling and you're pregnant and exhausted, the model isn't sustainable.
- **YouTube as the pivot:** Kaila rebuilt her business through YouTube content — area guides, market updates, neighborhood tours. Inbound leads arrive pre-warmed.
- **Inbound > Outbound:** YouTube leads watch multiple videos before reaching out. By the time they call, they already trust her. Conversion is far higher than cold calls.
- **Content-to-lead funnel:** YouTube video → Google search result → lead watches → lead contacts agent. No cold outreach required.
- **Key message for Gogo's audience:** You don't have to cold call to build a $10M+ business. Social/content platforms create leverage that calling never can.
