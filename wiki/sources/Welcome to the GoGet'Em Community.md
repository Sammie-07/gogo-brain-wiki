# Welcome to the GoGet'Em Community

> Gogo's official welcome video for new GoGet'Em Community members. Covers the full community structure, onboarding steps, the 2026 Social Media Challenge framing, audit club, affiliate program, and Done With You upgrade.

## Key Takeaways

- **Community age:** ~1.5–2 years old at time of recording. 350+ members already enrolled when video was shot.
- **URL:** gogetemcommunity.com. Has its own app — recommended over desktop.
- **Topics covered inside:** Mindset, structures, AI, VAs, branding, marketing, trackers, taxes, reducing taxable income, building a team, S Corp vs LLC, hiring your children, retirement, wealth building.

### 2026 Social Media Challenge (GSMC)
- The featured 2026 program. 36+ videos in January alone just for fundamentals.
- Metaphor: **"Building your social media house"** — foundation → walls → windows → doors → insulation → roof. Skip a brick and the house crumbles at year end. Do not skip steps.
- Watch daily — during morning routine, walks, cooking, driving. Find your habit and commit.

### Onboarding Steps (in order)
1. Log in at gogetemcommunity.com or download the app
2. Check the Welcome section inside the community
3. Join the **group chat** — team available Mon–Fri 9–5; reminders, audit notices, live alerts
4. Schedule your **onboarding call** with Natalia (15–30 min walkthrough of everything you're getting)
5. Join the **Facebook group**
6. Follow steps for the **100 Audit Club** (if you want Gogo's eyes on your brand in 2026)
7. Set up your **affiliate link** (10% of referred member's payment; bring 10 = membership is free)

### 100 Audit Club
- Gogo personally audits 100 members' brands in 2026 — domains, social profiles, emails, email signatures, yard signs, everything.
- Application required. First 100 approved get in; after that, closed.
- Approved members get a separate monthly audit call in addition to the main monthly live.

### Monthly Live
- One big live per month with Gogo + Q&A. Show up live when possible.
- Dates posted in the calendar weeks ahead. Reminders via chat, email, Facebook group.

### Affiliate Program
- 10% of every referred member's monthly payment, ongoing.
- Link found in dashboard → top right dots → Affiliate.
- Bring 10 paying members → your own membership is effectively free.

### Done With You (DWY) Upgrade
- Access to **Gogo's exact team** (same people who set up her ManyChat, Instagram, bio, website, funnels).
- Live Tech Support: **Tuesdays & Wednesdays at 3pm ET** — group call, bring your stuck points.
- Payment options: upfront, 2-payment, or 3-payment plan.

### Support
- **Natalia** — GoGet'Em Community manager. Answers all emails.
- Chat: Mon–Fri 9–5 for quick questions
- Email: support@gogetemcommunity.com for after-hours or non-urgent items
