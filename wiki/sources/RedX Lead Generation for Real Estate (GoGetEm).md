# RedX Lead Generation for Real Estate (GoGetEm)

> GoGet'Em Community training. Keeley from RedX presents; Randy Gammo gives mortgage market update; Clency and Devin (Team Gogo members) demonstrate how they use RedX in practice.

## Key Takeaways

- **RedX** = lead generation tool providing agents with expired listings, FSBOs, and geographic farming data — focused on listing leads.
- Critical in low-inventory markets: RedX specializes in helping agents find listings to work when inventory is scarce.
- Randy Gammo mortgage market context: Rates ~6.99% 30-year fixed, 6.375% 15-year fixed. Inventory surge expected Nov–Feb (at time of recording). Fed meeting upcoming.
- Randy's special offer for Team Gogo: 1% buydown paid by National Mortgage (rates drop 1% for client's first year), and affiliate loan-status dashboard for agent pipeline visibility.
- United Wholesale Mastermind: Randy offered to fly US agents in, cover hotel, spend day at UWM offices + night out.
- Clency and Devin demo: Step-by-step how Team Gogo agents practically use RedX for listing generation.
- **Gogo's endorsement principle:** "I only ever promote anyone who deserves a spot. When you're recommending someone, they're an extension of your reputation."
- "This is the newest tool we started using with our team." — Gogo
