# How to Become the Obvious Choice in Your Market

> A GoGet'Em Community live niche-training where [[Gogo Bethke]] runs agents' real applications through her AI "second brain" to produce a personalized niche, Instagram bio, top-8 marketing ideas, SEO plan, 12-week content calendar, and 90-day marketing plan — the training behind the $24.99 [[Niche Finder]] report.

## Key Takeaways
- **Be the only, not the best.** The goal is to be "the only realtor for X" — e.g. "the only realtor for first responders buying new build in Martin County" — not the best realtor for everyone. Narrowing makes you the obvious choice for a specific person.
- **Your niche comes from your real life.** The application asks about kids, marriage, pets, languages, past jobs, hobbies, and personal story ("have diarrhea of the personality mouth"). Gogo works with foreigners (she is one) and type-1-diabetic families (her son has it) because those are lived truths — authentic niches convert.
- **Niches evolve as you evolve.** Gogo's niche 15 years ago was young stay-at-home moms wanting a 3-bed/2-bath with a yard; today it would be empty-nesters downsizing to the lake. When you feel "burnt out," you've usually just outgrown a phase of life — rerun the report; it's okay for your niche to change.
- **You can run multiple niches.** Run the report separately for buyers vs. sellers, or for luxury, agent attraction, spiritual-journey clients, divorce, etc. — a different set of answers yields a different report.
- **Instagram bio must name the niche in the first ~5 words**, be under 150 characters, read smoothly aloud, no em-dashes or bullets. Pick ONE of the three generated options and own it. Drop brokerage name and "years of experience" — clients don't care which brand you're under.
- **Top 8 marketing ideas** are filtered from the GoGet'Em marketing framework and tied to the agent's actual life/niche. Don't start all eight — pick two or three that excite you, run the "how to start" first step this week, then add more once they're on autopilot.
- **DM-keyword automation should be one step, never two.** The bio's "DM me [keyword]" triggers an auto-message that delivers the lead magnet AND captures the email in the same message, then feeds it straight to the CRM ([[Lead Generation]]) for follow-up — you collect emails to convert them, not to hoard them.
- **SEO = exact keywords in exact order.** Google "spiders" crawl every site; if your page doesn't literally say "best realtor in Martin County for new construction," you won't rank. Put the primary keyword (plus longtails) naturally on every page — don't keyword-stuff, but "massage" it in.
- **Build your OWN website in Claude**, not a BoldTrail/[[Lofty]]/KVCore template. Own yourname.com, add a page per niche (fishing, veterans, coastal living, retirement communities) so Google finds them all on one site.
- **12-week blog calendar**: publish one 800–1,500-word article per week on your website blog, each with niche keywords; repurpose every article into an Instagram carousel, a reel, and a LinkedIn post via Claude to maximize mileage. Remove all AI signatures so it sounds like you.
- **12-week SOCIAL content calendar** is separate from the blog calendar and is color-coded (meeting = blue, stories = yellow, feed = purple, reel = green, giveaway = pink).
- **90-day marketing plan in three phases**: Month 1 (wk 1–4) = Foundation ("plant your flag," get on camera weekly, establish brand signature); Month 2 (wk 5–8) = Momentum (launch lead magnet, run paid ads, hit 5+ coffees/week with partners); Month 3 (wk 9–12) = Scale (repurpose top reels into YouTube, make the lead magnet permanent, build repeatable monthly rituals). Then rinse and repeat.
- **$5/day paid-ad test** to a lead-magnet landing page (e.g. relocation PDF targeting feeder-market zip codes), running 30 days before reviewing cost-per-click and scaling.
- **Builder / referral pipelines**: coffee with one builder rep per week, put their brand on the closing gift, interview them on your podcast — one agent reported 3–4 closings last year from builder-rep relationships. For veterans: VFW/American Legion posts, JAG attorneys, and military-base transition offices are pre-earned-trust referral partners.
- **The $24.99 AI Niche Report / "second brain."** Two options: (1) free — copy Gogo's exact prompt script into your own Claude; the tradeoff is it lacks her 15 years of experience and pulls generic info from the internet. (2) Paid $24.99 — runs on Gogo's AI "second brain" (built over months from every YouTube video, presentation, and post she's ever made), which costs data out of her Claude, hence the charge. This was the first-ever public run of that brain.
