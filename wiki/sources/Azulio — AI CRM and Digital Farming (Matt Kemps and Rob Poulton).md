# Azulio — AI-Powered CRM, Agentic AI & Digital Farming (Matt Kemps & Rob Poulton)

> A weekly [[GoGet'Em Community]] daytime training where Azulio co-founders [[Matt Kemps]] and [[Rob Poulton]] demoed their done-with-you AI real estate platform — custom AI agents, agentic lead-source hand-offs, conversation memory, AI voice calling, social management, and AI-driven "digital farming" lead generation — with TeamGogo-discounted pricing.

## Key Takeaways
- [[Azulio]] is a "done-with-you" AI platform built on the GoHighLevel backend, pre-configured ("master mode") for real estate; positioned as a full setup-for-you alternative to self-serve CRMs (Follow Up Boss, YLOPO, BoldTrail). Headline claims: 400%+ ROI, 40 hours/week saved per team, speed-to-lead under 15 seconds via high-trust channels like iMessage.
- **Custom AI agents per listing**: each yard sign carries a QR code feeding a property-specific AI agent that knows square footage, floor plans, builder, MUD/PID status, video location, and the agent's showing calendar, and can book showings via text or call.
- **Agentic AI by lead source**: AI hands off seamlessly between knowledge bases and voices (demo: FSBO agent "Wren" transferred a caller to cash-offer specialist "Jessica") with business infrastructure built around the transfer — defined as "AI to AI, make it seamless, doing an entire process for me."
- **Conversation memory**: the AI remembers a lead's identity, type, and prior conversation months later. Example — a Facebook lead (David Hansford) was put under contract at $395K (FMV ~$405K) for a one-week cash-offer close, listing won over the phone without ever meeting in person.
- **Automation safety**: any reply into Azulio kills the drip automations and exits the workflow so scheduled texts and AI never double-message a lead.
- **Voice AI / TCPA**: outbound AI calling requires an opt-in (a new GoHighLevel release allows opt-in via a Meta lead form); inbound is unrestricted. Reported stats: ~60% answer rate, ~82% appointment-set rate. Voices via ElevenLabs, selectable by language/accent/gender with a high-quality tier; "Jessa" is their highest-converting voice. Team leads can listen to live calls, review transcripts, and thumbs-up/down to train the agent.
- **Digital farming**: agents get optimized neighborhood / golf-course-community websites (example: Cimarron Hills) stripped of CSS so AI models (Claude, GPT) index the keyword content; LLMs consume a new site within ~5 minutes to 5 hours of going live, producing free leads with no ad spend.
- **Website visitor tracking**: full session recordings of visitor cursor movement, device/browser/timestamp, plus skip-traced contact info (email, LinkedIn) and DNC check — even when no form is filled. Out-of-area leads route through the GoGo Map referral network for a referral fee.
- **Luxury deal example**: one QR-code lead chain produced a $1.3M listing sale (closed ~2 weeks) plus a $4.5M buy-side (Matt as REMLO partner) — $5.8M volume for ~15 minutes of actual work.
- **Social media management**: multi-platform scheduling (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Google Business, TikTok, Snapchat), per-channel customization, AI copywriting, and social listening for trending hashtags — Hootsuite-like, with all stats in one dashboard.
- **Personal AI assistant**: every account gets an AI agent textable via iMessage/Siri that creates contacts, adds tags (e.g., SOI), books calendar slots, logs attraction/recruiting prospects, and returns market research — "think of it as a little agent."
- **Other features**: AI-generated CMA + listing presentation + cash offers produced in ~10 seconds and sent straight to the contact. Cash offers sourced via eXp's iBuyer partner plus ~15 other iBuyers.
- **Pricing**: retail $1,695/mo + $5,000 setup. TeamGogo pricing $595/mo (no digital farming) or $995/mo (with digital farming), setup fee waived — must mention "TeamGogo." Setup 3–4 weeks; results in 30–60 days (≤90 days). Weekly Azulio training Wed 11am CST; open AI training Thu 11am CST; contact via calendar.azulio.ai.
- **Mortgage Minutes** ([[Jason Dupree]], Texana Bank): rates ~6.65% avg (down from ~7% a year prior), oil ~$90/barrel, expects rate cuts. Promoted the [[REMLO Program]] — Real Estate Mortgage Loan Officer, licensed in all 50 states, ~1 week onboarding, ~0.5% of mortgage (~$1,500 on a $300K loan); qualification is 6 buy-side transactions in the prior 12 months. Team Gogo is the #1 REMLO group at Texana Bank.
