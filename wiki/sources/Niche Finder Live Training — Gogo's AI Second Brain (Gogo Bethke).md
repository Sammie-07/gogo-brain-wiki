# Niche Finder Live Training — Using Gogo's AI Second Brain to Build a 90-Day Marketing Plan

> An open-to-the-public [[GoGet'Em Community]] / #teamgogo daytime training where [[Gogo Bethke]] live-coaches agents through the Niche Finder — an AI tool running on her "second brain" that turns a short questionnaire into a personalized Instagram bio, marketing ideas, SEO strategy, and a day-by-day 90-day marketing plan.

## Key Takeaways
- The **Niche Finder** runs on Gogo's AI **"second brain"** — an AI trained over the prior months on all her trainings, podcasts, speaking engagements, and [[GoGet'Em Community]] content, encoding her ~15 years of marketing and branding experience; agents complete a ~10-question questionnaire and the report is generated by her brain in her cloud.
- **Pricing/access**: the branded report run through Gogo's brain is **$24** (referenced loosely as "$20"); alternatively the team gives away a free prompt/script agents can paste into their own [[Claude]]/ChatGPT for a generic, non-Gogo-grounded version. The tool took a week to build.
- Each report outputs six things: **(1) 3 Instagram bio options, (2) top 8 marketing ideas** filtered to the agent's life/niche, **(3) an SEO strategy** (primary keyword + 5 long-tail keywords + title tag/meta description), **(4) a 12-week blog content calendar** (articles 800–1500 words), **(5) an internal linking plan, (6) a day-by-day 90-day marketing plan** (foundation weeks 1–4, momentum 5–8, scale 9–12).
- **Execution discipline**: pick only **2 (max 3)** of the 8 marketing ideas, get them fully running and scheduled, then add the next one — avoids analysis paralysis and burnout.
- **Bio rule**: your bio must name your niche in the **first 5 words** — "If it does not name your niche in the first 5 words, you are invisible." DM-CTA bios ("DM me [word]") require a **ManyChat** backend to function.
- **AI search now reads social posts**, not just websites — ChatGPT and other AIs surface agents from Instagram/TikTok content, so keyword discipline in the right order on socials now drives AI recommendations. [[Radek Walesa]] confirmed a live example: he landed his first ChatGPT-sourced lead (a luxury Poznań residential client) and appeared in AI recommendations even to strangers not connected to him.
- **Run the report separately per avatar** — buy-side ideal client, listing-side ideal client, and ideal agent recruit/downline are three different niches yielding three different marketing plans; blend them across the week (e.g., Mon/Wed sellers, Tue/Thu buyers, Fri/Sat agents).
- **Pivot, don't restart** your social account when content has drifted — "Never, ever, ever. You just pivot." Followers move with you; niches change as you change through life phases.
- **SEO mechanics**: niche keywords must appear in the right order on the website AND socials; use WordPress + Yoast SEO or Rank Math; blog posts can be added to KVCore / Bold Trail; submit pages to Google Search Console; results take 3–6 months. Repurpose each blog into an IG carousel, reel, and LinkedIn post.
- Content needs **~8 exposures** before someone registers who you are and what you do.
- **Lead magnets must be gated** (name/email/phone) so the agent captures contact info and follows up; always ask new leads exactly where and how they found you so you can do more of what works.
- **REMLO segment** ([[Jason Dupree]]): oil down to ~$70/barrel, rates expected to drop toward early-year levels, on pace to shatter refinance records since 2021; agents can become loan originators and keep **~0.5%** of the mortgage amount as lender-side commission; requires **6 buy-side transactions** to qualify; one federally chartered bank license covers all US states.
- IMPORTANT NOTE ON THIRD-PARTY TOOLS: the generated report recommended **Buffer and Hootsuite** for post scheduling, and on the call Gogo explicitly flagged that she personally does not use or know those tools — illustrating that brain-grounded reports can still surface third-party platforms the source has never used, so agents should vet any recommended tool before adopting it.
