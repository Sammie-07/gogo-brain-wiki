# How to Host Open Houses (Summary)

> Condensed written summary of Gogo's open house system — pre-event, day-of, and post-event. Reinforces the full walkthrough already in the wiki.

## Key Takeaways

- Schedule in MLS by **Thursday** — Zillow/Realtor.com take 2–3 days to sync.
- Promote on all social media + email database + door knock 20–50 neighboring homes (bring a lender for safety and pre-approvals).
- Day of: 40 directional signs with balloons, all lights on, comfortable temp, bake cookies, digital sign-in sheet for lead capture.
- **Business card trick:** Claim to be out of cards, offer to text contact info directly — you get their number instead of hoping they call.
- Security sweep before leaving: physically check every door and window on the main floor (visitors may have unlocked them intentionally).
- **Before leaving the driveway:** Send personalized selfie-style video texts to every attendee with traffic numbers, urgency, and a clear CTA.
- All leads into CRM; follow up throughout the week with property status updates.
