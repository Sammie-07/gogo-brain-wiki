# Updating Your LinkedIn Profile

> GSMC 2026 training by Kristy Waker. Quick walkthrough for setting up or updating a LinkedIn profile — focused on securing the custom URL and logging it in the Brand Tracker. Full profile build-out (headshot, cover photo, bio) happens in a later pass once those assets exist.

## Key Takeaways

- **Two-pass approach:** First pass = secure your custom URL and log it in the Brand Tracker. Second pass = add headshot, cover photo, bio, and links once those assets are created across all platforms.
- **Custom URL:** Edit via pencil icon (right side of profile page) → change from auto-generated string to your name or brand name (e.g., `linkedin.com/in/christywaker`). Do not use "real estate team" for a personal profile — use your actual name.
- **Personal vs. business account:** Personal profile = your name. Business account = only needed if hiring team members or collecting job applications.
- **Brand Tracker:** Paste the LinkedIn URL into the spreadsheet immediately after updating. Checkbox it off.
- **About section, experience, licenses/certifications** are all editable. You can control which sections are visible to which audiences.
- **Sequence in GSMC:** LinkedIn comes after domain, Instagram, Facebook, and business email — part of the full brand infrastructure build.
