# Marketing Through FREE Neighborhood Events (Stacey Leslie)

> GoGet'Em Community session with Stacey Leslie — $100M producer in 2024 with zero ad spend. Full system for using neighborhood Facebook groups and vendor-sponsored community events to generate leads.

## Key Takeaways

- **$100M in 2024, zero ad spend:** Stacey's entire lead generation system is organic — community presence, events, and Facebook groups. No paid advertising.
- **Pick a community → join or create FB group:** Establish presence in the Facebook group for your target neighborhood. If one doesn't exist, create it.
- **"Never give the answer on a post":** When community members ask questions in the group, respond with "Send me a DM" rather than answering publicly. Drives private conversations → relationship → lead.
- **Post newsworthy community content first:** Lead with value (local news, events, restaurant openings) before anything real estate. Build trust before asking for anything.
- **Vendor-sponsored events:** Events cost the agent nothing because local vendors (restaurants, businesses) sponsor in exchange for exposure.
  - Example: Pumpkin Patch → evolved into "Coco and Caroling" event — 3,000 attendees, $15K cost, fully vendor-covered.
- **Engage posts one-by-one (not simultaneously):** Commenting on many posts at the same moment pushes you down the algorithm. Engage sequentially to stay at the top of the feed.
- **Capture contact info at events:** QR code at event → collects name + contact info → adds to database.
- **"7 times to remember you":** Prospect must encounter you approximately 7 times before they remember who you are. Events + Facebook posts + DMs compound toward that number.

## Quotes

> "Never give the answer on a post. Always send them to your DMs. That's where the relationship starts."

> "Three thousand people showed up and it cost me nothing. The vendors paid for everything."

> "They have to see you seven times before they remember you. Events accelerate that."
