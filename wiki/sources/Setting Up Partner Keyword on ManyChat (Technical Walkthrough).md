# Setting Up 'Partner' Keyword on ManyChat (Technical Walkthrough)

> Step-by-step walkthrough of how the "partner" ManyChat keyword flow is configured — triggers, messages, buttons, 23-hour follow-up, and the cloneable template. Reinforces content already in the wiki.

## Key Takeaways

- Trigger: any comment or DM containing the word "partner" on any post.
- Immediate message: *"I am so excited you are interested in changing brokerages and maybe joining me at EXP."* → button: **"I am in"**
- After click: *"Perfect. Jump into my calendar and let's talk."* → **"Partner with me"** button (links to partner page)
- 23-hour follow-up if button not clicked: *"Hey, did you get a chance to jump into my calendar yet?"* → "Yes, I did" (congratulatory) or "Not yet" (re-prompt to book)
- Write every message in your natural speaking voice — mannerism and tone must sound like you.
- **Cloneable template** available in the lesson description — clone into your ManyChat, swap out the text.
- Requires a ManyChat account (sign-up link in lesson description).
