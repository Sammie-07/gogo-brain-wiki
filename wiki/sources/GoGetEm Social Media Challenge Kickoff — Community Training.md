# GoGet'Em Social Media Challenge Kickoff — Community Training

> Live kickoff call for a GoGet'Em social media challenge — covering the non-negotiable business page rule, monthly brand tracking habit, training calendar access, community structure, brand naming validation, and LLC entity formation.

## Key Takeaways

- **Business page vs. personal page is non-negotiable:** Running real estate through a personal account caps you at 5,000 followers, eliminates ads capability, and destroys any chance of a private life. Business page = unlimited followers, ads, and proper separation.
- **The system:** Post on your business page first, then share it to your personal. Personal friends still see everything — now it runs like a business.
- **Monthly brand tracking habit:** Log follower counts and monthly views on the 2nd of every month (set a phone reminder). Takes 5–10 minutes. Comparing January to December shows compounding growth.
- **The only reason to look back is to see how far you've come** — but you can only see growth if you tracked the starting point.
- **GoGet'Em Training Calendar:** Kristy grants access to a shared calendar. Accept the email invite to see all upcoming community trainings.
- **Community structure:** Three foundational series — Business Mastery, Money Mastery, Master Your Mind — live inside the community platform.
- **Anderson Advisors:** Gogo's personal LLC/trust/legal entity provider. She holds 27 LLCs under an all-inclusive unlimited package. Recommended for members doing entity formation.
- **Brand naming validation — two-step before committing:**
  1. Go to Instagram → search the exact name → if nothing pops up, it's available
  2. Go to GoDaddy.com → check the same name with .com extension
  3. If BOTH are available → that is your brand
- **Only one of each:** On Instagram and .com, only one exists — you must own both. Facebook duplicates are fine. Instagram and .com are unique assets.
- **Once the name clears:** Buy the .com → build the website → run the brand everywhere.
