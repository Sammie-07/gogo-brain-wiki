# Add Links & Contact Info to Instagram

> Step-by-step walkthrough of updating an Instagram business profile with bio, links, and contact information. Part of *Gogo's Social Media Challenge 2026*.

## Key Takeaways

- Go to Instagram → bottom-right profile icon → **Edit Profile** to access all update fields.
- **Bio:** Tap the bio field → paste your short bio (who you are, what you sell, your niche). Keep it tight and clear.
- **Links:** Up to **2 links** on an unverified account; up to **5 links** once Instagram-verified. Each link can have a custom title and an icon (logo, image, or emoji) displayed next to it. Add → type URL → add title → add icon → Done.
- **Contact Options** (under Profile Information): Add up to three contact methods:
  - Business email address
  - Business phone number (choose Call or Text)
  - WhatsApp phone number
- Use your **BoldTrail smart number** (from Brand Tracker) as the business phone — not your personal cell or eXp shared line.
- Once saved, profile visitors see: bio → links → Contact button (reveals email + phone options).
