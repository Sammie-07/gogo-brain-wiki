# How to Edit Videos in CapCut (GoGet'Em Community)

> Walkthrough by a Gogo team member showing her standard CapCut editing workflow — the same process used when editing Gogo's own Reels. Covers lighting adjustment, skin retouching, captions, text overlays, volume, trimming, and export.

## Key Takeaways

- **CapCut is the team's go-to video editor** — Gogo and her team use it for all Reels editing (alternative to editing inside Instagram)
- **Workflow: New project → select video → Add → edit → export → upload to Instagram**
- **Camera roll access:** Recommended to grant access video by video (not full library) for privacy

### Edit Steps in Order

**1. Lighting — Adjust**
- Bottom bar → Edit (scissors) → scroll to **Adjust** (between Overlay and Filters)
- **Auto Adjust** (leftmost option) — brightens the scene automatically. **Paid feature** (purple diamond = paid)
- **Free alternative:** Manually drag sliders for Brightness, Contrast, Saturation, Brilliance, Sharpen

**2. Skin — Retouch**
- Bottom bar → **Retouch** → Face
- **Even** slider: evens out skin tone (drag up slightly)
- **Smooth** slider (far right): smooths skin texture (drag up slightly)
- Tap **Apply to All** → Confirm → checkmark

**3. Text Overlay**
- Bottom bar → **Text** → Add Text → type content → checkmark
- Drag to reposition; pinch orange bar at ends to extend duration
- Keep text on brand: match your colors, fonts, and visual theme

**4. Auto Captions**
- Bottom bar → Text → **Auto Captions** → choose template → Generate
- CapCut listens and transcribes automatically
- Review and fix errors: tap a caption word → **Edit Captions** → tap and retype → checkmark

**5. Volume**
- Bottom bar → Edit → **Volume** → drag slider up to increase loudness

**6. Trim / Split**
- Play video → pause at exact cut point → bottom left: **Split** → select unwanted segment → **Delete**
- Use case: remove the camera operator's voice from the start before Gogo begins speaking

**7. Export**
- Hit **Export** → video saves to camera roll → upload to Instagram

### Free vs. Paid Features

| Feature | Tier |
|---------|------|
| Manual brightness/contrast/saturation adjustments | Free |
| Auto Adjust (one-tap lighting) | Paid (purple diamond icon) |
| Auto Captions | Free |
| Retouch (even + smooth) | Free |
| Trim / Split / Delete | Free |

**Purple diamond icon = paid feature.** All core editing tasks are doable on the free version.

### Brand Consistency Rule

> "You do want to keep on brand with what you—like your colors, your fonts. You kind of want to have a theme going with everything that you share."

Text overlays, color choices, and fonts must match your personal brand across all content.
