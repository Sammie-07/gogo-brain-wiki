# Elevate Your Agent Attraction Game — Gogo Bethke (GoGetEm)

> 3-hour GoGet'Em International Women's Day session — Gogo Bethke on the full agent attraction system: content strategy, personal brand positioning, co-sponsorship, and the eXp organization model.

## Key Takeaways

- Recorded on International Women's Day — Gogo notes it's a major holiday in Romania/Hungary but underacknowledged in the US.
- This is a deep-dive master class on agent attraction — one of Gogo's primary teaching topics.
- Core framework: agents are attracted through **content + community + co-sponsorship** — the three levers of sustainable agent attraction.
- **Content for agent attraction**: not just buyer/seller content — agents need to see your business, your results, and your life. "Brag without bragging" by storytelling your wins.
- **Co-sponsorship with Gogo**: agents who partner with Gogo through eXp get access to Team Gogo resources, GoGet'Em access, and her co-sponsorship — a key differentiator for attracting talent.
- **The organization model**: Gogo's team is global — agents in any state or country can join. The local "team" is different from the broader "organization."
- Session ran 3 hours 3 minutes — thorough coverage including live Q&A with audience members.

## Quotes

> "You're going to be showing up for the next 12 months consistently. That's what separates the top agents from everyone else."
