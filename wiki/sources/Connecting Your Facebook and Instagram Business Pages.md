# Connecting Your Facebook and Instagram Business Pages

> Step-by-step walkthrough of linking a Facebook Business Page to Instagram via Meta Business Suite, and granting team access.

## Key Takeaways

- To edit or post on behalf of a Facebook Business Page you must **switch** to the page — you cannot act from your personal profile.
- Go to **Settings → Permissions → Linked Accounts** to connect Instagram. Click "Not connected" and follow the prompts.
- When you link Facebook and Instagram, Meta automatically creates a **Meta Business Suite** — a unified dashboard for managing both profiles, ads, messages, and comments.
- Meta Business Suite enables **secure team access**: assign other people to work on your pages without sharing passwords.
- Once connected, manage everything from **Professional Dashboard → Page Access** — grant granular access per role (content, ads, insights, messages, settings).
- Week 1 of Gogo's system-building curriculum covers setup and infrastructure; Week 2 covers bio, design, and customization.
