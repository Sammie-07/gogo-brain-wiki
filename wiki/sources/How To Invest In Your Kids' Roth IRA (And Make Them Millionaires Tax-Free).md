# How To Invest In Your Kids' Roth IRA (And Make Them Millionaires Tax-Free)

> Toby Mathis (Anderson Business Advisors) on how to legally hire your kids, document earned income, and fund a Roth IRA that compounds for decades — completely tax-free.

## Key Takeaways

- Parents can legally **hire their children** as employees in their business — paying them a salary for legitimate work.
- That earned income qualifies the child to open and fund a **Roth IRA** — tax-free growth from childhood.
- A child's Roth IRA funded from age 8–18 compounds for 50+ years, creating millionaire-level outcomes through time alone.
- Documentation is critical: the work must be real, the pay market-rate, and records maintained.
- Tax benefit: the salary paid to the child is a business deduction; if the child earns under the standard deduction threshold (~$14K), they pay zero income tax.
- This strategy stacks with other Toby Mathis wealth-building frameworks: hire family → reduce business taxes → build generational wealth simultaneously.
- Roth IRA distributions in retirement are completely tax-free — no RMDs, no income tax on withdrawals.
