# Quotes I Live By

> Slide deck of the core quotes and principles Gogo uses in presentations. Accompanied by personal stories that give each quote meaning.

## Key Takeaways

- Each quote is a platform for a personal story (elephant story, airplane story, Rolex story, airport story)
- Gogo positions herself as always engaging: always in the center or front, "the importance of engaging"
- "Once you see it, you can't unsee it" — used when referring to business opportunities and mindset shifts
- "Start worrying when they STOP talking. You became irrelevant."
- "People don't do what you say. People do what you do."
- "When you allow them to get to know you, they will love you. When they love you, they'll trust you. When they trust you, they'll do or buy anything you say or sell."
- "I'm looking for the people who are looking for me."
- "A NO is a MAYBE."
- "You don't have a TIME problem. You have a PRIORITY problem."
- "Business moves with the speed of TRUST."
- Final slide closer: "It's your own life, you can FUCK it up however you want to."
