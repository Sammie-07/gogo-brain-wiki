# AI For Real Estate Agents: How to Use AI to Generate Leads

> Full live training on using social media + AI to close 1 deal/month. Three versions of this training were posted to YouTube (also published as "AI For Realtors: How to Use AI to Close 1 Deal Every Month" and "How Realtors Can Use AI to Generate Leads and Close 1 Deal a Month" — all the same content). This is the definitive source page.

## Key Takeaways

**The math goal: 1 closing/month = $126,000/year**
- $350k avg price × 3% = $10,500/transaction × 12 months = $126,000
- "If you figure out how to do one a month, you can do two a month. It's just math."

**Core formula:** Consistency at the front end + automations at the back end.

**Identify your ideal client — "If you talk to everyone, you convert no one."**
- Don't target by price point. Target by who you are, what you love doing, who you enjoy spending time with.
- Your ideal client is someone similar to you — you already speak their language, go to their places, share their interests.
- Gogo's ideal client when she was selling: young families, 30s, kids, pets, wanted land/barn, Livingston County Michigan ($350k avg). She met them at school drop-off, zoo field trips.
- Content should reflect who you are → attracts your people → repels mismatches.
- "The fastest way to get miserable commissions is to work with people you have nothing in common with."

**Hashtag strategy:**
- Use ~20 hashtags per post (not 30 — one over the limit deletes all of them).
- First 10 = your lifestyle interests (yoga, golf, your neighborhood, your hobbies).
- Next 10 = the specific post topic.
- Always geo-tag the location in every post.
- Stay consistent — always fish in the same pond.

**Personal brand basics:**
- Post a photo that looks like you NOW. Not your best photo from 10 years ago.
- Same photo everywhere: signs, business cards, email signature, all social platforms.
- Own yourname.com.
- **Never include your location in your brand name** — you may move. Gogo spent 14 years building "Gogo's Real Estate" brand in Michigan and now lives in Florida. Lesson learned.

**Bio optimization with ChatGPT:**
- Prompt structure: "Act like a social media expert on Instagram. I am helping [name] who covers [area] and wants to specialize in [sellers/buyers]. Give me an optimized bio with one call to action targeting [ideal client]."
- Add a ManyChat keyword in the bio call to action (e.g., "DM me 'value' for a free home analysis").

**Call to action in every post:**
- Treat the audience like they're 3 years old — spell out exactly what you want them to do.
- Buyer CTA example: "Tap below to browse live listings."
- Seller CTA example: "Want to know how much your home is worth? Comment 'seller'."

**ManyChat keyword system:**
- Set up keywords like "buyer," "seller," "value," "community."
- When someone comments or DMs the keyword, automation sends them a personalized response + link.
- Set up once, works forever. Integrates with CRM.

**KV Core IDX feed:**
- Share listings directly from KV Core to social media.
- The link captures visitor info when they try to view more than 3 photos (requires Facebook login).
- KV Core then cross-references the email/Facebook to find them on LinkedIn, Google → enables personal outreach.

**Funnels (landing pages):**
- Every traffic source (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, Google Business) funnels into landing pages.
- Separate pages for buyers, sellers, renters, specific niches.
- Go High Level used for multi-platform scheduling + landing page building.

**78% rule:**
- 78% of buyers/sellers go with the first agent who contacted them.
- Speed of response = conversion. Automations make you "first" even while sleeping.
- 5-minute rule: respond within 5 minutes of a lead entering your CRM.

**Tools stack:**
- KV Core (free with eXp) — CRM + IDX feed + landing pages
- ManyChat (free tier available) — keyword automations on Instagram/Facebook
- Go High Level — advanced automation + multi-platform posting
- ChatGPT — bio writing, content strategy, hashtag research

**Brand integrity:**
- Never market products you don't use or love. One endorsement for the wrong thing destroys credibility permanently.
- Be the same person online as in person — authenticity converts.
- "Your brand is what people say about you when you are not in the room."
