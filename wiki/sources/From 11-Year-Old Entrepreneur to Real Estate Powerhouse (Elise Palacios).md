# From 11-Year-Old Entrepreneur to Real Estate Powerhouse (Elise Palacios)

> Gogopreneur episode featuring Elise Palacios — started a cleaning business at 11, managed adults at 12, became a top real estate agent and investor, now coaches through Reinvest Real Estate.

## Key Takeaways

- **Cleaning business at 11:** Started solo, quickly hired adults 3–4× her age to manage. Her first taste of leverage and team-building.
- **Real estate path:** Worked at KW, then moved to eXp Realty for agent-owned model and rev share.
- **Reinvest Real Estate:** Coaching program with Lou Raven and David Brook — focused on helping agents invest their commission income into real estate assets.
- **Luxury Bahamas STR:** Owns a short-term rental in the Bahamas as a luxury investment property. Example of moving income from active to passive.
- **First million-dollar listing story:** Specific pivotal deal that validated her ability to operate at the top of the market. Confidence over credentials.
- **CPR Exercise** (from John Kitchens):
  - **C**ommitment — what are you committing to?
  - **P**enalty — what happens if you break it?
  - **R**eward — what's the reward for following through?
  - Used as an accountability structure for goals and client commitments.
- **"I found something fascinating" protocol:** When delivering bad news to clients, open with curiosity rather than alarm. Reduces emotional spike, keeps conversation productive.

## Quotes
