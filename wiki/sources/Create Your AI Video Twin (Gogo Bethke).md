# Create Your AI Video Twin (Gogo Bethke)

> GoGet'Em Community training on using Higsfield + HeyGen to create AI avatar videos without being on camera.

## Key Takeaways

- **Higsfield.ai** ($350/year, Chrome extension) recreates any Pinterest/Instagram photo with your face using a trained avatar
- **Soul ID Character** = your personal avatar. Upload ~80 photos from every angle, SMILING to capture your real teeth — otherwise AI invents random teeth
- Create separate avatars for different hair styles; don't mix straight + curly in same avatar
- **HeyGen** animates your Higsfield photo into a speaking video using a script
- HeyGen voice cloning adds an accent on top of existing accents — workaround: use "record audio" to overlay your real voice reading a printed script
- Workflow: Pinterest image → Higsfield recreate → download → HeyGen photo-to-video → script → voice → Advanced: Warm Welcoming motion → generate
- Use **Sora** for 15-second imagination/B-roll videos; **HeyGen** for scripted talking-head videos
- Team can do this on your behalf when you don't feel like filming
