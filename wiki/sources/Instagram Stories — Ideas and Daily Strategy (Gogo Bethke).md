# Instagram Stories — Ideas and Daily Strategy (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo's complete philosophy and system for Instagram Stories — what they are, how many to post, how to build the habit, and 30 categorized content ideas across real estate, agent attraction, and digital products.

## Key Takeaways

### Stories vs. Feed (Storefront)
- **Feed (storefront)** = permanent; first thing strangers see; communicates your business identity; must stay focused on real estate
- **Stories** = 24-hour format; behind-the-scenes; personality-first; where followers get to know you as a human
- Stories keep people engaged between transactions. Without them, followers leave between years 1 and 7.

### Volume Target
- Minimum: **10–15 stories per day**
- Gogo's actual rate: **one story every hour** during waking hours (8am–midnight) = ~15 stories/day
- *"Do you want to make millions? That's what you have to do."*

### Habit-Building System
- Set an **hourly phone alarm** — every time it goes off, post a story
- Takes **90 days** to make it automatic; after 90 days, the brain prompts you without the alarm
- Until then, the alarm is non-negotiable

### The 5-5-5 Formula
Per day: **5 business + 5 personality + 5 lifestyle**
- All business = pitch, pitch, pitch → audience unfollows
- People are on the market every 7 years — for 6.5 years they need to be entertained, not sold to

### 30 Story Content Ideas

**Real Estate (10 ideas):**
1. Good morning from the office — what you're working on today
2. New listing sneak peek
3. Market update in one sentence (what buyers/sellers need to know this week)
4. Behind the scenes of a showing or listing prep
5. Client win or closing celebration
6. FAQ story ("buyers ask me this all the time...")
7. Neighborhood spotlight — coffee shops, parks, schools
8. Local business/development news ("new hospital bringing 4,000 jobs — guess who needs to live here?")
9. Before and after staging or clean-up
10. Poll ("Would you buy this house? Yes / Absolutely") + CTA (DM me if you're on the market)

**Agent Attraction (10 ideas):**
1. This is why I chose my brokerage (honest reason)
2. Screenshots of a training Zoom or community call
3. What most agents don't realize about this business
4. Lifestyle story — "I built my business so I can do this"
5. Poll ("Still feeling stuck in real estate? Yes / Yep")
6. Behind the scenes of team support or collaboration
7. If I were starting over, I'd do this differently
8. Testimonials or shoutouts from agents who joined
9. FAQ story (Do I have to recruit?)
10. CTA ("Curious? DM me 'info'")

**Digital Product (10 ideas):**
1. This made me money while I was offline
2. Screen recording showing what the product helps with
3. Problem-based hook ("If this sounds familiar, you need this")
4. Before and after results from using the product
5. Who this is for / who this is NOT for
6. FAQ (Do I need tech skills?)
7. Testimonial or DM screenshots (names hidden or visible)
8. Price anchor (one transaction vs. this product cost)
9. Lifestyle story ("built once, sells over and over")
10. CTA ("Comment 'link' and I'll send it to you")
