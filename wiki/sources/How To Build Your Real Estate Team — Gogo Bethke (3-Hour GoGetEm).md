# How To Build Your Real Estate Team — Gogo Bethke (3-Hour GoGetEm)

> 3-hour GoGet'Em master class on building a real estate team from scratch — hiring, structure, contracts, culture, and scaling.

## Key Takeaways

- Comprehensive team-building training — covers the full lifecycle from "solo agent" to "team leader."
- **Why build a team**: time leverage — your team does the transactions while you focus on $600/hour activities.
- **Hire slow, fire fast** — one of Gogo's core principles. The wrong hire costs you time, energy, and money. The right hire multiplies you.
- **Team structure options**: buyer's agent team, listing team, hybrid — each with different compensation and management implications.
- **The team leader's role shift**: from producer → recruiter → trainer → manager → CEO. Each phase requires different skills.
- **Retention**: agents stay when they grow. Build a learning culture — training, Tuesday calls, GoGet'Em access.
- **What goes in team agreements**: splits, lead handling, exclusivity, marketing standards, exit clauses.
- The "1980s song" intro — Gogo played music while waiting for attendees to join, setting a fun, high-energy tone.
