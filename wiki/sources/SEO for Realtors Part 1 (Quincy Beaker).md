# SEO for Realtors Part 1 — Fundamentals (Quincy Beaker)

> GoGet'Em Community training. Quincy Beaker: 25 years SEO experience; worked with Toyota and major companies. Gogo met him at Tony Robbins Top-10 Influencers Mastermind — he won first place with 6 Instagram followers by outranking Tony Robbins' own website for the challenge. Part 1 of a 2-part series.

## Key Takeaways

- **Three organic reach levers:** Social media posts + Email database + SEO/website. Most agents only use social.
- **Why have a website?** Know the purpose before optimizing. You waste social media effort sending traffic to a broken or wrong-worded website.
- SEO = helping Google understand who you are so your page shows up when people search for what you do.
- **The Google Spiders concept:** Google bots crawl your website. If the words on your site don't match what your target clients are searching, you won't be found.
- "Know enough to be dangerous." You don't need to be an SEO specialist — but you need to know enough WHY you have a website.
- **Quincy's proof:** He built a website for the Tony Robbins influencer challenge with just 6 Instagram followers — and his website outranked Tony Robbins' own site.
- **Gogo's takeaway:** Meeting Quincy changed how she thinks about websites. Now she hires his team to audit everything.
- Any future business website: get it done right from day one.
- Email and SEO are organic reach methods that don't require daily posting and don't die when algorithms change.
