# How Your Feelings Can Sabotage Your Life and Success (Gary Coxe)

> GoGet'Em Webinars guest session with Gary Coxe, speaker and behavioral change expert. Core distinction: believing you deserve success vs. actually valuing it enough to act on it consistently.

## Key Takeaways

- **Belief vs. Value distinction** — You can believe you deserve something (success, wealth, health) without actually *valuing* it enough to make it a priority. Behavior follows values, not beliefs.
- **Feelings as sabotage mechanism** — Negative feelings (fear, doubt, unworthiness) override rational belief. You say "I know I should do this" but feelings win in the moment.
- **The gap:** Most people focus on mindset and belief reprogramming, but the real lever is: what do you actually *value* at a subconscious level? Values drive consistent action.
- **Gary Coxe's framework** focuses on identifying the subconscious values and feelings that contradict stated goals, then reprogramming them.
