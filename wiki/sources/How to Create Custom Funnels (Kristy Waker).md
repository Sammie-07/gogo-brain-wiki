# How to Create Custom Funnels (Kristy Waker)

> Kristy walks through three ways to build niche-targeted funnels: BoldTrail's built-in page editor, GoHighLevel's AI builder, and Lovable (new standalone AI landing page tool). Includes GoGet'Em service tier pricing.

## Key Takeaways

- **Three funnel-building platforms covered:** BoldTrail (built-in, no extra cost), GoHighLevel (AI builder), Lovable (standalone AI, free tier)
- **Best tool = the one you actually use.** Doesn't matter which platform — execution beats platform choice every time.
- **BoldTrail page builder:** Web & IDX → Manager → Pages → choose template or build from scratch. Drag-and-drop rows and content blocks (icons, video, text, images). Must replace all placeholder filler text. Video blocks require a YouTube URL. Lives inside your KVCore domain — no additional platform needed.
- **GoHighLevel AI builder:** Sites → Funnels → New Funnel → Build with AI. Describe your ideal client in 1–2 sentences. Example output from "moms of elementary school kids who love dogs": full headline, empathetic copy, and layout — ready to plug in your lead form.
- **Lovable (lovable.dev):** Standalone AI landing page builder. Free: 5 credits/day; 1 credit = 1 full page. From 2 sentences it generated a fully designed page (warm cream/sage/terracotta palette, school districts + pet-friendly neighborhood hooks, testimonials section). NOT natively connected to your CRM — use as a standalone landing page.
- **Lovable + ChatGPT pro tip:** Use ChatGPT's "Lovable Assist" custom GPT first to generate an optimized prompt (include brand, colors, ideal client, contact info). Then paste into Lovable. Better results, only 1 credit used.
- **Canva builds websites too** — mentioned as another option.
- **GoGet'Em service tiers:**
  - **Done-With-You:** Tue + Wed live calls; Gogo's full team (designers, funnel builders, automation techs, ads manager) available to help you build in real time; support staff can attend too
  - **Funnels Built For You:** $300/funnel or 4 for $1,000; built in GoHighLevel (requires GHL account); start-to-finish build
  - **Website Built For You:** Full animated custom websites; higher tier; pricing on consultation
