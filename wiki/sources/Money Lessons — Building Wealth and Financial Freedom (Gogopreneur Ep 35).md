# Money Lessons — Building Wealth and Financial Freedom (Gogopreneur Ep 35)

> Team-narrated Gogopreneur episode recorded while Gogo was traveling in Europe. Covers her personal money journey — from $420k income + $89k debt to financial clarity — plus her portfolio, ADE principle, and income types.

## Key Takeaways

### The Debt Confession
- Was earning ~$420k/year while carrying $89k in credit card debt.
- Had three Netflix accounts she wasn't canceling. "When you make good money you just stop watching."
- Unworthiness rooted in Romanian family guilt — felt guilty having money when family back home didn't. Subconscious self-sabotage through spending.
- Breakthrough: conversation with a stranger at an airport who mirrored her pattern back to her.

### Mindset Shift
- **Bob Proctor:** Introduced concept of *vibrational currency* — you attract money at the vibration you hold about it.
- **$150k goal story:** Publicly committed to earning $150k in one year early in career. Achieved it. Proved that specificity + public accountability works.
- **ADE Principle:** **Automate, Delegate, Eliminate** — the filter for every task. If you can automate it, do. If you can delegate it, do. If it doesn't need doing, cut it.

### Three Income Types
1. **Active income:** Trading time for money (transactions, closings).
2. **Passive income:** Money working without direct time input (investments, rentals, stock).
3. **Recurring income:** Income that repeats automatically (rev share, subscription, royalties).
- Goal: shift the ratio from active toward passive + recurring.

### Portfolio Details (at time of recording)
- 1,880-door syndication investment.
- Short-term rental in South Carolina.
- eXp revenue share (recurring).
- Stock (EXPI + other).

### Advisory Team ("Board of Directors")
- CPA, financial advisor, real estate attorney, business attorney — minimum team for any agent making serious money.
