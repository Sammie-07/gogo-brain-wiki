# VA Pay Tutorial (Kristy Waker)

> [[Kristy Waker]] (Gogo's Director of Operations and first hire) walks through the mechanics of paying virtual assistants — platform selection, time tracking, and salary vs. hourly considerations.

## Key Takeaways

- **Veem.com as the payment platform:** Veem is free when connected directly to a bank account. Supports international transfers to VAs in any country. No per-transaction fees with bank account linked (credit card adds a fee). Recommended over PayPal and Wise for international VA payroll.
- **Master time sheet:** All VAs log hours into a central master time sheet. Kristy reviews it weekly. The time sheet is the single source of truth for VA hours and task accountability.
- **Salary vs. hourly:** For VAs doing consistent, recurring work (content scheduling, CRM updates, email management), salary is cleaner — no time-tracking friction. For VAs doing variable project work, hourly ensures the business only pays for actual output.
- **Payroll cadence:** Gogo's team pays VAs biweekly. Consistency in payroll timing builds trust and reduces VA turnover.

## Quotes

> "Veem is free when you connect your bank account. There is no reason to be paying fees to pay your team."
