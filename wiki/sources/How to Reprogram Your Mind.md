# How to Reprogram Your Mind (Rock Thomas)

> GoGet'Em Webinars session with Rock Thomas, coach and author. Focused on identity-level reprogramming — changing how you see yourself as the foundation for changing behavior.

## Key Takeaways

- **Rock Thomas** — mindset coach, known for "I am" identity work. Interview session hosted by Gogo on GoGet'Em Webinars; original content available on YouTube (Goldcast video).
- **Identity-level change:** Behavior change is short-lived unless rooted in identity. "I am a person who…" statements shape what actions feel natural vs. forced.
- **Reprogramming mechanism:** Repetition + emotion = new neural pathway. Affirmations without emotional charge don't work. Repeated belief + felt experience rewires the subconscious.
- **The "I am" statement:** What follows "I am" is your most powerful identity declaration. Gogo ties this to her own practice — *I am a millionaire*, *I am a leader*, not "I want to be."
- **Environmental design:** Who you spend time with reshapes your identity baseline. Proximity to high-performers accelerates identity reprogramming.
