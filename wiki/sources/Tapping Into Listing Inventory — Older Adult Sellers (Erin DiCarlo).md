# Tapping Into Listing Inventory — Older Adult Sellers (Erin DiCarlo)

> Erin DiCarlo and her business partner Lauren, founders of The Dovetail Company (Boston market), teach GoGet'Em agents how to tap into the massive and underserved market of older adult sellers.

## Key Takeaways

- **The market size:** Over 3 million seniors will move and leave their homes this year. Most agents are dabbling without fully building out their services to meet older adults' unique needs.
- **Why agents miss it:** "The aging population isn't sexy." But it's simultaneously the most rewarding work and a massive business opportunity. Both in the same package.
- **The Dovetail Company model — "Move first, sell later":**
  1. Assess the client's situation (where are they going? What's the plan?)
  2. Manage the move — help the client and their belongings physically relocate
  3. Prepare the home for sale (without the client and their belongings present)
  4. List and sell — homes sell faster and for more money vacant
- **Why "move first" works:** Older adults' belongings, furniture, and lifetime of décor suppress buyers' imaginations. A cleared, prepared home presents dramatically better. The process is also emotionally smoother for the client.
- **Erin's certifications:** Certified Geriatric Care Manager, Certified Dementia Practitioner, Certified Senior Adviser, Certified Senior and Specialty Move Manager. These credentials differentiate her from a regular agent.
- **Client case study — Bo and Barbara:** Married 61+ years. Barbara diagnosed with Alzheimer's nearly 10 years ago. Bo still working full-time. Active life had to shift instantly when Barbara's cognitive decline accelerated. Erin helped them navigate from their long-term home into a continuing care retirement community in the Boston market. She still sees them twice a year — "Bo invites us to his apartment for a Tito's on the Rocks and just talk about life."
- **eXp expansion:** Joined eXp Realty to expand their mission nationwide. Now trains agents in other markets on how to replicate the Dovetail model.
- **For GoGet'Em members:** This is an untapped niche in most markets. The infrastructure to serve older adults well is the competitive moat — not just the license.
