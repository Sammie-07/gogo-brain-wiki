# How to Be a Successful Entrepreneur

> GoGet'Em Community webinar. Gogo's core philosophy on building and managing a real estate team, with a focus on agent attraction strategy — specifically, attracting people who already align with your values. Full training gated at gogetemwebinars.com.

## Key Takeaways

- **"I'm looking for the people who are looking for me."** The foundation of agent attraction. Share who you are, your work ethic, your values — the right people will self-select.
- **What she stands for** (and attracts around): brand building, leveraging, building multiple companies, passive income, working part-time (Mon–Wed), financial/location/time freedom.
- **What she does NOT stand for** (and filters out): cold calling, door knocking, buying Zillow/Trulia leads, mega-team model with ISC department and 85+ agents.
- *"If you want to become the best cold-caller on the face of the Earth, don't join my downline."* She can't help with what she doesn't know or believe in.
- **"Everybody is getable."** No agent is truly off-limits. Two approaches: (1) find a pebble in their shoe — what's currently bothering them about their brokerage; (2) wait it out — eventually the broker will do something that makes them call you first.
- Agent attraction is patience + authentic positioning, not pressure.
- **Topics covered** (gated): when to start a team, team structure, finding the right team members, role-specific hiring (Admin, Buyers Specialist, Listing Specialist), team contract essentials, commission structure, listing/buyers specialist responsibilities, accountability, trackers, team member restrictions.
- Full session is members-only (GoGet'Em Community).
