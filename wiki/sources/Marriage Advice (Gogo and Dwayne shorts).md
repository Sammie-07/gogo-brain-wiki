# Marriage Advice (Gogo and Dwayne shorts)

> Personal short-form content from Gogo Bethke on navigating marriage when both partners are high-D personalities. Practical communication tool they use.

## Key Takeaways

- **Both high-D:** Gogo and Dwayne are both high-Dominance personalities on the DISC profile — both fixers, both problem-solvers, both action-oriented.
- **The friction:** When one wants to be heard and the other immediately jumps to solving, it creates conflict.
- **Solution — hand signals:** Ear signal = "I just need you to listen." Mouth signal = "I want your solution." Removes the ambiguity about what the speaker needs.
- **Alex Hormozi insight referenced:** Women generally talk to process and be heard; men generally want to provide solutions. Communication gap is biological/psychological, not personal.
- **Gogo's self-awareness:** "I'm a dude" — acknowledges she tends toward solution-mode herself, making her a fixer talking to a fixer.
- **"Be mad with me":** What she sometimes needs — validation and solidarity, not problem-solving.
