# Wine Not eXp — Monthly Presentation (May 2026)

> Gogo Bethke's recurring last-Wednesday recruiting webinar walking guest agents through the full [[eXp Realty]] opportunity — cap math, mentorship, ICON, stock, 7-generation revenue share, teams, and Team Gogo tools — to convince them to join eXp under a [[#teamgogo]] sponsor.

## Key Takeaways
- **Format:** Run the last Wednesday of every month (nicknamed "Wine Not eXp" because Gogo presents with a glass of wine); evolved from weekly Wednesday calls. Guests are prospective recruits invited by their Team Gogo sponsors; Gogo does no follow-up — the sponsor follows up.
- **Cap & commission:** 16,000 cap, 80/20 split nationwide (identical in every state). Costs: 149 to join (startup + first month), then 85/month; per-deal 25 broker fee + 60 risk-management (E&O) fee. E&O caps at ~750/year (~12–13 deals). A 250 post-cap transaction fee only begins AFTER capping, replacing the 20% split.
- **Capping math:** ~9 transactions / ~2.7M closed volume to cap. Worked example — 300k house at 3% (9,000 commission): nets ~6,759.25 before cap, ~8,231.75 after cap (or ~8,826.75 with a 595 transaction fee where a state permits charging it).
- **Mentorship program:** Brand-new agents (under 3 transactions in prior 12 months) auto-roll to a 60/40 split for their first 3 deals with a state-certified mentor (mentor must have 6+ deals in prior 12 months). Mentor earns 10% of the mentee's first 3 commissions. State broker can waive for documented life-circumstance gaps. Graduate to 80/20 after 3 deals.
- **ICON program:** Cap PLUS 20 transactions (rentals count) in your anniversary year = ICON, returning the full 16,000 cap in company stock. Anniversary date = 1st of the month after joining. Example agent Yasmin is a 7x ICON with ~100k in stock (77,836.96 vested/available, 22,142.29 unvested, ~23k in a legacy account).
- **5 ways to earn stock (4 free, 1 paid):** (1) opt-in 5% of commission buys stock at a 5% discount — "a coupon for stocks," immediately yours; (2) 200 on your first transaction each year; (3) 400 when an agent you sponsored closes their first; (4) 400 when you cap; (5) 16,000 when you ICON. The 4 gifted types vest over 3 years; the paid 5% type is yours immediately.
- **Stock mechanics:** You receive 16,000 *worth* of shares at the current price, not 16,000 cash. Ticker changed from EXPI to **AGNT** (NASDAQ). Price ~4.74 at call time (joined at ~3; historical high ~90). Example: 16,000 / 4.74 = 3,375 shares; at 10/share = 33,750. Gogo's own account holds 46,100.36 (she has never ICONed; earns mostly via sponsoring 20–40 agents/year at 400 each).
- **Revenue share:** eXp runs on 50% of revenue; paid 7 generations deep with per-generation annual maximums — Gen1 1,400, Gen2 1,600, Gen3 1,000, Gen4 600, Gen5 400, Gen6 1,000, Gen7 2,000. You never pay your sponsor; rev share comes from the company dollar (10% of the 20% split). Gogo's lifetime revenue share: 4.3M over 8 years.
- **Sponsorship structure:** One sponsor grants you a 7-generation upline ("buy one, get six free"); co-sponsorship lets you name two sponsors. To preserve upline/training access through Gogo, she sponsors once, then co-sponsors thereafter.
- **Team structures (4):** husband/wife (blend into one cap), self-organized (no minimums), standard team (qualify with 6M volume OR 30 deals in prior 12 months; members cap at 8,000), Mega ICON team (40M AND 175 deals; members cap at 4,000). 8k/4k-cap members cannot ICON — only the 16k team lead can.
- **CRM of Choice & tech:** Pick one of Lofty, kvCORE, or Close (changeable yearly; Gogo prefers kvCORE). Stack includes eXp World (3D avatar platform), Sisu, free Canva Pro (in the 85/month), SkySlope, MyeXP single sign-on, Instacard, Regus office membership, Build-A-Sign (up to 60% off yard signs — coupons shared by [[Kristy Waker]]), Mira (eXp AI assistant), and the new in-house "hub" replacing Slack.
- **FastCap:** Recommended as the first program to join; opens ~every 6 weeks, only ~2,000 of 89,000 agents enrolled at a time, includes free Realty.com leads for a year (kept after graduating).
- **Other programs:** Team Leader Academy; eXp Luxury (paid certification); eXp Commercial (separate cap/MLS, required if most deals are commercial — residential side OK for commercial under 2M); **REMLO** (become a certified loan originator, earn ~0.5% of the mortgage as a "full-service agent"; example payout 1,104.64); NAEA certified listing/buyer's agent free via the Honey Badgers downline; eXp Referral status at 50/month (keep stock, rev share, insurance, trainings) vs 85/month full agent.
- **Team Gogo scale:** **1,660 agents** across 7+ countries (the call's dashboard showed ~1,645 agents, 99 FLAs, 47 FLQAs as of this call); 1.3B volume year-to-date (May), projecting 3.5–4B for 2026 (~120M in commissions at 3%). Production history: 2019 70M, 2020 200M, 2021 1B, 2022 1.8B, 2023 2.1B, 2024 3B, 2025 3.5B.
- **eXp corporate facts:** founded 2009, NASDAQ since 2018, now in 27 countries; revenues referral program paid out 1B+ in 2023 and ~1B in 2024; 24/7 support line.
