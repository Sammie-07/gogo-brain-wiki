# Setting Up Your Google Business Profile for Success (Kristy Waker)

> Step-by-step walkthrough of creating and managing a Google Business Profile, with emphasis on using the branded domain email for faster verification. Part of *Gogo's Social Media Challenge 2026*.

## Key Takeaways

- Google Business Profile is **free** — no excuses not to have it.
- **Use your branded domain email** (e.g., christy@christysrealestateteam.com) to create the profile — Google recognizes the domain match and can verify you instantly vs. the standard 5-day wait.
- Business type = **Service** (not local store, not online retailer) for realtors.
- Service areas: list 2–4 cities/towns you actually serve. Too many signals you don't specialize anywhere.
- Phone number = your **BoldTrail/KVCore smart number** (from Brand Tracker). Never the eXp shared round-robin number.
- Verification address = your **home address** — it's hidden from the public. Never use the state broker's address (you won't receive the postcard there).
- Instant verification is possible when your Google account email matches your business domain.
- **Google reviews** are among the most important trust signals — grab your review link from the live profile and send it to past clients.
- **Posting:** Cross-post social content (new listings, just solds, open houses, home value posts) directly to your Google Business profile. VAs can handle this as part of the weekly scheduling workflow.
- Access your profile anytime via Google's **9-dot menu → Business Profile Manager** (formerly Google My Business).
- **"Done With You" program:** Live support every Tuesday and Wednesday at 3pm ET for agents who get stuck on setup.
