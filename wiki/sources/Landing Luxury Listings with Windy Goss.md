# Landing Luxury Listings with Windy Goss

> GoGet'Em guest training — Windy Goss on how she went from $300K–$500K homes for nearly 20 years to landing 12 luxury listings in 12 months, including a $12M property.

## Key Takeaways

- **The pivot story**: Windy didn't move into luxury for more money — she moved for **freedom**: sell less, earn more, create more time.
- **The Magical Black Book Strategy** — her signature system:
  1. Pull expired luxury listings straight from the MLS
  2. Create high-end "Black Books" — beautiful marketing materials in a physical format
  3. Add a personal letter and personal branding
  4. FedEx them directly to the seller's door
- **Positioning before the listing**: luxury sellers need to see you as credible before they meet you. Your videos, your marketing, your brand — all must look and feel luxury before you have the listings.
- **Content formula for luxury**: demonstrate expertise, share market knowledge, showcase your aesthetic and professionalism.
- **Advertising**: Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube ads to showcase listings and attract high-end clients.
- Tools and systems for polished, consistent luxury marketing.
- Core mindset: "You don't have to be someone else to succeed in luxury. You just need the right mindset, a solid system, and the confidence to go for it."
- Results: 12 luxury listings in 12 months after implementing the system, including a $12M property.
