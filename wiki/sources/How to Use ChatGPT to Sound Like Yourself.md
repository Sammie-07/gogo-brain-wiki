# How to Use ChatGPT to Sound Like Yourself

> GoGet'Em Webinars session. Gogo covers how to customize ChatGPT to match your brand voice and combine it with ManyChat for lead generation. Full content gated.

## Key Takeaways

- **Customize ChatGPT for your voice** — train it with examples of your writing so outputs sound like you, not generic AI. Authenticity is non-negotiable; AI amplifies your voice, it doesn't replace it.
- **ManyChat quiz automations** — beyond keyword triggers, ManyChat can run automated quiz sequences that expand email lists and qualify leads.
- **Content creation with AI** — use ChatGPT to speed up the writing process while maintaining brand integrity.
- **Maintaining brand integrity with AI:** AI tools should amplify your unique identity, not dilute it. If it doesn't sound like you, fix the prompt or edit the output before publishing.
