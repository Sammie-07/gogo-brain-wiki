# Can We Have It All?

> Short Gogopreneur podcast clip. Gogo challenges the binary-choice mindset instilled in childhood and replaces it with an abundance framework.

## Key Takeaways

- **We are trained from childhood to choose.** "Do you want chips or ice cream? Pick one." This embeds a scarcity frame — you can't have both.
- **That's a lie worth unlearning.** You can have a career and freedom. A home and an empire. Children and financial independence.
- **The reframe:** When you catch yourself saying "I have to choose between X and Y," replace it with: "Why do I have to choose? Why can't I have both?"
- **The problem isn't capability — it's the trained belief** that wanting everything is greedy or unrealistic.

## Quotes

> "You can have it all. We can all have it all. The problem is we are all trained that we have to choose between this or that."

> "From now on, ask yourself: why do I have to choose? Why can't I have it all?"
