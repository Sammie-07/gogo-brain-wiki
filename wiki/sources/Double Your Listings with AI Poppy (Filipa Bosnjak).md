# Double Your Listings with AI Poppy (Filipa Bosnjak)

> GoGet'Em Community training on Poppy AI — a visual AI whiteboard that combines ChatGPT, Claude, and Grok in one canvas for brand-specific content creation.

## Key Takeaways

- **Poppy AI** (poppy.ai) is a visual whiteboard combining ChatGPT/Claude/Grok — eliminates endless scrolling through chat history
- Drag-and-drop links, PDFs, YouTube videos, Instagram posts, screenshots onto a "board" — all connected to the chat
- One board can hold all context permanently (unlike ChatGPT which loses context); break boards down by client, platform, or project
- **Brand voice setup**: plug in 2-3 of your posts → ask Poppy to summarize your language, energy, tone → from then on all content stays authentic to that voice
- **Use cases**: research competitors by linking their Instagram/LinkedIn, create course outlines from voice notes, build YouTube thumbnails modeled on successful creators, automated newsletters via API integration
- Poppy uses ChatGPT's image capabilities to recreate thumbnails in your style
- **API/Zapier integration**: can auto-send newsletters, auto-reply to DMs in your voice
- Bootstrapped Silicon Valley startup — founders personally help users set up automations
- Plans: annual and lifetime options; 25% referral discount; can share boards with team
- Gogo's team can build a board for you during onboarding

## Quotes

> "You're not changing really anything that you're doing, all you're doing is popping all of this information back into a program like this and all you're doing is clicking that summarize button."

> "This makes you special because it's taking everything that you are and it's just amplifying it versus you're competing with a ChatGPT that's taking information from the wide ether of generic stuff."
