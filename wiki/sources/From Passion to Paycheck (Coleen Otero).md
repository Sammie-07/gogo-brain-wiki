# From Passion to Paycheck (Coleen Otero)

> Gogopreneur episode with Coleen Otero — founder of CEO Chick, author of *Brand to Bucks*, and brand-to-business strategist. Framework for turning a personal passion or skill into a monetized business.

## Key Takeaways

- **CEO Chick:** Coleen's platform/brand. Notably a **nonprofit** — structured to reinvest in women entrepreneurs.
- **Book:** *Brand to Bucks* — her framework for building a personal brand that generates revenue.
- **Origin:** Came from the beauty industry. Realized the principles of personal branding apply universally.
- **"Problems produce products and services":** The clearest path to a profitable offer is solving a problem you've personally lived and solved. Your struggle becomes your product.
- **Decision framework:** Who / What / When / Where / Why / How — run every major business decision through these 6 lenses before committing.
- **Hire your opposite:** Don't hire a second version of yourself. Hire for the skills and personality traits you lack.
- **Hello Jen:** VA company in the Philippines that Coleen recommends. Positions VAs as a lever for solopreneurs.
- **"courses for CEOs.com":** Her educational platform for CEO-level entrepreneurs.
- **M Factor (4 elements of magnetic business):**
  1. **Magnetism** — personal brand pull
  2. **Manpower** — the team behind you
  3. **Money** — the financial systems
  4. **Mental toughness** — the mindset to sustain it
- **Practical note:** Gogo relates — came from a different world (real estate), but brand-to-business principles apply directly.

## Quotes

> "Problems produce products and services. Your biggest struggle is your most valuable asset."

> "Hire your opposite. You don't need a mini-me — you need someone who fills your gaps."
