# The Rabbit Hole — Natalie Davison's Trust Ladder for Warm Lead Generation

> Guest training for the [[GoGet'Em Community]] in which eXp ICON agent [[Natalie Davison]] teaches a five-stage "Rabbit Hole" (Trust Ladder) framework for replacing cold-lead grinding with warm, referral-driven business built on visibility, familiarity, and trust.

## Key Takeaways
- First session in [[#teamgogo]]'s new ICON guest-teacher series; [[Natalie Davison]] is an eXp ICON agent in New Brunswick, Canada (market avg price point $389,000), known for "wild optimism and truth-telling."
- Core thesis: cold leads, door-knocking, cold-calling, and "broken-record" follow-up all "suck" — the goal is **fewer, more intentional conversations** with people who already care who you are. One real conversation outweighs 25 fake, transactional ones.
- The **Rabbit Hole** framework (originally the Trust Ladder) has five stages: (1) **Visibility** — they see you; (2) **Familiarity** — they recognize you and feel they know you; (3) **Trust** — they believe in you before they ever need to hire you; (4) **Opportunity** — referrals, repeat clients, podcasts, stages, and partnerships flow in; (5) **Advocacy** — people talk about you in rooms you are not in. Advocacy is the goal because it eliminates the need to cold-call.
- Build an intentional "rabbit hole" of content so that when someone meets you (e.g. at pickleball) and looks you up, there is a journey to consume — converting a cold lead to warm before the next encounter. Minimum starting point: be findable on Facebook or Instagram with your face on it.
- Visibility tactics: community involvement (board of the Boys & Girls Club, coaching youth sports), showing up in person at events, open houses, buyer seminars. At open houses Natalie captures sign-in info simply by smiling and showing genuine enthusiasm.
- Stat: it costs **6 to 20 times more** to acquire a new lead than to keep an existing one.
- Niche-community ROI (Natalie's own numbers): being a "football mom" reliably yields **2–3 transactions/year**; pickleball (joined ~1.5 years ago) produced **4 transactions this year** and growing, with 12–15 people/day engaging her on daily movement; a middle-school acquaintance (Leslie Roper) who silently followed her on Facebook for ~a decade brought **3 transactions in 2022**.
- **Audition principle**: clients hire you to be a professional marketer, so how you market yourself in your community *is* the audition. Brand your hobby activities (e.g. Jeep "ducks") because that time is a business-development expense you "can't afford not to" brand.
- **Borrowed trust / amplification**: guesting on others' podcasts or stages lets you borrow their audience; [[Gogo Bethke]] endorsing Natalie on Instagram Stories is real-time advocacy. Natalie's own path traces the framework exactly — heard Gogo on a real estate podcast in 2021 (visibility) → followed on Instagram (familiarity) → ~3 years of familiarity/trust → moved her business to eXp in 2024 (opportunity) → now being advocated for.
- **Be yourself as a daily practice, not a state**: let your light shine; the things that are magic about you are also what you will be most criticized for, so put them on a pedestal and protect them. Don't be a "secret agent" — marketing yourself is your job.
- Production history: licensed in 2005 (part-time alongside her high-producing mother-in-law), ran a marketing business for 4 years, returned to real estate in 2021 and did ~3 deals in 3 days, hitting ICON production immediately because the rabbit hole was already built.
- [[Gogo Bethke]] reinforced trust with the rule "if you don't have anything nice to say, don't say anything" — she only endorses what is true, so her word carries weight; she closed by reading the [[Marianne Williamson]] poem "Our Deepest Fear" on shining your light.
- Housekeeping: the **eXp Hub** is now the single central app for all links (App Store section holds MIRA, eXp University, Bold Trail, MyEXP, Canva, SkySlope, state sites), rolled out Thursday with a mobile version; new monthly **Roundtable** (mastermind-style, no speaker) launches June 17 on the third Wednesday; onboarding via TeamGogo.team is $25; all trainings auto-recorded to a private YouTube channel.
- Mortgage Minutes ([[Jason Dupree]]): avg rate ~6.6% (vs >7% a year ago), purchase apps +7% and refi apps +15% week-over-week; new products include using equity in other owned properties as qualifying income and a nightly-calculated "credit-card-style" mortgage; **REMLO** of the month Melissa Albert added a $5,800 commission check via the [[REMLO]] program.
