# Money Frequency

> Gogo's personal episode on her relationship with money — from poverty mindset growing up in Romania to building wealth in America. Emphasis on shifting your frequency around money and surrounding yourself with the right financial advisory team.

## Key Takeaways

- **Money frequency concept:** Your relationship with money is a frequency you can tune. People who grew up without money often carry a scarcity frequency that repels wealth even when income rises.
- **Gogo's backstory:** Immigrated from Romania with little — money was scarce and not discussed openly. Reprogramming her beliefs about money was necessary to scale her income.
- **Advisory team is non-negotiable:** Gogo attributes much of her wealth-building success to surrounding herself with the right financial advisors (Anderson Advisors referenced in other episodes).
- **Track your numbers:** You can't raise your money frequency if you're afraid to look at your finances. Gogo tracks everything — P&L, pay log, affiliate payouts, revenue share.
- **Mindset + mechanics:** Both are required. Mindset shift without financial literacy = money leaks. Financial literacy without mindset shift = self-sabotage.
