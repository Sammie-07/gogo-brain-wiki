# Managing a High Performing Team — Gogo Community Call

> Gogo's GoGet'Em community live call on building and running a team that can function without the owner. Gogo announces a quarter-year vacation (June–August), explains her income structure, walks through her full VA team, and covers the 90/10 profit principle.

## Key Takeaways

### Team Structure

- **Kristy (Director of Operations, 14 years):** Handles finances, bookkeeping, contracts, technology, NDA reviews, tax support calls, all hiring processes. Described as the "TinGogo Mama." Gogo's only American employee. High C (DISC) personality — sees the details Gogo misses.
- **Alejandra (Executive Assistant, Argentina):** Manages Gogo's calendar, emails, Trello board, attends every Zoom call, schedules all Team GoGo trainings and speaking engagements, handles hotel/travel logistics.
- **Delegation of hiring:** Gogo stepped out of the hiring process entirely — Kristy and Alejandra hire for their own departments. Logic: if they're the ones training the new hire, they should be the ones selecting the new hire. Bad hire = their problem.
- **Cross-training mandate:** Gogo assigns multiple team members to learn every key function. When Facebook ads coaching is added, four VAs all learn it together. If one leaves, three remain.

### Income Structure

- **W2 + 1099 + S-corp dividends:** Gogo draws W2 from companies she owns, receives 1099s from various income streams, and takes S-corp dividends. Multiple income types create financial resilience and loan qualification strength.
- **90/10 principle:** 90% of income kept, only 10% spent on operations. In a $1.5M revenue year, total team/system cost was ~$143K (≈9.5%). Goal for GoGet'Em members: run lean before scaling wide.

### Systems & Tools

- **Trello:** Project management board — Gogo has never logged in. Alejandra manages it. Every idea Gogo has goes onto the Trello board. Tasks have deadlines and owners.
- **Fixer (AI email tool):** Attends Zoom calls, takes notes, drafts email responses, links to calendar for scheduling. Free for eXp Icon agents. Gogo doesn't check email — Fixer handles drafts, she approves.

### Quarter-Year Vacation

- **Plan:** June + July + August off (≈quarter of a year) — first time in 14 years
- **Enabler:** 14-year Kristy tenure, VA team with 3–6 year tenures, documented systems
- **Philosophy:** "You have to build a team that can run without you. That's the whole point."

### Camera-On Philosophy

- Gogo insists on cameras on for GoGet'Em live calls — "if your camera is off, there's a reason why it's off. It's usually: let me do a load of laundry."

## Quotes

> "You don't have an assistant until your assistant has an assistant. That's how you know you made it big."

> "I paid Kristy 143 out of 1.5 million. That's 9%. My goal for you — 90% profit, 10% expenses."

> "I delegated hiring to the people who are going to have to train them. Because if they pick wrong, that's their three months wasted, not mine."
