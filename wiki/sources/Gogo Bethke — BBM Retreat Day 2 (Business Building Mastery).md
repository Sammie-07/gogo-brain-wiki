# Gogo Bethke — BBM Retreat Day 2 (Business Building Mastery)

> 71-page slide deck from the Business Building Mastery (BBM) Retreat, Day 2. Covers the full leverage playbook: hourly rate math, VA team economics, passive income architecture, schedule design, tracker suite, automation tools, and investment types. Orange/green/white tropical design.

## Key Takeaways

- **Three audience types** addressed by the content: (1) want more money, (2) have money but want time back, (3) want to work less AND make more — Gogo claims she solved all three
- **Hourly rate formula**: $250K ÷ 2,080 hours (40 hrs × 52 weeks) = $120/hr. Sellers average 10 hours of agent time; buyers average 16. Any task below your hourly rate should be delegated.
- **VA math (full detail)**: 14 VAs across 9 job capacities (Executive Assistant, Social Media, Design/Media, Tech/Numbers, Onboarding, FB Groups Manager, TV Show, Website Creator, Real Estate Team). Cost: ~$4/hr × 14 = $56/hr for 14 concurrent hours of work. Gogo's framing: "If I said 'give me $56 and I give you 14 hours of work done' — would you do it?"
- **Total weekly leverage math**: 14 VAs × 40 hrs = 560 VA hours + Gogo's 24 personal hours = **584 hours/week** = **30,368 hours/year** = **10.8× the average person** (who works 2,080 hrs/year)
- **Revenue result**: Gogo makes **$2.7M working 24 hours/week** (Mon–Wed only). Cannot remember last time she answered an email, DM, or put something in her calendar.
- **Before VAs**: made $800K with ONE assistant, working 40 hrs/week with head spinning
- **8×3=24H work week**: Mon–Tue–Wed, 8 hours each = 24-hour work week. Thu–Sun completely off.
- **In-person assistant types listed**: House Assistant, House Cleaner, Personal Chef (in addition to virtual VAs)
- **Passive income categories**: Stocks, Investments, Ownership. Affiliate income at 10–20% avg commission.
- **Profit share vs. revenue share**: Revenue share comes off the top — independent of brokerage profitability. Advantage: you benefit regardless of company P&L.
- **Money mindset section**: Common limiting beliefs ("money doesn't grow on trees," "hard work = money," "for the love of money it's the root of all evil") reframed with "Money is NEUTRAL — just makes you more of who you already are."
- **Red Light / Green Light exercise** used as workshop — stop doing red lights, keep green lights
- **DISC Assessment**: Gogo ID/Is style (Work pattern 6621 Adapted, 3543 Natural). Used for hiring, team building, communication
- **Tracker suite** (5 tools): Lead Tracker, Greatness Tracker, Profit & Loss, Pay Log, Money Tracker
- **Automation stack**: ManyChat (15 keywords: PARTNER, EVENT, EVENTS, AA, REDX, WEBINAR, WEBINARS, RETREAT, INSTACARD, BUYER, SELLER, POSH, NEWSLETTER), ChatGPT, vidIQ, Zapier, HighLevel, ClickFunnels, SendGrid (Aug 2023: 618,534 emails/month, 43.96% unique opens)
- **Investment types endorsed**: Short Term Rental (Airbnb), Long Term Rental, Syndications, Stocks, Ownership in Companies
- **Holistic success principle**: "I don't want anything to give while you're making money." Non-negotiables: Health (physical and mental), Happy Relationship, Marriage, Children, Friends and Family
- **Professional team for money**: CPA, Financial Advisor, Tax Attorney — Anderson Advisors recommended
- **vidIQ use**: YouTube video description and title optimization; Gogo's YouTube has 12.7K subscribers, 325 videos
