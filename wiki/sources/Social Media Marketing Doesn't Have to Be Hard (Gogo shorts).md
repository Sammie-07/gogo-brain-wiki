# Social Media Marketing Doesn't Have to Be Hard (Gogo shorts)

> Short-form personal content from Gogo Bethke on how she started using social media for real estate — framing social media as accessible, not intimidating — and directing viewers to the GoGet'Em Community.

## Key Takeaways

- **Origin story:** Licensed in 2011. Walked into her first real estate office and realized no one was teaching agents how to actually get clients.
- **The 80/20 rule in real estate:** 80% of Realtors quit in their first year. 20% of agents do 80% of the business. Gogo decided from day one she would be in the 20%.
- **Social media as the solution:** Traditional methods (door-knocking, cold calling, Zillow at $4,000/month) were either inaccessible or unappealing. Social media was free, scalable, and natural to her.
- **No sphere of influence:** Came from Romania at 21, knew nobody. Social media was how she found strangers — and let strangers find her.
- **gogetemcommunity.com/socialmedia/** — the resource linked from this content.
- **Key message:** Social media marketing is accessible, learnable, and already in your pocket.
