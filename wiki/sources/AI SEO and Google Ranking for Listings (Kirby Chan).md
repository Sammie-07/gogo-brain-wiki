# AI SEO & Google Ranking for Listings (Kirby Chan)

> A #teamgogo / [[GoGet'Em Community]] guest-expert training by [[Kirby Chan]] (Toronto-area agent) on ranking on Google for listing niches and getting **cited by AI** (ChatGPT / Gemini / Claude) — the funnel behind his "AI SEO Boot Camp." Feeds [[eXp Realty]] revenue-share attraction.

## Key Takeaways

- **"Book one listing appointment a day," work ~3 hrs/day, focus on listings over buyers.** He credits a listing-first focus for a faster path to eXp **ICON** (6 months this year vs. 9 last).
- **Don't fight a saturated metro — dominate a smaller city.** Toronto has ~100k agents; he owns **Richmond Hill** (~10k agents, few online) and ranks on Google in ~8–9 months. Pick the winnable geography.
- **Google's "Helpful Content" update rewards genuinely useful content over authority/backlinks** — a new, thin site can outrank Zillow/Redfin if the article actually helps and holds the reader (dwell time / low bounce).
- **"Google real estate" = own the SERP for one keyword cluster** (e.g., "Richmond Hill downsizing") across website, blogs, videos, and AI-Overview citations.
- **Four assets to build (one per day):** (1) one **service page per niche** (evergreen), (2) supporting **blogs** on subtopics, (3) **Google Business Profile**, (4) a **niche Instagram**.
- **Niche-page method:** build ONE service page (downsizing, upsizing, probate, divorce, first-time buyers, expired listings), master its ranking, then move to the next niche.
- **Reviews — drip ~1/week, never bulk.** Google flags 10-at-once / same-location / event spikes. Ask *everyone* (vendors, the appliance salesman), not just clients; photo reviews rank/engage stronger; never buy Fiverr reviews or use fake emails.
- **AI-written content is fine for Google** — it doesn't penalize AI authorship; ranking is driven by helpfulness and reader behavior.
- **Website:** a templated **Lofty** site (~$50–60/mo + custom domain) can rank; **don't** buy Lofty's ~$500 SEO upsell — do real optimization instead.
- **"Parasite SEO":** post daily self-promoting posts ("top/best agent in [city]") on LinkedIn, Rate My Agent, etc. Humans won't read them — but **ChatGPT / Gemini / Claude do, and cite you** when asked "best agent in X."
- **The AI-first funnel (GEO — get cited by AI):** buyers increasingly ask ChatGPT/Gemini/Claude for an agent and call directly without visiting the site ("ChatGPT told me to call you"). Being the AI-cited name is the goal.
- **AI video pipeline (~5 min/video):** [[Carrie Soave|Claude]] writes the script → **HeyGen** AI twin (or Captions.ai) reads it → an AI editor adds B-roll. Run separate **niche Instagram accounts** with the AI twin so your main personal brand stays 100% real.
- **Compliance:** in the US you generally can't claim "#1 agent" (licensed by state, not nationally) — use **"best," "top-producing,"** or third-party community awards (Toronto Star Platinum, Three Best Rated, Community Votes) as legitimate "best" language.
- **Foreign-language SEO:** write content (and a separate site/GBP/Instagram) in a second language (Portuguese, Chinese) for near-zero competition and much faster ranking; AI writes and voices it.
- **Attraction engine:** the system doubles as a recruiting funnel (the "$15 AI SEO Boot Camp"): cold-outreach the #4/#5-ranked agent in a *sister* city (not your own), invite them to the boot camp, then co-sponsor into [[Real Estate Teams|eXp revenue share]]. Growth cited: 10 (2024) → 21 → 146 agents.
