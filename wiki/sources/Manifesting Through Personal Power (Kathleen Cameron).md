# Manifesting Through the Use of Your Personal Power

> A returning-favorite masterclass by manifestation coach [[Kathleen Cameron]] — co-hosted in Gogo's community with Natalia Shine — teaching business owners to trade masculine hustle for a "personal power" (God-essence) approach where identity and vibration, not strategy, create results.

## Key Takeaways
- **Personal power = the "God essence" within.** Everyone carries a creator energy (call it soul, Christ consciousness, infinite intelligence, the universe); tapping it means remembering you are a spiritual being having a human experience, not alone and not merely surviving like other species.
- **The 95% invisible rule.** ~95% of any manifestation happens behind the scenes and stays unseen until it hits 95% — like an Instagram upload bar that shows nothing until it completes; you must surrender and trust rather than constantly checking for proof.
- **Manifestation = vibrational match, not creation.** The money/love/house already exists; you don't "make" it, you move it into your experience by becoming a vibrational match and removing vibrational resistance (e.g., you can't shame others' wealth and expect wealth).
- **Identity over strategy.** None of this is about tactics or right/wrong action; results flow from self-concept — what you believe to be true about who you are. Challenge every limiting label with "According to who?"
- **Masculine grind vs. feminine receptive.** Her old self was hustle/grind, heavy masculine, more hours = same money; pouring into feminine energetics (visualize, meditate, dream, surrender, receive) is what unlocked million-dollar months while working less — "the effortless way" (less effort, not zero).
- **Imagination is the #1 business tool.** Everything begins in the mind (universal law); act from the assumption it's already done. Visualize the reality you'd love and it comes to fruition (restaurant waits, reservations, outcomes).
- **Authenticity is magnetic.** Being out of integrity (confident outside, doubting inside) leaks; prospects feel inauthenticity and go elsewhere. When you're certain of yourself, the world is certain of you.
- **Law of compensation = three beliefs.** Bob Proctor's version (importance of the work, how good you are, how replaceable) is really identity: you're paid greatly when *you believe* your work is the most important, that you're the best at it, and that you're irreplaceable.
- **Self-love is imperative in business.** Love is the highest frequency (the God energy). Real self-love is unconditional — how you treat yourself with no money, weight gained, or a breakup — not external validation.
- **No disappointment, guilt, or shame.** These are the lowest-frequency, useless emotions that manifest more of the same; she has removed disappointment from her consciousness. There is no problem, only the solution — don't dwell more than a moment in the problem.
- **Empowerment beats "high vibe."** Happy ≠ rich; if it did, all happy people would be wealthy. What matters is radical self-belief and empowerment (bold decisions, risk-taking), not mood.
- **Pair spirituality with identity/personal-growth work.** Spiritual practices alone won't quantum-leap you if your identity stays the same; you must heal wounding and limitations. Tools/rituals only work if you believe in them; ask "What am I pretending not to know?"
- **Detachment / no-strings state.** "I want you but I don't need you" — love your current life fully while desiring more; attachment and neediness repel. If a manifestation misses your timeline, hold it, you may be at 94%.
- **Christian case study ($2K→$50K/mo).** A hustler working 16-hr days, 6–7 days/week for $2,000/mo joined her program, cut to 4–6 hrs/day, and made $12,000 in 3 weeks; by week four heading toward a ~$50,000 month, forced to redefine his masculinity around identity, not grind.
