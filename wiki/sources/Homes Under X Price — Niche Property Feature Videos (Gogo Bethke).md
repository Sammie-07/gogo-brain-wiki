# Homes Under X Price — Niche Property Feature Videos (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo demonstrates three video formats for weekly niche property feature content — in-person showing, iPhone screen recording with voiceover, and Instagram green screen — each ending with a ManyChat keyword CTA.

## Key Takeaways

- **Weekly niche video commitment:** Pick ONE niche (homes under $X, waterfront, horse farms, etc.) and create a video about it once a week. Consistency = expertise. One video means nothing.
- **Price = buyer type (reinforced):** Post homes under $175K → attract $175K buyers. Post $3M homes → attract $3M buyers. You choose your buyer by what you feature.
- **Three shooting formats:**

| Format | How | Best For |
|--------|-----|----------|
| **In-person** | Schedule a showing, ask listing agent permission to video | Most engaging; requires travel |
| **Screen recording + voiceover** | iPhone screen record (hold button → enable mic) + scroll while talking | Fast, no travel, property right on screen |
| **Green screen** | Screen record WITHOUT voice → Instagram Stories → green screen filter → shoot yourself in front of the video | Personal face on camera + property visible; very professional |

- **iPhone screen record with voiceover:** Swipe down → hold screen record button → turn on microphone → tap start → 3-2-1 → go. Scroll through property while narrating. Tap stop, save to camera roll, done.
- **Instagram green screen:** Swipe right in Instagram → Stories → find green screen filter → swipe up to import your screen recording → shoot yourself in front of it.
- **Listing agent outreach script:** "Even though I don't have a buyer right now, I'd love to tour and feature this property. I have 10,000 followers in the local market and my goal is to find a buyer for you. Would you be interested?" Answer will be yes.
- **Always end with a keyword CTA:** Tell them exactly what to do. "Comment KEY → my system sends you the link." ManyChat delivers automatically.
- **Description = post copy too:** Write the same CTA in the post description. Some people watch, some people read — serve both.

## Quotes

> "If you post a video about homes under $175,000, you're going to get buyers looking under $175,000. If you post $3 million homes, you get $3 million buyers. You get to choose."

> "You don't need videographers. Don't go into spending money. Green screen, screen recording — either way works great."
