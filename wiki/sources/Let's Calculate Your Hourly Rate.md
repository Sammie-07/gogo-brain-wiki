# Let's Calculate Your Hourly Rate

> Rise Retreat Day 1. Gogo walks attendees through calculating their personal hourly rate, using DISC assessments when hiring VAs, and identifying passive income streams.

## Key Takeaways

- **Hourly rate formula:** Annual income ÷ 2,080 = hourly rate. This number anchors all delegation decisions.
- **DISC assessments for hiring:** When hiring VAs or team members, use DISC to match personality profile to role. High D = director/ISA; High C = admin/detail; High S = support/relationship.
- **Passive income types Gogo recognizes:**
  1. Commissions (from closed transactions)
  2. Referral fees (from agent-to-agent referrals)
  3. Affiliate income (from products/services she recommends)
  4. Stock (eXp stock awards)
  5. Revenue share (eXp rev share from sponsored agents)
