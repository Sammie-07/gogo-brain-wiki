# PLAN Your Path from Production to Legacy (Lou Raven and Elise Palacios)

> Lou Raven and Elise Palacios of Reinvest Real Estate (Connecticut) present their PLAN framework and PITY system for taking real estate agents from active production to a legacy business.

## Key Takeaways

- **Who they are:**
  - **Lou:** Corporate background (400 employees, 30 retail locations) → real estate investor (15 years) → agent (7 years). Added $1M net worth in 3 years, built 3 six-figure businesses in 3 years. Father to a 2-year-old; now prioritizes family time over hustle.
  - **Elise:** Entrepreneur since age 11; managing employees by 15-16; financially independent at 16; six-figure income at 19; first investment property at 20. Featured twice on HGTV House Hunters. Building a luxury STR in the Bahamas.
- **Reinvest Real Estate:** Their organization for agents who want to build legacy businesses alongside their active production. Based in Connecticut.
- **Legacy Playbook:** Released December 2024 — formal publication of what they've been teaching for 4-5 years.
- **PITY framework:** The overarching business system = Planning, Implementation, Tracking, Increasing Profit. Their GoGet'Em appearances covered PLAN (session 1) and Increasing Profit (session 2).
- **PLAN:** Their business planning format/acronym — the structured approach to laying out what your business needs to look like to generate the income and freedom you want. Covered in depth in this 52-minute session.
- **Core philosophy:** Active production is not the goal — it's the starting point. The goal is a business that doesn't require your constant presence. "Earning more while working less."
- **For GoGet'Em members:** Practical, structured framework that goes beyond motivational frameworks into actual business plan construction.
