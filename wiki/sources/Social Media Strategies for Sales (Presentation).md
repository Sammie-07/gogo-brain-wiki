# Social Media Strategies for Sales

> Presentation slide deck covering social media as Gogo's sole lead source — built entirely without knowing anyone when she arrived in the U.S. in 2003.

## Key Takeaways

- Zero paid leads: everything built through social media from a standing start (ZERO people known in 2003)
- Career milestones: Broke Agent $16k → Top Producing Agent $350k → GRET $500k → #teamgogo 1,660 agents, $4B closed → Bootcamp 3x 2 Comma Club → GoGet'Em Community (7 digits) → The Circle Coaching
- Must be on every platform: Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook, etc.
- Profile must be fully optimized: same headshot everywhere, same brand name, powerful direct bio, Instagram linked to Facebook Business Page
- B-rolls are non-negotiable: short, scroll-stopping content drives audience growth, engagement, and business growth
- Social media posting rules: always tag people, always tag location, always use relevant hashtags
- Brand must be built around YOU — be in photos and videos 80% of the time
- Leverage through technology first before humans: ManyChat, then VAs
- ManyChat use case: 9,000-person event → keyword → 6,000 messages in 20 seconds (would require 1,200 employees at 5 sec/text)
- Sell on Stan.Store for digital products
- Own your audience via email list — not just social followers
- Lead magnet strategy: freebie or Facebook Group to capture emails
- GoHighLevel for email sends and email list management
- Paid ads: $20/day x 30 days = $600/month; 1 closed deal at $10k commission = 16.7x ROI
- Reverse engineer 2026 goals in 5 minutes using commission math
- Social media success pillars: picture, name, bio, links, posts/reels, hashtags, location, keywords in descriptions, tagging, consistency, ManyChat, Facebook-Instagram link, VA distribution, ChatGPT for content ideas and copywriting

## Quotes

> "People do business with people, so your brand needs to be built around you. You have to be in your photos and videos 80% of the time."

> "If you're still doing $10/hour tasks, you'll never reach $1,000/hour goals."

> "You can have all the platforms but you don't have to be the one posting it."
