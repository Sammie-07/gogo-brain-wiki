# Optimizing Your Google Business Profile for Real Estate Agents (Kalie Kinsella)

> A #teamgogo / GoGet'Em live training hosted by [[Gogo Bethke]] with guest **[[Kalie Kinsella]]** ("That Google Girl"). The **"Build it, Prove it, Feed it"** framework for ranking a real estate agent's Google Business Profile (GBP) in the AI-search era.

## Key Takeaways

- **Core framework: "Build it, Prove it, Feed it."** Build the GBP, prove your business + expertise are legitimate, then continuously feed it fresh content/engagement.
- **Google now ranks confidence/validity, not just businesses.** Its local search relaunched (~June 6) running ~30× more verification checks; GBP now behaves like social media (wants frequent updates), not a static listing. "Google doesn't rank businesses now — it's ranking confidence, ranking validity."
- **List as an individual practitioner / Service Area Business (SAB)** — remote, no fake office. A P.O. box counts as a fake office and triggers suspensions. Don't create profiles to rank in cities you don't actually serve.
- **Top 5 setup priorities:** (1) **Identity & ownership** — claim/own the profile under a branded Google Workspace email; enable 2-step verification; keep yourself sole owner, assistants/vendors as "managers" only. (2) **Address/service area** — specific cities, zips, communities in drivable range, never the whole state. (3) **Categories & services** — primary "Real Estate Agent/Consultant" (not "agency/brokerage" unless you ARE the brokerage); add only relevant secondaries (too many can hurt). (4) **Complete every field** — hours (never 24/7), website, appointment link. (5) **Visual trust** — real local photos/video, not Canva stock (Google scans Canva inventory).
- **"SEO" → "Search Everywhere Optimization"** (credited to trainer Ryan McClure): "if you're not everywhere, you're nowhere."
- **The name field is an identity field, not a keyword field** — must match your legal LLC/DBA exactly (Google cross-checks public/IRS records); keyword-stuffed names ("Best Arizona Realtor") cause suspensions. Google gives no weight to a real estate license.
- **Brand syndication:** identical name, phone, logo, headshot across GBP, website, LinkedIn, FB, IG, TikTok. Google scans your socials for consistency; a missing LinkedIn has caused suspensions.
- **Reviews:** ask after every closing (script provided); vendors/title/lender/co-op agents count; review-for-review trades are fine, but never buy/pay/reward reviews. Respond within 24–48 hrs; clear a backlog at one/day (not all at once — looks bot-like).
- **Rankings compound over 3–4 months, not overnight.** Review the profile monthly as a calendar appointment. "Attention is currency online."
- **Own your website** (not the brokerage homepage, which pushes the brokerage's contact info) so you control keywords/updates.
- **AI/Gemini changed search behavior** — buyers enter long, specific prompts (relocation + new construction + commute), so profiles must show hyper-local, service-specific proof. Google checks GBP first, then website, then reviews.
- **Content:** publish GBP updates like social posts (market updates, community guides, open houses, "question of the day"); **blogging is back** as a top ranking driver; short/long-form video + authentic local content build authority. "You are more than a real estate agent — you are a lifestyle guide for the area you service."
- **5-Day Google Visibility Challenge:** Day 1 audit/ownership; Day 2 categories/services/hours/appointment links; Day 3 visual proof (headshot, logo, local photos, 30–60s video); Day 4 review system (post-closing email + text templates, direct link); Day 5 publish an update + record baseline metrics (calls, clicks, direction requests, appointments) + schedule monthly maintenance.
- **Secret tool: [answerthepublic.com](https://answerthepublic.com)** to find the actual questions people ask in your area for hyper-local content. Use a signed **marketing-release agreement** before using clients'/guests' photos/voice/video (template shared in community chat).
- **Warnings:** photos take 7–14 days to verify (don't re-upload); GBP can't post most MLS listings (legal/validity conflicts); Google's paid "verified relocation lead" / **Local Services Ads (LSAs)** program now spans 50 states (Gogo skeptical vs. Zillow/Realtor.com/Trulia spend); beware lead-scam texts spoofing Google.
