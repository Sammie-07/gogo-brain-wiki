# How to Grow Your Email List

> GoGet'Em Webinars session. Full training on email list building, lead magnets, engagement, and the technical setup needed to manage and grow a database. Full content gated.

## Key Takeaways

- Start now — whether you have 200 or 2,000 emails, the best time to begin email marketing is immediately.
- **Lead magnets** are the primary list-building tool — free downloads, templates, guides exchanged for an email address. (Gogo's own method: free resources on gogobethke.com — social media branding guide, ChatGPT prompt, VA hiring guide, podcast guide.)
- **Technical setup covered:** CRM/email platform setup, list segmentation, contact management.
- **Engagement tactics:** improving open rates and click-through rates; content that keeps the audience wanting more.
- **Analytics:** track campaign performance to continuously refine strategy.
- Full training gated at gogetemwebinars.com.
