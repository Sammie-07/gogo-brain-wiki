# Digital Business Card Webinar GoGet'Em (Alex RealGrader)

> Alex (founder of RealGrader/Instacard) teaches GoGet'Em members how to use, customize, and maximize digital business cards — a tool selected by NAR REACH 2023 and deployed across 88,000 eXp agents.

## Key Takeaways

- **What Instacard is**: a digital business card that agents text to prospects — contact info, social links, and a one-tap "save to contacts" experience.
- **Google review integration**: the card includes a direct Google review request — key for local SEO and social proof.
- **How contact capture works**: when someone taps your digital card, their information is captured in a live dashboard — you know who viewed it.
- **Customization**: photo, branding colors, links to social media, bio, listings, etc.
- Selected by NAR REACH 2023 program — vetted real estate technology.
- Deployed to 88,000+ eXp Realty agents as a standard tool.
- Alex's background: helping realtors with technology since 1999; mother was a retired agent.

## Quotes
