# The Simple Formula to Turn Social Media into Cash

> Short-form content from Gogo Bethke tracing her full business evolution from Facebook agent in 2011 to multi-stream entrepreneur. The distilled formula for turning social media presence into income.

## Key Takeaways

- **Gogo's evolution timeline:**
  1. Facebook 2011 → real estate leads
  2. Instagram → expanded reach
  3. eXp rev share → first passive income stream
  4. Bootcamps → knowledge monetization begins
  5. Digital products → scalable income
  6. Local team (50/50 model) → leveraged production
  7. Investments → wealth building
  8. GoGet'Em Community → community + recurring revenue
- **The formula:**
  - Build brand → DMs to commissions → knowledge to programs → help people at scale
- **gogetemcommunity.com/socialmedia/** — linked resource for this content.
- **Demonstrates compounding:** Each income stream was built on top of the previous. No stream replaced another — they layered.
