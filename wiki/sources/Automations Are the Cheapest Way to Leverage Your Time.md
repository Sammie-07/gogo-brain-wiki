# Automations Are the Cheapest Way to Leverage Your Time

> Short-form clip demonstrating ManyChat's automated Instagram DM sequence — specifically the welcome message triggered when someone follows Gogo's accounts.

## Key Takeaways

- **"Automations are your cheapest way to leverage."** Cheaper than a VA's time, cheaper than your hourly rate. Works 24/7 — even while sleeping or on vacation.
- **ManyChat now allows automated DMs to new followers.** The initial message is text-only (not video), but you can build a sequence that leads to a video via a trigger word.
- **Two separate welcome sequences** shown: one for personal Instagram (@gogosrealestate) and one for the GoGet'Em Community Instagram.
  - Personal: welcomes new follower to her channel, explains what she shares, offers "partner" keyword to join Team Go at eXp, and "community" keyword to join GoGet'Em Community.
  - GoGet'Em Community: welcomes them, explains the community does one training/week covering mindset → AI → hiring → firing → training → anything a successful entrepreneur needs.
- **Trigger keywords in use:** "partner" (eXp partnership), "community" (GoGet'Em Community membership).
- **The core idea:** set up once, works forever — buyers, sellers, partners, clients all get personalized journeys without Gogo manually typing a word.

## Quotes

> "Automations are your cheapest way to leverage. It is cheaper than a VA's time and it is definitely cheaper than your hourly rate. And it works for you all the time, even when you're sleeping, even when you're on vacation."
