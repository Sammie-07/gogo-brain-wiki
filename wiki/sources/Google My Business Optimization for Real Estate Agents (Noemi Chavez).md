# Google My Business Optimization for Real Estate Agents (Noemi Chavez)

> Noemi Chavez trains Team Gogo's Wednesday 1pm community (1,000+ members, multiple countries) on Google Business Profile optimization — one of the most overlooked free marketing tools available to real estate agents.

## Key Takeaways

- **The problem:** Real estate agents think Google My Business (now Google Business Profile) = just posting addresses. They set it up, post a few listings, and forget it. They're missing the vast majority of its power.
- **What Google My Business actually does:** Controls how your business appears in Google Search and Google Maps when someone searches for a real estate agent in your area. It's often the first thing a potential client sees — before your website, before social media.
- **What Noemi teaches agents to optimize:**
  - Complete business information (hours, categories, services, description)
  - Reviews: how to ask for them, respond to them, and make them work for you
  - Google Posts: regular content updates that appear in search results
  - Q&A: proactively seeding and answering questions
  - Photos: proper categories, quality, and frequency
  - Insights: understanding how people find you and what they do next
- **How Noemi got introduced:** Her cousin Sotero Lopez (Team Gogo Wednesday meeting host) asked her to review his Google profile. She showed him everything he was doing wrong. The transformation was compelling enough to bring her in front of the full community.
- **Why it matters:** Clients Google you before they call you. What they find on your Google Business Profile shapes their first impression and decision. Most agents have no idea what that impression currently looks like.
- **Free tool:** Google Business Profile is completely free. The ROI on time invested in optimizing it is exceptionally high for any agent who gets local search traffic.
