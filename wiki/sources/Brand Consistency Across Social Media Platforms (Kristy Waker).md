# Brand Consistency Across Social Media Platforms (Kristy Waker)

> Wrap-up tutorial for GSMC 2026 brand build sequence — once all URLs are collected in the Brand Tracker, go back through every profile and make everything consistent and cross-linked.

## Key Takeaways

- Once all platform URLs are in the Brand Tracker, do a **second pass** through every profile to update: cover photo, bio/description, website links, and cross-links to other platforms.
- **Cross-linking rule:** Every platform must link to every other. Instagram → Facebook + website. YouTube → Instagram + Facebook + website. Website → all social platforms. Each platform is an entry point to the full brand ecosystem.
- Same cover photo, same headshot, same username — everywhere. A stranger who finds you on Instagram should immediately recognize you on Facebook, YouTube, and Google.
- Brand Tracker tip: use the collapse/hide button to clean up helper content once all URLs are logged — data stays available without cluttering the working view.
- "Done With You" support program: upgrade for hands-on help with GSMC setup.

## Quotes

> "Everything is going to be the same: your usernames everywhere, your cover photos, your website links, everything is going to be the exact same and all link back to each other."

> "If they're on Instagram, they can get to your Facebook, to your website. If they're on YouTube, they can get to your Instagram, your Facebook, your website."
