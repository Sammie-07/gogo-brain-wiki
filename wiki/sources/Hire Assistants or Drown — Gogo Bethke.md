# Hire Assistants or Drown — Gogo Bethke

> Ultra-short (~47-second) clip from The Brain🧠 making the mathematical case for hiring two cross-trained assistants instead of one.

## Key Takeaways

- **The math**: Your 40 hours + 2 assistants × 40 hours = **120 hours of productive work per week** — without anyone burning out.
- When nobody's drowning, the side effect is simple: you sleep like a baby.
- **Cross-training is mandatory**: all of Gogo's help is cross-trained so that when one leaves, life goes on. No single point of failure.
- Reframe: Stop saying "I can't believe I'm paying two assistants" and start saying "where will I be in a year with 120 hours of work done per week?"
- The compounding effect of this over 12 months is the difference between stuck and scaled.

## Quotes

> "If you take your 40 hours and two people's 40 hours, now we are talking 120 hours every week work done. Plus, you sleep like a baby."

> "This is why all of my help is cross-trained."
