# Creating 3-Way Calls with Calendar — GoHighLevel Setup (Kristy Waker)

> Full GoHighLevel calendar setup for Gogo's three-way call system — the mechanism that lets Team Go members bring agent prospects directly onto a Zoom with Gogo for closing. Part of *Gogo's Social Media Challenge 2026*.

## Key Takeaways

**What the three-way call is:**
- Team Go member identifies an agent prospect interested in eXp
- All three — member, prospect, and Gogo — jump on a 30-min Zoom
- Gogo closes the agent into eXp under the member's sponsorship
- Lives on the "Partner With Me" page as a calendar booking button

**GoHighLevel calendar setup (step by step):**
1. GHL left menu → **Calendars** → top-right gear icon → Calendar Settings
2. **Prerequisites first:** Connections tab → connect your Google Calendar + Zoom (or Google Meet)
3. Calendar name: "Three-Way Calls" (or your equivalent)
4. Add description (template provided in GSMC lesson — swap "Gogo" for your name and update the partner page link)
5. Custom URL: must be unique (GHL will flag duplicates)
6. Meeting duration: **30 minutes**
7. Click **Advanced Settings** for full configuration

**Availability settings:**
- Meeting intervals: every hour and half-hour (not 15-min — keeps calendar clean)
- Scheduling notice: **12 hours minimum** (prevents same-day bookings; ensures prep time)
- Date range: **14 days rolling** (calendar always shows next 14 days; nothing beyond)
- Max bookings per day: 4 (adjust to capacity)

**Forms and payment settings (CRITICAL):**
- Attach your three-way call question form
- Enable **"Allow guest addition"** — the prospect must be added as a guest
- Enable **"Require guests for booking"** — this is the gate. Without this, anyone can book a three-way call with you for any reason. With it on, the booking cannot be confirmed unless the member adds the agent prospect as a guest.

**Confirmation and notifications:**
- Confirmation: redirect to partner page; auto-confirm (no manual approval needed)
- Enable "Send invites and update emails to attendees" — must be on
- Enable "Allow Google/Outlook/iCloud calendars to send invitations"
- **After saving**, go back into the calendar → Notifications → enable Email + SMS for: appointment confirmed, rescheduled, cancelled, and reminders. These are off by default and must be manually turned on post-save.

**Customization:** Upload logo, set brand colors (buttons, etc.), calendar name visible on widget.

**To add to your website:** Calendars → find your calendar → Share button → copy link → paste into your funnel/website button.
