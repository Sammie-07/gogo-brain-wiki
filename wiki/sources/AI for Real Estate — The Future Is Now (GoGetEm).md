# AI for Real Estate — The Future Is Now (GoGetEm)

> GoGet'Em Community training. Two-part session: Syed (Gogo's AI assistant, based in Pakistan) covers AI fundamentals; Phil Stringer (AI educator and speaker) takes it to advanced applications. 40+ attendees.

## Key Takeaways

- **Technology is here and it's here to stay.** You can fight it or use it. "Might as well just know about it."
- AI is not new — GPS, Siri, Alexa, Google Maps are all AI. What's new is ChatGPT-style text AI going mainstream (late 2022).
- **AI needs a command from you.** It doesn't think on its own. Give it a query → it runs. Quality of prompt = quality of output.
- **Practical AI applications for agents:**
  - Smart assistants (Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant)
  - Google Lens: photograph any product → AI identifies it, finds alternatives and where to buy
  - ChatGPT: Write listing descriptions, follow-up emails, market updates, social media posts, blog posts
  - Image recognition for research and competitive analysis
- **Gogo's use model:** "I use AI — meaning I ask my team to use it." She comes up with the concept; team executes via AI tools.
- Phil Stringer's message: Even if you don't use a specific application today, knowing what's possible lets your brain find the connection point later.
- You may not know every use case today — but exposure plants the seed for future implementation.
