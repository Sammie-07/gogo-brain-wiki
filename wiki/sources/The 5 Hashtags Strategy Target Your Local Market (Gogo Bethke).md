# The 5 Hashtags Strategy: Target Your Local Market

> Gogo's live ChatGPT demo showing how to find 5 hyper-local niche hashtags for any specialty area — be brutally specific, then use those same 5 on every niche post going forward.

## Key Takeaways

- **Specificity is the strategy:** Don't say "waterfront in Michigan" — say "Chain of Lakes in Pinckney, Livingston County." The more specific you are, the less competition you face and the higher you rank.
- **ChatGPT prompt formula:** "Act like the best social media marketer. Research every platform and algorithm. Give me the 5 best hashtags to build my name in [exact niche + exact location] as a go-to realtor."
- **Live demo result:** Niche = Chain of Lakes in Pinckney, MI → Output: #ChainofLakesMI, #PinckneyWaterfront, #LivingstonCountyLakes, #LakefrontLifestyleMI, #ZukeyLakeRealEstate
- **These 5 become your niche kit:** Use them on every post related to that niche. Your ideal client searches those terms — they'll find you.
- **The starting question:** "What are you known for? What is that niche?" Answer that first. Then go to Chat for the hashtags.
