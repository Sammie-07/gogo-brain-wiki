# Creating and Customizing Your YouTube Channel (Kristy Waker)

> Two-part walkthrough — creating a YouTube channel URL/handle and then fully customizing the channel — presented by Kristy Waker as part of *Gogo's Social Media Challenge 2026*.

## Key Takeaways

- Create your YouTube channel from your Google account → click your profile icon → "View your channel" → "Customize channel."
- Set a **custom handle** (e.g., youtube.com/@KristysRealEstateTeam) — this is the URL you share everywhere; it must match your brand handle on all other platforms.
- Log the YouTube link immediately in your **Brand Tracker** — that's the URL you copy-paste across every platform.
- Upload banner and profile photo for brand consistency — same banner as Facebook, website, LinkedIn. Same headshot everywhere.
- Add your **website links and branded email** in the channel's Links section — these display publicly on the channel page.
- Add a **video watermark** (logo/icon) — it appears at the corner of every uploaded video.
- Customize the **Home tab layout** — organize sections (Videos, Shorts, Playlists, Live streams) in whatever order best serves your audience. Up to 12 sections.
- **YouTube Studio** (youtube.com/studio) — the full management dashboard once you start uploading: analytics, content list, settings, permissions.
- Grant team access via **Settings → Permissions** → invite by email address → set role (Manager, Editor, etc.). This allows a second Google account to manage the channel without sharing passwords.
- Week 1 = setup and infrastructure. Week 2 = design, bio, and customization. Don't skip the setup phase.
