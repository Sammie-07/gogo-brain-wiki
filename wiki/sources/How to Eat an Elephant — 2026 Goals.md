# How to Eat an Elephant — 2026 Goals

> Full goal-setting training session. [[Gogo Bethke]] runs a live group through her complete goal-setting system: mindset foundations, CPR framework, commission math, reverse engineering, and ChatGPT prompts.

## Key Takeaways

- **Big, scary goals are mandatory.** If your goal doesn't frighten you, you settled. Most people set low goals to protect themselves from disappointment — Gogo rejects this entirely.
- **Current GPS location:** Before setting a destination, write down honestly where you are right now financially and behaviorally. Look yourself in the eye and own it.
- **No plan B:** Having a fallback confuses your internal GPS. The universe cannot take you to two destinations simultaneously.
- **"If I cannot imagine it, I cannot have it."** If you feel a twitch of "there's no way," sit in meditation until you can vividly see the goal. Do not proceed until the doubt is gone.
- **"What I don't change, I accept."** Personal responsibility — where you are is the standard you allowed.
- **Set a specific deadline.** Without a date, the universe has the rest of your life to deliver.
- **CPR framework:** Commitment + Penalty + Reward. Penalty and reward must both be genuinely high-stakes. Replace "if I hit my goal" with "when I hit my goal."
- **Reverse engineer:** Break annual goal → quarterly → monthly → weekly → daily → hourly. At the daily level, it should feel trivially small.
- **1% daily change = 365% annual change.** Compounding is the mechanism.
- **Stay in your lane.** Know your strengths (ask 5–10 trusted friends). Only pursue goals within your God-given skills.
- **Track everything.** 16 business trackers down to the penny. Business decisions, not feelings decisions.
- **Celebrate wins.** Dangling carrots (specific rewards for specific milestones) drive performance.
- **Commission math live example:** $120k income at $430k avg price / 3% commission at eXp = ~11 transactions = 1/month.
- **ChatGPT prompts** available (paid package) for reverse-engineering goals, marketing plans, ideal client identification, and agent recruiting.
