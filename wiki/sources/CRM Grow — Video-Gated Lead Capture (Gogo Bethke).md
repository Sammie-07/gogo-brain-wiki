# CRM Grow — Video-Gated Lead Capture (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo explains CRM Grow — the third-party tool that embeds videos on her "Partner With Me" website and requires name, email, and phone number before a visitor can watch. Even if they never schedule a call, she captures them as a lead.

## Key Takeaways

- **CRM Grow** is a third-party paid service that embeds videos into your website behind a lead capture gate — visitors must provide name, email, and phone number before the video plays.
- Gogo uses it on **gogobethke.com/partner** — every video on the page is mandatory to watch before an agent can schedule a call with her.
- **The double capture:** Even if the agent watches but never books, Gogo knows who they are and that they're actively interested in switching brokerages (because they're voluntarily watching eXp videos).
- Can be used beyond agent attraction — also applicable to listing videos and buyer content to capture seller/buyer leads.
- Gogo has 1,660 agents in her downline built through this system and religiously works agent attraction as a dedicated income stream.
- Context: She spent 8.5 years at brokerages that did not allow downlines or revenue share — joined eXp specifically for agent attraction.
- Paid service — link available in GSMC lesson description.
- Website (gogobethke.com/partner) vs. simpler funnel alternative: team can build either; funnel template also available free for DIY.
