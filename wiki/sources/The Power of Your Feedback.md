# The Power of Your Feedback

> "What Would Gogo Do" segment. Gogo explains how community feedback directly shapes the GoGet'Em Community curriculum and product development.

## Key Takeaways

- **"What Would Gogo Do"** — recurring segment/format within GoGet'Em where community members submit questions or situations for Gogo to advise on.
- **Feedback loop:** Member feedback directly determines what trainings, webinars, and tools get built next. GoGet'Em is explicitly community-driven.
- **Power of feedback:** Gogo frames community participation not just as learning but as co-creation — members who give feedback are building the resource they're also using.
- **Community trust signal:** Being responsive to feedback builds loyalty. Members feel heard; the product improves; retention increases.
- Reinforces Gogo's philosophy that her community is her business partner, not just her customer.
