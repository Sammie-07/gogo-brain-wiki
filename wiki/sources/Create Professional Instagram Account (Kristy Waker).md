# Create Professional Instagram Account (Kristy Waker)

> Step-by-step walkthrough of creating a new Instagram business account from a phone, switching to a professional profile, and connecting it to a Facebook Business Page. Part of *Gogo's Social Media Challenge 2026*.

## Key Takeaways

- Create from phone: Instagram → bottom-right profile icon → tap username → **Add an Instagram account** → Create new one.
- During setup: skip profile photo, contacts, and follower suggestions — come back to those later.
- Switch to professional: Settings → **Account Type for Professionals** → Switch to Professional Account.
- Choose category (Entrepreneur, etc.) and type: **Creator** (public figures, influencers) or **Business** (retailers, service providers). Either works — both give you a professional profile. You need one or the other to brand your Instagram account.
- Connect Facebook Business Page during setup (optional but recommended): enables cross-posting Instagram to Facebook and unlocks ads/shopping tools. Log in with the same personal Facebook account used to create the Business Page — brands must match (e.g., "Kristy's Real Estate Team" on both).
- **Meta Verified** eligibility: not available immediately on new accounts. Requires a fully set-up business profile and Meta Business Suite. Check later once profile is complete.

## Quotes

> "Either one is fine. It doesn't matter which one you choose, just one or the other. But if you want to brand your Instagram account, it needs to be a professional business profile."
