# Amplify Your Influence with René Rodriguez

> GoGet'Em Community session with René Rodriguez on the nature of influence — arguing that influence is not about follower count but about authentic storytelling, owning your narrative, and controlling how others perceive you.

## Key Takeaways

- **René Rodriguez:** Influence and behavioral expert. GoGet'Em Community presenter.
- **Influence ≠ followers:** Influence is not measured by audience size. It's about the depth of authentic connection and the clarity of your message.
- **The power of narrative:** The stories you tell shape how others see you, your message, and your products. Narrative is the core of sales and leadership.
- **Mind the gaps:** Every story has gaps — details you leave out, intentionally or not. If you don't fill those gaps, your audience fills them for you — usually with their own fears, assumptions, and biases. Negative interpretation by default.
- **Control the narrative:** Be intentional about what you share and how. Leave no room for misinterpretation. Fill the gaps before your audience does.
- **Authentic connection:** Influence comes from authentically connecting with people — not performing, not positioning, but genuinely showing up as yourself.
- **Storytelling in sales:** In real estate, coaching, or any sales context, the agent/coach who tells a more compelling, coherent story wins the relationship.

## Quotes

> "Influence isn't about how many followers you have. It's about whether the right people feel genuinely connected to you."

> "If you don't fill the gaps in your story, your audience will. And they'll fill them with their worst assumptions."

> "Control your narrative or someone else will control it for you."
