# Money Mindset Mastery — Production Accelerator Event

> Presentation subtitled "What They Don't Teach You About Wealth as an Entrepreneur." Covers the mindset gap between agents and entrepreneurs, delegation, credit, fractionalized real estate, affiliate income, passive income, and taxes.

## Key Takeaways

**Background:** Arrived in the U.S. with $200, knew no one, didn't speak English well — but had work ethic and belief. Now: 9 companies, 3x 2 Comma Club, $15B+ in real estate sales.

**Agent vs. Entrepreneur Mindset:**
| Agent Mindset | Entrepreneur Mindset |
|---|---|
| "I can do everything and save that money" | "Learn to leverage: it needs to get done just not by me" |
| "If I work harder I'll grow faster" | "Automate it, delegate it, or eliminate it" |

**CEO Questions:**
- "What can I delegate?"
- "Who can do this better than me?"
- "How can I make this repeatable?"
- "What's the vision and how do we scale to it?"

**Key principle:** Poor people ask "How much does it cost?" Wealthy people ask "How much money will this make me?"

**Hourly Rate:** $250,000 ÷ 2,080 hours = $120.19/hr — delegate everything below that rate

**Red Light / Green Light** — core time audit exercise (red = admin, green = revenue activities)

**Listing vs. Buy Side:** Listing = $1,000/hr (10 hrs average); Buy Side = $400/hr (25 hrs average)

**Gogo's current hourly rate: $3,086/hr** — can no longer justify working with sellers ($30,860 per 10-hour deal)

**Credit:**
- Never use cash; use credit cards for points/travel
- Chase Ink and AMEX as favorites
- Pay on 1st AND 15th (statement date and due date timing)

**Fractionalized Real Estate:**
- RealBricks: invest as little as $100 in fractional real estate for rental income and appreciation

**Affiliate Income:**
- Your favorite service/platform likely has an affiliate program — most people don't know it
- Passive income stream with zero extra work once set up

**Taxes / "Big Beautiful Bill":**
- 6,000-pound vehicle deduction referenced
- Cost segregation strategy mentioned
