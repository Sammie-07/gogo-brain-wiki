# Expert Mentors Live Annual Kick-Off with Gogo Bethke (Honey Badger Nation)

> Gogo's 5th annual new-year conversation with Expert Mentors Live / Honey Badger Nation — presenting her four core systems for working less while growing more: Greatness Tracker, Automate/Delegate/Eliminate, Stan Store, and ChatGPT + ManyChat.

## Key Takeaways

- **Gogo's 2024 resolution**: "Work less. I want to replace myself. I don't want to retire — I still want to do things, just don't want to have to do things."
- **Four Systems Presented**:
  1. **Greatness Tracker** — daily tracking of business activities; makes the invisible visible; what gets measured gets managed.
  2. **Automate / Delegate / Eliminate** — every task must pass this filter before it stays on your plate.
  3. **Stan Store** — digital product storefront; simple, mobile-first; Gogo uses it to sell trainings and resources.
  4. **ChatGPT + ManyChat automations** — AI-powered content + keyword-triggered DM automation for lead capture.
- Fifth consecutive annual interview — signals Gogo's consistent presence in the broader real estate education space.
- Framing: "I worked so hard for the last few years. When you do things over and over again, it becomes a habit. Now 2024 is going to change."

## Quotes

> "I don't want to retire — I still want to do things, just don't want to have to do things."
