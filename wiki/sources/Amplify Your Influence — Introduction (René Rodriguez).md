# Amplify Your Influence — Introduction (René Rodriguez)

> GoGet'Em Live Training, 30-minute overview session. René Rodriguez is a leading authority in leadership and influence. Author of *Amplify Your Influence*. Uses behavioral neuroscience. Gogo met him through a shared podcast (200th episode) and liked his approach so much she signed up for his in-person speaker training in Minnesota.

## Key Takeaways

- **Influence is the root skill.** "If you can grow your skill set of influence, all the other things begin to happen." 30 years of experience distilled to one word: influence.
- **The opposite of influence = insignificance.** Walking into a room and no one notices. Telling a joke and no one laughs. Sharing an idea no one cares about. Selling a product and no one buys. Casting a vision and no one follows.
- **Why people follow Gogo (and leaders like her).** "The reason why you're following people like Gogo and trying to learn is that you want to really have a bigger impact on the world."
- **Behavioral neuroscience applied.** Uses neuroscience to explain why people follow, trust, and buy — communication patterns that trigger action.
- **Book:** *Amplify Your Influence* — covers the full framework in depth.
- **Speaker training program.** In-person, Minnesota. Gogo enrolled: "I really like the way he spoke and I liked it so much when I actually signed up for his speaker training."
- **Background.** 30+ years. Based in Florida (moved from Michigan). Recently back from Dubai.
