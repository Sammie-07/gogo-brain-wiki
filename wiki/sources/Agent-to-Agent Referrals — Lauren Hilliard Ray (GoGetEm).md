# Agent-to-Agent Referrals — Lauren Hilliard Ray (GoGetEm)

> Lauren Hilliard Ray (Dallas-Fort Worth, eXp Realty) on how she built a referral-based real estate business — signing 80+ agent-to-agent referrals in 12 months using Facebook groups, Google reviews, and Instagram optimization.

## Key Takeaways

- **What is an A2A referral?** An agent in one market sends a client to an agent in another market. Referring agent earns 20–35% of the commission on close — without doing the transaction work.
- Lauren's result: **80+ A2A referrals signed** in 12 months in the Dallas-Fort Worth market alone.
- **How she generates referrals**:
  - Active in agent Facebook referral groups (finds agents in other markets needing a DFW connection)
  - Consistent Google review building — when an agent searches for "best Dallas agent," she shows up
  - Instagram optimization — agents checking her out see a professional, trustworthy profile
  - **Text replacement shortcuts** — types 2 letters and her full follow-up message appears instantly
- eXp Realty connection: eXp's culture of collaboration and resource-sharing makes A2A natural.
- **Leadership positions** that drive referrals: Vice Chair of eXp Women's Impact Network; Co-leader of eXp Young Professionals (~2,000–3,000 agents) — visibility in the network generates inbound referrals.
- Her husband Spencer Ray retired from corporate because of referral income from her network.
- Valentine's Day session; Lauren was pregnant (baby announcement during training).
