# Business Email — Creating Your Business Gmail (Google Workspace Acct)

> GSMC 2026 training by Kristy Waker. Step-by-step walkthrough for creating a Google Workspace business email tied to your domain. Part of the brand setup sequence — follows domain purchase, precedes further brand infrastructure.

## Key Takeaways

- **Prerequisite: own your domain first.** Cannot create a domain-tied business email without it. If you don't have a domain, go back to the domain training.
- **Google Workspace, not Microsoft.** The majority of realtors use Google — it meshes with Docs, Sheets, Calendar, and Gmail. Microsoft accounts create friction with these shared tools.
- **Start with Starter plan** (~$6/month, 14-day free trial, 30 GB storage). Upgrade to Standard only when you need more storage (e.g., for videos).
- **Admin email = a personal address you'll have forever.** Never use a brokerage-associated email as the admin account — you could lose access when you change brokerages.
- **Brand Tracker row 12** = business/company email field. Once set up, log it there.
- **Domain verification via GoDaddy:** Click "Sign in to GoDaddy" → Google connects automatically and verifies domain ownership. May take up to 24 hours for Gmail to be fully live — monitor both old and new email during transition.
- **DNS metaphor:** Domain = your address. Website = the house. DNS settings = the GPS telling everything where to go.
- **Two-step verification: enable and enforce immediately.** Do not turn it off — re-enabling is a major pain. Apply to every user added to the domain.
- **Brand the workspace:** Upload business logo to Admin Console. Add the same headshot you use everywhere.
- **Create a separate Chrome profile** for the business email — keeps business bookmarks, passwords, and sessions fully separate from personal.
- **"Done With You" program** available for agents who need hands-on help with setup.
