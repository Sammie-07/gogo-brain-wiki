# Securing Your Domain

> Part of [[Gogo Bethke]]'s *Gogo's Social Media Challenge 2026* series. Presented by [[Kristy Waker]] — a GoGetter demonstrating the GoDaddy domain purchase process on-screen. Companion to [[Same Username Everywhere]].

## Key Takeaways

- **Two domains to secure:** yourname.com (personal brand) and yourbusinessname.com (business brand)
- **Domain = address only.** Like a house number — it just points visitors somewhere. The website, funnel, or landing page comes later. Right now the goal is to lock in the name.
- **GoDaddy walkthrough:**
  - Search your exact brand name (should match the Instagram handle you already verified)
  - First-year pricing is discounted (~$4.99 first year); renews at regular price after
  - **Enable auto-renew** — set it annually and don't touch it
  - **Skip all add-ons**: domain privacy, protections, extras — say no to everything
  - **Do NOT buy the GoDaddy/Microsoft email** — real estate runs on Google; professional email will be set up through Google separately
  - Complete purchase → copy domain URL → paste into Brand Tracker (website field) → check it off
- **Mindset:** "We're just locking in and securing our brands right now. Building the website comes later."

## Quotes

> "Your domain is literally just like your house's address — it's going to be the address for your website."

> "We're just trying to lock in and secure all of our brand links so that we can update our professional profiles and make sure that we're consistent across all platforms."
