# Self-Selling Seller System LIVE (Austin Rankin)

> Austin Rankin — face-based real estate coach who went from zero sales experience to $116M+ closed in 4 years — teaches GoGet'Em members his "Self-Selling Seller System": a conversation framework for generating consistent listing appointments through cold calling and prospecting, without buying leads or building a personal brand.

## Key Takeaways

- **Austin's origin:** From a small town in Kentucky. No sales experience, no family in sales, shy and introverted, poor speller. "I am nobody special." Became #1 agent in his company in 4 years by selling $116M+ through one system.
- **"Face-based" real estate:** Everything rooted in real human conversation — not automation, not personal brand, not social media. The face behind the call is the product.
- **The Self-Selling Seller System:** A conversation framework designed so that sellers convince themselves to work with you based on how you structure the questions. They're not being sold — they're arriving at the conclusion on their own.
- **This is the second appearance:** His first GoGet'Em session covered the conversation overview. This second session goes much deeper on conversation structure, scripting, and question design. "Actual structure, actual new skills, and new strategy — not mindset fluff."
- **Core skill: ask better questions.** The conversation IS the product. Prospecting success depends entirely on how you structure the questions — not how much you talk or how confident you sound.
- **Interactive training format:** Austin requires participation. Attendees write things down and respond throughout the session. This isn't passive training.
- **From zero to 70+ homes/year:** Four years. No leads purchased, no paid advertising, no personal brand. Just conversations, repeated and refined.
- **The introvert angle:** "I'm a very introverted person yet I can do this. So if I can do it, every one of you that maybe are scared to pick up a phone or just don't know what to do, you guys are capable of it."

## Quotes
