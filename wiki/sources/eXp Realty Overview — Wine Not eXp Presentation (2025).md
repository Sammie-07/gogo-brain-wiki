**The 7 Reasons She Switched:**
1. Cap ($16,000)
2. Commission cut 80/20
3. ICON program
4. Training
5. Revenue Share
6. Stocks
7. Health Insurance

**Commission Structure:**
- 80/20 split until $16,000 cap
- Standard costs: $149 startup, $85/month cloud brokerage fee, $25 transaction review fee, $60 risk management fee (capped at $750/year for E&O)
- Before cap example: $300k house → $9,000 commission → minus 20% ($1,800) + $60 E&O + $25 review + 5% stock options = $6,759.25 net; ~9 transactions to cap
- After cap: $9,000 → minus $250 transaction fee + $25 review + $60 E&O (until capped) + 5% stock = $8,231.75; add $595 transaction fee = $8,826.75

**ICON Program:**
- After capping, close 20+ more transactions → become ICON agent → receive $16,000 cap back in company stocks (EXPI)
- Yasmin Rogers: 7x ICON agent cited as example

**Mentor Program:**
- Certified Mentor earns compensation for time/knowledge provided to new agents
- Additional income stream; creates lasting relationships; you shape careers, boost confidence
- "Real estate changed your life — mentorship is how you pay that forward while still building your business"
- KV Core (BoldTrail) and referrals system tied to #teamgogo map (teamgogo.team/map)
