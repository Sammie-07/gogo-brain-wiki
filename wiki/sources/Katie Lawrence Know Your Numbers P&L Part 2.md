# Katie Lawrence Know Your Numbers P&L Part 2

> Katie Lawrence (Union Home Mortgage) teaches GoGet'Em members three financial tracking tools: the Pay Log, Money Tracker, and P&L Statement — the foundation of a profitable, organized real estate business.

## Key Takeaways

- **Core thesis**: "You go to real estate school and they teach you how many square feet are in an acre. But they don't teach you how to be business people." Financial tracking is the missing curriculum.
- **Three tools** (all free Excel spreadsheets provided):
  1. **Pay Log** — track every paycheck, what deal it came from, when it arrived
  2. **Money Tracker** — categorize every expense in real time
  3. **P&L Statement** — monthly profit and loss overview; are you actually profitable?
- **Rule #1**: Separate personal and business finances immediately. One personal card; one business card; separate bank accounts for each.
- **Blue time**: Set aside at least 1 hour/week working ON your business (not in it) to maintain these tools.
- Real-world impact: The host's contact started using Katie's Impact Tracker from Part 1 — landed a $1M+ listing (her first) by tracking activities and making consistent calls.
- Part 2 builds on Part 1 (Impact Tracker) — these tools work as a system.

## Quotes
