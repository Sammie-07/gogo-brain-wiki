# How to Pick a Sponsor at eXp

> Gogo makes her pitch for why agents should pick her as their eXp sponsor, while also sharing her full brokerage history and what to look for in a sponsor.

## Key Takeaways

- **Brokerage history:**
  - Started at **Real Estate 1** (Michigan) — stayed 7.5 years
  - Moved to **Keller Williams** — February 2018
  - Moved to **eXp Realty** — November 2018
- **What to look for in a sponsor:** Someone actively successful in the business (not just in eXp), who trains and supports, has a track record of producing agents, and is accessible.
- **Revenue share is the differentiator** — eXp's model means your sponsor has financial incentive to help you succeed (their rev share grows when you do).
- **Gogo's value proposition:** Active agent ($1M+ earner), proven trainer, GoGet'Em Community resources, strong team systems, access to her frameworks.
- **Warning:** Many sponsors are passive — they recruited the agent but offer no ongoing support. Evaluate the sponsor's training program before signing.
