# Market Education & Neighborhood Insights (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo's step-by-step system for doing monthly neighborhood market report videos — from pulling MLS data to shooting on location to capturing leads with a ManyChat keyword. The Pine Creek origin story illustrates why "no one owns a neighborhood."

## Key Takeaways

- **No one owns a neighborhood.** Free market, free speech — market wherever you want. Her broker told her a competitor "owned" Pine Creek; she ignored it and broke in anyway.
- **Once a month per neighborhood.** Pull the report, study it, practice in the mirror, go shoot on location. Repeat monthly. This is what builds the "neighborhood expert" reputation — not one video, but consistent monthly presence.
- **Step 1 — Pull the report:** MLS data (active listings, days on market, price per sqft, expired listings) + ChatGPT for lifestyle context (restaurants, schools, cool things about the neighborhood).
- **Step 2 — Print and study:** Circle what to mention, cross out what you don't understand. Hold the paper while shooting — don't try to memorize everything.
- **Step 3 — Shoot on location:** Go to a recognizable landmark or the neighborhood entrance sign. Bring a person (friend, employee, teenager who drives) or a phone stand. The backdrop signals the location instantly.
- **Step 4 — CTA with keyword:** End every video with a keyword CTA. Example: "Comment PINE and my system will automatically send you the report and listings for this neighborhood."
- **Bonus 1 — Build a squeeze page:** Create a KV Core/BoldTrail squeeze page for that specific neighborhood — auto-updates as new listings hit the MLS.
- **Bonus 2 — ManyChat keyword:** Keyword triggers delivery of the squeeze page link. Instagram can't use direct links in feed, so keyword is the workaround. Facebook/YouTube: post the direct link in the description.
- **ChatGPT research:** Use ChatGPT to research neighborhoods for lifestyle info (nearest restaurants, school districts, community features) — adds personality beyond dry MLS stats.
- **Consistency = expertise:** Doing it once produces nothing. Doing it monthly for a year makes you the neighborhood authority. "You're doing circles around everybody else who never even dared to turn the camera on."
