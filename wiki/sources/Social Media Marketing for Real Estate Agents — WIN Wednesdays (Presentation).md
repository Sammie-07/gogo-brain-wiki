# Social Media Marketing for Real Estate Agents — WIN Wednesdays

> Presentation for eXp Women's Impact Network (WIN) Wednesdays. 2026-focused social media strategy for real estate agents.

## Key Takeaways

- Social media in 2026 is about strategy, systems, and consistency — not pretty pictures
- AI is your assistant: use it to personalize content, captions, and ads so you're talking to specific buyers, sellers, and investors
- Short-form video runs the show: Reels, TikTok, Shorts — hook in 3 seconds or you've lost them
- Carousels = education and authority: step-by-step guides, market insights, listings, value posts — if someone swipes through, you're winning their trust
- Make social media shoppable: booking links, DMs, ManyChat keywords — make it easy to take action
- Stories = daily connection: behind the scenes, new listings, real-time updates; show your life, your work, stay visible
- Go Live or get left behind: virtual open houses, Q&As, real conversations — people want access, not perfection
- Let clients market for you: testimonials, before-and-afters, real stories — social proof sells faster
- Create once, distribute everywhere: one video on Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Shorts
- Data over feelings, always: track engagement, reach, conversions; cut what doesn't work, double down on what does
- Algorithms reward consistency, not luck
- Local always wins in real estate: location tags, local hashtags, neighborhood content

**2026 New Focus Areas:**
1. AI-driven automation and personalization across all platforms
2. Short-form video dominance on multiple platforms
3. Seamless social commerce integration (ManyChat keyword: comment "SOCIAL" on @gogosrealestate)
4. Affiliate marketing and influencer collaborations
5. Local SEO-focused hashtags
6. Monitor social media insights constantly
