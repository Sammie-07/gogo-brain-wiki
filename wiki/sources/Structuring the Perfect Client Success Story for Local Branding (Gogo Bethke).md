# Structuring the Perfect Client Success Story for Local Branding

> Gogo's 10-step system for turning a buyer's entire journey — from contract to closing — into social media content that builds local brand, pre-educates future clients, and generates organic referrals.

## Key Takeaways

- **10-step content system:** Every stage of the buying process is a content opportunity. Audience gets invested in the client's story; future clients get pre-educated on the market.
- **Always under contract:** No exceptions. Contract = clients take you seriously. Horror stories of clients switching agents "never happened to me because they were under contract."
- **Marketing release form at signing:** Sign the face/image/voice/likeness release at the same time as the buyer's agency. Permission is never a friction point again — you can film at any moment.
- **Client intro = storytelling hook:** Introduce Jessica and Adam with story details (kids, dogs, yard). Audience gets invested and wonders what they'll find.
- **Property tour snippets pre-educate:** Show what exists at what price → future clients arrive with realistic expectations ("I only found 2 homes under $300K with an acre in Pinckney").
- **Home inspection = content goldmine:** 3–4 hours of nothing to do = educational video time. "Bats in the attic" video educates all future clients watching — so you don't have to explain it again.
- **Closing table:** B-roll of signing + b-roll walking out with packet + selfie video. High-five. "It only took us 29 days."
- **5-star review script:** "I had a five-star experience working with you. I hope you had the same. Would you please leave me a review on these three platforms?" Pre-frames a five-star response. Send via text immediately + automated email when Kristy marks "closed."
- **Review platforms:** Google, Facebook, Zillow (and Realtor.com).
- **Monkey does, monkey do:** When Jessica and Adam's cousin sees their closing video, they're next.

### The 10 Steps

| Step | Content Opportunity |
|------|-------------------|
| 1. Initial contract signing | Establishes credibility; headache-free business |
| 2. Marketing release form | Signed upfront — no permission friction ever |
| 3. Client intro + storytelling | Hook: "Meet Jessica and Adam..." |
| 4. Property tour snippets | "Only 2 homes exist under $300K with an acre" |
| 5. Property tours | Audience comes along for the ride |
| 6. Home inspection | Educational content; "bats in the attic" library |
| 7. Educating clients on-camera | Teach once → educates all future clients |
| 8. Closing table | B-roll + selfie video + keys handoff |
| 9. Client testimonial video | Couch setting, post-move-in, calm setting |
| 10. 5-star review request | Script → text + auto email to 3 platforms |
