# eXp Tools Update — The Hub, my eXp, Mira AI & TeamGogo Map (Kristy Waker)

> A weekly #teamgogo / [[GoGet'Em Community]] call where [[Kristy Waker]] walks agents live through eXp's evolving tech stack — the new Hub platform, the my eXp dashboard, the Mira AI assistant, and TeamGogo's Claude-rebuilt agent map — bookended by a mortgage-market update from [[Jason Dupree]].

## Key Takeaways
- **Mortgage update ([[Jason Dupree]], Texana Bank):** rates dipped to a low average of 6.37% but are back to ~6.5%; he advised locking loans Friday to hedge a Middle East peace-plan outcome that could spike rates Monday. Purchases up 5% YoY, refinances up 29% YoY. He predicts "the biggest summer in real estate we've seen in quite a few years" and pushes the REMLO program (loan-originator add-on making agents full-service without a second job); his "What is REMLO" call runs at 3pm, link gogobethke.com/remlo.
- **TeamGogo map rebuilt with Claude AI:** the old map (launched ~last fall) malfunctioned and grouped agents on its own; the team rebuilt it in 2 weeks using Claude. Same URL (map.teamgogo.team), same source spreadsheet, new map + list views and country filters; still has kinks, feedback requested.
- **Map automation:** every business day Rafael (#teamgogo Support VA) exports the new-agent list from Gogo's my eXp, which auto-populates the map, calendar, and drive and triggers welcome emails — new agents are added within ~1 business day. One PIN per agent unless dual/multi-licensed; no state-wide pin coverage.
- **The Hub** is eXp's new communication platform, built personally by [[Glenn Sanford]] to replace Workplace (originally Facebook-built) and likely Slack — Kristy heard Slack may sunset in 6–8 weeks (unconfirmed). Link: hub.exprealty.com; not yet in Okta or my eXp (grab link directly); mobile app coming to app stores.
- **Hub features:** DMs including multi-person DMs (Slack couldn't), to-do lists/checklists with templates, groups (state, eXp Realty Announcements, eXp University, water cooler, referral), a Discover tab to find/join groups (public = auto-join, private = admin approval), customizable feeds, stories, KCGI on-air, live studio, and a lightbulb "report an issue" button Glenn actively reads. Downside: agents aren't auto-added — you can't DM someone until they've created a profile.
- **Adoption tip ([[Beth Manning]]):** "you don't eat an elephant in one sitting" — schedule one hour a week (e.g. Wednesdays after the TeamGogo meeting) to explore the Hub, ideally with an accountability partner.
- **my eXp** is eXp's main portal for transactions, capping/icon status, payment methods, 1099s, W-9s, and ICA. Dashboard shows units, volume, commission, capping history; the ICON program tab breaks out cap transaction fee, company commission, GCI, closed transactions, cultural commitment (events), and grants.
- **Revenue-share dashboard (Rafael):** org summary now extends to level 15 (useful for large downlines like the Honey Badgers / Fast Forward Movement); new active/inactive/off-boarding filters and name search on the org summary (previously required exporting the whole list). As of last week, co-sponsee/correspondent agents now count in the org summary — explaining any jump in org count. Payout report / periodic overview shows last-paid detail and downloadable past-year transaction reports.
- **Family/organization tree** now shows photos, filters by sponsee/co-sponsee/co-sponsor and active/inactive, and drills level by level. A direct sponsor leaving eXp creates a permanent "blank hole"/spacer that can't be filled or reassigned; TeamGogo informally takes orphaned agents "under our wing" via an internal form/database, treating them like Gogo's frontline.
- **Mira** is eXp's AI assistant (built on ChatGPT, eXp-exclusive), in my eXp and the Hub. Answers natural-language questions about your own personal/downline production, revenue share, and org size (Kristy's = 16 agents), plus toolkit/knowledge-base content (a knowledge base called CODA is being built). Privacy by design — can't pull other agents' numbers. Builders met with Kristy and Rafael last week; Kristy noted "they're not realtors" so they miss what agents need. Downline contact-info retrieval is intended but currently buggy.
- **Onboarding changed:** the old onboarding checklist is gone, replaced by AI video/slideshow trainings (Agent Essentials), which Kristy dislikes — she has asked corporate for a one-page checklist back. New agents get a 4-step quick-start: (1) activate Okta account / eXp email, (2) complete broker/state requirements + join association & local MLS, (3) click their state button for the new per-state website, (4) attend the live startup session. The two flagship platforms eXp is tying everything into are my eXp and the Hub.
- **eXp University** (eXpuniversity.com) houses all state websites and live/recorded trainings, with paths for teams, attractors, new-to-eXp, states, and international. Highlight: ICON Lead Mastery trainings now include downloadable agent playbooks with scripts and tools (social media, leads, cold calling) — Kristy learned this ~a month prior in a training with [[Brian Ellington]].
- **Incoming teams/brokerages** go through standard onboarding plus their team leader's own tools/CRMs/trainings; a Team Resource Guide lives in the toolkits.
