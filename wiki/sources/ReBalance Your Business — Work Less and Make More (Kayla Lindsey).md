# ReBalance Your Business — Work Less and Make More (Kayla Lindsey)

> GoGet'Em Live Training. Kayla Lindsey — Charlotte NC real estate agent and mom running multiple six-figure businesses. Topic: leveraging your time so you can build income without sacrificing your life. Demand so high she's launching a full course on it.

## Key Takeaways

- **The core problem.** "You work really really hard and then you don't live." Making money and having a life are not mutually exclusive — but most agents build one at the expense of the other.
- **Major shifts in business = time leverage.** Kayla made specific shifts that allow her to go to the beach, surf, play with her son — while still running multiple six-figure businesses.
- **Work Less and Make More framework.** Leverage is about systems, people, and processes that generate output without requiring your personal time. The shift: from doing everything to becoming the CEO of your operation.
- **Designed for parents and busy professionals.** Built specifically for those who want to live the life they've been building — not just accumulate money while missing it.
- **Charlotte NC market.** Active agent AND running multiple businesses simultaneously.
- **Course launching.** The demand inside GoGetEm was so high — "Taylor you're a mom, you run multiple six-figure businesses like how are you doing this and still being a mom?" — that she created a full course.
- **Connects to Gogo's own arc.** Gogo: "I truly achieved financial freedom and time freedom and location freedom in my life and I want to be able to share what I did to do that." Kayla embodies the next generation of this teaching.

## Quotes

> "I made some major shifts in my business and these shifts have actually allowed me to leverage my time where I could spend more time with my family doing the things that I want to do such as going to the beach surfing playing with my son — all of the things that we've been working so hard for."

> "Show of virtual hands — how many of you guys have run into that problem where you work really hard which is great but you work really really hard and then you don't live."
