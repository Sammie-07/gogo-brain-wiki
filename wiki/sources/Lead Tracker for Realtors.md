# Lead Tracker for Realtors

> Gogo demonstrates her Lead Tracker — a spreadsheet system she has used since 2015 to manage her pipeline, segment leads by timeline, and visualize potential commission income.

## Key Takeaways

- **In use since 2015** — one of Gogo's foundational business systems.
- **Lead categories (A/B/C system):**
  - **A leads** = 0–3 months to transact
  - **B leads** = 3–6 months to transact
  - **C leads** = 6+ months (nurture long-term)
- **Row turns green** when a lead goes under contract — visual progress tracking.
- **Pipeline commission tracking:** Each lead row includes projected commission, so you always know the potential dollar value in your pipeline.
- **Why it works:** Forces you to look at every lead and assign a realistic timeline. Prevents leads from going cold by default — if it's on the tracker, it gets followed up.
- **Not a CRM replacement:** The Lead Tracker is a simple, manual spreadsheet. It supplements a CRM (Gogo uses KV Core) rather than replacing it — the simplicity is the point.
