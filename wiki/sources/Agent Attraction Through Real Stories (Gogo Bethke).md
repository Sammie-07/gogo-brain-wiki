# Agent Attraction Through Real Stories (Gogo Bethke)

> Short video on a second type of agent attraction content: instead of pitching eXp directly, show agents already in your downline or team succeeding — the "monkey does monkey do" social proof strategy.

## Key Takeaways

- **"Facts tell, stories sell" applied:** Don't pitch brokerage benefits every time. Show agents already in your organization succeeding. Let social proof do the convincing.
- **"Monkey does monkey do"** — Gogo's coined phrase. People watch each other and mirror behavior. Seeing a peer succeed with you is the most powerful recruiting tool.
- **What to show:** Successful agents in your downline, masterminds you're running, agents at your events, team celebrations (top producers, top agent attractors, icons of the month), agents on vacation with you, agents at your home
- **Why Gogo celebrates top producers and icons of the month:** So the audience (other agents) sees real people in her organization succeeding — makes them think "if Jessica succeeded with Gogo, I can too"
- **Content goal:** Plant ideas. Put thoughts into the viewer's head. Let them process. When they're ready, they raise their hand.
- **This is Friday content** — agent attraction day in the GSMC 2026 calendar
- **Mix of content types:** Some direct-to-camera pitches (direct CTA) + some backstage/social proof content (indirect, trust-building). Both are needed.
- **"Business is a spiritual game"** — Gogo's closing line, acknowledging the psychological/invisible side of marketing

## Quotes

> "Sometimes the best way of convincing someone to take a certain action is by showing them that somebody else already did."

> "Monkey does, monkey do. People watch each other and get inspired by each other."

> "All you need to do is put ideas into their head. Let them process it a little bit and when they are ready, they will raise their hand."

> "I know I'm telling you business is a spiritual game."
