# Toby Mathis Tax Strategies and Asset Protection (GoGetEm)

> GoGet'Em guest training by Toby Mathis, Esq. (Anderson Business Advisors) on the combined strategy of tax minimization and asset protection for real estate agents and investors.

## Key Takeaways

- Toby teaches that **tax strategy and asset protection are two sides of the same coin** — you can't fully protect what you haven't structured correctly.
- **Three-layer protection system**:
  1. Entity formation (LLC, S Corp, Trust) — separates personal from business
  2. Asset titling — move assets into entities so they don't appear in your personal name
  3. Insurance — E&O, umbrella policies as last line of defense
- **The asset search test**: before suing someone, attorneys do asset searches. If your name is clean, the case often doesn't proceed.
- Tax strategy for agents: S Corp election to reduce self-employment tax; solo 401K or SEP IRA to shelter income; home office deduction; vehicle deduction.
