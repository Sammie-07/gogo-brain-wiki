# How to Find Filters on Instagram (Laura Kurz)

> Laura's method for finding Instagram filters via the Browse Effects library — the complementary approach to Gogo's story-discovery method. Covers browsing, saving, and applying filters to both Stories and Reels. Part of GoGo Social Media Challenge 2026.

## Key Takeaways

- **Laura's method — Browse Effects library:**
  1. Profile photo → plus sign → camera button
  2. Scroll filter circles all the way to the right until you reach the **magnifying glass with two stars** (last circle)
  3. Tap it → Browse Effects
  4. Categories: Trending, Aesthetic, and more
  5. Tap a filter to preview → tap the **flag icon** (bottom left of filter preview) to save it
  6. Saved filters now appear in your Stories camera circles
- **Saving while browsing:** After tapping a filter to preview, tap anywhere at the top of the screen to return → flag icon appears → tap to save (flag turns gray = saved)
- **Using saved filters in Reels:** + → Reel → camera button → saved filters appear automatically in the reel camera
- **Critical rule:** You can only **add new filters** from the Story camera settings. You **cannot discover or add new filters** from inside the Reel recording screen. But once saved in Stories, all filters appear in Reels too.
- **Filter categories worth browsing:** Trending (most popular right now), Aesthetic
- **Examples mentioned:** C3 VSCO, "pretty huji"

## Quotes

> "When you're in the reel section, you cannot add any new filters. You can only add new filters when you are in the story settings. But once you go into your story settings and you do all the steps I just showed you and you save your filters, they will show up over here when you go to record a reel."
