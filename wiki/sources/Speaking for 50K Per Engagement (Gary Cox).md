# Speaking for $50K Per Engagement (Gary Cox)

> Gary Cox's training on building a speaking business that commands $50K+ per engagement. Covers the sizzle reel, the closer mindset on stage, the role of humor, and how to get booked and re-booked.

## Key Takeaways

- **Sizzle reel is the sales tool:** A professional speaker's sizzle reel functions like a listing presentation. It must show the speaker in action on real stages — not a self-recorded video at home. Investment in a quality sizzle reel pays for itself with one booking.
- **The closer mindset on stage:** Every talk has a transformation arc. The speaker's job is to move the audience from where they are to where they want to be — and every technique (story, data, humor, challenge) serves that close.
- **"Funny is money":** Humor is not optional in elite speaking. Audiences remember the speaker who made them laugh. One well-placed story or callback can anchor the entire keynote in memory. Humor is a delivery skill that can be learned and engineered.
- **Chunking your content:** Break content into digestible "chunks" — typically 3–5 key points, each with a story, a data point, and a takeaway. Audiences cannot retain more than 3–5 big ideas from a 45-minute keynote.
- **Testimonials as social proof:** Testimonials from event planners and past audiences are the second sales tool after the sizzle reel. Collect them systematically after every speaking engagement.
- **Getting re-booked:** The easiest booking is a re-booking. Deliver such a remarkable experience that event planners refer you without being asked. Follow up with a thank-you gift and a note within 48 hours.

## Quotes
