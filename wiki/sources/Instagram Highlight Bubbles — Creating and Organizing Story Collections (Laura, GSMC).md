# Instagram Highlight Bubbles — Creating and Organizing Story Collections (Laura, GSMC)

> Laura's step-by-step tutorial on creating and managing Instagram Highlight bubbles — the persistent story collections that live on your profile below your bio.

## Key Takeaways

### What Highlight Bubbles Are
- Collections of Instagram Stories organized into permanent, named categories on your profile
- Stories normally disappear after 24 hours; Highlights let them live indefinitely
- Appear as circular bubbles below the bio section, each with a title and cover image

### Why They Matter for Real Estate Agents
- Visitors who miss a story can still reference it later
- Useful categories: Home Tours, Testimonials, Speaking Engagements, Buyers FAQ, Sellers FAQ
- A well-organized highlight section shows professionalism and makes information permanently discoverable

### Visual Best Practice
- All highlight covers should **match** — uniform look signals a polished brand (example: Amy Peterson's profile)
- Character limit applies to highlight names — keep titles short
- Example titles: "Home Tours," "Testimonials," "On Stage" (with emoji), "Rise 2025"

### How to CREATE a New Highlight
1. On your profile, tap **"New"** (appears next to your existing highlights)
2. Select the story(ies) you want to include
3. Tap **"Next"** (top right corner)
4. Name the highlight (character limit applies) → tap **"Add"**
5. The new highlight bubble appears on your profile immediately

### How to ADD Old Stories to an Existing Highlight
1. Tap the highlight you want to add to
2. Tap the **three dots** ("More") at the bottom right
3. Tap **"Edit Highlight"**
4. Tap **"Stories"** (far right tab)
5. Browse your full story archive — stories already added show a checkmark (no risk of duplicating)
6. Tap the circle on the story you want to add → checkmark appears
7. Tap **"Done"** (top right)
