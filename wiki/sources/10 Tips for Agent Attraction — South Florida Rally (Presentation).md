# 10 Tips for Agent Attraction — South Florida Regional Rally

> Presentation on the 10-tip framework for building an agent attraction machine at eXp.

## Key Takeaways

Ten-point framework for agent attraction:

1. **Agent Attraction Mindset** — the foundation; you can't attract without believing in the opportunity
2. **Know the Product You're Selling** — understand eXp's full compensation model deeply
3. **Know Who Your Audience Is and Where They Are** — identify the agent avatar and the platforms they use
4. **Know Your Competition** — understand other brokerages; know how eXp compares
5. **How to Attract** — 3-way calls as the primary conversion tool; use existing relationships
6. **Tools for Attraction** — Partner With Me Page, CRM, Grow, Pink Sheet, GoHighLevel
7. **Videos** — video content for agent attraction on social media
8. **Bragging and Wins** — share results publicly; social proof is the magnet
9. **Onboarding System in Place** — have a clear onboarding path so new agents don't feel lost
10. **What Gets Celebrated Gets Duplicated** — publicly celebrate new agents and wins to create momentum
