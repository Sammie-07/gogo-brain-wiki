# The 5 Big Rocks to More Listings & Less Work

> A #teamgogo weekly training guest-taught by [[Knolly Williams]] (1,000+ solo transactions in 10 years, 153 in one year, no cold calls ever; 300+ agent eXp org) and hosted by [[Gogo Bethke]]. Opens with a [[Jason Dupree]] REMLO/mortgage minute. The 5 moves that drive listings, income, and freedom — "stop chasing deals and start attracting them."

## The 5 Big Rocks

1. **Move from default to design.** Most people live by default (career/spouse/city chosen by circumstance) — a Gallup poll of ~150K found ~70% dislike their jobs. Map what you truly "desire and require" and give your subconscious (~88% of behavior) one clear, consistent instruction. "The universe is waiting to take your order" — like a waiter, if you keep changing the order it never comes.
2. **Do the two.** Every day, 2 hours in the "lead-generation bunker" (e.g., 9–11am, undisturbed) doing only three things: (a) generate new seller prospects, (b) follow up with existing prospects, (c) create/design marketing plans. Behind on goals → 3 hrs/day × 6 days ("timeout"); on track → 2 hrs/day × 4 days. This is how he ran 100+ deals/year and never worked Fri–Sun.
3. **Set up your sphere of influence.** NAR 2025: 70%+ of business is referral, yet only ~9–11% of past clients reuse the same agent — because agents stop staying in touch. Fix with a set-it-and-forget-it **18-touch/year** system: monthly e-newsletter + quarterly postcard + call everyone twice a year (~a touch every 3 weeks), ~2 hrs/week, ~free.
4. **Set up a farm — be the "mayor" of your area.** Don't rely on SOI alone (referral-only breaks when the market shifts — "go get your unfair share"). Pick a geographic area and become famous in it so callers are pre-sold. Fundable for free (see below).
5. **Delegate everything.** His listing business is a **46-step process; only 3–5 steps require a license** (inputting into the MLS does not) — he does only those. "Delegate everything — you can" (crosses out "you can"). Own the business, not the job. "Claude just did it in 1.3 seconds."

**Bonus rock (agent attraction):** to reach your first 100 agents, focus on **25 directs**; simplest daily action = **invite 10 people/day** to the ~20–30 min monthly team meeting. Don't sleep on revenue share (his funded 3 years in Puerto Rico).

## Key Takeaways

- **The three buckets every lead comes from:** (1) **Sphere of Influence** (biggest — people who know/like/trust you, including a camera/social audience who "know you" but aren't on your list), (2) **Farm** (a geographic area you dominate), (3) **Niche/specialty** (what you're known for). All ~27 tactics fall into these three. "Three hooks in the water beats one." Distinct from [[Lead Generation#Speed to Lead — Sales System (Jason Matthews)|Jason Matthews's three *pipeline* buckets]] (new/conversion/nurture) — Knolly's three are lead *sources*.
- **"When you list, you last."** *The Millionaire Real Estate Agent* was really a study of the top 100 US agents — ~100% listing-focused. More listing inventory is also the #1 way to get more buyers.
- **10 → 30 deals math.** You don't need 100 deals; 30/year = 3/month. At a $500K average price that's roughly your average sale price as annual income. Example: Jose Velar (FL, eXp, kept his six-figure day job) closed 4 deals for $60K commission last month.
- **"It's the market" is a lie — proven live.** They pulled national existing-home-sales on screen: 2023 ≈ 4.66M, 2024 ≈ 4.68M, 2025 ≈ 4.74M — flat-to-growing. "It's not the market. It's always us. The answer is always you." Paired with Jim Rohn (redistributed wealth returns to the same pockets) and a gazelle-migration abundance analogy.
- **Lead-generate to your personality ([[Communication|DISC]]).** Knolly is a high-I (elevated S/C) who hates cold calls, so he never made one — marketed one-to-many. Take the free DISC test; from the ~27 strategies pick only the 3–4 that fit you.
- **Free farming playbook.** EDDM at ~$0.25/home; 1,000 postcards ≈ $250 mail + ~$50 print = $300 — fully offset by selling **4 sponsor ad spots on the postcard back at $75 each** (landscaper, roofer, pool, painter). Add an "Item of Value" coupon so recipients keep it. Send every other month → quarterly.
- **Open houses as "events," not open houses.** Wine tasting/happy hour; first hour neighbors-only (invite 20–30 nearby homes); sponsors pay (lender/title for wine, a restaurant does a free appetizer for the first 10–15). Neighbors laughing by the time it opens → sellers ask "how do I get this at *my* house?"
- **No listings yet? Hold others' open + circle-prospect.** Ask a listing agent to let you hold their listing open; put out your signs; become the neighborhood face. **Circle-prospect** each listing three times — just listed → under contract ("16 showings, who do you know selling?") → just sold — each with a different script.
- **Delegate the SOI/database setup.** Import contacts from phone, Facebook, old email into a CRM (a VA can); **scrub and tag** everyone (past client, agent, buyer/seller, neighborhood) so you can segment sends.
- **Handwritten notes** — "from the desk of [name]" Etsy cards; 5–10/day off social-media life events, drop a business card (not salesy).
- **Gogo tools plugged:** the [[Niche Finder]] ("second brain" of Gogo's 15 years — 2-min quiz → ideal client, bio, SEO keywords, 90-day plan).
- **[[Jason Dupree]] REMLO minute:** rates ticked back up on renewed uncertainty (Iran) → less buyer competition, so make offers now and refi in 12–24 months; bridge loans remove the home-sale contingency; a standout non-front-loaded-interest loan product (a recent $1.1M deal projected to save ~$900K in interest despite a higher rate); REMLO licensed in all 50 states day one (~463 REMLOs; shout-outs Macy and Melissa Albert).
