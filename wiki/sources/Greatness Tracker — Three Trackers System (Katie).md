# Greatness Tracker — Three Trackers System (Katie)

> Katie — lender with Union Home Mortgage, 12-year collaborator with Gogo Bethke — teaches GoGet'Em members the expanded three-tracker system: Greatness Tracker, Leak Tracker, and Money Tracker.

## Key Takeaways

- **Katie's relationship to Gogo:** 12+ years. She originated the Greatness Tracker concept that Gogo has used and taught throughout her career. The original version didn't include social media — Katie has updated it over time.
- **The three trackers:**
  1. **Greatness Tracker** — Daily/weekly activity log used for 90 days to discover where your leads come from most naturally. Finds your "greatness" — the activity that produces leads with the least friction and most enjoyment. "If you do what you love, you never work a day in your life."
  2. **Leak Tracker** — A spreadsheet for every lead entering your pipeline: Where did they come from? Buyer or seller? Price range? Pre-approved? Timeline? This reveals which lead sources actually convert, not just which ones generate volume.
  3. **Money Tracker** — Revenue in (commissions), lead costs (if any), and service costs (TC, photography, etc.). Reveals net profitability per transaction and per lead source.
- **The 90-day revelation:** After 90 days, you know exactly which activities produce your best leads. Double down on those — not on what works for someone else.
- **Why this matters:** Most agents track transactions but not upstream activities. You cannot optimize what you don't measure.
- **Different from the existing Greatness Tracker source:** The prior "The Greatness Tracker" source covered Gogo's version (15 face-to-face/week, 5 breaking bread/week, 60 phone calls/week). This session from Katie introduces the full three-tracker system with quantitative lead tracking and money management.
- **Katie as lender:** She teaches real estate agents these systems as a value-add service. A referral partner strategy — she trains your agents, and they send you buyers.
