# The Circle Sales Page (gogobethke.com/thecircle)

> Public-facing sales page for The Circle ⭕. Covers positioning, the four freedoms framework, full program experience breakdown, ideal client filter, testimonials, FAQ, and application process. Not real estate-specific — open to entrepreneurs across all industries.

## Key Takeaways

- **Tagline:** "You Built the Income. Now Build the Freedom."
- **Who it's for:** Entrepreneurs making consistent 6-figure income who are burnt out, lack passive income, and whose business cannot run without them. Not real-estate-specific — any industry.
- **Who it's NOT for:** People just starting out, people who want done-for-you, people who won't implement, people wanting leads handed to them.
- **Size:** 20 total seats. Opens once per year. Applications close when full. New seats only open when a member completes their term.
- **Duration:** 12 months.
- **Application:** By application only. Reviewed personally by Gogo. Investment discussed on Clarity Call (not listed publicly).
- **Calls:** 2 to 4 group coaching calls per month guaranteed. Most months it is 4.
- **WhatsApp:** Every member gets their own private WhatsApp chat with Gogo and her team for day-to-day questions and real-time feedback between calls.
- **Monthly 1:1 team session:** 1 hour per month with Gogo's expert team of 12 VAs covering tech, systems, and scaling. Implementation support, not just advice.
- **Recordings:** Every session recorded and available in the membership area for 2 weeks after each call.
- **No cancellation:** Committing for the full 12 months. No exceptions.
- **No +1s:** Program is for individuals only. Spouses, partners, and team members cannot join calls — they must apply separately.
- **VA access included** (called out in top-level stats).

## The Four Freedoms Framework

The core promise of The Circle, organized as four outcomes:

1. **Location Freedom** — Build systems that run whether you are in Tampa or Tuscany. Your business works. You live.
2. **Time Freedom** — Stop being the bottleneck. Build a real team (VAs, local support, right structure) so your calendar belongs to you again.
3. **Financial Freedom** — Create passive income streams. Build digital products, investment structures, and income that pays you whether you show up or not.
4. **CEO Mindset Freedom** — Stop running from survival mode. Step into the CEO identity: trust systems, lead your team, think like an owner.

## The 7 Circle Experience Pillars

1. **2 to 4 Group Coaching Calls Per Month** — Minimum guaranteed 2, most months 4. Every call actionable. You leave knowing exactly what to do next.
2. **Direct WhatsApp Access** — Private chat with Gogo and her team. Real-time feedback. Never waiting until the next call.
3. **1 Hour Monthly with Expert Team** — Full access to team of 12 VAs for tech, systems, and scaling. Implementation support.
4. **Systems, Automation & Tech Setup** — Software, apps, AI tools, trackers. Infrastructure built to run without you.
5. **Team Building Support** — Hiring VAs, structuring local and personal assistants. Full support system for business and life.
6. **Passive Income Architecture** — Digital products, investment structures, wealth-building strategies.
7. **12-Month CEO Identity Work** — Mindset, beliefs, habits, identity shifts. Called "infrastructure" by Gogo.

## The 6 Pain Points (Ideal Client Mirror)

Used on the page to qualify applicants emotionally:
1. Vacationing with laptop — physically elsewhere but mentally still at desk
2. Only makes money when working — no passive income or residual
3. Business cannot run without them — every decision comes back to them
4. Successful and exhausted — business is running them, not the other way around
5. Money not working for them — earning but not building wealth
6. Hit a ceiling — what got them here will not get them there
