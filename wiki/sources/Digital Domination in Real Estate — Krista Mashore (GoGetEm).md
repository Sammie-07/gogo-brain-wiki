# Digital Domination in Real Estate — Krista Mashore (GoGetEm)

> GoGet'Em guest training — Krista Mashore (2,300+ homes sold, top 1% agent, $45M+ online contracted sales in 4.5 years) on building a digital brand, persuasion psychology, and online lead generation.

## Key Takeaways

### Krista Mashore's Credentials
- 2,300+ homes sold; top 1% agent
- $45M+ in online contracted sales in 4.5 years
- Average $1M/month in sales for 36 consecutive months
- Gogo context: they entered Tony Robbins / Dean Graziosi challenge together — Krista won second place the prior year; that year they learned they'd meet Tony Robbins in April.

### Persuasion vs. Convincing
- **Nobody wants to be convinced** — people want to be persuaded.
- Persuasion = "ethically helping people say yes to themselves."
- You are always persuading: your kids, your spouse, your clients.
- "Selling is service. Every object in your life was sold."
- Your job: help clients say yes to their own goals through your guidance.

### The Success Cycle (Tony Robbins framework — applied to real estate)
- Unlimited potential → effective action → results → more belief → more action
- **Visualize results in advance from the outcome you want** — belief precedes effective action.
- Momentum starts with belief; the key to more results is momentum.

### Three Beliefs Required for a Sale
1. The client must believe in the **product** (the home/investment)
2. The client must believe in **you** (your expertise and trustworthiness)
3. The client must believe in **themselves** (that they can and deserve to do this)

### Digital Brand Building
- Build your brand to do the persuasion work before you're in the room.
- Video is the highest-trust medium — seeing you speak makes you feel known.
- Online presence = persistent, scalable persuasion.
