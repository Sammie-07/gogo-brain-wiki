# How to Unlock the Power of Social Media

> GoGet'Em Webinars session. Gogo reveals how social media has been her exclusive lead source — no cold calling, no Zillow, no door knocking. Covers platform strategy and team-based content distribution. Full training gated at gogetemwebinars.com.

## Key Takeaways

- **Social media is her only lead source.** *"Everything that I have done is through the power of social media. I do not cold call, I do not door knock, I do not buy leads from Zillow and Trulia. I do everything in my own power by building my own brand."*
- **At least 2 posts per day.** Minimum posting cadence — not a suggestion.
- **Platform workflow:** Gogo creates on Instagram. Her virtual assistants then push the content to all other platforms. Until you can afford VAs, you are the distribution team.
- **Platforms she uses:** Facebook Business page, Instagram Business page, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube. Same username everywhere (gogosrealestate on all).
- **"You have to be everywhere"** — even if you're doing it yourself at first. Consistency across platforms multiplies reach.
- **The tracker overlay:** Every lead source gets tracked to understand "where is your greatness." After enough data, you can see which channels produce — and double down.
