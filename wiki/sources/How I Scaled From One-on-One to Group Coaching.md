# How I Scaled From One-on-One to Group Coaching and Built 3 Seven-Figure Programs

> Interview with Arian Beyzaei. Gogo's most detailed public career narrative — from broke agent in 2011 to 3 Two Comma Club awards. Covers the full arc of how she built three separate coaching programs.

## Key Takeaways

**Career timeline:**
- **2003**: Moved to the US from Romania at age 21. No English fluency (3rd language), no connections, no college education.
- **2011**: Got real estate license. First year: $16,000. Doubled income roughly each year after.
- **Year 4+**: Kids went to school full-time → more hours → income accelerated.
- Started on **Facebook** (Gogo's Real Estate page) to find strangers, then moved to **Instagram** as primary platform for agent-to-agent conversation.
- **~2018/2019**: Switched to eXp Realty (~8th year of career). Built Team Go downline → revenue share became biggest income source.
- **3.5 years ago from 2025 recording**: Stopped selling real estate personally.
- **Not made less than $1M/year since 2019.**

**Three Two Comma Club awards (ClickFunnels — funnels with $1M+ in sales):**
1. **Gogo's Bootcamp Social Media** — started at ~$400, scaled to $7,000 for the "done with you" version.
2. **Go Boot Camp Agent Attraction** — built after becoming #1 female agent attractor at eXp.
3. **GoGet'Em Community** — the ongoing community platform.

**Team Go stats (as of this recording):**
- 1,660 agents
- $2.7B in sales year-to-date (2024), projecting $3.2–3.5B by year end.

**Email database:**
- ~25,000 email addresses
- One email/day = ~8.4M emails/year
- Built list by giving free downloadables in exchange for email address.

**Coaching program evolution:**
- Start: 1:1 coaching → hits ceiling (only 24 hrs, can't scale).
- Better: group cohort. Five 1:1 clients → invite to group call → one hour of Gogo's time instead of five, same total revenue.
- Current commitment length: **6 months** (tried 12 months, felt too long).
- **The Circle:** max 20 people, once/week meetings. Must qualify: 6-figure minimum income, average member income ~$600k. Comment "circle" on her Instagram to apply.
- Today she has max 4 coaching calls/day (beyond that she's "ready to pull my hair out").

**Social media for coaches:**
- "If you don't exist on social media, you don't exist."
- Organic social = close rate of ~9.5/10 because followers already trust you before the call.
- Paid leads = might close 1/40 because trust isn't built yet.
- ChatGPT workflow: define target audience → ask for ideal client profile + content plan → 90-day post calendar in chronological order → plug into scheduling tool.
