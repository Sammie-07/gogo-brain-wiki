# Pioneering In Your Assignment — GoGet'Em Guest (Louise)

> GoGet'Em guest training by Louise on identifying your assignment, cultivating entrepreneurial (pioneer) mindset, and executing ideas with alignment rather than fear.

## Key Takeaways

- **Your assignment** = what you've labored through, what trauma you've overcome and haven't given up on. In that place, you have **authority**. You've birthed something for other people.
- Entrepreneurial mindset = pioneer mindset. Employee mindset and entrepreneurship are incompatible — the entrepreneur cannot survive with employee thinking.
- **Alignment** = coming out of agreement with doubt, shame, and fear. We all feel these — the question is how much attention you give them.
- Being a pioneer means there's no one beside you — you're a spearhead, going first. Like David training in the wilderness before facing Goliath.
- Many creatives stay stuck **in the idea** — watering too many fields, never the one field in front of them that will bear harvest.
- Execution is a learnable skill, especially for emotional/creative types. It can be done in flow, with joy — not just grinding.
- When emotions and thought patterns are misaligned → financial frustration, brand stagnation, no traction.

## Quotes

> "In that place, you have authority. In that place, you have authority and you have birthed something for other people."

> "Alignment is coming out of agreement with doubt, shame, fear."

> "The world is waiting for you."
