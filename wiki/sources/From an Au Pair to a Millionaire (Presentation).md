# From an Au Pair to a Millionaire

> Slide deck of Gogo's core origin story and philosophical framework. Presented as "the 7 years at home — what I learned at home and what I learned in America." One of her most-used keynote presentations.

## Key Takeaways

**Origin Story:**
- Grew up in Transylvania (Romania); spoke Hungarian
- Came to U.S. as an au pair
- Started soul-searching at ~age 29: Oprah's Supersoul Sunday, Masterclass, Dr. Wayne Dyer, Tony Robbins, books, events
- Principle: always have a notebook; makeup time and learning time can overlap
- Background formed by communism: "If I hadn't lived through communism, I wouldn't have anything to compare a good life to. There's something good in every bad situation."

**Core Principles and Stories:**
- **The Ship Story:** A ship doesn't sink because it's surrounded by water — it sinks when water gets inside. "Be careful who you let into your life."
- **The Elephant Story:** Elephant tied to a stake as a baby can't break free — grows up believing it still can't, even though it can. You accepted limits as a child that no longer exist.
- **The Airplane Story:** Referenced but not described (live story)
- **The Broke Friend:** "Never ask a broke friend how to make millions. They'll tell you it's impossible because they don't know how to do it."
- **The Elderly Advice:** "Respect them, but don't follow all their advice. What couldn't be done back then can be done today."
- **Inner Voice:** "If your inner voice tells you to cross to the other side of the street — do it."
- **"Break the rule, not the law. It's better to ask for forgiveness than permission."**
- **Henry Ford:** "Whether you think you can do it, or you think you can't — you're right."
- **George Lucas:** "We are all living in cages with the door wide open." (Who put you there?)
- **Rolex Story:** Referenced; about knowing what you want (live story)
- **Airport Story:** Referenced; about showing up (live story)
- **"Once you see it, you can't unsee it."** — Used for business opportunity recognition

**Mindset Principles:**
- No matter how fast you run east, you'll never see a sunset — be careful about which goals you set
- "If everyone were like you, then you would be average."
- "The best marketing wins — it's important who you know, but more important who knows you."
- "If you want to have what the Top 1% has, you have to do what 99% is not willing to."
- "You have to learn to say NO. Any other questions?"
- "Good is the enemy of great — spend your time on great ideas, not just good ones."
- "You don't have to work harder, you have to work smarter." — Billionaires on yachts earn a billion times more with less physical effort
- "An hour of my time is an hour of my life." — Time is not renewable
- **John D. Rockefeller:** "I would rather earn 1% of 100 people's efforts than 100% of my own."
- **Tony Gaskins:** "If you don't build your own dreams, someone will hire you to build theirs."
- "How cheap is my mood?" — Don't let small things destroy your state
- "If you don't have questions, you don't get answers."
- "A NO is a MAYBE." — Rejection is not final
- "You don't have a TIME problem. You have a PRIORITY problem."
- "Business moves with the speed of TRUST."
- "Begin with the end in mind."
- "People don't do what you say. People do what you do."
- "When you allow them to get to know you, they will love you. When they love you, they'll trust you. When they trust you, they'll do or buy anything you say or sell."
- "I'm looking for the people who are looking for me."
- **Zig Ziglar:** "You can have everything in life you want, if you will just help other people get what they want."
- Final: "It's your own life, you can FUCK it up however you want to."
