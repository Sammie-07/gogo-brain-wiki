# The Power of Referrals (Brandon Barnum)

> Gogopreneur episode with Brandon Barnum — CEO of HOA.com, co-author of *Raving Referrals for Real Estate Agents* (features Gogo on pp. 231 and 265). Framework for systematically generating referral business.

## Key Takeaways

- **Brandon Barnum:** CEO of HOA.com; co-author of *Raving Referrals for Real Estate Agents* with Gogo Bethke as a featured contributor (pp. 231, 265).
- **HOA.com:** Platform serving the 375,000 HOAs in the US. 53% of US homeowners live in an HOA — massive underserved database.
- **Certified HOA Specialist:** Program for agents to differentiate by becoming the go-to expert for HOA-governed properties.
- **Income growth:** Brandon grew from $20K to $200K in 18 months through systematized referrals.
- **Shalmani Method — 5 Ps:**
  1. **People** — who you know
  2. **Purpose** — why they should trust you
  3. **Passion** — what drives you
  4. **Personality** — how you show up
  5. **Prosperity** — the mutual benefit of referring
- **Art of the Ask — 3 steps:**
  1. **Set the stage** — prime the conversation ("I'm going to ask you something in a moment")
  2. **Listen for referral triggers** — signals someone knows a buyer/seller (moving mentions, life changes)
  3. **Ask in peak referral state** — right after delivering great news or a win, not randomly
- **Expertise Publishing:** Platform mentioned for agents/professionals building a book-based brand.
- **AgentReferralQuiz.com:** Resource for agents to assess their referral readiness.
- **Gogo's intentional broken English:** Writing her book in her natural voice — non-native English speaker's authenticity is the brand, not a liability.
- **Gogo's schedule context:** Works Monday–Wednesday, 10am–4pm. Approximately 90% passive income. Referenced as outcome of systematized business.

## Quotes
