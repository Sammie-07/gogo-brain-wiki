# How to Maximize Productivity and Balance Your Time Effectively

> GoGet'Em Community session on calendar mastery, team communication, and squeezing maximum output from a limited workweek.

## Key Takeaways

- **"Time is LIFE."** More urgent than "time is money" — it's the one resource you can never replace.
- **Google Calendar discipline.** Color-coordinate everything. Live by it. Check the next day every night before bed.
- **"703 hours in a 40-hour week"** metaphor — by delegating and automating, you effectively multiply your hours.
- **Calendly** for scheduling — use pre-created meeting types. Never do scheduling manually.
- **WhatsApp group chats** for team communication (separate chats per business/department).
- **2 assistants in every meeting** — so notes, follow-ups, and action items are covered without Gogo having to track them.
- **Pre-scheduled recurring meetings** — e.g., Tuesday 11am podcasts are fixed on the calendar permanently, removing decision fatigue.
- **Manage direct access to Gogo** — not everyone gets her number or can book her directly. Assistants gatekeep.
- Full session is gated (GoGet'Em Community membership required).

## Quotes

> "Time is LIFE. We all have the same 24 hours in a day, but how we spend it separates the dreamers from the doers."
