# I Send One Email a Day

> Very short clip. [[Gogo Bethke]] describes her daily email newsletter practice and her [[GoGet'Em Community]] community.

## Key Takeaways

- Gogo sends **one email every day** — industry news, what she's working on, or books she's reading
- The newsletter community is called **[[GoGet'Em Community]]**
- Highly regarded — attendees and followers describe reading every edition
- To subscribe: comment "email" on her Instagram posts
- Format is personal and consistent — it reads like a dispatch from her day, not a marketing blast

## Quotes

> "I send one email a day of what's going on in the industry, what I'm working on, or what books I am reading."

*From a community member:* "She has some of the best newsletters I receive. If you want valuable newsletters, it is one of the best things that you can sign up for. I get it almost every day and I read every single one of them."
