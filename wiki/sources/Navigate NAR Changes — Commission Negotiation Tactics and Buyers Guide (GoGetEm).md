# Navigate NAR Changes — Commission Negotiation Tactics and Buyers Guide (GoGetEm)

> GoGet'Em training on the NAR settlement changes and how agents should adapt — commission negotiation scripts, buyer representation agreements, and positioning in a post-settlement market.

## Key Takeaways

- Context: NAR (National Association of Realtors) settlement in 2024 changed how buyer's agent compensation is handled — agents can no longer assume the seller pays the buyer's agent in MLS listings.
- Agents must now proactively **negotiate their compensation** with buyers before showing homes — requiring a signed buyer representation agreement.
- The session was attended by agents preparing for the August 2024 implementation deadline.
- **Buyer representation agreements**: agents must have written agreements specifying their compensation before showing property.
- **Commission negotiation tactics**: how to explain your value to buyers, what to say when they push back on your commission, how to position the conversation as a service discussion.
- eXpcon Miami in October 2024 was referenced — the speaker was preparing to attend.
- The "Buyers' Guide" component: providing buyers a transparent document explaining how real estate commissions work in the new environment.

## Quotes

> "The agents who will thrive through these changes are the ones who've been building their brand, building their value, and can articulate why they're worth being paid."
