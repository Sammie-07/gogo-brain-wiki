# Social Media Mastery Course — Weeks 1 & 2 (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo's paid 4-week intensive social media course. ~23–24 attendees with lifetime dashboard access. Week 1 (70 slides): mindset foundation before tactics. Week 2: social media philosophy, platform strategy, and preview of tracking (Week 3) and Q&A (Week 4).

## Key Takeaways

### Week 1 — Mindset First

- **"Get the 6 inches right first."** None of the trackers, templates, or tactics matter until your mindset is dialed in — the 6 inches between your ears.
- Gogo's personal origin story: grew up in Romania, moved out at age 14. Built everything from scratch. Informs her relentless work ethic and financial urgency.
- **Core motivational principles Gogo opens with:**
  - "Build your dreams or someone will hire you to build theirs."
  - "If you don't find a way to make money while you sleep, you will work until you die."
- **GoHighLevel (GHL) as the operating system:** Introduced in Week 1. GHL "buckets" = one sub-account per business line (GoGet'Em, agent attraction, Michigan team, social media quotes, etc.). ~$200/month for the full platform. Replaces having multiple CRMs and fragmented systems.
- **Delegation model for the course content:** Gogo instructs agents to send the recording to their VA. VA logs in and watches it so the agent doesn't have to re-explain context. The agent focuses on strategy; the VA handles execution.

### Week 2 — Social Media Philosophy

- Review and reinforcement of Week 1 mindset principles before diving into platform tactics.
- **Privacy philosophy:** "You only see what I let you see." Gogo curates what she shares publicly — selective transparency, not total openness.
- Preview of Week 3 (tracking and numbers) and Week 4 (pure Q&A — "I answer whatever you ask").
- Social media works as an amplifier of who you already are, not a mask. Authenticity is the strategy.
