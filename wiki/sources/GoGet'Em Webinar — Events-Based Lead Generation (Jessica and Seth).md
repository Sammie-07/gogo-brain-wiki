# GoGet'Em Webinar — Events-Based Lead Generation (Jessica and Seth)

> Two eXp agents — Jessica (Bay Area, 2x ICON, $30M/year) and Seth (Charleston SC, multi-6-figure in 2.5 years) — share how they built their real estate businesses entirely through community events, with zero paid leads.

## Key Takeaways

- **Jessica's background:** Event management and hospitality career before real estate. Got license in 2015 while pregnant, brand new to the Bay Area — no existing sphere, no cold call appetite, very limited time as a new mom.
- **The decision:** "How do I meet the most amount of people in the amount of time I had available?" Events were the answer.
- **Results (Jessica):** 2x ICON at eXp (top 1% of the company), $30M+ closed annually, all from sphere and community events. Zero paid leads, zero advertising.
- **Seth's context:** Charleston SC. 2.5 years in business. Multiple six figures. Generates all leads through local events.
- **Why events work:**
  - Warm leads — people who already feel like they know you before they need a realtor
  - Repeat clients who stay loyal over time (not one-time Zillow leads)
  - Community involvement creates organic referral loops
  - Efficient for time-constrained agents — plant seeds for future harvest
- **Event strategy:** Get deeply involved in your community. The relationship precedes the transaction — often by months or years.
- **Starting from scratch:** Being new to an area with no sphere is actually an advantage for event-based business — you're forced to meet everyone and events are the most efficient way.
- **The anti-grind model:** You don't need to door knock, cold call, or "grind" in the traditional sense. Events create enough volume through authentic relationship-building.
