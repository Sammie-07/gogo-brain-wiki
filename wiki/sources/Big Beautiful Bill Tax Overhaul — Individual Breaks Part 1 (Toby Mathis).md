# Big Beautiful Bill Tax Overhaul — Individual Breaks Part 1 (Toby Mathis)

> Toby Mathis, Esq. and former IRS lead trial attorney Scott Estill, Esq. break down the "One Big Beautiful Bill Act" — a 1,000+ page tax reform signed into law July 4, 2025. Part 1 covers individual taxpayer breaks.

## Key Takeaways

- The One Big Beautiful Bill Act is a sweeping, 1,000+ page tax reform package signed into law on July 4, 2025.
- **Individual tax breaks** are the focus of Part 1 — significant provisions affecting personal income taxes.
- Toby Mathis and Scott Estill (former IRS lead trial attorney) co-host the breakdown — dual perspective of tax attorney and IRS insider.
- Key individual provisions include changes to standard deductions, personal exemptions, and income tax brackets.
- Real estate investors and entrepreneurs have specific provisions that affect pass-through entities and individual returns.
- Understanding the bill is essential for 2025-2026 tax planning — many provisions are retroactive or take effect immediately.
