# Biz Mastery Week 2 — Running Multiple Companies

> Week 2 of Biz Mastery. Focused on how Gogo runs multiple companies while maintaining a 3-day work week. Covers Google Calendar mastery, meeting structure, Calendly, and communication systems.

## Key Takeaways

**703-Hour Week Explained:**
- Each of Gogo's VAs works 40 hrs/week
- Gogo works ~24 hrs/week (Mon-Wed, 8 hrs/day)
- Total: 14 VAs × 40 hrs + 24 hrs = 584 hrs of work output per week (previously referenced as 703 hrs in older versions)
- Average person works 2,080 hrs/year; Gogo's team gets 10.8x more done

**Google Calendar System:**
- Gogo lives by her Google Calendar; Lindsy (assistant) manages it in real time
- Color codes: Red = work, Green = trips/events, Blue = family, Purple = #teamgogo birthdays
- Two assistants attend every meeting (Kristy as Director of Operations + Lindsy as personal assistant)
- Nobody gets direct access to Gogo — everything routes through assistants

**Weekly Schedule:**
- Monday: back-to-back meetings for all companies (Leadership meeting 9-9:45am)
- Tuesday: 7:30-9:30 coaching calls → 10am GRET manager → 11am podcast → 12pm GoGopreneur editing
- Wednesday: 7:30-9:30 coaching → noon catch-up → 1pm #teamgogo → 8pm Wine Not
- Thursday-Sunday: OFF (unless working ON the business, not IN it — can schedule Thursdays only for that)

**Meeting Types (Full Roster):**
| Meeting | Attendees | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Leadership Meeting | Leaders only | Review VA reports, numbers, plans |
| GRET Meeting | RE team (Kristy, Randy, Priscila, Yusaf, ISA, agents) | Leads and referrals spreadsheet |
| #teamgogo Meeting | Full team | Each VA reports 5 minutes on their side; issues and solutions |
| Bootcamp Meeting | Kristy, Jonathan (tech), Ellen (design), Shreed (website), Carol (email/social) | Bootcamp content, improvements, issues |
| GoGopreneur Meeting | Kristy, Natalia, Shreed, Ellen | TV show/podcast schedule, guests |
| GoGet'Em Meeting | Full team | Monthly schedule, trainings, guest presenters, platform issues |
| Email Meeting | Carol + Priscila | Plan and build weekly emails |
| Catch-Up Meeting | Everyone | Wednesday noon check-in for any issues |
| Huddle Meeting | Kristy, Lindsy, Gogo | Daily quick meeting on pressing subjects, emails, schedules |

**WhatsApp Group Chats:**
- One WhatsApp group per company for overseas team communication
- Primary async communication channel between meetings

**Calendly Setup:**
- Free version works; paid ($10/month) adds: unlimited event types, multiple calendars, Stripe/PayPal integration, group events, workflows/reminders
- Setup: add profile pic, link Zoom, integrate Google Calendar
- Events: name, duration, location, description, invitee settings, available hours, custom link, questionnaire questions, automatic reminders
- Key settings: 24hr/48hr scheduling window, buffer time between meetings

**Calendly Pro Tips:**
- Customize the link (calendly.com/yourname/yourservice)
- Add questionnaire questions for pre-meeting info gathering
- Set automatic calendar invitations, email reminders, and text reminders
- Paid version allows charging for meetings

**Communication Style:**
- "Why I don't let people have direct access to me" — protected access = protected time
- "Why I have 2 assistants go to every meeting with me" — one takes notes, one manages follow-up
- Zoom meeting etiquette: minimize distractions, avoid multitasking, BRB in comments, be on time, camera on, prepare agenda, breaks for long meetings

**Phone Shortcut Hack:**
- Settings → General → Keyboard → Text Replacement to save frequently-typed phrases as shortcuts (auto-expand trick)

## Quotes

> "I live by my Google Calendar."

> "Thursday to Sunday I don't work. But if I get meetings that I can't fit during the week, I'll put it on a Thursday — as long as I'm working ON my business, not IN my business."
