# AI For Realtors — How to Use AI to Close 1 Deal Every Month

> Live training from Gogo Bethke on using social media and AI automation to generate one real estate deal per month. Companion to [[How Realtors Can Use AI to Generate Leads (Live Training)]]. Covers ideal client targeting, KV Core IDX lead capture, ManyChat automation, and personal brand building.

## Key Takeaways

- **The math:** 1 deal/month × $350K avg price × 3% commission = $10,500/month = $126,000/year. Presented as the "baseline" — achievable with these systems.
- **Attract your ideal client:** Define exactly who you want to work with (not just price point — personality, lifestyle, interests). Content built around your ideal client attracts that client and repels everyone else.
- **"If you talk to everyone, you convert no one":** Specificity in targeting is the difference between noise and conversion.
- **Build personal brand 24/7:** Social media works even while you sleep. Digital products, automations, and content compound around the clock.
- **Consistency at front end + automations at back end:** The two-part system. Content generates leads; automations follow up and convert.
- **KV Core IDX lead capture:**
  - Find a listing matching your ideal client's wants → copy the "direct link"
  - Share on social media → visitors see 3 free photos → must log in (Facebook or email) to see more → lead captured automatically
  - KV Core cross-references email against Facebook/LinkedIn/Google → provides profile link for personal outreach
- **Selfie video follow-up:** "Hey ABC123, I see you looked at 20 questions. I can personally answer them in 5 minutes. Call me back." Creates urgency and personal connection.
- **5-minute rule + 78% stat:** 78% of buyers go with the first agent to contact them. Five minutes is the window.
- **Personal brand principles:**
  - Be the same person online and in person
  - Only promote what you genuinely use and love — never accept paid promotion for things you don't believe in
  - Your brand is what people say about you when you're not in the room
- **GoGet'Em Community tech support:** Tuesdays and Wednesdays at 3pm ET (this recording; other recordings say Thursdays).
- **14-person team; $60/month:** Community size and price at time of recording (pricing has since been updated).
