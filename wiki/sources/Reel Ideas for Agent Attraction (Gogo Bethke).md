# Reel Ideas for Agent Attraction (Gogo Bethke)

> Friday is agent attraction day in the GSMC 2026 weekly content calendar. This video gives 10 reel ideas aimed at recruiting agents to a downline or team, explains why visual/number-comparison content works best, and introduces the "partner" ManyChat keyword CTA.

## Key Takeaways

- **Friday = agent attraction content** in the weekly posting schedule
- Goal: recruit agents to your downline at eXp, LPT, Real, or your brokerage
- **10 Agent Attraction Reel Ideas:**
  1. How to tell your brokerage is actually costing you money
  2. Brokerage comparison: same deals at eXp vs. current brokerage — show the dollar difference (visual, whiteboard or iPad screen share)
  3. Most agents don't know their real cost per closing (photos, signs, TC, cost of doing business)
  4. Most agents don't know what their brokerage actually provides — they're there out of habit
  5. How to tell if your split is helping or hurting you
  6. Why your production plateau isn't a motivation problem
  7. The standard I hold my agents to and why (about your local team/downline)
  8. How top agents decide where to hang their license
  9. If an agent brings me this problem, here's how I handle it
  10. The question every agent should ask before their next year
- **Best format**: Comparison/numbers videos — whiteboard or iPad screen share showing actual dollar figures; visual learners need to *see* the math to believe it
- **ChatGPT prompt**: "Every Friday I do agent attractions. I'm growing a downline at [brokerage]. Give me 12 ideas for the next quarter."
- **CTA**: "partner" keyword — must be set up in ManyChat before publishing (Sam covers setup in next video)
- **Weekly calendar confirmed**: Mon=sellers, Wed=buyers, Fri=agent attraction, Sun=emotional/spiritual
- Gogo has made millions in revenue share through consistent Friday agent attraction content
