# How Do You Know It Is Time to Hire a Virtual Assistant?

> Gogopreneur podcast episode — a full step-by-step VA hiring guide. Originally a GoGet'Em Community training, released publicly. Paired with the red light / green light exercise.

## Key Takeaways

- **The income ceiling.** Without help, Gogo's max income was ~$420k/year. After building her VA team: never less than 7 figures annually.
- **Hourly rate formula.** All income last year ÷ 2080 = hourly rate. This number is your filter: never do anything below it.
  - Example on-screen: $250,000 ÷ 2080 = **$120.19/hr**.
  - Sticky note it everywhere — on the washer, kitchen sink, desk. Every task you see becomes "is this worth $120/hr of my time?"
- **The math:** Hiring a house assistant at $25/hr vs. staying in your lane at $120/hr. Running laundry doesn't save $25 — it costs you $120 (the earning you gave up).
- **Virtual assistant vs. in-person assistant:**
  - VA (online): does anything with Wi-Fi — email, calendar, social media, graphics, data entry. Can't physically act in your location.
  - In-person assistant: physically present, can run errands, pick up yard signs, buy closing gifts, manage household.
- **VA hourly rates:**
  - Asian countries (India, Pakistan): ~$3–4/hr
  - South America (Brazil, Argentina): ~$5–6/hr
  - US in-person assistant: ~$18–30/hr
- **Gogo's VA team:** 14 VAs total. Most in Brazil; 2 in India, 1 in Pakistan. US staff: Gogo herself, Dwayne (husband), Kristy (Director of Operations), Mandy (house manager). Lindsay = admin assistant (Brazil, 2 years). Natalia = GoGet'Em Community + Gogopreneur show. Raphael = tech/websites/funnels. Carol = email/copywriting. Ellen = design.
- **Red light / green light for VA task assignment:**
  - Do the exercise for a full quarter to capture daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly tasks.
  - Every red-light item = VA or in-person assistant job description.
  - Online red lights: emails, social media posts/scheduling, DMs/comments, appointment setting, graphics/flyers, data entry.
  - Online green lights: coaching calls, deal negotiations, your own photos/videos, in-person relationships.
  - Physical red lights: yard signs, closing gifts, grocery shopping, office organization, event setup/breakdown.
  - Physical green lights: speaking engagements, board meetings, hosting events in person.
- **Ego is not your amigo.** Don't let ego answer the red/green question. It will justify doing everything yourself to "save $25." Your brain should answer it, not your pride.
- **We Clone You** (wecloneyou.com) — VA placement service. If you don't want to find/hire/train yourself, they do it. Gogo's link offers $1,000 off (first VA effectively free).
- **Promise to yourself:** Only do money-making activities. Assistants don't cost you money — they make you more money by freeing your time.
