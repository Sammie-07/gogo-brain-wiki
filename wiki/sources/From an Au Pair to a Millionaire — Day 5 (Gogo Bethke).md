# From an Au Pair to a Millionaire — Day 5 (Gogo Bethke)

> GoGet'Em 5-Day Live, Day 5. Gogo's most personal session — sharing her journey from au pair to millionaire. She prayed over all 280 members by name before the call. Deeply emotional. Core message: hardships are lessons not yet learned; she shares hers so members don't have to live them.

## Key Takeaways

- **Prayed over all 280 members by name.** Wrote each out by hand before the call. "I don't know if you can see that — there's 280 members and I hand wrote every single one of your names and I prayed over you and I prayed over your business."
- **Hardships are unlearned lessons.** "I look at hardship in our life that there are lessons that we just haven't learned yet and we are going to keep getting them until we learn them." Her mission: share hers so members can learn without suffering through them.
- **"Done chasing."** Gogo reached her own goals and describes a shift to calmness rather than pursuit. "I'm done chasing and I feel like I have reached my goals... nothing makes me happier than seeing you guys reach your goals."
- **Hungarian immigrant identity.** Has a Catalina from her hometown on the call who lives an hour away in Florida — they've never met in person. Asked how to say "selfless" in Hungarian before the call.
- **Gratitude for parents.** "As much as I went through in life, in the same time I feel so thankful for my parents for being so selfless and allowing me to go chase my dreams on another continent."
- **Her why for GoGet'Em.** Not the money — the transformation she sees in members. "That's my goal with the GoGetEm community — I hope that all of you get everything out of it that you signed up for but I also hope and pray that you will get more than you ever even know existed."
- **Hired Zee Slingsby for communication reset.** "I kind of got bored of myself and the way I speak... so I hired Z and her team for kind of like a communication reset." Had a 6-month contract, extended it, then invited Zee to teach inside GoGetEm.

## Quotes

> "I woke up early today, made my coffee and I wrote out every single one of your names. I don't know if you can see that — there's 280 members and I hand wrote every single one of your names and I prayed over you and I prayed over your business."

> "I want to share the stories that I went through and hopefully — if it speaks to you I believe it speaks to you for a reason — and hopefully you can learn that lesson without actually having to go through them. Because some of them were really really shitty and really painful."

> "I'm done chasing and I feel like I have reached my goals. I hopefully there is more out there but it's not at the cost of me having to chase it anymore."

> "Nothing makes me happier than seeing you guys reach your goals. It's like that parent that the child leaves the nest and you're like oh my gosh like go fly right."
