# Time Is Money — TC Empower Conference

> Presentation for Transaction Coordinators at a February 2026 Empower Conference. Three goals: create time, speed up time, slow down time, multiply time — because time is money.

## Key Takeaways

- TC industry context: average TC earns $100k/year, $430/file, 20 transactions/month, 40 hours/week
- Three categories of attendees: (1) want to make more money, (2) make enough but want time back, (3) want to make more while working less
- Hourly rate formula: $250,000 ÷ 2,080 = $120.19/hr; stop doing tasks that don't pay your hourly rate
- VA math: get one VA at $6/hr working 40 hrs = $960/month; free up 8 hours/day for client-getting activities
- Coffee meeting every morning = 5 new clients/week; 10 calls/day = 50 potential clients/week; 2 events/week = 5 new clients
- Math: 65 agents × 3 files/month × $430 = $83,850/month → $1,006,200/year from one VA hire costing $12,000/year
- This only works if you spend freed time exclusively on getting new clients
- "Give me $84 and I give you 14 hours of work done" — 14 VAs at $6/hr = $84/day
- Gogo earns $2,300/hr; 14 VAs at $6/hr = $84 total/day for equivalent of 14 full-time work hours
- Gogo works Mon-Tue-Wed only; Thu-Sun off for the rest of her life
- 584 hours of output per week (14 VAs × 40 hrs + Gogo's 24 hrs) → grew to $2.7M/year
- VAs cross-trained; all have SOPs; "Google it. Ask a co-worker. Then ask me."
- GoGet'Em discount code for TC audience: TC10 for 10% off
