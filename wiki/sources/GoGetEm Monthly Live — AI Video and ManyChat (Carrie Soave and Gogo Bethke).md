> GoGet'Em Community monthly live (one big live per month). Standard format: every live starts with 5 minutes of AI from Carrie Soave, then Gogo teaches. This session: Carrie demos AI video creation with Higgsfield + Pinterest; Gogo teaches ManyChat lead capture. International attendance (South Africa, Canada, etc.).

## Key Takeaways

### Carrie's AI Video Workflow (Higgsfield + Pinterest + ChatGPT)
1. **Go to Pinterest** → find an image/look you want to recreate as yourself
2. **Higgsfield Chrome extension** → yellow "Recreate" button appears on Pinterest images → click it
3. **Select your AI character** (upload 20–30 photos of yourself from multiple angles — one-time setup)
4. Click **Generate** → within ~2 minutes, image appears in Higgsfield under your images tab
5. For **video**: click image → click "Video" → get a Higgsfield prompt from ChatGPT
6. **ChatGPT prompt:** "Give me a Higgsfield prompt to turn this picture into a short video" + paste/attach the image → copy the output prompt into Higgsfield → generate video
7. **Upload to TikTok** → add trending sound → overlay text/quote → done
8. Add **ManyChat trigger word** at end of video caption (e.g., "comment PARTNER") → automation fires → lead captured

### ManyChat Integration
- Carrie brought in 500 ManyChat leads in one week using AI video content with trigger words
- Key: Don't post content without a lead capture mechanism. ManyChat is that mechanism.
- Gogo's community dashboard includes **AI Video Prompt Wizard** custom GPT (in the GPTs folder)

### Key Principles
- Carrie's content format: 5-second clips with heavy text overlay → forces replays → better reach
- Photo character uploads: choose someone with similar hair color to improve AI accuracy
