# Update Your Facebook Business Page Name & Username

> GSMC 2026 module by Kristy Waker. Quick walkthrough for agents who already have a Facebook Business Page and need to align the name/username with the rest of their brand.

## Key Takeaways

- **Facebook Business Page ≠ personal profile.** You manage the business page *through* your personal account — no need to log in separately. You can act on behalf of the page, invite team members, and access more marketing/advertising features.
- **Name vs. username are two different things:**
  - **Username** = what appears in the URL. This is what must match across all platforms.
  - **Name** = what displays on the page. Less critical for consistency.
- **Username must match everywhere:** Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn — all the same handle for brand recognition and consistency.
- **To update:** Settings → Page Setup → Edit name or username.
- **Name change limit:** Once every 60 days; Facebook reviews before confirming.
- **Username changes:** No stated restriction — can be updated freely.
- **Page Access:** Settings → Page Access → Add New → search by name → grant access. Team members then log in with their personal accounts and switch to manage the business page — doesn't affect either account's security.

## Quotes

> "You always want to make sure your username matches all of your business accounts — Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and LinkedIn — so you can be consistent."
