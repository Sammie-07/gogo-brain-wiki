# How We Can Work Together (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo explains the "How We Can Work Together" pinned post — a one-time piece of content that lists your 3 money makers and their keyword CTAs, permanently solves the "broken record" problem, and sits pinned on your profile forever.

## Key Takeaways

### The Problem It Solves
Agents with multiple income streams (buyers, sellers, agent attraction, digital products, coaching) feel like a broken record — cycling through the same pitches every week. The "How We Can Work Together" post centralizes everything in one permanent post that lives on the profile. Post it once; it works forever.

### The Rule: Maximum 3 Money Makers
- Too many options → overwhelm → lost prospect
- Narrow your services down to the **top 3 ways you make money**
- Examples for real estate agents: buyers + sellers + agent attraction; or buyers + sellers + digital product; or buyers + sellers + investors

**Gogo's 3:**
1. Team Gogo organization at eXp (revenue share) — keyword: "Partner"
2. GoGet'Em Community (coaching community) — keyword: "Community"
3. The Circle (1:1 coaching, max 20 clients) — keyword: "Circle"

### Each Section Needs Its Own Keyword CTA
- Every money maker gets a comment keyword that triggers a ManyChat automation
- Don't assume people know how to reach you — spell it out explicitly
- The keyword routes the follower to the correct page/funnel for that specific service

**Format example:**
- "Interested in buying a home? Comment BUYER"
- "Thinking about selling? Comment SELLER"
- "Want to join my team? Comment AGENT"

### Connection to ManyChat
Each keyword must be set up in ManyChat before publishing. The automation handles follow-up — the post does the initial work, ManyChat delivers the link/calendar/funnel automatically.

### Must Be Pinned
After publishing, pin to profile. This post should be one of your three permanent pinned posts — it is evergreen content. Gogo's version was posted April 10, 2025 and still pinned as of February 2026 (almost a year later).

### The Two Non-Negotiable Pinned Posts
Gogo identifies two posts every agent must have pinned:
1. **Root Story** — who you are and why they should follow you
2. **How We Can Work Together** — how to become a client/partner/member

> "These two things alone will generate you so many leads because they live on your profile."
