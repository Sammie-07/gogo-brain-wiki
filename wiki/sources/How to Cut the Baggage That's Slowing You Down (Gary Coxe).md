# How to Cut the Baggage That's Slowing You Down (Gary Coxe)

> GoGet'Em Community session with Gary Coxe using an aviation metaphor to help entrepreneurs identify and release the emotional baggage, toxic relationships, and mental patterns that slow their progress.

## Key Takeaways

- **Gary Coxe:** Personal growth expert and life strategist. Known for producing in one session what therapists often can't achieve in a year. Endorsed by NBC (paid $1M+) and Dr. Phil (3 years). See also: [[Control Your Emotions Control Your Income (Gary Coxe)]].
- **Aviation metaphor — drag:**
  - In aviation, drag is the aerodynamic force that slows an aircraft.
  - Some drag is necessary — like wing lift creates drag, but without it you can't fly. Some necessary resistance is part of life.
  - Unnecessary drag = emotional baggage, toxic relationships, self-doubt, distractions. These slow you down without adding lift.
- **Core framework:**
  - **Identify** what's slowing you down
  - **Evaluate** whether it's necessary drag (helps you fly) or unnecessary drag (just weight)
  - **Release** what doesn't serve you
  - **Result:** Don't just move forward — take off
- **Mindset + behavior shift:** Gary specializes in not just talking about change but producing actual behavioral shifts in a single session.
- **Related to Gary's other GoGet'Em session:** [[Control Your Emotions Control Your Income (Gary Coxe)]] covered feelings being fabricated and faith as action.
