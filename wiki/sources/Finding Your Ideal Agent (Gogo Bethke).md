# Finding Your Ideal Agent (Gogo Bethke)

> The agent-attraction parallel to the ideal client framework. Gogo walks through a ChatGPT prompt to define your ideal agent prospect — the same low-hanging-fruit principle applied to recruiting rather than buyer/seller lead gen. Her ideal: $4–9M producer, 2+ years in, full-time, individual top performer.

## Key Takeaways

- **Same principle, different target:** Just as buyers/sellers need an ideal client profile, agent attraction needs an ideal agent profile. Low-hanging fruit = agents who are just like you, one or two steps behind where you are now.
- **Team GoGo updated stats (as of recording):** 1,660 agents total organizational count, 46 states, 7 countries, $3.7B closed in 2025. Gogo + Dwayne personally attracted 270+ to front line.
- **Gogo's ideal agent:** $4–9M annual production (capped but not yet ICON), 2+ years in the business, full-time, individual top producing solo agent. At $4M they know what they're doing; at $9M they're already high performers. She doesn't have to hand-hold. She IS them — just a few years ahead.
- **ChatGPT prompt — 4 sections:**
  1. **Demographics** — Experience (min 2 years), age (~35–45), gender, career stage (full-time)
  2. **Psychographics** — Motivations (financial freedom, work-life balance, high commissions), career goals (brand, team, volume), pain points (leads, splits, support), values (training, mentorship, flexibility, referrals)
  3. **Business Model Preferences** — Commission structure appeal, desired support (lead gen, marketing, training), culture fit (collaborative, high energy, family-like)
  4. **Technology & Social Behavior** — Tech comfort level, tools needed (CRM, automations), online presence goals, platforms used, content they engage with
- **Closing prompt:** "Based on these details, what content should I create to attract agents? What platform should I focus on? How can I position my offer in a way that appeals to them?"
- **Never messaged an agent out of 1,660.** Every one raised their hand inbound. Content handled objections before they were asked. When the agent says they're ready, the conversation is already 80% done.
- **Success story strategy:** Share agents like Cat West — multiple-times ICON, retired husband, mortgage arm, 150+ agent team. Prospects find themselves in the story and say "if she can do it, I can do it."
- **Team GoGo referral map:** Agents refer to each other within the org — credited as the primary reason many agents' production has doubled or tripled after joining.
- **Don't promise what you can't deliver on technology.** If you're not tech-savvy, don't lead with tech. Only offer what you actually have. Team GoGo: in-house onboarding, dedicated Kristy call, full tech stack.
