# YouTube Mastery — Convert Views into Real Estate Sales with Kaila Lindsey (GoGetEm)

> GoGet'Em guest training — Kaila Lindsey (Southern Oregon Coast) on building a YouTube presence that converts viewers into real estate clients.

## Key Takeaways

- Kaila Lindsey teaches real estate YouTube strategy to GoGet'Em members ("Go-Getters").
- Session ran ~2 hours; high-energy start — Gogo jokes about 10am Pacific = "morning time," California audience.
- YouTube for real estate: YouTube content has long shelf life (videos rank in search for years, not hours like IG/Facebook).
- **The conversion path**: viewer finds video via search → watches, builds trust over multiple videos → reaches out when they're ready to buy/sell.
- YouTube is a **search engine**, not a social platform — SEO fundamentals matter (titles, descriptions, tags, thumbnails).
- **High-converting video types for real estate**: neighborhood tours, "moving to [city]" guides, market update videos, home-buying process education.
- Consistency over production quality: a video published beats a perfect video never published.
- This session may overlap with Gogo's "Cold Calling Burnout to YouTube Breakthrough" content in the library.

## Quotes

> "YouTube is the gift that keeps giving. A video you posted 3 years ago can bring you a client today."
