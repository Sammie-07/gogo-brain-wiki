# Gogopreneur — What It's Like to Travel for Public Speaking (Episode 4)

> Gogopreneur Episode 4. Gogo speaks at an entrepreneurial event (not real estate) for the first time. Her sister attends — first time seeing Gogo speak on any stage. Notable as the episode where she explains her 9-business empire to a non-real-estate audience.

## Key Takeaways

- **First entrepreneurial (non-real estate) speaking engagement.** Previous stages were always in the real estate world. *"I have built nine businesses and even though they're kind of tied to real estate fields, they're mostly social media marketing, online marketing, hiring, systems."*
- **Her sister flew in** — first time seeing Gogo speak after 20 years apart building the American dream.
- **"The most costly thing in life is a closed mind."** Opening line of her talk. If you're not open to new ideas and opportunities, they won't show up.
- Her talk covered: leveraging, how to work 14 hours in one hour, hiring, running numbers, creating time, making more money.
- *"I didn't come to America to build somebody else's dream. I'm not hireable."*

## Quotes

> "The most interesting thing that's going to cost you the most in this life is a closed mind. If you're not open to new ideas, if you're not open to new opportunities, they are just not going to show up."

> "I didn't come to America to build somebody else's dream. I'm not hireable."
