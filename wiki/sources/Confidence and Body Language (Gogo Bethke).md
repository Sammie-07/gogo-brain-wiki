# Confidence and Body Language (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo's philosophy on showing up on camera: confidence from self-knowledge, not caring about critics, using filters, the two-content-tier system (polished vs. raw), and the "their opinion doesn't pay your bills" mindset. Part of GoGo Social Media Challenge 2026.

## Key Takeaways

- **Source of confidence:** "Really loving myself. Just knowing who I am." People who won't like you won't like you regardless — they're not your people.
- **On critics:** Let them talk. You should start worrying when they *stop* talking — that's when you became irrelevant.
- **On appearance:** Gogo doesn't overthink it. "Sometimes I look hit and sometimes I look hot." She uses filters on low-energy days when she doesn't want to do makeup. (Filters tutorial flagged as a follow-up video.)
- **Physical reality:** Gained 96 lbs in each pregnancy. Acknowledges certain angles don't flatter her body type. Solution: take a quick photo of yourself before recording to check your position, posture, and fit. "If you need to suck it in, suck it in."
- **Two-content tiers:**
  - **Polished content** (feed) — as put-together as possible; this is what you run ads to
  - **Raw/authentic content** (stories) — no makeup, just woke up, sipping coffee; this is what makes people trust you as a human
  - "I'm not going to run ads to when I'm looking hit, but I'm going to let you see me when I'm looking hit."
- **The minimum bar:** Anything you create "will do circles around the people who never created anything." Showing up imperfectly beats not showing up.
- **On critics' opinions:** "I start caring about your opinion as soon as I can deposit it into my bank account." Their opinion doesn't pay your bills, doesn't let you retire, doesn't send your kid to college.
- **The people filter:** "I'm looking for the people who are looking for me." (Credit: Brandon Barnum.) Create content for the people who will resonate — not to satisfy the critics. The rest are simply not your people.
- **The business reframe:** "Their opinion doesn't pay your bills, but when they use you as their realtor, it does. So, create content so they can choose you as a realtor."
