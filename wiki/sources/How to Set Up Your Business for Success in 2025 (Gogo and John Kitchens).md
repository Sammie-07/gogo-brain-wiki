# How to Set Up Your Business for Success in 2025 (Gogo and John Kitchens)

> GoGet'Em Community session with Gogo Bethke and John Kitchens on structuring your real estate business for 2025. Covers identifying revenue sources, strategic hiring, and emerging tools including REMLO and RealBricks.

## Key Takeaways

- **Identify where your money comes from:** Map every income source → hire to cover everything else → double down on the top 2 producers. Don't spread thin; concentrate on what's already working.
- **Hire before you can afford it:** Kristy (Gogo's assistant) was hired 11–12 years ago — before Gogo felt financially ready. The hire creates the capacity to earn more, not the other way around.
- **"40 hours alone → 80 hours with assistant":** An assistant effectively doubles productive hours. The leverage math makes hiring early rational.
- **Free ebook:** Gogo has a free resource on her assistant hiring process for agents ready to make their first hire.
- **REMLO program:** Mentioned as a relevant tool/program for agents setting up for success in 2025 (origination/lending adjacent).
- **RealBricks:** Fractionalized real estate platform — allows agents and investors to participate in real estate with smaller capital commitments.
- **John Kitchens:** Co-presenter. Known for coaching/frameworks including the CPR accountability structure.

## Quotes

> "Hire before you can afford it. The hire is what makes you able to afford it."

> "Forty hours alone becomes eighty hours with an assistant. That's the math."
