# Co-Sponsorship at eXp — Gogo's Offer (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo's personal pitch explaining her co-sponsorship offer: any eXp agent can co-sponsor incoming agents with her, gaining access to Team GoGo + upline org trainings at no extra cost. Accessed via gogobethke.com/partner.

## Key Takeaways

- **Co-sponsorship = existing eXp agent + Gogo jointly sponsor an incoming agent.** The incoming agent gets two sponsors: you (primary) and Gogo (co-sponsor).
- **What you gain:** Access to Team GoGo trainings, Honey Badgers training, and Fast Forward Movement training (Gogo's upline organizations). Automatically included — zero extra charge on the Team GoGo side.
- **What the incoming agent gains:** Same full access to all trainings in Team GoGo and its upline organizations.
- **Where to initiate:** gogobethke.com/partner → scroll to "Choose me as your co-sponsor at eXp" section → watch the explainer video → schedule a co-sponsorship call directly from that page.
- **Team GoGo profile (2025 stats):**
  - 156 agents
  - ~$4 billion in sales in 2025
  - Primarily solo agents; some small teams; no mega ICON teams
  - Many ICON-level agents
  - 46 US states, 7 countries
  - Can co-sponsor from any US state or in 27 countries
- **Target agent profile:** Rockstar solo agents building a local team — not a 120-agent mega operation.
