# Email Subject Lines (Carol, GSMC)

> Carol (Team Gogo email strategist) breaks down subject line strategy: mobile-first length, curiosity frameworks, real examples from Gogo's inbox, emoji usage, and how to use ChatGPT to generate subject lines. Part of GSMC 2026 copywriting month.

## Key Takeaways

- **Mobile-first rule:** Most people open email on their phone. Subject lines get cut off. Keep the core message in the first 4–6 words so the meaning survives truncation.
- **Core goal of every subject line:** Make them feel something — curiosity, intrigue, FOMO, opportunity — so they open. Always ask: *"How do I want this person to feel when they read this?"*

### Subject Line Strategies (with examples from Gogo's inbox)

| Strategy | Example | Why it works |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **Casual / personal** | "Come hang out with me tomorrow night" | Feels like a friend, not a brand. Inspires curiosity about what's happening. |
| **Short + direct** | "Free training: Create your AI twin" | Gets the point in 5 words. No ambiguity. |
| **Bold statement** | "What actually makes money on Instagram?" | Challenges the reader. Creates curiosity. They need to know the answer. |
| **Friendly + casual** | "Just a little tip on sales for you" | Low pressure, high trust. Feels like advice from a colleague. |
| **Ironic / pattern interrupt** | "Shortest email ever" / "Do not open this email" | Reverse psychology. The irony compels the open. |
| **Income opportunity** | "How dem agents add 1,600 plus per deal" | Specific number + income hook. High relevance for agents. |
| **Future promise** | "This is how / This is why" | Implies the answer is inside. Creates an open loop they want to close. |

### Emoji Usage
Emojis in subject lines make the email look:
- Casual and friendly
- Less corporate
- More personal and human

Use them selectively — one or two at most.

### ChatGPT for Subject Lines
If stuck, prompt ChatGPT with specifics:
- *"Give me a subject line with 4–6 words maximum that inspires curiosity."*
- *"I need a subject line that highlights an income opportunity."*
- *"Write a subject line promoting a free training I'm running."*
- Paste the full email body for even better results — ChatGPT can extract the natural subject.

## Quotes

> "The feeling of the person that is going to read it. That's basically it."

> "There's just something about these short and bold statements that just makes us feel curious about it, that just makes us want to open it."
