# Marketing Made Easy — Mega Marketing Event

> February 2026 presentation: turning online presence into a business asset that attracts leads, clients, and income without chasing.

## Key Takeaways

**Foundation — Brand Audit:**
- Attention is the new currency; a confusing brand leaks money every day
- Everything must connect: content, links, messaging flow into one simple functional system
- "You need to look over your brand" = name, face, message must match everywhere online
- Same professional headshot, same username, same bio on every platform — "stop looking like five different people"
- Regularly check: are all links live and working?
- Profile must have branded email and smartphone number

**Bio Strategy:**
- Bio is not a resume; it's a decision-making tool that tells people who you help and what to do next
- Use ChatGPT prompt to create bio (provided in deck)
- One bio must speak to buyers, sellers, AND agents simultaneously
- Instagram allows 5 clickable links — make it idiot-proof so people can work with you

**CRM / Lead Capture:**
- Must be set up to actually capture leads (KVCore/BoldTrail or Lofty)
- Once everything is connected, profile works for you 24/7 even when offline
- Own yourname.com — simple attraction funnel with story, proof, and value

**ManyChat / Automations:**
- Set up ManyChat keywords and CRM automations AFTER links and systems are in place
- Gogo's 3 big money makers: #teamgogo (PARTNER keyword), GoGet'Em Community (COMMUNITY keyword), The Circle (CIRCLE keyword)

**Content Strategy:**
- Strategy first, execution with confidence after
- Three content pillars in real estate: Buyers, Sellers, Agents
- Every post must support at least one pillar — if it doesn't speak to buyers, sellers, or agents, it's not moving business forward
- Study top producers in your market — not to compare, but to understand what's working
- Post attraction content: lifestyle, leadership, local expertise, closings, achievements
- Create niche landing pages for specific buyer/seller types (open houses, ocean front, condos)
- Know your ideal client exactly before creating content

**Reels / Video:**
- Buyer reels → ManyChat keyword "BUYER" → landing page → leads
- Seller reels → home valuation page → keyword "SELLER"
- Agent attraction reels → keyword "PARTNER"
- Hook people in 3 seconds; use CapCut and Instagram for editing; use trending audio

**Carousels:**
- High-save carousels build authority; always end with ManyChat keyword CTA
- Example: Gogo's "16 Things I Track" carousel

**Stories:**
- Polls, questions, stickers turn passive viewers into active conversations

**Email:**
- Gogo sends one email per day = 6.5M emails/year
- At 1% conversion = 65,000 potential leads
- GoHighLevel for email management
