# SEO for Realtors Part 2 — Implementation (Quincy Beaker)

> Follow-up to Part 1. Quincy Beaker goes deeper into website SEO implementation for realtors. Gogo met Quincy at Tony Robbins top influencers mastermind (Quincy 1st place; Gogo and Christa Mishor 2nd/3rd).

## Key Takeaways

- **The link in your Instagram story goes somewhere.** If that page is slow, broken, or missing the right words, you lose the lead. "I would be wasting my time if the website didn't work."
- **Words = search ranking.** If you want to be known for a specific thing, those exact words must appear on your website. Google serves your page to people searching those exact phrases.
- Social media drives traffic → website converts it. Both must work. One without the other is a leak.
- **Quincy's story (Gogo's telling):** He had 6 Instagram followers — 4 probably family, 1 wife, 1 mother. But his website beat Tony Robbins' own site for a challenge selling Tony Robbins tickets.
- SEO sits alongside email and social as the three organic reach channels — no ad spend required for any of them.
- Gogo's referral: After working with Quincy, she hired his team to audit everything. He's the expert — bring him in.
- **Part 2 focus:** Practical implementation of website optimization (vs. Part 1 = fundamentals/why).

## Quotes

> "I can't take you to make a million dollars if you're over here doing $20 activities." (analogous principle from Gogo — invest in getting the website right)
