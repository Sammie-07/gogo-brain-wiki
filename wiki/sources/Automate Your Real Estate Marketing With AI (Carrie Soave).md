# Automate Your Real Estate Marketing With AI (Carrie Soave)

> Carrie Soave's webinar covering AI tools for every stage of real estate social media — from idea generation to video editing to automated posting and lead capture.

## Key Takeaways

**Idea Generation Tools:**
- **answerthepublic.com** — shows what your audience is asking on Google
- **cohesive.so** — AI specialty site with one-click prompts by category (YouTube scripts, Instagram captions)
- **vid.iq** — YouTube-specific AI with Chrome extension; generates title, description, tags, thumbnail; rates video viral potential
- **ChatGPT** — requires prompt engineering, priming, and custom instructions for best results

**Prompt Stacking:**
- Series of prompts that build on each other to guide ChatGPT to a complete outcome
- First prompt: assign ChatGPT a role + upload market report + ask to search web for hot topics + request 1-month content calendar with keyword-optimized captions
- Always end every prompt with: "Ask me any questions you need me to answer to best perform this task"
- Paid ChatGPT ($20/month) required for web search capability

**Video Editing AI:**
- **Captions app** — eye gaze redirect (reads script but looks at camera), animated captions, B-roll, sound effects — best for short-form
- **Sub-magic** — caption templates modeled after Hormozi-style creators, better B-roll than Captions
- **Veed** — long-form video editor with eye gaze redirect — best for long-form
- **InVideo AI** — faceless video from text (no camera required) using B-roll
- **Wizzard** — transcribes video, additional editing features

**Scheduling/Automation:**
- **Loomly** — Carrie's favorite (cheapest, schedules to stories + reels simultaneously)
- **Metricool** — metrics-heavy, good insights on content performance
- **Repurpose.io** — repurposes content across platforms automatically

**ManyChat:**
- Automates lead gen, lead nurture, and lead conversion inside Instagram/Facebook DMs
- Entire funnel without leaving social media
- Carrie offers done-for-you buyer and seller ManyChat automations

**Content Creation Workflow (Remixing Viral Videos):**
1. Find viral YouTube video in your niche
2. Paste URL into youtubetranscript.com → get full transcript instantly
3. Paste transcript into ChatGPT → rewrite in your tone using proper prompt framework
4. Use vid.iq to generate title, description, tags, thumbnail
5. Record new video → edit in Veed → post to YouTube with vid.iq details
