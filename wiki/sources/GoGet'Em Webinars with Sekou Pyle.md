# GoGet'Em Webinars with Sekou Pyle

> Sekou Pyle — old-school cold-calling coach and GoGet'Em guest — on prospecting, persistence, and lead generation for real estate agents.

## Key Takeaways

- Sekou Pyle is an old-school cold-calling coach who works only with the most motivated agents — mirroring Gogo's philosophy of "I only work with the most motivated buyers and sellers in the market."
- His core teaching: **prospecting is a phone-in-hand discipline** — not a marketing game, not a social media play; it's direct outreach done consistently.
- Agents fail at cold calling not because of rejection but because of **inconsistency** — they call for 3 days and quit.
- Connection before conversion: Sekou builds rapport fast through humor, energy, and authenticity — modeling the same for agents.
- Gogo's endorsement: "If anybody can make me cold call in this world, that would be you." She would buy anything he sells in every color twice.
- The most motivated agents are the ones who succeed regardless of market conditions — Sekou only coaches those who are already leaning in.
