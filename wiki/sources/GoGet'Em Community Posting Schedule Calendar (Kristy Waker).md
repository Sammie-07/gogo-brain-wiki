# GoGet'Em Community Posting Schedule Calendar (Kristy Waker)

> Kristy walks through the GoGet'Em Community's shared Google Calendar — a weekly posting and IRL event structure that repeats every week. Color-coded (red = feed, blue = stories), filled with daily content ideas. Access requires Gmail/Google Workspace email; Brand Tracker has a backup spreadsheet tab.

## Key Takeaways

- **What it is:** A shared Google Calendar managed by Gogo's team, auto-invited to all GoGet'Em Community members. One week of content, repeated every week. Members view only — Gogo's team manages the content.
- **Color system:**
  - Red = feed posts (what to post publicly on your feed)
  - Blue = stories (daily story content ideas)
  - IRL (top of each day) = in-person / real-life events (networking)
- **10–15 stories per day.** Every entry has details, examples, and content ideas when clicked.
- **IRL event schedule (mirrors Never Eat Alone):**
  - Sun/Sat: Open house
  - Mon/Wed: Coffee with an expert (lender, title, attorney, inspector)
  - Tue/Thu: Coffee with anybody (friends, family, neighbors)
  - Fri: Coffee with agents (agent attraction)
- **Giveaway schedule:** Starts Sundays, runs through Tuesday, with step-by-step instructions per day. Weekly local small business giveaway.
- **Access requirements:** Must use Gmail or Google Workspace email. eXp email does NOT have a Google Calendar — if you signed up with an eXp email, contact support@gogetumcommunity.com to switch.
- **One invite per person.** Assistants must be granted access through your personal calendar — not via a second calendar invite.
- **Manage overwhelm:** Uncheck the calendar in Google Calendar left sidebar to hide it; re-check when needed. It stays linked — just toggled invisible.
- **Brand Tracker backup:** Third tab "Social Posting Schedule" in the Brand Tracker spreadsheet contains the same content in spreadsheet form. Use the + buttons to expand collapsed rows. If your Brand Tracker predates January 15, 2026, grab an updated copy.
- **One week of content, repeated forever.** Routine is the goal — once in the rhythm, content ideas come automatically. It "becomes another language."
- **Infrastructure backbone:** Everything runs on Google (Workspace, Calendar, Drive, Docs). If you followed Kristy's brand setup steps, your email is already Google-based and calendar access is seamless.
