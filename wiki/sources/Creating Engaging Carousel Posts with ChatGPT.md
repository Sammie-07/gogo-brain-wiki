# Creating Engaging Carousel Posts with ChatGPT

> Short walkthrough demonstrating how to use an active ChatGPT conversation (already containing market data) to generate a complete carousel slide outline — then transfer to Canva for design.

## Key Takeaways

### The Core Workflow
1. Pull market statistics or neighborhood data into ChatGPT (same session where you're doing your research)
2. Prompt: *"Create a carousel post based on the stats above"*
3. ChatGPT outputs a slide-by-slide outline with content per slide, ready to copy
4. Take the outline into Canva → copy/paste content per slide into your branded template
5. Ellen (Team Gogo VA) covers the Canva design side in a companion video

### Key Principle
The data is already in context — no need to start a new session or re-enter information. One continuous chat from research → carousel outline → done.

### Additional Capability
ChatGPT can also generate the actual visual carousel layout, not just written content. Useful for agents who want AI to propose the design as well.
