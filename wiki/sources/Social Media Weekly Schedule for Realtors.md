# Social Media Weekly Schedule for Realtors

> Gogo's prescribed 5-day weekly social media posting schedule. Each day has a specific content type matched to a buyer or seller intent signal.

## Key Takeaways

- **Day-by-day schedule:**
  | Day | Content Type | Purpose |
  |---|---|---|
  | Monday | Any listing (KV Core IDX link) | Buyer lead — search homes |
  | Tuesday | Your listing (if active) | Your inventory / brand |
  | Wednesday | Search for homes post | Direct buyer CTA |
  | Thursday | Open houses (yours or local business) | Buyer + community |
  | Friday | Home value link | Seller lead generation |

- **Friday = seller lead day:** The home value post is specifically designed to attract homeowners curious about what their home is worth — top-of-funnel seller lead.
- **KV Core IDX links** are used throughout — every property post ties back to a lead capture page, not just a listing photo.
- **Consistency over perfection:** Posting the same structured content every week trains your audience to expect it and builds the brand over time.
- **This is a floor, not a ceiling:** Gogo posts more than 5x/week, but this schedule ensures the minimum strategic coverage regardless of how busy you are.
