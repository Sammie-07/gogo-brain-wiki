# The Future of Real Estate Transactions with Cryptocurrency (Eric Cruz, Propy)

> GoGet'Em Community session with Eric Cruz of Propy — the platform that pioneered crypto real estate transactions, NFT properties, and blockchain-recorded deeds. Full overview of how crypto is entering mainstream real estate.

## Key Takeaways

- **Propy:** First company to transact real estate with cryptocurrency, record a property deed on blockchain, and sell a property as an NFT.
- **$5B+ in transactions:** Volume processed with zero wire fraud incidents (blockchain's core security advantage).
- **24-hour closings:** Smart contracts automate the closing process — dramatic speed improvement over traditional 30-45 day closes.
- **Wire fraud elimination:** Blockchain-based transactions remove the wire fraud vulnerability that costs the industry billions annually.
- **KYC + AML compliance:** Crypto buyers go through Know Your Customer and Anti-Money Laundering verification — not the Wild West.
- **Bitcoin-backed loans:** Buyers can use Bitcoin as collateral for loans without selling their crypto position.
- **Seller gets USD:** Sellers don't need to understand crypto. Coinbase converts crypto payment to USD — seamless for traditional sellers.
- **Smart contracts — Hawaii deal:** 100% automated transaction, executed on a Saturday, no human involvement needed. Recorded in 4 minutes.
- **Market data:** 11.5% of 2022 first-time buyers used crypto. Bitcoin halving cycle historically precedes price appreciation periods.
- **94% of crypto holders:** Gen Z + Millennials — the next generation of buyers is already crypto-native.
- **CCA course:** Certified Crypto Advisor — 8 lessons + badge for agents who complete. Differentiator for early adopters.
- **Licensed markets:** Currently Florida, Arizona, Colorado. Expanding to 10 states.
- **Title company acquisition incentive:** $10K finder fee for agents who bring title companies into Propy's network.
