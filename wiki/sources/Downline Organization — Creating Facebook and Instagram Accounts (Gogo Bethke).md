# Downline Organization — Creating Facebook and Instagram Accounts (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo explains why she created separate Instagram and Facebook profiles for Team Gogo (her downline organization), how those accounts are managed, and why every agent attraction system eventually needs a dedicated org page.

## Key Takeaways

- **Name your organization first.** Gogo's local team = "Gogo's Real Estate Team." Downline/org = "Team Gogo" / "Team Gogo International Family of Agents"
- **Create separate Instagram + Facebook** for the org — linked so posting on one posts on the other
- **Why separate?** As downline grows (Team Gogo = 1,660 agents, 46 states, 7 countries), agent celebrations and shoutouts would drown your personal feed. Dedicated org account keeps your personal profile clean and focused
- **What goes on the org account:** Benefits, events, agent achievements (capping, iconing, building teams), agent stories, production shoutouts — Team Gogo all day every day
- **Belongs together, not on personal profile:** Agents feel like they belong somewhere. The org page gives them a home.
- **Management:** Full-time VA manages the Team Gogo Instagram page. When revenue share is flowing, you're happy to pay for this. Until then, you manage it.
- **Follow your own agents:** The org account actively follows, promotes, and shares agents' stories and production posts — giving them additional exposure and helping them connect with each other
- **Sponsor shopping use case:** When a potential recruit is evaluating whether to join you, send them to the Team Gogo profile. They can see the community, identify agents they know, and reach out directly — more credible than Gogo forwarding a list of names
- **Content split:** Most agent-related content goes to the org page. You curate what crosses over to your personal profile
- **Homework:** Name your org. Create the Facebook page and Facebook group. Create and link the Instagram page.

## Quotes

> "When people start 'sponsor shopping,' they want to see who you are and talk to agents who already decided to partner with you to see if it's all true. I just send them to the Team Gogo profile and let them decide who they want to talk to."

> "So everybody can feel like they belong somewhere."

> "When you get to the point where you have hundreds of agents and revenue share is coming in, you're more than happy to pay a VA to manage that account for you."
