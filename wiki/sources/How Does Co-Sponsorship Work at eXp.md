# How Does Co-Sponsorship Work at eXp

> Gogo explains eXp Realty's co-sponsorship feature, launched May 2025. Agents joining eXp can now designate two sponsors instead of one, splitting revenue-share credit.

## Key Takeaways

- **Co-sponsorship is new as of May 2025** — eXp Realty added the ability for an incoming agent to choose two sponsors simultaneously.
- **Two-position arrangement:** The agent picks a Primary Sponsor and a Co-Sponsor. Revenue share is split between both according to eXp's formula.
- **Why it matters:** Agents who were influenced or recruited by multiple people can now honor both relationships. Encourages collaboration over competition in agent attraction.
- **Gogo's take:** This is a significant structural change — the old single-sponsor model created conflict when two people contributed to an agent's decision to join. Co-sponsorship resolves that.
- Agents already at eXp are not retroactively affected — applies to new agents joining after the feature launched.
