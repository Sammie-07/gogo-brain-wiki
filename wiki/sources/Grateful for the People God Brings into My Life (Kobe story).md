# Grateful for the People God Brings into My Life (Kobe story)

> Personal short-form content from Gogo Bethke about Team Go agent Valerie Restrepo going above and beyond to help Gogo's son Kobe — who has Type 1 diabetes — during a medical emergency while traveling.

## Key Takeaways

- **Kobe:** Gogo's son; has Type 1 diabetes. Uses a Continuous Glucose Monitor (CGM) to track blood sugar.
- **The situation:** Kobe traveled across Florida to fish; his Dexcom G7 CGM device died/malfunctioned mid-trip.
- **Valerie Restrepo:** Team Go agent who located a replacement Dexcom G7 through neighborhood Facebook groups, then drove at midnight to meet Kobe at a 7-Eleven.
- **Medical outcome:** Kobe went low at 4am (blood sugar 55, with arrow pointing down — a dangerous level). The device detected it and alerted in time.
- **Theme:** Team Go as a family, not just a business. The people drawn into Gogo's orbit reflect her character and values.
- **Community tool:** Neighborhood Facebook groups used as a practical resource, not just for lead generation.

## Quotes

> "This is why I'm grateful for the people God brings into my life."
