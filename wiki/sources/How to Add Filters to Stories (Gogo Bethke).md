# How to Add Filters to Stories (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo's method for finding and saving Instagram filters: discover them while watching other people's stories, tap the filter name under their username, save effect. Part of GoGo Social Media Challenge 2026.

## Key Takeaways

- **Gogo's filter usage:** 90% of the time she uses a filter; 10% shows the real, unfiltered her
- **Why filters:** "Sometimes I don't feel like putting makeup on, but I feel like looking good for the camera"
- **Shooting workflow:** Always shoots video inside Instagram even if posting elsewhere — this applies the filter. Then downloads the filtered video to repurpose as an ad or cross-platform content
- **Gogo's method — find filters from other people's stories:**
  1. Tap on any person's Story
  2. Look for the filter name displayed under their username (e.g., "moody," "flawless," "caramel")
  3. Tap the filter name → "Save Effect" button appears → tap to save
  4. Saved filters appear in your Stories camera circles at the bottom
- **Finding saved filters while recording a Story:** Swipe right from main screen → selfie camera → scroll through circles at bottom → tap to apply
- **Green screen filter:** Available in the same circles. Select it → choose a background image → use for presentations, Q&A overlays, teaching content
- **Gogo's favorite filter:** The most realistic-looking one (doesn't name it specifically — "this one right here")
- **Copy a filter from someone's past Story:** Tap the filter name shown below their username in the Story → save it to your library
- **Laura has a different method** for finding filters (browsing the Effects library directly — separate GSMC video)
