# How to Track Business Growth with Effective Tools and Strategies

> GoGet'Em Community session presenting Gogo's full suite of business trackers — financial, time, lead generation, and more. The most detailed public view of the 16 trackers she references.

## Key Takeaways

- **"If you don't track it, you can't grow it."** Tracking is non-negotiable.
- **Weekly prospecting tracker** — categories include: open houses/events, hours prospected, thank-you cards, leads generated. One sheet = one week. Review at end of week to see truth of your activity.
- **Lead tracker** — each lead rated A (ready in 0–30 days), B (30–60 days), C (90+ days). Daily routine: call + text + email every lead on the list.
- **Revenue share tracker** — for eXp agents: track your downline activity.
- **Money tracker** — all income sources.
- **Profit & Loss tracker** — income vs. expenses.
- **Pay log tracker** — what goes out to each team member.
- **Affiliate payout tracker** — "If you open your mouth to pitch a product or service, you better get paid for it."
- **Social media post tracker** — logs daily post activity.
- **Number meetings** — Gogo + Kristy meet monthly (~1 hour) to review all income, expenses, and company tabs. Quarterly meeting with board of directors.
- She owns **Real Estate Community USA** (Rec USA) — largest real estate Facebook group in the US with ~500,000 members.
