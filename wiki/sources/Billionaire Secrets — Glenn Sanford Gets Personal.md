# Billionaire Secrets — Glenn Sanford Gets Personal

> Gogopreneur episode featuring eXp Realty founder Glenn Sanford. Personal background, the origin of eXp, INTJ profile, investment philosophy, and lessons for agents.

## Key Takeaways

- **Personality:** INTJ — deep thinker, positive, risk-taker. Those 3 traits define who he is.
- **First company at 15.** Entrepreneurship was always the path.
- **Billionaire moment:** When EXPI (eXp stock) hit $5/share in 2016. Stock peaked later at $85.
- **eXp origin:** Born from the 2008 financial crisis. Glenn had built a traditional brokerage, watched it collapse, and rebuilt from scratch as a virtual/cloud model.
- **Business philosophy:** Vistage member (CEO peer group) → moved to Abundance 360 with Peter Diamandis. Focuses on exponential thinking.
- **Books recommended:** *Influence* (Cialdini), *7 Habits of Highly Effective People*, *The Entrepreneur's Manual*.
- **"4 As":** eXp agents can do business **Anyone, Any Time, Anything, Anywhere.** No geographic or time restrictions.
- **Future concerns:** Bitcoin (currency disruption), quantum computing (encryption risk). Thinks in decades.
- **Judo:** Has practiced for 27 years. Discipline + mastery outside business.
- **Agent-owned model:** eXp's core innovation — agents earn equity (stock), rev share, and act as co-owners. No offices = overhead eliminated, value passed to agents.

## Quotes

> "The 4 As: anyone, any time, anything, anywhere."

> "I had built a traditional brokerage. I watched it die in 2008. I had to reinvent everything."
