# Business Foundations: Mindset, Systems, and Building Your Team with Virtual Assistants

> Week 1 of the paid 4-week Business Mastery webinar series. Covers DISC personality types, the hourly rate formula, the red/green light exercise, and Gogo's complete VA hiring and management SOP.

## Key Takeaways

- **DISC framework:** Gogo is high D/I; hires high C VAs to compensate. Always request two DISC assessments (standard + Tony Robbins) before hiring.
- **Hourly rate filter:** Annual income / 2,080 = your rate. Gogo's: ~$300/hr (sales), ~$2,300/hr (coaching). Every decision runs through this number.
- **Red/Green exercise:** Run for a full quarter (not just one day) to capture all task frequencies. Red = delegate; green = only you.
- **"Business moves at the speed of trust"** — 12 years to build a reputation, 2 minutes to ruin it.
- **VA hiring SOP:** Red/green list first, Google Form, Facebook group posting, DISC + video screening, color-code applicants, Calendly interview, contract, 2-4 weeks attending all meetings before tasks begin, week-by-week delegation ladder.
- **TopTracker** for VA time tracking; **Veem** for international payments; **WhatsApp** per-business groups for communication.
- **Cross-train minimum two** people in every role — never one person deep in any skill.
- **Buffer call:** Kristy holds an orientation call with each new VA to explain Gogo's direct personality before Day 1.
- **Three-warning system:** first warning, second warning, fired. Communicated on day one.
- **VA as income generator:** Train VAs well enough and they can run revenue-generating services. Saeed's onboarding sessions pay for his own salary.
- **First 90 days are an expense, not a return.** After 90 days: highly productive.
- **VA mindset shift:** Stop asking "how much will this cost?" and start asking "how much money will this make me?"
