### Team Types — Additional Detail

**Standard Team** (minimum $6M or 30 transactions in prior 12 months):
- Team leader: $16k capper, can ICON
- Agents: $8k half-cappers, cannot ICON
- **Mandatory minimum split: 25% to team leader on every transaction** (no exceptions — cannot waive for personal deals on the team)
- Can take more than 25% but 25% is the floor

**Mega Icon Team** ($40M + 175 transactions + guarantee 10 agents cap):
- Team leader: $16k capper
- Agents: $4k quarter-cappers
- **Mandatory minimum split: 25% per transaction**
- 10 agents capping at $4k = $40k + team leader's own $16k = $56k committed annually
- Must maintain those guarantees to keep Mega Icon team status

### REMLO Clarification

- REMLO earners are **"lender assistants" — not originators**
- Cannot make decisions on the mortgage file: no authority to approve/deny, no say on appraisal qualification
- Because they are not decision-makers, there is no conflict of interest in also being the buyer's agent
- **Does NOT appear on the settlement statement** — payment comes separately
- Melissa Albert's approach: "Here's my three recommended lenders — I'm also a recommended lender. I don't do anything different, we just shop for the best rate."
