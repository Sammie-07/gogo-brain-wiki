# Zee Slingsby — $10K Months to $10K Weeks (Day 2 — Offer Suite)

> Day 2 of Zee Slingsby's GoGet'Em training — building a tiered offer suite that creates multiple entry points for clients and maximizes lifetime value.

## Key Takeaways

- **Offer suite concept**: you need more than one product at one price. A tiered suite captures clients at different budgets and commitment levels.
- **Three-tier structure** (Zee's model):
  1. **Low-ticket** (~$97–$297): digital product, mini-course, or challenge. Builds trust, easy yes, captures leads.
  2. **Mid-ticket** (~$1,000–$3,000): group coaching program or done-with-you course. The core transformation.
  3. **High-ticket** (~$5,000–$25,000): 1:1 coaching, mastermind, intensive. Highest touch, highest outcome.
- **The ascension model**: clients enter at low-ticket, get results, naturally ascend to higher tiers.
- **Real estate agent application**: low-ticket = buyer's guide / seller checklist; mid-ticket = home-buying course; high-ticket = agent coaching or buyer consultation intensive.
- Revenue math: 100 clients × $97 = $9,700. 10 clients × $1,000 = $10,000. 2 clients × $5,000 = $10,000. Different paths to $10K.
- Zee teaches that the goal of the offer suite is to serve clients at every level — not to upsell, but to create access.

## Quotes

> "Your high-ticket offer isn't for everyone. Your low-ticket offer is how everyone finds you."
