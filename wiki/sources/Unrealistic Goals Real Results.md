# Unrealistic Goals, Real Results (Verl Workman)

> GoGet'Em Webinars session with Verl Workman, founder of Workman Success Systems and co-author of *Raving Referrals for Real Estate Agents*. Focus on setting goals that scare you — and the identity shifts required to achieve them.

## Key Takeaways

- **Verl Workman** — real estate coach, Workman Success Systems founder, author of *Raving Referrals for Real Estate Agents*.
- **"Unrealistic" goals are the only ones worth setting** — goals you can easily achieve don't require growth. Only goals that feel out of reach force the identity and behavior changes that produce real results.
- **Breaking the poverty cycle:** Verl and Gogo both discuss the mindset inherited from families without wealth — and why each generation has to consciously break the pattern.
- **Raving Referrals framework:** Referral-based business is the highest-quality, lowest-cost lead source. Building a referral culture requires consistent relationship investment.
- **Goal-setting mechanics:** Write the goal, set the date, work backwards to daily behaviors. Don't manage the outcome — manage the activity.
