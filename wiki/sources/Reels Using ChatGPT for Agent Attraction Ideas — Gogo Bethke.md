# Reels: Using ChatGPT to Generate Agent Attraction Ideas — Gogo Bethke

> GoGet'Em video on using ChatGPT to generate a quarter's worth of Friday agent-attraction reel ideas. Includes 10 specific reel ideas and the full content calendar framework.

## Key Takeaways

- **Friday = Agent Attraction Day** — non-negotiable in Gogo's content calendar. Content schedule: Mon = sellers, Wed = buyers, Fri = agent attraction, Sun = spiritual/emotional.
- Consistency is the name of the game: "I do the same things over and over again, and that's what's going to make us millions."
- **ChatGPT prompt framework**: Tell it your niche, brokerage, tone of voice → ask for 12 reel ideas per quarter → arrange chronologically → have VA plug into calendar.
- Batch creation: Gogo creates an entire quarter of content in one sitting.
- **ManyChat "partner" keyword** must be set up before posting agent attraction reels — it's the CTA at the end of every Friday video.
- Visual content wins: most agents are visual learners. Show numbers on a whiteboard or iPad — don't just say them.

## 10 Friday Agent Attraction Reel Ideas

1. How to tell your brokerage is actually costing you money
2. If you closed the same deals at [brokerage X] vs. where you are — here's what you'd make (visual number comparison)
3. Most agents don't know their real cost per closing
4. Most agents don't know what their brokerage actually provides
5. How to tell if your split is helping or hurting you
6. Why your production plateau isn't a motivation problem
7. The standard I hold my agents to — and why
8. How top agents decide where to hang their license
9. If an agent brings me this problem, here's how I handle it
10. The question every agent should ask before their next year
