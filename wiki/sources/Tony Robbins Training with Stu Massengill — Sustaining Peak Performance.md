# Tony Robbins Training with Stu Massengill — Sustaining Peak Performance in Any Economy

> Stu Massengill (Tony Robbins' top Peak Performance Strategist) on why some businesses thrive while others barely survive — shared in Gogo's GoGet'Em community.

## Key Takeaways

- **The SAM Framework — three pillars of success:**
  - **Strategy**: a clear, actionable plan before you start
  - **Action**: massive, committed action — successful people go all in, not halfway
  - **Mindset**: 80% of success is mindset and psychology; only 20% is know-how (the "how-to")
- Most people get stuck in the "how-to" and never start. When you truly want something, the how reveals itself.
- Your habits and patterns produce your results — examine your patterns, not just your outcomes.
- Peak performance is about **sustaining** it, not just achieving momentary highs.
- Mindset is the multiplier: belief that a goal is reachable unlocks potential, drives action, and creates momentum.
