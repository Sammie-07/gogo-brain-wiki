# From $10K Months to $10K Weeks (Zee Slingsby)

> GoGet'Em Community session with Zee Slingsby on scaling income from $10K/month to $10K/week through group coaching, pricing mindset, and retention strategy.

## Key Takeaways

- **"Don't need anyone to say yes":** The energy of desperation or needing clients repels them. Scarcity energy is visible and undermines sales. Operate from abundance — if this client doesn't sign, another will.
- **Group coaching ≠ inferior product:** Most coaches resist group models because they think it devalues their 1:1. Wrong framing. Group creates community, peer accountability, and often better outcomes than 1:1.
- **Paying for proximity:** Clients pay to be close to the person and the room, not just for information. Price accordingly.
- **Retention > acquisition:** It costs far more to acquire a new client than to keep an existing one. Build your model around keeping people, not constantly selling new ones.
- **Time > money:** At a certain income level, time becomes the scarcer resource. Design your offers so you can serve more people without proportionally more hours.
- **Don't lower prices because people aren't signing:** If clients aren't converting, the problem is your *messaging* — not your price. Lowering price signals low value and attracts wrong clients.
- **$10K/week pathway:** Achieved by moving from 1:1 (capped by hours) to group programs (leveraged) while maintaining or increasing price per head.

## Quotes

> "You don't need anyone to say yes. That energy — when you need the client — they feel it and they run."

> "If people aren't signing, you don't have a pricing problem. You have a messaging problem."

> "Retention is cheaper than acquisition. Stop spending all your energy on new and take care of what you have."
