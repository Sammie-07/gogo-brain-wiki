# How to Find Trending Ideas (Gogo Bethke)

> Gogo's system for never running out of content ideas: watch Instagram, save what you like into folders, adapt it to your voice when you need it. Part of GoGo Social Media Challenge 2026.

## Key Takeaways

- Gogo explicitly does not consider herself creative — "I just steal other people's creative"
- Her system: watch Instagram Reels passively → spot something compelling → save it as a reference
- **The mechanism:** three dots (top right of any Reel) → "Save" → choose or create a folder
- **Folder structure:** "For Later" (general ideas) + specific folders (e.g., "Social Media Challenge 2026," "Agent Attraction Videos," "Buyers and Sellers Videos")
- **Dual-save:** save the video as idea AND save the audio separately if you like the sound
- **When creativity dries up:** open the saved folder, pick a video, recreate it
- **Adaptation rule:** "I massage the words a little bit because I don't want it literally word for word… to make it sound more like me." Keep the same music if it worked.
- The same music that made the original video work will work for yours — don't overthink the soundtrack
- Philosophy: borrowing publicly available content ideas is the same as purchasing coaching — you're paying for someone else's experience so you don't have to start from scratch
- **Homework:** Start a "For Later" saved collection tonight. Every time you see something you like, save it.

## Quotes

> "You're borrowing content ideas; I do the same thing. I just copy it from other people in different ways."

> "When your creativity well dries up, you just go to that saved video content and you create from there."
