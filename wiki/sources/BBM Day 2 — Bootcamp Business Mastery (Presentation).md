# BBM Day 2 — Bootcamp Business Mastery

> Day 2 of Gogo's Bootcamp Business Mastery (BBM) event. Focuses on "make money and not trade time," passive income types, and the full VA leverage model.

## Key Takeaways

**Hourly Rate Calculation:**
- Same formula: GCI ÷ (hrs/week × 52); example: $250,000 ÷ 2,080 = $120/hr
- Listing side vs. buy side hourly comparison
- TC cost analysis: what you save vs. what you lose by not using a TC

**Wake-Up Money (Passive Income Types):**
1. Affiliate income — products/services you love; advocate and get paid (10-20% average)
2. Profit share — business must be profitable first
3. Revenue share — comes right off the top before profit calculation
4. Stocks and investments
5. Business ownership

**"List of Things You're Not Going to Do From Now On"** — exercise: write down everything you'll stop doing personally

**Money Relationship Reset:**
- Examine limiting beliefs: "money doesn't grow on trees," "hard work = money," "for the love of money it's the root of all evil"
- Money is neutral: it just makes you more of who you already are

**The VA Math:**
- 14 VAs in 9 job capacities (including TV Show Assistant and Real Estate Team Assistant)
- In-person assistants: house assistant, house cleaner, personal chef
- "Give me $4/hour and I give your life back" — 14 VAs at $4/hr = $56/day
- Works Mon-Tue-Wed; Thu-Sun off; 8 × 3 = 24 personal work hours/week
- But team works 14 × 40 = 560 hours → total output = 584 hours/week → went to $2.7M/year

**Red Light / Green Light:** core time audit exercise for every BBM attendee

**DISC Assessment:** required for all hires (Kristy's + Gogo's shown as examples)

**Schedule:** Monday back-to-back meetings; Tuesday: podcast, live webinar (2nd Tue), coaching; Wednesday: #teamgogo, Wine Not; Thu-Sun OFF

**Trackers:** Lead Tracker, Greatness Tracker, P&L, Pay Log, Money Tracker — all linked

**Technology Systems:**
- ManyChat: automated messaging, time-saving for FAQs and customer interactions
- Keywords used: PARTNER, EVENT, AA, REDX, WEBINAR, RETREAT, INSTACARD, BUYER, SELLER, POSH, NEWSLETTER
- ChatGPT: for YouTube descriptions, titles, content creation
- SendGrid: email platform (historical reference)

**Anderson Advisors:** recommended for taxes and business structure

**Health alongside wealth:** "I don't want anything to give while you're making money. Health (physical and mental), happy relationship, marriage, children, friends, and family."

## Quotes

> "Wake up money — make money and not trade time."
