# Tax Strategy

Tax minimization and wealth-preserving structures for real estate agents, investors, and entrepreneurs — a recurring theme across Gogo's GoGet'Em and coaching content.

## Core Idea

Every dollar saved in taxes is a dollar available to reinvest, compound, or deploy into assets. Tax strategy is not optional for serious wealth-builders — it's the difference between building wealth and paying someone else to build theirs.

## Key Frameworks and Tools

### Business Structure
- **Sole proprietor** — default, worst tax structure. Subject to full self-employment tax (15.3%) on net profit.
- **LLC** — liability protection, minimal tax benefit alone unless combined with S Corp election.
- **S Corporation** (or LLC taxed as S Corp) — splits income into salary + distribution. Only salary subject to SE tax. Saves thousands annually above ~$40K net profit.
- **C Corporation** — flat 21% tax rate (locked in by TCJA, preserved by Big Beautiful Bill 2025). Best for retained earnings at corporate level.

### Real Estate–Specific Tools
- **Depreciation**: write off property cost over 27.5 years (residential) or 39 years (commercial) while it appreciates.
- **Cost segregation**: accelerate depreciation by reclassifying building components — take deductions faster.
- **1031 Exchange**: defer capital gains by rolling proceeds into like-kind property.
- **Bonus depreciation**: 100% first-year deduction on qualifying property (extended/modified by Big Beautiful Bill 2025).
- **Capital gains rates**: long-term (1+ year) at 0%, 15%, or 20% — far lower than ordinary income.

### Family and Business Integration
- **Hire your children**: pay them a market-rate salary for legitimate work. Deductible to you; below their standard deduction threshold = zero taxes for them.
- **Fund their Roth IRA**: earned income qualifies minors for Roth IRA — tax-free compounding for 50+ years.
- **Family on payroll**: spouse and children on payroll can shift income to lower brackets and fund retirement accounts.
- **SETC (Self-Employed Tax Credit)**: COVID-era credit (Alex Moeller) for self-employed individuals — up to $32,220 for sick/caregiving days in 2020–2021.

### The Big Beautiful Bill (2025 Tax Reform)
- Signed into law July 4, 2025 — 1,000+ pages.
- **QBI deduction made permanent**: 20% deduction for pass-through entities (S Corps, partnerships, sole proprietorships). Massive win for real estate agents.
- **Corporate rate unchanged**: 21% flat for C Corps.
- Real estate NOT classified as a "specified service industry" — realtors receive full QBI deduction regardless of income level.

### Asset Protection Overlay
- Move assets into entities — LLCs, trusts — so personal name doesn't appear in public records.
- Wyoming LLC: no member disclosure requirement — highest privacy protection.
- Land trusts: remove real property from personal name while maintaining control.
- Goal: appear to own nothing personally while controlling everything through entities.
