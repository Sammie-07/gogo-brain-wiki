# Kalie Kinsella

Google Business Profile / local-SEO specialist known as **"That Google Girl"** (Orlando Living Real Estate); guest trainer in Gogo's community on ranking agents in the AI-search era.

## Overview

Kalie Kinsella teaches the **"Build it, Prove it, Feed it"** framework for Google Business Profiles (GBP). Her thesis: after Google's ~June 2026 local-search relaunch, Google "ranks confidence and validity," running far more verification checks — so a GBP must be legitimate, consistent across the web, and continuously fed with fresh local content like a social account.

## Key Facts

- **"Build it, Prove it, Feed it"** — the signature GBP framework (see [[Lead Generation]]).
- **List as an individual practitioner / Service Area Business** (remote, no fake office; a P.O. box triggers suspensions).
- **Name field = identity field, not keywords** — must match your legal LLC/DBA (Google cross-checks IRS/public records); keyword-stuffing causes suspensions.
- **Brand syndication** — identical name/phone/logo/headshot across GBP, website, LinkedIn, FB, IG, TikTok; Google scans socials for consistency.
- Advocates **blogging** (back as a top ranking driver), authentic local photos/video (not Canva stock), and a **5-Day Google Visibility Challenge**. Tool she recommends: **answerthepublic.com**.
- Cites trainer **Ryan McClure** for "search everywhere optimization."
