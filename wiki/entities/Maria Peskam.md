# Maria Peskam

Trainer and VA placement expert in the GoGet'Em community; creator of the D×E=V hiring formula.

## Overview

Maria Peskam teaches the GoGet'Em community how to find, hire, onboard, and retain virtual assistants. She developed the D×E=V (Desire × Execution = Value) formula and the four C's cloning system. She oversees the GoGet'Em special VA placement program at $9,500/year.

## Key Facts

- **D×E=V formula:** Desire × Execution = Value — skill is not enough; desire and execution are the real multipliers
- **Four C's cloning:** Content, Context, Criteria, Consequences — document all four before delegating any task
- **10/80/10 rule:** Give 10% context, let VA handle 80%, review the last 10%
- **GoGet'Em VA special:** $9,500/year for English+Spanish bilingual VAs on US business hours
- **Hire for attitude, train for skill:** Coachability > credentials in VA hiring
- **Cross-training mandate:** Every VA should know at least one other role
