# Gary Cox

Professional speaker and trainer on the business of speaking at $50K+ per engagement.

## Overview

Gary Cox trains entrepreneurs and real estate professionals on building speaking careers at the premium level. His training covers the mechanics of the speaking business — sizzle reel creation, stage delivery, humor as a skill, and the re-booking cycle.

## Key Facts

- **Speaking at $50K+/engagement:** Teaches the full pipeline from positioning to booking to delivery
- **"Funny is money":** Core principle — humor is a learnable delivery skill and a key differentiator in elite speaking
- **Sizzle reel:** The primary sales tool for a speaker — must show real stages, not home video
- **Chunking content:** 3–5 key ideas per keynote; audiences don't retain more
- **Testimonials:** Systematic collection from event planners and attendees; the second sales tool after the sizzle reel
- **Re-booking:** Easiest booking is a re-booking; the goal of every engagement is to get invited back or referred
