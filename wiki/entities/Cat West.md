# Cat West

eXp top producer and agent-attraction specialist; built a faith-based (Christian) personal brand that recruits agents through Instagram. (Distinct from [[Kat]].)

## Overview

Cat West (Oviedo, FL) is an eXp agent who turned an authentic, faith-forward Instagram presence into an agent-attraction engine. She teaches that "controversial" authenticity beats bland safety — that leaning into her Christian identity magnetizes aligned agents (and even non-Christian prospects who value the authenticity). She co-presented Gogo's "What Top Agents Do Differently on Instagram" panel.

## Key Facts

- **~28 agents personally sponsored** to eXp via Instagram, plus a database of ~555 qualified leads.
- Generated **~$100K in revenue share** from her sponsored organization, on top of transaction GCI.
- Defines a precise **agent avatar** ($5M+ annual production, Christian values, mom of young kids) and works a 6–12 month relationship timeline before partnership.
- Content mix: **reels** to drive the initial follow, **stories** to build ongoing relationship, lifestyle/personal content to humanize, talking-head videos as the essential (harder) format.
- Started coaching at the end of 2024 as an additional income stream.
- Philosophy: *"Just be who you are and the people that are supposed to work with you… are going to want to work with you no matter what."*
