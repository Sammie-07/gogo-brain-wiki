# Carrie Soave

International AI coach, consultant, and speaker; 15-year realtor; official AI adviser to eXp Realty and a Team Gogo agent partner.

## Overview

Carrie Soave is an AI educator who teaches real estate agents and entrepreneurs to move from using AI as a chatbot to building AI *infrastructure* that runs their business. A 15-year realtor, she reports building a $1M business in ~5 months with a single employee by leaning on AI systems. She is an official AI adviser to eXp Realty and a recurring trainer inside Gogo's [[GoGet'Em Community]].

## Key Facts

- **Signature framework:** *"ChatGPT is a Conversation. Claude is a Command Center."* — positions ChatGPT as a superior chatbot but Claude as an AI *operating system* for business automation.
- Teaches the **Autonomy Spectrum** (Level 0 chatbot → Level 1 assistant w/ connectors → Level 2 agentic Cowork/Dispatch → Level 3 fully autonomous scheduled agents).
- **Operators vs. Spectators** thesis: operators own infrastructure and scale without headcount; spectators rent tools and manually copy-paste — and the gap widens weekly.
- Maps the **five layers of Claude**: Claude Chat + connectors, Claude in Chrome, Claude Cowork (desktop "employee"), Claude Dispatch (mobile), and Claude Code.
- Advocates a **Cowork folder structure** (About Me / Outputs / Projects / Templates) plus global instructions, and markdown files for token efficiency.
- Official **AI adviser to eXp Realty**; built a $1M business in ~5 months with 1 employee using AI.
