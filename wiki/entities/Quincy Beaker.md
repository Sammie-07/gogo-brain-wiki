# Quincy Beaker

SEO expert with 25+ years of experience; GoGet'Em Community trainer; placed 1st at Tony Robbins Top 10 Influencers Mastermind.

## Overview

Quincy Beaker is a search engine optimization specialist with major corporate clients including Toyota. Gogo met him at the Tony Robbins Top 10 Influencers Mastermind, where Gogo and Krista Mashore placed 2nd and 3rd — and Quincy placed 1st despite having only 6 Instagram followers. His proof of concept: a well-optimized website can outrank social media presence, including outranking Tony Robbins' own website for Robbins-related search terms.

## Key Facts

- 25 years of SEO experience
- Corporate clients: Toyota and other major brands
- Instagram: ~6 followers at time of mastermind win
- Won Tony Robbins Top 10 Influencers Mastermind against Gogo Bethke and Krista Mashore
- His website outranked Tony Robbins' own site in a competition
- Teaches two-part SEO series inside GoGet'Em Community

## Core Teaching: Three Organic Reach Levers

1. Social media (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok)
2. Email database (owned audience)
3. SEO/website (Google search presence)

Most agents use only one. Quincy emphasizes stacking all three for durable, algorithm-resistant lead flow.

## Philosophy

> "Why do you even have a website in the first place? Know the purpose before you optimize."

> "I'd be wasting my time if the website didn't work." — on social media driving traffic to an unoptimized site

Words on your site must match what people actually search for. Google sends spiders to read your text — if the words don't match search intent, you're invisible.
