# Alex Moeller

Entrepreneur, founder of Prosperity Tax Group and Wi-Fi Money; tax credit specialist and social media figure with ~2 million followers; Day 1 speaker for GoGet'Em Community's Master Your Money week.

## Overview

Alex Moeller is a Hungarian-born immigrant entrepreneur who came to the US with $200 and no contacts. He built a social media presence of approximately 2 million followers and has leveraged that platform to educate self-employed Americans about government tax credit programs. He founded Prosperity Tax Group, specializing in the SETC (Self-Employed Tax Credit) and previously the ERC (Employee Retention Credit), and Wi-Fi Money, a community for making money online. He was invited by Gogo Bethke as the first speaker of the GoGet'Em Community's one-year anniversary Master Your Money live event series.

## The SETC Program (as taught by Alex)

The Self-Employed Tax Credit is a pandemic-era IRS program for self-employed individuals (1099, Schedule C, single-member LLC, partnership) who paid taxes in 2020 and/or 2021 and were impacted by COVID-19. It is non-taxable, non-repayable, and separate from PPP loans or ERC.

**Maximum:** $32,200 per individual; $64,400 married filing jointly  
**Deadline:** Approximately May of the year following the program's active window  
**Qualifying income:** Net taxable 1099 income only — higher taxes paid = higher credit  
**Self-attestation:** No proof of COVID impact required — only your declaration
