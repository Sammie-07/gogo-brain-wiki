# Jessica DeShane

Escrow Manager and Closer at [[Clear Title]]; co-presented the title installment of the "Anatomy of a Transaction" series.

## Overview

Jessica DeShane is the Escrow Manager and Closer at [[Jennifer Worthington]]'s [[Clear Title]] (4 years in title; previously a licensed real estate agent for 8+ years). She presented the closer/escrow side of how title works and what title needs from agents.

## Key Facts

- Escrow Manager & Closer at [[Clear Title]] (4 yrs in title; 8+ yrs prior as a licensed agent).
- Frames title as **neutral** — a third party to the contract, lender, underwriter, and state/federal law — whose job is to find problems early, not create them.
- "5 things title needs from agents": complete/accurate contract info, early notice of special situations (trust/probate/estate/divorce/POA), HOA info up front, clear communication on closing changes, identity & documentation.
- Estimates only ~25% of agents respond to the standard post-title-request email; everything must be in writing or it "doesn't count."
