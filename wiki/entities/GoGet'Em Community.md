# GoGet'Em Community

[[Gogo Bethke]]'s broader community of real estate agents and entrepreneurs — distinct from [[wiki/entities/#teamgogo]] (her agent organization within [[eXp Realty]]). Members are called **GoGetters**.

## Overview

The GoGet'Em Community is Gogo's public-facing membership community for real estate agents and entrepreneurs. ~1.5–2 years old; 350+ members at time of welcome video recording.

- **URL:** gogetemcommunity.com (also has its own app)
- **Members:** GoGetters
- **Newsletter:** Daily email — industry news, what Gogo is working on, books she's reading. Described as one of the best newsletters in real estate.
- **Topics covered:** Mindset, AI, VAs, branding, marketing, trackers, taxes, reducing taxable income, building a team, S Corp vs LLC, hiring your children, retirement, wealth building — "anything I've learned in my 14-year career"
- **Training series:** *Gogo's Social Media Challenge 2026* (GSMC) as featured 2026 program; recurring monthly lives
- **Combined volume with Team Go:** 10,000+ transactions/year
- **Support:** Natalia (community manager); chat Mon–Fri 9–5; support@gogetemcommunity.com

## Membership Tiers

| Tier | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Do It Yourself (DIY)** | Full access to video library, GSMC, monthly live, group chat, Facebook group |
| **Done With You (DWY)** | Everything in DIY + live access to Gogo's exact team (ManyChat, design, bio, website/funnels). Live Tech Support: Tues & Wed 3pm ET |

## Onboarding Steps (New Member)

1. Log in at gogetemcommunity.com or download the app
2. Check the Welcome section
3. Join the **group chat** (team available Mon–Fri 9–5)
4. Schedule **onboarding call** with Natalia (15–30 min)
5. Join the **Facebook group**
6. Apply for **100 Audit Club** (if desired)
7. Set up **affiliate link**

## 100 Audit Club

Gogo personally audits 100 members' brands in 2026 — domains, social profiles, emails, signatures, yard signs. Application required; first 100 approved get in, then closed. Approved members get a separate monthly audit call with Gogo on top of the monthly live.

## Affiliate Program

10% of every referred member's ongoing payment. Dashboard → top right dots → Affiliate → copy link. Bring 10 paying members = your membership is effectively free.

## Relationship to Team Go

[[wiki/entities/#teamgogo]] is the formal agent organization (1,660 partners within eXp Realty). The GoGet'Em Community is broader — it includes agents at any brokerage, aspiring agents, and entrepreneurs who follow Gogo's content and teaching. Some GoGetters eventually join Team Go.

- Comment "email" on Gogo's Instagram posts to subscribe to the daily newsletter
- Attend open-to-public trainings at teamgogo.team/open (last Wednesday of each month)
