# Knolly Williams

Best-selling author, speaker, and listing-focused solo agent turned team builder; guest trainer in Gogo's community. Creator of the **"5 Big Rocks"** framework and the **Triple My Listings** system.

## Overview

Knolly Williams built a listing-first solo business — **1,000+ personal transactions in his first 10 years** (~100/yr average; a peak year of **153 deals solo**), with **no cold calls, ever, and no team production** — through leverage, systems, and disciplined daily lead-gen. He has since built a **300+ agent organization at [[eXp Realty]]** using co-sponsorship, revenue share, and automation without adding hours. He came on Gogo's #teamgogo call (Jul 9, 2026) to teach the 5 Big Rocks that drive listings, income, and freedom.

## Key Facts

- **Career:** #1 in Austin by his 4th year (per the Austin Business Journal) → recruited to Keller Williams by **Gary Keller** → moved to [[eXp Realty]] 7+ years ago. Revenue share funded a 3-year, all-expenses move to Puerto Rico.
- **Listing-first thesis:** "When you list, you last." Cites *The Millionaire Real Estate Agent* (Gary Keller / [[Jay Papasan]] / Dave Jenks) as really a study of the top 100 US agents — ~100% listing-focused. More listings is also the #1 source of buyers.
- **Delegation:** runs a **46-step listing process; only 3–5 steps require a license** — he does only those and delegates the rest. "Delegate everything — you can" (he crosses out "you can").
- **Lead-generate to your personality:** high-I on [[Communication|DISC]]; never cold-called in 10 years — marketed one-to-many instead. From ~27 seller-lead tactics, pick only the 3–4 that fit you.
- **Products:** free book **Triple My Listings** (triplemylistings.com); the **Success With Listings Toolkit** (customizable seller book + toolkit, overnightsuccesstoolkit.com); a "Listing League"/Inner Circle coaching program. Free DISC test link and Gogo's [[Niche Finder]] also promoted on the call.

## The 5 Big Rocks

1. **Move from Default to Design** — most people live a default life (a Gallup poll: ~70% dislike their jobs); map what you truly "desire and require" and give your subconscious (~88% of behavior) one consistent instruction. "The universe is waiting to take your order."
2. **Do the Two** — a daily 2-hour "lead-generation bunker" doing only three things: generate new seller prospects, follow up with prospects, or build marketing plans. Behind on goals → 3 hrs/day × 6 days; on track → 2 hrs/day × 4 days.
3. **Set Up Your Sphere of Influence** — a set-it-and-forget-it **18-touch/year** system (monthly e-newsletter + quarterly postcard + call everyone twice a year), ~2 hrs/week, ~free. (NAR 2025: 70%+ of business is referral, yet only ~9–11% of past clients reuse their agent — because agents don't stay in touch.)
4. **Set Up a Farm — Be the "Mayor" of Your Area** — dominate a geographic area so callers are pre-sold; fundable for free (sponsor-funded postcards, EDDM, local-news content, community group).
5. **Delegate Everything** — the 46-step/only-3–5-need-a-license insight; own the business, not the job.
- **Bonus (agent attraction):** to reach your first 100 agents, focus on **25 directs**; simplest daily action = **invite 10 people/day** to the monthly team meeting. Don't sleep on revenue share.
