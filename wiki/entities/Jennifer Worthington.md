# Jennifer Worthington

#teamgogo agent (27 years in real estate) and founder/owner of **Clear Title**; led the four-part "Anatomy of a Transaction" training series and is a top REMLO producer.

## Overview

Jennifer Worthington is a longtime [[#teamgogo]] agent who also founded [[Clear Title]] (a Michigan title company, est. 2022). She taught the "Anatomy of a Transaction" educational series for the [[GoGet'Em Community]], using a body metaphor to map each role in a deal — clients/agents = the heart, transaction coordinators = the nervous system, lenders = the lifeblood, and **title = the spine**.

## Key Facts

- 27 years in real estate; founder/owner of [[Clear Title]] (2022).
- Created and led the 4-part "Anatomy of a Transaction" series for the community.
- Top **REMLO** producer (named in the Texana Bank top-10), including a >$500K refi for a client she had helped buy <12 months earlier.
- Core teaching: title is the coordinating partner of a transaction, not a commodity — "relationships cover a multitude of sins in high-pressure situations."
