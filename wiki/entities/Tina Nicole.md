# Tina Nicole

Real estate professional, eXp agent, and certified Working Genius trainer; friend and guest trainer in Gogo Bethke's community.

## Overview

Tina Nicole brings Patrick Lencioni's Working Genius framework to real estate teams. She trained Gogo's staff and GoGet'Em community, applying the six-type model (WIDGET) to Gogo's actual team map. A former solo agent who sold 130 homes/year before discovering she was hiring people "just like her" — the problem the framework solves.

## Key Facts

- **Working Genius (WIDGET):** Wonder, Invention, Discernment, Galvanizing, Enablement, Tenacity
- **Gogo's type:** GT (Galvanizing + Discernment) — the only G on her team
- **Three stages of work:** Ideation (W+I), Activation (D+G), Implementation (E+T)
- **Application:** Hiring by missing genius type; task assignment by genius zone; retention through genius-zone alignment
- **Personal type:** Inventor + Discerner (D at bottom, crushed by "prove it")
- **Background:** Former Michigan top producer, 130 homes/year solo, discovered Working Genius through a hiring failure pattern
