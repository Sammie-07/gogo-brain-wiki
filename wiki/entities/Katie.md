# Katie

Lender with Union Home Mortgage; 12-year collaborator with Gogo Bethke; originator of the Greatness Tracker and expanded three-tracker system.

## Overview

Katie is a lender (not a Realtor) who has worked alongside Gogo Bethke for 12+ years. She is the originator of the Greatness Tracker concept that Gogo has used throughout her career. Katie expanded the system to three trackers — Greatness Tracker, Leak Tracker, and Money Tracker — and teaches this to real estate agents as a value-add service and referral relationship builder.

## Key Facts

- **Role:** Lender at Union Home Mortgage
- **Relationship with Gogo:** 12+ years; originated the Greatness Tracker Gogo has used and taught widely
- **Three trackers she teaches:**
  1. Greatness Tracker — discover where your leads flow naturally (90-day process)
  2. Leak Tracker — track every lead by source, type, timeline, pre-approval status
  3. Money Tracker — revenue in vs. lead and service costs; net profit per source
- **Approach:** Teaches realtors (not buyers/sellers); her training is a referral partner strategy
- **GoGet'Em:** Guest trainer
