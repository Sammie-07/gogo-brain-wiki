# Allison

eXp broker-owner who built her entire Instagram strategy around a single relocation niche (Staten Island → New Jersey); GoGet'Em panel guest.

## Overview

Allison is an eXp broker-owner who rejected "everything to everybody" content in favor of one specific target — people relocating from Staten Island to New Jersey. That singular focus made her the obvious choice for a defensible, evergreen audience and produced measurable Instagram-attributed revenue. She co-presented Gogo's "What Top Agents Do Differently on Instagram" panel.

## Key Facts

- **52 deals / ~$26M volume**, with **$250K+ in transactions directly attributed to Instagram**.
- Calculates a **dollar-per-post** ROI (annual IG-attributed revenue ÷ post count ≈ ~$250/post) — every post is a measured investment.
- Chose **relocation** deliberately because "everybody needs to move from one place to another" — evergreen demand, unlike price-range or luxury pivots.
- Films **4 neighborhood vlog tours every Friday** (9am–2pm) with a camera operator; keeps a one-month content buffer; scripts with **Claude** trained on her voice.
- Hires an **in-house editor on retainer** rather than a $1,500+/mo content agency.
- Rejects separate personal/business accounts: *"Nobody's buying eXp… They're buying you."*
- Runs [[ManyChat]] → [[GoHighLevel]] pipelines to qualify leads by timeline and buyer/seller status.
