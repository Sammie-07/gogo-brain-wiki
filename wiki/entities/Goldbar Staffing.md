# Goldbar Staffing

Nationwide real-estate staffing / virtual-assistant placement company founded by [[Jason Matthews]]; also active in commercial real estate. Places and trains full-time VAs (setters, admin, transaction support) for agents and teams, positioned around the "buy back your time" delegation philosophy.

## Overview

Goldbar Staffing is the delegation/staffing partner Jason Matthews presents through in Gogo's [[GoGet'Em Community]]. Its pitch is the VA-ROI reframe: a placed assistant costing $800–$1,500/month should generate multiples of that in closed business, and the agent's job is to protect high-value time (content, being the brand, closing) while the VA absorbs the low-value work.

## Key Facts

- Founder: [[Jason Matthews]] (~$300M career NY residential sales background).
- Model: **full-time, cross-trained VAs** placed as the agent's own employee (not a rotating "rented" worker); recommends always keeping **two** cross-trained VAs so operations survive illness/turnover.
- Delegation platform/tool referenced on calls: **"Goldie."**
- Training approach: SOP-first ("I do it/you watch → you do it/I watch → you do it alone"); expect up to **90 days** to fully ramp a VA.
- Appears alongside Gogo as her recurring staffing/sales-systems authority.
