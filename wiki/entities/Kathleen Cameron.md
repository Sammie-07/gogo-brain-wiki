# Kathleen Cameron

Former registered nurse turned manifestation and mindset coach; launched coaching in 2020, scaled rapidly to 8 figures.

## Overview

Kathleen Cameron is a Canadian coach (Toronto-based, with presence in Malibu) who transitioned from RN to entrepreneur after meeting Bob Proctor in 2019. Her framework centers on manifestation through identity — you must *be* the version of yourself who has what you want before it materializes. She scaled her coaching business to 8 figures quickly after launch and speaks on stages alongside Gogo Bethke.

## Key Facts

- **Background:** Registered nurse → entrepreneurship
- **Coaching launched:** 2020
- **Based in:** Toronto (Canada) / Malibu, CA
- **Influence:** Met Bob Proctor in 2019 — pivotal moment in her transformation
- **Scale:** Reached 8-figure coaching business

## Core Teaching — Manifestation Identity

- **Identity before manifestation.** You don't attract what you want — you attract what you are. You must own the identity of the version of yourself who already has the thing before it arrives.
- **Imagination as the primary tool.** What you can vividly hold and believe in your imagination, you can experience in reality.
- **Practical + spiritual alignment.** Both mindset work (practical, logical) and spiritual connection (something larger than yourself) are required. Neither alone is sufficient.
- **Energetic alignment.** Your vibration must match what you're calling in. This is not passive — it requires active identity adoption.
