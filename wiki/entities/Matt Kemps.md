# Matt Kemps

CEO and co-founder of [[Azulio]]; founder/team lead of Lone Wolf Realty Group (eXp), a top-1%-by-volume Austin-area agent.

## Overview

Matt Kemps is a top-producing Austin/North Austin agent (luxury, new construction, relocation) and the CEO/co-founder of [[Azulio]], an AI-powered "done-with-you" real estate platform. He guest-demoed Azulio for the [[GoGet'Em Community]], showing AI agents, agentic hand-offs, and digital farming live.

## Key Facts

- Founder & team lead of **Lone Wolf Realty Group** (brokered by eXp); top-1%-by-volume in greater Austin.
- CEO & co-founder of [[Azulio]] (with [[Rob Poulton]]).
- Cited a single QR-code lead chain that produced a $1.3M listing sale plus a $4.5M buy-side ($5.8M volume) for ~15 minutes of work.
- Thesis: "It doesn't feel like a cold call now... I know what they did and what they were interested in."
- Teaches Azulio's **Thursday open AI training** (11am CST); Azulio-specific training runs Wednesdays 11am CST.
