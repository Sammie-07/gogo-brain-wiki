# Alyssa Stalker

Team Gogo member (3+ years); email marketing and organic marketing expert; founded two multiple 7-figure businesses.

## Overview

Alyssa Stalker is a GoGet'Em Community member and Team Gogo agent who has built two multiple 7-figure businesses. She trains inside the GoGet'Em Community on email marketing, emphasizing the owned-asset nature of email lists versus the borrowed-audience nature of social media followings.

## Key Facts

- Team Gogo member for 3+ years
- Founded two multiple 7-figure businesses
- Specialty: email marketing and organic marketing
- Teaches inside GoGet'Em Wednesday training sessions

## Core Teaching: Email as Owned Asset

- Email list = owned asset. Social following = borrowed audience.
- When Instagram or Facebook change their algorithm, you don't lose your email list.
- Build both, but know which one you control.
- Consistency in email matters more than volume.
- Email is a relationship channel, not a broadcast channel — be present before someone needs you.
