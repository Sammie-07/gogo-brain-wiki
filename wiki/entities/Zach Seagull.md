# Zach Seagull

Founder and CEO of Small Biz Heroes; alternative and conventional business financing expert.

## Overview

Zach Seagull has just under a decade of experience in alternative and conventional financing. He founded Small Biz Heroes, which funds millions of dollars per month across a wide range of lending products. He appeared in the GoGet'Em community at the request of Ben, an Army veteran and GoGetter member who connected him to Gogo.

## Key Facts

- **Company:** Small Biz Heroes — funds millions/month in SBA loans, lines of credit, working capital loans, merchant cash advances, equipment financing, invoice factoring
- **Expertise:** Business credit building, alternative lending, fintech lenders, business funding at every stage
- **Framework taught:** "The Hero's Journey to Capital" — step-by-step business funding from foundation to advanced
- **Key concepts:**
  - Three C's banks require: Credit, Collateral, Cash Flow (post-2008/09 all three required)
  - Fintech/private lenders only require ONE of the three C's
  - Business credit foundation: consistent address across IRS, Secretary of State, Dun & Bradstreet, Equifax, Experian Business, 411 directory, website, business checking, merchant processing
  - Build trade lines in batches of 3 with vendors reporting to bureaus
  - Four business credit scores: Paydex, Equifax Business, Experian Business, SBSS
  - Funding products by stage: startup → 0% credit card stacking; 1-3yr → revenue-based; 5yr+ → SBA
- **Philosophy:** "Just because one lender says no doesn't mean it's the end of the road."
