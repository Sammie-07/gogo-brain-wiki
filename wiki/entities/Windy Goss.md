# Windy Goss

Real estate agent and luxury listings trainer; GoGet'Em guest expert on breaking into the $1M+ market.

## Overview

Windy Goss spent nearly 20 years selling homes in the $300K–$500K price range before systematically transitioning to luxury real estate. After implementing her own systems, she landed 12 luxury listings in 12 months — including a $12M property. She teaches the "Magical Black Book Strategy" as the primary prospecting tool for expired luxury listings.

## Key Facts

- Former mid-market agent who transitioned to luxury after nearly 20 years
- Landed 12 luxury listings in 12 months including a $12M property after system implementation
- Motivating principle: sell **less**, earn **more**, reclaim **time**
- Creator of the **Magical Black Book Strategy**: pull expired luxury MLS listings, create premium physical marketing materials, FedEx to seller
- eXp Realty affiliated; featured in GoGet'Em community training (October 2025)
- Teaches luxury positioning — how to build a brand that signals luxury before you have luxury listings
