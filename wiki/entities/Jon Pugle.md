# Jon Pugle

Sphere-of-influence and referral trainer; GoGet'Em guest who teaches relationship-first business generation as the moat against AI.

## Overview

Jon Pugle teaches agents to stop chasing strangers and instead systematically work the people who already know, like, and trust them. His central thesis: automations and lead-gen tools will be commoditized by AI, but genuine human connection cannot be — so relationship mastery is both the fastest path to business and the durable competitive moat.

## Key Facts

- **Handwritten notes** as the core habit: cites a record-holding car salesman (13,000+ cars/year on monthly notes) and an agent who made **$40,000 from 40 notes** in one month; prescribes **5 notes/day**.
- **"Antidote for AI is human connection"** — the relationship game is AI-proof job security.
- **30-second gratitude videos** sent to specific people; **situational triggers** ("driving by your house," post engagement) to remove sales pressure.
- **Interest-based CRM tagging** (golf, kids, hobbies) + ChatGPT to send monthly personalized touches.
- **The 200-person power list:** warm 3 months with value, then ask each for one introduction via three-way text → expect ~150 yeses, ~75 intros, ~50 closings.
- **Board of Directors dinner:** host your 12 top referral sources, ask their input — "people participate in things they author."
- **4 client-appreciation events/year** with 6+ touches each; **connector economics** (hold vendors to ≥1 referral/year); **VIP intake form** for personalized surprises. *"Treat people's children better than you treat them."*
