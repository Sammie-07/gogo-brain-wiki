# Savannah Gutierrez

Brenham TX real estate agent; creator of the poker run open house strategy; neighborhood domination specialist in master plan communities.

## Overview

Savannah Gutierrez is a Brenham, Texas agent who markets in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. She developed the "poker run" open house format and a comprehensive neighborhood domination strategy built around consistent presence in master plan communities. Her system requires agents to attend 6 of 8 possible open house days per month and emphasizes that every detail — from Fiji water to thick corrugated signage — signals professionalism and wins business.

## Key Facts

- **Location:** Brenham, Texas; marketing to DFW master plan communities
- **Specialty:** Open house events; poker run format; progressive open houses; neighborhood domination
- **Team:** Agents required to do 6 of 8 open house days/month
- **Poker run format:** Buyers visit multiple listings, collect a playing card at each stop, best poker hand at the final stop wins a prize
- **Community strategy:** Lives in her own master plan community (grown from 150 to 2,800 homes over 10 years in real estate); also markets 4 other neighborhoods
- **Event partners:** Home stager/designer, local restaurant samples, financial advisor, insurance agent
- **Signage philosophy:** Thick corrugated plastic signs with logo; signage is brand; clients have chosen her based solely on sign quality
- **GoGet'Em role:** Community member trainer
