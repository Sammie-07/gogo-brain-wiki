# Juan Carlos Baronetti

Founder of Goldbar Staffing; eXp agent and delegation/staffing authority; guest presenter in Gogo's community on reclaiming time through hiring.

## Overview

Juan Carlos Baronetti teaches producing agents to escape low-value work by staffing correctly. He appeared as the delegation/staffing guest in Gogo's [[GoGet'Em Community]] "Fundamentals" training, framing content creation as the highest-ROI activity an owner can protect and everything else as delegable.

## Key Facts

- Founder of **Goldbar Staffing** (assistant/VA placement for real estate teams).
- Core message: most agents trade a **$50–$200/hour** skill set for **$10/hour** tasks (email, CRM, scheduling); a placed assistant reclaims ~30 hours/week to reinvest in content and revenue activity.
- Cites the data that top companies pour 25+ hours/week into content — the owner's job is to guard that time, not do admin.
- Presents alongside Gogo as her recurring delegation authority.
