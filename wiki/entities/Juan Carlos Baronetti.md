# Juan Carlos Baronetti

CEO of [[Goldbar Staffing]]; delegation/staffing authority and guest presenter in Gogo's community on reclaiming time through hiring.

## Overview

Juan Carlos Baronetti teaches producing agents to escape low-value work by staffing correctly. He appeared as the delegation/staffing guest in Gogo's [[GoGet'Em Community]] "Fundamentals" training, framing content creation as the highest-ROI activity an owner can protect and everything else as delegable. (A separate Goldbar presenter, [[Jason Matthews]], later co-taught the "Speed to Lead" session.)

## Key Facts

- **CEO of [[Goldbar Staffing]]** (assistant/VA placement for real estate teams).
- Core message: most agents trade a **$50–$200/hour** skill set for **$10/hour** tasks (email, CRM, scheduling); a placed assistant reclaims ~30 hours/week to reinvest in content and revenue activity.
- Cites [[Alex Hormozi]]: top companies pour **25+ hours/week** into content — the owner's job is to guard that time, not do admin.
- Presents alongside Gogo as a recurring delegation authority.
