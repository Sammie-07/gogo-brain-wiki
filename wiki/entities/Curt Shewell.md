# Curt Shewell

Real estate trainer specializing in time-blocking, scheduling systems, and productivity for agents.

## Overview

Curt Shewell teaches the red/yellow/green calendar system and a 4-day work week structure designed to take agents from $100K to $400K+ without adding staff. He applies discipline-based scheduling frameworks to real estate production.

## Key Facts

- **Red/Yellow/Green system:** Red = income-producing (protect), Yellow = support activities (batch), Green = personal (block in advance)
- **4-day work week:** 8:30–11am new business; 11am–12pm follow-up; afternoons for appointments; one full day off minimum
- **$100K → $400K math:** Time-blocking unlocks capacity for 2–3× more clients without more hours
- **Open house strategy:** Sundays for open houses; Monday mornings blocked for immediate follow-up
- **Follow-up system:** New leads get same-day touchpoint within 1 hour; active pipeline gets weekly check-in
