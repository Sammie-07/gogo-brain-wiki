# eXp Realty

Cloud-based ("cyberspace") real estate brokerage with no physical offices. Founded 2009; NASDAQ-traded since 2018. Operates in 50 US states and 27 countries. [[Gogo Bethke]]'s brokerage of choice; she joined October 25, 2018 (anniversary date: November 1) and has been a vocal advocate since.

**Countries (27):** Canada, UK, Australia, South Africa, India, Mexico, Portugal, France, Puerto Rico, Brazil, Italy, Hong Kong, Colombia, Spain, Israel, Panama, Germany, Dominican Republic, Greece, Turkey, Peru, Egypt, Romania, Netherlands, South Korea, Japan. (Spain: no license required — 1+ year experience only.)

## Key Facts (as taught by Gogo)

- **Cap**: $16,000/year — once an agent pays $16k in splits to eXp, they stop paying the 20% company split for the rest of that anniversary year
- **Split**: 80/20 (agent keeps 80%) until cap
- **Post-cap transaction fee**: $250 per transaction after capping
- **Icon program**: Additional milestone/benefit tier above capping
- **Revenue share**: Agents earn revenue share from agents they sponsor into eXp
- **Stock**: Agents can earn eXp stock

## Commission Math Example (Gogo's walkthrough)

For an agent with a $430k average sale price at 3% commission:
- Average commission: $12,900
- Pre-cap: agent nets $10,320/transaction (after 20% to company)
- Transactions to cap: ~6 (pays $2,580/transaction × 6 ≈ $16k cap)
- Post-cap: agent nets $12,650/transaction (after $250 fee, no split)
- To make $120k GCI: ~11 transactions total (1/month, take December off)

## Co-Sponsorship (New — May 2025)

eXp launched a co-sponsorship feature in May 2025. Incoming agents can now designate **two sponsors** — a Primary Sponsor and a Co-Sponsor — with revenue share split between both. Resolves the conflict when two people meaningfully influenced an agent's decision to join. Applies to new agents only; does not retroactively affect existing arrangements.

## 500 Legacy Program

A retirement/legacy path announced by eXp: an agent with **500+ agents across levels 1–7** who is an ICON (or is accepted into the program) can **unlock all 7 revenue-share levels** without meeting the usual FLQA requirements per level. Aimed at agents who have built a large organization but have dropped below their qualifying FLQA count and still want to be paid on every level.

**Not automatic** — it carries heavy ongoing obligations: attend and teach at eXp events, run breakout sessions, show up for quarterly meetings and eXp Cons (the requirements must be fulfilled "on the eXp level"). Gogo doesn't use it — she keeps her FLQAs and has "no qualifications, I can do whatever." It's the fallback she'd apply for only if she fell below her allowed FLQA and wasn't an ICON.

## Mentor Program

Certified Mentors earn compensation for time and knowledge given to new agents. Turns experience into a recurring income stream. More than income — it creates lasting relationships, shapes careers, and boosts the mentor's own growth. "Leaders don't just close deals — they create them."

- New agents' first 3 transactions: 60/40 split (vs. normal 80/20)
- Extra 20% goes to company; eXp pays mentor 10% of that extra 20%
- Example: mentee's $10k commission = $1,000 to the mentor for each of the first 3 transactions
- After 3 mentored transactions, agent graduates to 80/20

## REMLO (Real Estate Mortgage Loan Originator)

Agents can become licensed loan officers and earn on the mortgages their buy-side clients use. Income range: ~$1,000–$5,000+ per file (Alexis on Team Gogo averages $4,000–$5,000; first-timers often earn ~$1,000+). Qualification requires at least 6 buy-side transactions/year. Available in all 50 US states (federal MLO license — not state-specific). Not yet available in Canada.

**Key legal point:** REMLO earners are "lender assistants" — they cannot make decisions on the mortgage file (no authority to approve/deny, no say on appraisal), which is why there is no conflict of interest. Payment does NOT appear on the settlement statement.

**2026 scale:** REMLO runs through **Texana Bank** (one federal charter covers all 50 states — no 20-hour course, no SAFE exam). Pay is ~0.5% of the mortgage amount. The program topped **450+ agents** nationwide by mid-2026; **#teamgogo is the #1 REMLO group at Texana Bank**. [[Jason Dupree]] runs the weekly "Mortgage Minutes" update and the "What is REMLO?" sessions for the team.

**Link:** gogobethke.com/remlo

Agents who don't want to do active transactions can hold their license at eXp for $50/month (vs. $85/month for active). Benefits: revenue share still builds; health insurance access; eXp University; up to 3 personal deals/year.
