# Beth Manning

eXp Realty agent and organization leader; grew from $2.7M to $10M production in one year after joining Team GoGo.

## Overview

Beth Manning is a real estate agent sponsored by Gogo Bethke at eXp Realty. She came from KW (Keller Williams) and experienced one of the most dramatic documented production jumps in the GoGet'Em community: $2.7M to $10M GCI in a single year. She attributes this directly to plugging into every training — Wednesdays (production), Thursdays (tech), Fridays (agent attraction) — and associating with people performing at a higher level. She has built a 29-agent organization in eXp and is working toward replacing her husband's income so he can retire.

Her agent-to-CEO talk centers on the fundamental shift required to build a business rather than just generate transactions: stop doing the work yourself and start building infrastructure.

## Key Facts

- **Production:** $2.7M (before GoGo/eXp) → $10M (Year 1 with GoGo)
- **Previous brokerage:** Keller Williams
- **Current:** eXp Realty, sponsored by Gogo Bethke
- **Organization size:** 29 agents in eXp downline
- **Goal:** Retire her husband (Italian)
- **Trainings attended:** All three weekly calls + all events; reaches out directly to people performing at a higher level
- **Gogo connection:** Gogo sponsored Beth at eXp; Beth was on a buy-side deal involving son and mom in Michigan

## Core Teaching (Agent-to-CEO)

- **CEO works ON the business, not IN it.** The shift isn't operational — it's identity-level.
- **Build systems while still producing.** Don't exit production prematurely. Build the infrastructure first, then step back.
- **Delegate lowest-value activities first.** Hire for what consumes your time at the lowest dollar-per-hour rate.
- **Association accelerates growth.** "It's because of the people I associated with." Deliberate proximity to higher performers is not optional — it is the mechanism.
- **Put yourself out there even when nervous.** "The more nervous you are and the more you're putting yourself out there, you too can more than double your business."

## Quotes
