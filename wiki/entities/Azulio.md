# Azulio

AI-powered "done-with-you" real estate CRM/platform built on GoHighLevel, co-founded by [[Matt Kemps]] and [[Rob Poulton]].

## Overview

Azulio is an AI real estate platform (~1 year in operation) built on the GoHighLevel backend, pre-configured for real estate. It is positioned as a fully set-up alternative to self-serve CRMs (Follow Up Boss, BoldTrail, etc.). Contact: calendar.azulio.ai.

## Key Facts

- **Custom AI agents per listing:** each yard-sign QR code feeds a property-specific AI agent that knows the listing details and the agent's calendar, and can book showings by text or call.
- **Agentic AI by lead source:** AI hands off between knowledge bases/voices (e.g., an FSBO agent transfers a caller to a cash-offer specialist).
- **Conversation memory:** remembers a lead's identity, type, and prior conversation months later; any human/AI reply kills the drip automations so leads are never double-messaged.
- **Digital farming:** AI-optimized neighborhood websites (CSS stripped) get indexed by LLMs (Claude, GPT) within minutes to hours, generating leads with no ad spend; visitor sessions are recorded and skip-traced.
- **Voice AI:** ~60% answer rate, ~82% appointment-set rate; voices via ElevenLabs; outbound requires TCPA opt-in.
- **Speed-to-lead under 15 seconds** via high-trust channels (iMessage); the pitch is that most teams lose in the gap between lead arrival and first real conversation, not from bad leads. Claimed outcomes: 400%+ ROI, ~40 hours saved per week per team. The metric that matters is *appointments set*, not raw leads.
- **Custom AI agents by lead source:** conversation adapts by lead type (FSBO, cash buyer, new construction, "Austin homes with pools"), each pulling from a different knowledge base.
- **Personal iMessage/Siri assistant:** every user gets an assigned agentic AI they text or dictate to via Siri — it creates contacts, assigns tags/pipelines, blocks calendar time, and runs market research ("life's too short to open another app").
- **AI social media management** (Hootsuite-like, included in base tier): one post scheduled across all channels (GBP, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Snapchat) with per-channel customization, AI-written copy, and social listening for trending hashtags.
- **Website visitor tracking / skip tracing:** captures anonymous visitors (no form fill), skip-traces email/phone, checks DNC, records full session replay, and can surface the visitor's LinkedIn.
- **Built on GoHighLevel** with a "master mode" real estate layer; a recent GoHighLevel change lets the TCPA opt-in live on a Meta lead form. "Done-with-you": the Azulio team builds out the CRM, automations, AI agents, website, IDX, and calendar for the agent.
- **Pricing:** retail $1,695/mo + $5,000 setup; #teamgogo pricing $595/mo (no digital farming) or $995/mo (with), setup fee and retail pricing both waived (mention "team go"). Setup takes **3–4 weeks**; results in **30–60 days** (~90-day outer bound).
