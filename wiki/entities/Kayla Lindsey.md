# Kayla Lindsey

Charlotte, NC real estate agent and entrepreneur; runs multiple six-figure businesses while prioritizing family time. Creator of the "Work Less and Make More" framework.

## Overview

Kayla Lindsey is a Charlotte, NC-based real estate agent and mother who built multiple six-figure businesses while maintaining time freedom to spend with her son and pursue activities she values (beach, surfing). She became known in the GoGet'Em community for embodying the leverage model Gogo teaches — not just earning income but designing a life while doing it. Demand for her framework inside GoGetEm prompted her to launch a full course on the topic.

## Key Facts

- **Market:** Charlotte, NC
- **Businesses:** Multiple six-figure operations running simultaneously
- **Personal:** Mother; prioritizes family time and lifestyle (surfing, beach)
- **Course:** Launched "Work Less and Make More" / ReBalance Your Business after community demand
- **GoGo connection:** Community member; her story mirrors Gogo's own arc from producer to freed entrepreneur

## Core Teaching

- **The core problem:** "You work really really hard and then you don't live." Making money and having a life are not mutually exclusive — but most agents build one at the expense of the other.
- **Leverage = systems, people, processes.** Output without your personal time. The shift is from doing to becoming CEO of the operation.
- **Major shifts, not marginal improvements.** Kayla made specific structural shifts — not incremental tweaks — that freed her time while income continued.
- **Built for parents and busy professionals.** Designed for those who want to live the life they've been building, not just accumulate money while missing it.

## Quotes

> "I made some major shifts in my business and these shifts have actually allowed me to leverage my time where I could spend more time with my family doing the things that I want to do such as going to the beach surfing playing with my son — all of the things that we've been working so hard for."
