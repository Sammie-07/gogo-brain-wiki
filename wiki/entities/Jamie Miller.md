# Jamie Miller

Communication expert specializing in conscious vs. unconscious communication and how it shapes leadership, team culture, and client relationships.

## Overview

Jamie Miller is a communication coach brought into the GoGet'Em community through Team GoGo's open monthly community meeting. His focus: most communication happens on autopilot — default patterns installed in childhood, triggered under pressure. Conscious communication is the deliberate practice of choosing words, tone, and presence rather than defaulting to inherited patterns.

Gogo's connection to Jamie's work is personal: she hired Zee Slingsby for a "communication reset" herself, recognizing that even seasoned teachers benefit from dedicated attention to how they speak. Jamie's appearance reflects that same belief.

## Key Facts

- **Specialty:** Conscious communication, leadership language, psychological safety
- **Format:** Interactive — teaches, then opens for real-time Q&A on specific situations participants face
- **Gogo connection:** Brought in through Team GoGo open community meeting; Gogo's own communication coaching (with Zee Slingsby) reflects the same principle

## Core Teaching — Conscious Communication

- **Conscious vs. unconscious.** Most communication is autopilot — default patterns learned in childhood, activated under stress. Conscious communication = deliberate choice of words, tone, and presence.
- **Awareness before change.** You can't change what you don't see. Step one: identify your patterns — what works and what doesn't.
- **Communication as leadership.** How leaders speak to their teams determines team performance. Unconscious leadership creates confusion, fear, and underperformance.
- **Psychological safety.** Created through consistent conscious language. Psychological safety is not softness — it is the prerequisite for team performance.
- **Words create reality.** "I have to prospect" vs. "I get to connect with people today" — same action, different emotional reality and different energy brought to execution.
- **Self-communication.** The way you talk to yourself also follows conscious/unconscious patterns. Self-talk under stress defaults to least-conscious (most critical, most fearful) patterns.
