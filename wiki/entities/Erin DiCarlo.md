# Erin DiCarlo

Boston market real estate agent; co-founder of The Dovetail Company; nationally recognized specialist in older adult sellers; certified geriatric care manager.

## Overview

Erin DiCarlo co-founded The Dovetail Company with her business partner Lauren after 22+ years serving older adults through major life transitions. They hold multiple specialized certifications and built a "move first, sell later" model that transforms outcomes for clients navigating end-of-chapter life changes. They joined eXp Realty to expand their mission and model nationwide by training agents in other markets.

## Key Facts

- **Location:** Boston market
- **Experience:** 22+ years serving older adults
- **Organization:** The Dovetail Company (co-founder with Lauren)
- **Certifications:** Certified Geriatric Care Manager; Certified Dementia Practitioner; Certified Senior Adviser; Certified Senior and Specialty Move Manager
- **Model:** "Move first, sell later" — help client and belongings relocate, then prepare and sell the vacant home
- **Why it works:** Homes sell faster and for more money without the client and their belongings present
- **Market size:** 3+ million seniors moving this year
- **eXp:** Joined to expand mission nationwide; trains agents in other markets on their model
- **Client relationship:** Maintains long-term relationships post-transaction (client Bo invites Erin to his apartment twice yearly for Tito's on the Rocks)
- **GoGet'Em role:** Guest trainer
