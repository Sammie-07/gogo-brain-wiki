Mortgage loan officer at Union Home Mortgage with ~30 years of experience; construction loan specialist; known for systematic gifting as a relationship-building and prospecting strategy. Flamingo brand.

## Overview

Katie Lawrence is a loan officer at Union Home Mortgage with approximately 30 years in the mortgage industry. Her career path: title insurance → construction loan draws at a bank → originating loans (1994) → managing 26 direct LOs + processing staff → back to solo origination. Her depth in construction loans — a product 1 in 500 LOs truly understands — is her primary market differentiator.

She appeared in the GoGet'Em community to teach systematic gifting as a relationship-building and prospecting tool. Her personal brand identity centers on pink flamingos: a childhood love she has turned into a market-differentiating signature that agents and clients remember and reference unprompted.

## Gifting Framework (5 Steps)

1. **Have a brand** — pick something authentic, own it completely, make it the thing people associate with you
2. **Have a plan** — gifting is systematic, not random; know who, what, and when
3. **Have a list** — maintain it; know exactly who you're reaching out to
4. **Birthday club** — track birthdays, send something every year; almost no one does this → top-of-mind advantage
5. **Drip campaign for new friendships** — ongoing contact rhythm for new relationships; small token gifts as door openers; consistency beats cost
