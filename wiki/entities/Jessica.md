# Jessica

Bay Area CA eXp agent; 2x ICON; $30M+/year through community events and sphere with zero paid leads.

## Overview

Jessica is a Bay Area (inland of San Francisco) eXp agent who built a $30M/year real estate business entirely through community events and sphere of influence — without buying a single lead or running paid advertising. She got her license in 2015 while pregnant, new to the area with no existing sphere, and leveraged her event management background to create a business model that worked within her constraints as a new mother.

## Key Facts

- **Location:** Bay Area, California (inland of San Francisco)
- **Production:** $30M+ closed annually
- **Status:** 2x ICON at eXp (top 1% of the company)
- **Method:** 100% sphere and community events; zero paid leads; zero paid advertising
- **Background:** Event management and hospitality career before real estate
- **Licensed:** 2015; started with no local sphere (moved from Southern California)
- **Family:** Mom of 2 boys (17 months apart)
- **Local team:** Has a micro-team serving her local market
- **Organization:** Strong national eXp community
- **GoGet'Em role:** Webinar guest trainer on events-based business development
