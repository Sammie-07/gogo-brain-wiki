# Aaron Novello

Real estate agent licensed since 2006, 2000+ personal transactions, coach to $1M+ GCI clients, and eXp downline builder.

## Overview

Aaron Novello is a high-production real estate agent and coach who appeared in the GoGet'Em community to address the mindset required to navigate a historically difficult market. Licensed in 2006, he has personally completed 2,000+ transactions and coaches clients doing over $1M GCI annually. He has also built a large eXp Realty downline.

His GoGet'Em session focused on reframing 2024 — statistically one of the worst years for real estate transactions in 15 years — as a "season of disappointment" that is instructive rather than permanent. His approach blends market pragmatism with direct mindset coaching: no excuses, stay the course, don't confuse a season for a verdict.

## Core Teaching — Season of Disappointment

- **2024 in context.** On track for fewer transactions than 2008 (Great Financial Crisis). Toughest year to sell real estate in 15 years. "If you made it through this year, give yourself some claps."
- **Two camps exist.** A small camp is thriving. The vast majority are in a season of disappointment. Both are real — acknowledging the difficulty is not weakness.
- **Seasons are cyclical, not permanent.** Most agents treat temporary seasons as permanent conditions — then make permanent decisions (quit, pivot, stop prospecting) at exactly the wrong moment.
- **Disappointment is data, not a verdict.** The hardest seasons contain lessons that winning seasons cannot teach.
- **What you do during invisible results determines future results.** Activity during low-visibility periods is what separates the agents who bounce back from those who don't.
- **Resilience framework:** Don't quit. Don't pivot without cause. Stay visible. Keep prospecting. Serve the community.
