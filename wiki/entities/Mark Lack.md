# Mark Lack

Top-10 US influencer, Bitcoin holder, and entrepreneur. Effectively retired at 34 with tens of millions of dollars in Bitcoin. Gogo's primary resource for Bitcoin education after meeting him at a Tony Robbins mastermind.

## Overview

Mark Lack is a serial entrepreneur who has studied over 3,000 hours of Bitcoin content. He is not a certified financial advisor -- his goal is awareness, not advice. His mentor and model is Michael Saylor (MicroStrategy), who took his company from a $1.5 billion to $25+ billion market cap by using Bitcoin as a treasury asset.

Gogo says she took a full 180 on her position on Bitcoin after speaking with Mark and begged him to come speak to the GoGet'Em Community. He presented Day 5 of the 5-Day "My Money Lessons" event.

## Key Facts

- Age at time of recording: 34
- Status: effectively retired
- Bitcoin holdings: tens of millions of dollars
- Hours of Bitcoin study: 3,000+
- Mentor: Michael Saylor (MicroStrategy founder)
