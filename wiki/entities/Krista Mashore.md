# Krista Mashore

Real estate agent turned digital marketing coach; one of the top real estate coaches in the US.

## Overview

Krista Mashore built a multi-eight-figure coaching and digital marketing business for real estate agents after a career selling 2,300+ homes and landing in the top 1% of agents nationwide. She is known for her origin story — surviving severe childhood abuse, foster care, and juvenile detention — which she now shares publicly to demonstrate that success has no prerequisite biography. She and Gogo Bethke mastermind together and speak regularly; they view each other as allies rather than competitors.

## Key Facts

- **Production:** 2,300+ homes sold in career; top 1% of agents nationwide almost every year
- **Online business:** $45M+ in contracted sales generated through online marketing over 4.5 years
- **Awards:** $25M production award; $10M production award
- **Personal history:** Left home at 13 due to physical abuse (father unaware); lived on the street, in cars, and in friends' closets; juvenile hall for 3.5 months; placed in foster care; couldn't read until 4th grade despite being a straight-A student
- **Learning disability:** Reads content multiple times before it clicks; has channeled this into extreme communication mastery
- **Coaching focus:** Digital marketing for real estate agents; content creation, funnels, social media
- **Relationship with Gogo:** Regular mastermind partner; Gogo's GoGet'Em guest; "there is plenty of real estate business to go around"
- **GoGet'Em appearance:** Presented her origin story and core mindset framework; demonstrated that background is not destiny
