# Russ Laggan

VP of Training at eXp and face of eXp University; ~18-year recruiting/attraction trainer.

## Overview

Russ Laggan is the VP of Training at [[eXp Realty]] and the face of eXp University. With ~18 years in attraction/recruiting, he guest-taught a #teamgogo session reframing agent attraction as a natural **byproduct of production** rather than a separate second job.

## Key Facts

- VP of Training at eXp; runs the eXp University "Instagram Likes to Leads / ICON Lead Mastery" training (~18 contributor playbooks, including [[Gogo Bethke]]'s).
- Based in Livonia, MI; was "poached" to eXp from a fast-growing competitor.

## Core teaching: attraction as a byproduct ("Would you like fries with that?")

- House sales = "the hamburger," attraction/rev-share = "the fries." Doing attraction as a separate 20%-of-time effort triggers self-sabotage (a "house divided" nets ~80%, not 200%).
- Two spheres of influence (primary SOI + a secondary SOI of agents/vendors met through deals); ~7% SOI conversion benchmark; one listing ≈ 820 contacts via "super connectors."
- The **market update is the central magnet**: build it in Gemini, deliver as a YouTube video, and answer "How's the market?" with "I just did a market update — where should I send it?" (captures contact info, delivers value).
- Recruiting recipe: (A) where are you now, (B) where do you want to be, (C) how do we get there — mapped to **5 stages of want**. "I don't close shit. You close yourself. I provide the environment."
