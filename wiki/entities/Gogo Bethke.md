# Gogo Bethke

Retired real estate agent, team leader, coach, and content creator. Founder of [[wiki/entities/#teamgogo]] International and the [[GoGet'Em Community]]. Known for high-energy training style, math-based goal setting, and combining law of attraction with practical business systems.

## Career Timeline

- **2003** — Moved to the US from Romania at age 21. English was her third language. No connections, no college education.
- **2011** — Got real estate license. First year income: **$16,000**.
- **2011–2018** — Roughly doubled income each year. Built Facebook page (Gogo's Real Estate) to reach strangers; later moved to Instagram as primary platform for agent-to-agent conversation.
- **Year 4** (2015 approx.) — Sons started school full-time → more working hours → income broke six figures.
- **October 25, 2018** — Joined eXp Realty (anniversary date: November 1; ~7.5 years as of early 2026). Was doing 52 transactions/year at KW when she switched. "A box full of trophies and some pending deals. No money coming in monthly, no retirement plan, nothing." Built [[wiki/entities/#teamgogo]] downline; revenue share became her largest income source.
- **~2019** — Has not made less than $1M/year since. Current annual revenue: **$2.7M**, working 24 hours/week (Mon–Wed only). System produces **30,368 hours/year of total output** across herself + 14 VAs = **10.8× the output of a solo operator** (who works the average 2,080 hrs/year).
- **3.5 years before 2025 recording** — Stopped selling real estate personally. No longer holds a license.
- **Today** — Runs coaching programs, [[GoGet'Em Community]], and Gogopreneur podcast. Peak personal production was 40–50 transactions/year.

## Agent Attraction Philosophy

> "I'm looking for the people who are looking for me."

Gogo does not attempt to recruit everyone — she shares who she is and what she stands for, and the right people self-select. Values she attracts around: brand building, passive income, working part-time, multiple companies, freedom. She does zero cold calling, door knocking, or Zillow lead buying — and makes no apologies for it.

"Everybody is getable" — but on a long time horizon. Find a pebble in their shoe, or wait until their broker pushes them away. (See [[Real Estate Teams]].)

## Teaching Style

Direct, high-energy, math-first. Mixes concrete calculations (commission math, PPP) with mindset/spiritual principles ([[Mindset]] / visualization). Uses personal stories and live audience participation. Runs CPR exercises and goal-reverse-engineering sessions in real time.

Signature phrases:
- *"If I cannot imagine it, I cannot have it."*
- *"What I don't change, I accept."*
- *"No plan B."*
- *"Time is money — come to find out."*
- *"Stay in your lane."*
- *"If you want what the top 1% has, you have to do what 99% is not willing to do."*
- *"Your ego is not your amigo."*

## Business Philosophy

Money exchanges hands when you solve a problem — and a human is always on the other end. Everyone is in the people business. Stay in your lane: only do what you're naturally gifted at; hire out everything else. If it feels like work, it's a red light. Track everything down to the penny so you make business decisions, not feelings decisions.

> "If you love what you do, you never work a day in your life."

> "Today I get paid to be me because I stay in my lane."

> "The person who made $50,000 could have not made millions. That means I had to become a different person with a different mindset."

## Continuous Learning Stance

Attends events, masterminds, and seminars constantly. Has attended 8–9 Tony Robbins events. Reads every book she can. Currently employs a business coach and an SEO coach. Rule: as soon as you feel like the smartest person in the room, you're in the wrong room — find the next mastermind.
