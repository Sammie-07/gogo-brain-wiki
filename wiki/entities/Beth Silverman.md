# Beth Silverman

eXp ICON agent and [[#teamgogo]] "OG" (St. Petersburg, FL); known for an investor-focused business and "Tip Tuesday" coaching for her downline.

## Overview

Beth Silverman is a longtime [[#teamgogo]] ICON agent in the St. Petersburg, FL market, with a business that has shifted from ~90% to ~65% investor-focused. She is a regular contributor on community calls — especially on niche-pivoting, "deal fatigue," and the case for building attraction alongside production.

## Key Facts

- eXp ICON agent; Team Gogo OG; St. Petersburg, FL.
- Runs "Tip Tuesday" coaching for her downline.
- Block-calendars the monthly **Roundtable** mastermind (third Wednesday of each month).
- Core point: "Attraction is, like, a literal full-time business, the same way as production... attraction over time will pay you a lot more. Production will pay you in the immediate, not over time."
