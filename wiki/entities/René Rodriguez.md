# René Rodriguez

Behavioral neuroscience-based influence expert, bestselling author of *Amplify Your Influence*, and speaker trainer.

## Overview

René Rodriguez has studied behavioral neuroscience and leadership for over 27 years, applying brain science to influence, persuasion, and human decision-making. He is the author of *Amplify Your Influence* and runs AMPLIFII, a training platform. He was born in Michigan but now lives in Florida. He trains speakers and corporate leaders on how to communicate in a way that creates genuine buy-in rather than surface-level compliance.

His core premise: influence is the root skill underneath all other leadership, sales, and communication skills. You can have the best product, the best plan, and the best team — but without the ability to move people, none of it gets done.

## Key Facts

- **Book:** *Amplify Your Influence* (bestseller)
- **Company/platform:** AMPLIFII
- **Background:** 27+ years studying behavioral neuroscience and leadership
- **Origin:** Michigan; now based in Florida
- **Specialty:** Speaker training, corporate leadership, influence and persuasion science
- **Clients:** Includes speakers, real estate leaders, corporate teams

## Core Teaching

- **Influence as the root skill.** All other business skills — selling, leading, negotiating, parenting — are downstream of your ability to influence.
- **Behavioral neuroscience.** Human decision-making is predictable. Understanding how the brain processes risk, trust, and value allows you to communicate in alignment with how people actually decide — not how they think they decide.
- **Authentic vs. manipulative influence.** Rodriguez distinguishes between influence that serves the person being influenced (ethical) and manipulation that serves only the influencer (unethical).
- **Real estate application.** Every listing presentation, buyer consultation, and negotiation is an influence event. Agents who understand influence science close more consistently.
