# Clear Title

Michigan title company founded by [[Jennifer Worthington]] in 2022; the title partner featured in the "Anatomy of a Transaction" series.

## Overview

Clear Title is a Michigan-based title company. It works with multiple underwriters, contracts with a network of professional insured closers (enabling mobile/RON closings nearly anywhere), and is adopting stronger identity verification to combat wire/identity fraud.

## Key Facts

- Founded 2022 by [[Jennifer Worthington]]; [[Jessica DeShane]] is Escrow Manager/Closer.
- Title pull costs ~$200 per pull in most Michigan counties; turnaround ~48 hrs for title back + ~24 hrs for exam (~3–4 days), longer for rural/Upper Peninsula counties requiring manual courthouse searches.
- Adopting **Certified ID** (selfie + ID verification) to identify parties earlier than the closing table — title insurance underwriters now require stronger identity guarantees than a license at closing.
- Supports **RON** (Remote Online Notary / Zoom) closings; cited mobile closings on a Danube cruise ship, in Portugal, Uganda, a US embassy in Mexico, Disneyland, and coffee shops.
