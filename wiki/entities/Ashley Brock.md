# Ashley Brock

CEO and founder of Paid Ads Academy — a paid advertising training program for entrepreneurs and their teams. Presented a Google Ads strategy training inside [[Gogo Bethke]]'s community.

## Background

- 10+ years in paid advertising
- $200 million in total ad spend managed
- Mother of two kids under five (as of recording)
- Hosts the *Win With Paid Ads* podcast

## Paid Ads Academy

The "go-to advertising training program for entrepreneurs and their teams." Covers: Facebook, Instagram, Google, YouTube, TikTok, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Spotify, Amazon, and more.

Philosophy: teach entrepreneurs and their teams to run ads themselves, not just outsource it. Emphasis on understanding strategy, not just tactics.

## Relationship to Gogo Bethke

[[Gogo Bethke]] brought Ashley in to train her community. Gogo is described by Ashley as a "rainmaker" — someone with an unusual level of skill that brings an unprecedented amount of revenue to a company.

## Key Teaching: Google Ads for Realtors

See [[Paid Advertising]] for the full framework. Core ideas:
- Intent-based advertising (Google) vs. interruption-based (social)
- LSA vs. Google Search Ads
- Quality Score as the lever for cost efficiency
- Find + Remind strategy (Google + Facebook/Instagram retargeting)
