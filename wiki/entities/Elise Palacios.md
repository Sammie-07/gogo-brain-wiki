# Elise Palacios

Connecticut-based entrepreneur, real estate agent, and investor; co-founder of Reinvest Real Estate; co-creator of the PLAN/PITY framework.

## Overview

Elise Palacios has an exclusively entrepreneurial background — she started her first business at 11, was managing a team of employees by 15-16 (often twice her age), was financially independent at 16, and had a six-figure income by 19. She bought her first investment property at 20. She co-founded Reinvest Real Estate with Lou Raven. She has appeared twice on HGTV's House Hunters and is currently building a luxury short-term rental in the Bahamas.
