# Lauren Hilliard Ray

Dallas-Fort Worth real estate agent (eXp Realty), agent-to-agent referral specialist, and GoGet'Em guest trainer.

## Overview

Lauren Hilliard Ray built a referral-based real estate business after joining eXp Realty in 2021. She leverages agent Facebook groups, Google reviews, and Instagram optimization to generate A2A (agent-to-agent) referrals — signing 80+ referrals for her DFW market in 12 months. She retired her husband Spencer Ray from corporate through their combined real estate income.
