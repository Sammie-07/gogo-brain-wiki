# Jason Matthews

Founder of [[Goldbar Staffing]]; former top-producing New York residential agent (~$300M in career sales) turned nationwide real-estate staffing/delegation authority; recurring guest presenter in Gogo's community on reclaiming time, building sales systems, and hiring VAs.

> Note: earlier auto-captioned transcripts mis-rendered his name as "Juan Carlos Baronetti." **Jason Matthews is canonical.**

## Overview

Jason Matthews teaches producing agents to escape the "real estate roller coaster" (referral-hostage income, then plateau) by building a controllable, systemized sales-and-follow-up operation staffed with cross-trained VAs. He appeared as the delegation/staffing guest in Gogo's [[GoGet'Em Community]] "Fundamentals" training and returned to co-teach the **[[Speed to Lead - Building a Follow-Up System That Converts (Jason Matthews)|Speed to Lead]]** session, framing content creation as the highest-ROI activity an owner must protect and everything else as delegable.

## Key Facts

- Founder of **[[Goldbar Staffing]]** (assistant/VA placement for real estate teams; also does commercial real estate). Their delegation platform/tool is "Goldie."
- Real-estate background: ~**$300M** in New York residential sales before moving into staffing nationwide.
- **Delegation economics:** most agents trade a **$50–$200/hour** skill set for **$10/hour** tasks (email, CRM, scheduling); a placed assistant reclaims ~30 hrs/week to reinvest in content and revenue activity. Reframe: don't ask "what does a VA cost" ($800–$1,500/mo) — ask "what will it make" ("Give me $1,200 and I'll give you back $48,000").
- **Sales-system frameworks** (see [[Lead Generation#Speed to Lead — Sales System (Jason Matthews)]]): the three business "buckets" (new/qualified leads → conversion → nurture); the **Setter / Closer / Overseer** role stack; the funnel math (~100 leads → 5–6 real opportunities); and the Dan Martell "Buy Back Your Time" hourly-rate/opportunity-cost assessment (he built a Claude-powered version).
- Cites that top companies pour **25+ hrs/week** into content — the owner's job is to guard that time, not do admin.
- Presents alongside Gogo as her recurring delegation/sales-systems authority (put on the call by "JC").
