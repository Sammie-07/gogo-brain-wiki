# Jason Matthews

Real-estate sales-systems and staffing trainer from [[Goldbar Staffing]]; former top-producing New York residential agent (~$300M in career sales), now working nationwide in staffing and commercial real estate. Guest presenter in Gogo's community on speed-to-lead and follow-up systems. Distinct from [[Juan Carlos Baronetti]], Goldbar's CEO.

## Overview

Jason Matthews teaches producing agents to escape the "real estate roller coaster" (referral-hostage income, then plateau) by building a controllable, systemized sales-and-follow-up operation staffed with cross-trained VAs. He co-taught Gogo's **[[Speed to Lead - Building a Follow-Up System That Converts (Jason Matthews)|Speed to Lead]]** session.

## Key Facts

- From **[[Goldbar Staffing]]** ([[Juan Carlos Baronetti]]'s real-estate staffing company; also does commercial real estate). Their delegation platform/tool is "Goldie."
- Real-estate background: ~**$300M** in New York residential sales before moving into staffing nationwide.
- **Sales-system frameworks** (see [[Lead Generation#Speed to Lead — Sales System (Jason Matthews)]]): the three business "buckets" (new/qualified leads → conversion → nurture); the **Setter / Closer / Overseer** role stack; the funnel math (~100 leads → 5–6 real opportunities); and the Dan Martell "Buy Back Your Time" hourly-rate/opportunity-cost assessment (he built a Claude-powered version).
- **VA economics:** don't ask "what does a VA cost" ($800–$1,500/mo) — ask "what will it make" ("Give me $1,200 and I'll give you back $48,000"). Hire at 75% capacity; keep two cross-trained VAs; SOP-first, ~90-day ramp.
