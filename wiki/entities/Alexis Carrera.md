# Alexis Carrera

Michigan [[eXp Realty]] listing agent and [[Jason Dupree|REMLO]] in [[#teamgogo]]; a recurring GoGet'Em contributor. Creator of a **Marketing Services Agreement** ("skin in the game") that shifts listing marketing-cost risk to the client. Instagram: @thealexiscarrera.

## Overview

Alexis Carrera teaches listing-side business protection. After absorbing ~$1,200 in marketing costs when a seller fired her mid-listing, she built a contract so clients — not the agent — bear the marketing-cost risk, framing marketing as an out-of-pocket expense separate from the commission.

## Key Facts

- **Marketing Services Agreement — two options:** (A) agent advances costs and the seller **reimburses documented third-party marketing expenses** on cancellation/withdrawal/termination (optionally **escrowed with a title company** up front to guarantee payment); (B) the **client pays vendors directly** (agent may offer a commission credit at closing).
- **Rules she teaches:** always specify **a dollar amount AND a payment deadline**; clear the agreement with your broker and state before using. "You get the Porsche, you pay for the Porsche" — commission pays for negotiation/representation; marketing is a separate external cost.
- A [[Jason Dupree|REMLO]] agent; active on the #teamgogo Niche Finder and agent-attraction content (shares her process on Instagram).
