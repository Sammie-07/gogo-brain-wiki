# Stu Massengill

One of Tony Robbins' top Peak Performance Strategists; GoGet'Em guest trainer (July 2024).

## Overview

Stu Massengill is a Peak Performance Strategist trained under Tony Robbins who brought the Robbins framework to Gogo's GoGet'Em community in a session on "Sustaining Peak Performance in Any Economy." His teaching centers on the SAM framework: Strategy, Action, Mindset.

## Key Facts

- Tony Robbins–trained Peak Performance Strategist
- Featured in GoGet'Em Community training (July 31, 2024)
- Delivered the **SAM Framework**: Strategy, Action, Mindset as three pillars of success
- Emphasis: 80% of success is mindset; 20% is the "how-to"
- Teaching: habits and patterns produce results — examine the pattern, not just the outcome
- Promoted discounted access to Tony Robbins' Unleash the Power Within (UPW) event as part of session
