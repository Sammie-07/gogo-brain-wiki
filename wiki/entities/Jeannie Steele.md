# Jeannie Steele

Top-producing solo [[eXp Realty]] agent in [[#teamgogo]] who dominates a tiny rural market (Broadwater County, Montana; ~8,000 people) through a **give-first**, community-embedded brand. Guest trainer in [[GoGet'Em Community]]; the living case study for "be the name they already know."

> Auto-captions mangled her name ("Genie/Jeanie/Jenny"). **Jeannie Steele is canonical.**

## Overview

Jeannie Steele proves you don't need a big market, budget, or following. She built near-total market share of an 8,000-person county by giving her way in — handmade gifts, institutional gratitude, and town-tradition events — rather than cold-calling or door-knocking. The tactical, rural, give-first counterpart to [[Kirby Chan]]'s "dominate a small market" and [[Knolly Williams]]'s "be the mayor."

## Key Facts

- **Rankings (2025):** #38 in the US by closed transactions, ~#85 by volume among eXp individual producers; **#1 by transactions, #5 by volume inside #teamgogo** (~1,700 agents).
- **Production:** ~85 closings solo, nearly **$30M**, almost entirely within one **8,000-person county** — captured ~85 of ~86 county transactions.
- **Give-first system:** she + her 4 kids handmake **~100 gifts/month**, delivered *unbranded* to Main Street businesses; branded keepsakes (a funny Kleenex) compound over years.
- **Institutional gratitude:** holiday gifts to the sheriff's dept (29 + K-9), the hospital (~100), and assisted living.
- **Town-tradition events:** free catered Ghost Stroll (Halloween, ~1,000 kids) and Christmas Stroll at her physical Main Street office.
- **Niche:** builder/land — works with 5 builders; land + spec-home deals drive her transaction count (hence high transaction rank, lower volume rank).
- **Positioning:** "I don't compete" (12+ yrs, 5 competitive listings, 0 wins) and a **non-negotiable listing fee**; wins on speed and marketing ("the best *marketer* wins"), including a trained-from-scratch photographer and pro photos even on cheap land.
- Rebuilding systems with AI (Claude) after losing a 5-year assistant.
