# Craig Siegel

WSJ and USA Today bestselling author, performance coach, and host of *The CLS Experience* podcast. Speaker on identity transformation and energetic expansion.

## Overview

Craig Siegel is a former Wall Street trader turned mindset and performance coach. He is the author of *The Reinvention Formula* and hosts *The CLS Experience*, a top-ranked podcast. He has run 9 marathons. He met Gogo Bethke at a Dallas event, and she brought him into the GoGet'Em community.

His core teaching: identity — not effort, tactics, or market conditions — determines your ceiling. Until you change who you are at the identity level, behavior changes are temporary. He integrates Kabbalah (specifically the concept of expanding the vessel to receive more light) into practical business coaching.

## Key Concepts

- **Energetic set point:** Each person has an invisible ceiling that caps their results. Effort without raising this set point produces diminishing returns.
- **Expanding the vessel (Kabbalah):** You must increase your capacity to receive before more can arrive. Cannot pour more water into a vessel that isn't larger.
- **Quantum leaps:** Transformation is not linear. When identity shifts, results can jump dramatically without proportional increase in effort.
- **Identity over strategy:** The strategy is rarely the problem. The person executing it is.
