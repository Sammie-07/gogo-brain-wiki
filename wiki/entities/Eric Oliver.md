# Eric Oliver

VP of Channel Partnerships at Cost Segregation Authority; 7-year cost segregation specialist.

## Overview

Eric Oliver is VP of Channel Partnerships at Cost Segregation Authority. His accounting degree led him to discover cost segregation after already investing in real estate. He has been doing cost segregation for approximately 7 years and delivered the technical presentation to the GoGet'Em community alongside Dawn Lavanway.

## Key Facts

- **Company:** Cost Segregation Authority — VP of Channel Partnerships
- **Background:** Accounting degree; discovered cost segregation while job searching and investing in real estate; "hasn't turned back since"
- **Tenure:** ~7 years in cost segregation
- **Key frameworks:**
  - 45L credit: $500-$5,000/door for energy-efficient construction (2023-2032)
  - Cost segregation = accelerated depreciation on real estate assets
  - Three IRS tests for segregatable components
  - Bonus depreciation (currently 60%, phasing down)
  - Catch-up depreciation for existing properties
  - Rate arbitrage: deduct at income rate, recapture at capital gains rate
- **Resources:** CSA reports are 40-50 pages (vs. industry standard of 100-200 pages), designed for CPA readability
