# Eric Cruz

VP of Sales at Propy; blockchain-based real estate settlement company.

## Overview

Eric Cruz is the VP of Sales at Propy, the first company to NFT a property, transact real estate with cryptocurrency, and record a deed on a blockchain. He presented to the GoGet'Em community on how blockchain technology is solving the wire fraud and title fraud epidemic in real estate, and how Propy enables under-24-hour closings through on-chain transactions.

## Key Facts

- **Role:** VP of Sales, Propy
- **Location:** St. Pete, Florida
- **Propy:** First to NFT a property; first to use crypto in real estate transaction; first to record deed on-chain
- **Track record:** ~$5 billion in transactions, zero wire fraud, zero title fraud
- **Technology:** Blockchain-based title, escrow, and settlement; Coinbase collaboration
- **Speed:** Settlements under 24 hours vs. traditional 30–45 days
- **Wire fraud context:** $10B+ lost to wire fraud since 2022; title insurance has less than 4% claim rate
- **KYC/AML:** Full Know Your Customer + Anti-Money Laundering verification built into purchase process
- **GoGet'Em role:** Guest presenter on proptech and crypto real estate
