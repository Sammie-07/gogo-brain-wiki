# Austin Rankin

Face-based real estate coach; top producing listing agent; creator of the Self-Selling Seller System.

## Overview

Austin Rankin is a real estate agent and coach from a small town in Kentucky who went from zero sales experience to $116M+ closed in 4 years, becoming the #1 agent in his company. He built his business without buying leads or building a personal brand. A self-described introvert who can't spell, he mastered conversation-based selling through sheer repetition and refinement of his questioning framework, which he now teaches as the "Self-Selling Seller System."

## Key Facts

- **Production:** $116M+ closed in 4 years; 70+ homes/year; #1 agent in his company
- **Background:** Small town Kentucky; no sales experience; shy introvert; poor speller
- **Method:** Cold calling with specific conversation framework; zero lead buying, zero paid ads, zero personal brand
- **System:** Self-Selling Seller System — conversation framework that helps sellers convince themselves to work with the agent
- **GoGet'Em:** Second appearance; this session went deeper on conversation structure and scripting
- **Teaching approach:** Requires participants to write and participate; tangible skills and scripts vs. mindset fluff
- **Core ability:** "He asks questions so nonchalantly that clients convince themselves to work with him" — Gogo's description
- **Style:** "Face-based" — everything rooted in real human conversation, not automation or platforms
