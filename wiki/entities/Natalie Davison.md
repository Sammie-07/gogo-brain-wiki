# Natalie Davison

eXp ICON agent and keynote speaker (New Brunswick, Canada); creator of the "Rabbit Hole" / Trust Ladder framework for warm lead generation; first guest in #teamgogo's ICON guest-teacher series.

## Overview

Natalie Davison (@natalie_i_davison) is an eXp ICON agent based in New Brunswick, Canada (avg price point ~$389K), known for "wild optimism and truth-telling." A former marketing-business owner, she returned to real estate in 2021 and hit ICON production immediately because she had already built her "rabbit hole." She kicked off [[#teamgogo]]'s ICON guest-teacher series.

## Core Framework: The Rabbit Hole (Trust Ladder)

Five stages from stranger to advocate:
1. **Visibility** — they see you.
2. **Familiarity** — they recognize/feel they know you.
3. **Trust** — they believe in you.
4. **Opportunity** — referrals, repeat clients, podcasts, stages, partnerships flow in.
5. **Advocacy** — people talk about you in rooms you're not in (the goal — eliminates cold-calling).

Build a "rabbit hole" of content so when someone meets you (e.g., at pickleball) and looks you up, there's a journey to consume that converts them from cold to warm before the next encounter.

## Key Facts

- Thesis: have **fewer but more intentional** conversations with people who already care who you are — "one of those is worth more than 25 fake, transactional ones."
- It costs **6–20× more** to acquire a new lead than to keep an existing one.
- Niche-community ROI (her own): "football mom" = 2–3 transactions/year; pickleball = 4 transactions in ~1.5 years and growing.
- "Audition" principle: how you show up and market yourself in your community **is** the audition; brand your hobbies.
- "Be yourself as a daily practice"; cites Marianne Williamson's "Our Deepest Fear."
