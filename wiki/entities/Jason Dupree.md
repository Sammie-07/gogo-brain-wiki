# Jason Dupree

#teamgogo's mortgage partner (Texana Bank) who opens the weekly community calls with a "Mortgage Minutes" market update and runs the REMLO program.

## Overview

Jason Dupree is a loan officer at Texana Bank and the recurring mortgage partner for [[Gogo Bethke]]'s [[GoGet'Em Community]] / [[#teamgogo]] calls. He delivers a short weekly rate/market update at the top of the Wednesday trainings and champions the **REMLO** program, which lets real estate agents become licensed loan originators in all 50 states under the bank's federal charter.

## Key Facts

- Loan officer at **Texana Bank**; recurring "Mortgage Minutes" presenter on #teamgogo calls.
- Runs the weekly **"What is REMLO?"** info session (typically 3pm ET the same day as the Wednesday call); link: gogobethke.com/remlo.
- Spring 2026 rate commentary: average ~6.5–6.65%, down roughly 40 bps from a year prior; repeatedly predicted "the biggest summer in real estate in years."
- Tracks REMLO performance for the team: #teamgogo is the **#1 REMLO group at Texana Bank**; in one month team REMLOs earned $21K+ in lender commission (company-wide REMLO commission $107K+, $21M / 65 units).

## REMLO program (Real Estate Mortgage Loan Originator)

- Agents become licensed loan officers in all 50 states **day one** under the bank charter — no 20-hour course, no SAFE exam (you become a bank employee).
- **Qualification:** 6 buy-side transactions in the prior 12 months.
- **Pay:** ~0.5% of the mortgage amount (~$1,500 on a $300K loan); per-file averages cited at ~$1,600–$2,200. Also earns on HELOCs/bridge loans previously referred out.
- Program topped **450+ agents** nationwide by mid-2026; framed as a "4-year head start" on competitors.
- REMLO-of-the-month examples: Melissa Albert (+$5,800 in a month), J.R. St. Julian, Kelly LaFave, [[Jennifer Worthington]], Morgan Tower.
