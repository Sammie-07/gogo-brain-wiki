# Sekou Pyle

Real estate prospecting and cold-calling coach; GoGet'Em guest expert (October 2023).

## Overview

Sekou Pyle is a high-energy, old-school cold-calling coach who works exclusively with motivated real estate agents. His philosophy mirrors Gogo's: he only coaches agents who are already leaning in on their own businesses. Gogo's endorsement is emphatic — "If anybody can make me cold call in this world, that would be you."

## Key Facts

- Cold-calling and prospecting specialist — phone-in-hand, direct outreach methodology
- Works **only** with highly motivated agents — selectivity is part of his brand
- Featured in GoGet'Em Webinar (October 18, 2023)
- Gogo's quote: "I would buy anything that you're selling in every color twice over"
- Core teaching: agents fail at cold calling through inconsistency, not from rejection
- Uses Muhammad Ali quote: "I don't start counting until it hurts" as a mindset anchor for persistence
