# Lou Raven

Connecticut-based real estate agent, investor, and coach; co-founder of Reinvest Real Estate; co-creator of the PLAN/PITY framework.

## Overview

Lou Raven came from a corporate background managing 400 employees across 30 retail locations before transitioning to real estate and investing. He has been investing for 15 years, has been an agent for 7 years, added $1M to his net worth in 3 years, and built 3 six-figure businesses in 3 years. He co-founded Reinvest Real Estate with Elise Palacios and released the Legacy Playbook in December 2024.

## Key Facts

- **Location:** Connecticut (Farmington area)
- **Corporate background:** Managed 400 employees, 30 retail locations
- **Real estate:** Agent 7 years; investor 15 years
- **Results:** Added $1M net worth in 3 years; 3 six-figure businesses in 3 years
- **Organization:** Co-founder of Reinvest Real Estate with Elise Palacios
- **Framework:** PITY = Planning, Implementation, Tracking, Increasing Profit; PLAN = their business plan format
- **Legacy Playbook:** Released December 2024 — formal publication of their frameworks
- **Family:** Father to a 2-year-old; shifted to more family time as a result of building scalable systems
- **GoGet'Em appearances:** Two sessions (PLAN framework overview + Increasing Profit deep dive)
