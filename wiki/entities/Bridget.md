# Bridget

30+ year real estate veteran with a lending background; creator of Commercial Edge Training for residential agents.

## Overview

Bridget entered real estate through lending — she was a top producer in both residential and commercial loans before transitioning to real estate sales. She created Commercial Edge Training, a course and mentorship program that teaches residential agents how to break into commercial real estate without abandoning their existing business. She is a GoGet'Em community member who appeared to train members on commercial basics.

## Key Facts

- **Experience:** 30+ years in real estate
- **Background:** Top producer in residential and commercial lending → transitioned to commercial real estate sales
- **Commercial Edge Training:** Creator; designed specifically for residential agents wanting to enter commercial
- **Core thesis:** Same license, just need a mentor and the knowledge. Commercial is learnable.
- **Key tools she teaches:** Cap rate analysis, 1031 exchanges, portfolio advisory, deal sourcing
- **Community:** Lives and works in an affluent area where clients naturally own commercial properties
- **GoGet'Em:** Community member who volunteered to train peers
