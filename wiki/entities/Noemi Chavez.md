# Noemi Chavez

Digital marketing specialist; Google My Business optimization expert; cousin of Sotero Lopez (Team Gogo); GoGet'Em Wednesday community trainer.

## Overview

Noemi Chavez teaches real estate agents how to fully optimize their Google Business Profile — the most overlooked free marketing tool available to agents. She was introduced to the GoGet'Em community by her cousin Sotero Lopez at the monthly public Wednesday meeting. She reviewed Sotero's Google Business profile and showed him "everything he was doing wrong" — the result was compelling enough to bring her in front of the full community.

## Key Facts

- **Specialty:** Google Business Profile (Google My Business) optimization for real estate agents
- **Family connection:** Cousin of Sotero Lopez, Team Gogo member and Wednesday meeting host
- **GoGet'Em appearance:** Monthly public Wednesday session
- **Core message:** Agents think Google My Business = just posting addresses; in reality it's a powerful visibility and lead generation tool when fully optimized
