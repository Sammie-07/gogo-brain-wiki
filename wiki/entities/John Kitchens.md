# John Kitchens

Real estate coach, host of *Expert Mentors Live*, and creator of the Agent to CEO framework.

## Overview

John Kitchens coaches real estate agents on the transition from solo producer to CEO. He hosts *Expert Mentors Live*, a recurring interview and training series. Gogo Bethke has appeared annually on Expert Mentors Live for six consecutive years as a New Year kickoff guest. Kitchens is known for applying CEO-level business frameworks — vision architecture, machine mindset, theory of constraints, and people systems — to real estate teams and solo agents.

## Key Facts

- **Expert Mentors Live:** Host and producer. Gogo's annual New Year appearance has been a tradition for six years running.
- **Agent to CEO framework:** Core curriculum focused on transitioning from in-the-machine operator to on-the-machine CEO.
- **Key frameworks taught:**
  - Vision = Core Values + Purpose + BHAG
  - Machine Mindset (work on the machine, not in it)
  - Theory of Constraints (identify the bottleneck, fix it, repeat)
  - Traffic → Conversion → Fulfillment
  - Hormozi Value Equation
  - Roosevelt "Man in the Arena"
  - Freedom = Financial Number + Perfect Day
  - 8 stages of business evolution
  - Imitate before innovate
- **People & Team Building (Ep. 149):** Covers FAC (Functional Accountability Chart from Verne Harnish's *Scaling Up*), agreements vs. expectations, the enthusiastic rehire test, ones-and-threes performance scoring, and compensation-drives-behavior principle.
- **Expert Mentors Live Ep. 184:** Leadership as the primary constraint — your business is elastic to your leadership ceiling. Self-leadership first. Community: Honey Badger Nation (7,000+ members). Business evolves in stages but stops at the leader's level until the leader grows.
